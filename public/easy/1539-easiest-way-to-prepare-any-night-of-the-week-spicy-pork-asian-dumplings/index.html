The ingredients needed to prepare Spicy Pork Asian Dumplings:
  1. You need Meat
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 lb Pork Tenderloin, minced
  3. Provide 30 Dumpling Wraps
  4. You need Vegetables
  5. You need 1 stick Celery, minced
  6. Get 4 clove garlic, minced
  7. Get 3 Red Chile Peppers, minced
  8. Use 1 medium Ginger Root, minced
  9. Prepare 1 Yellow Onion, minced
  10. Prepare Spices
  11. Provide 1 dash Salt n Peppa
  12. Take 1 dash Chinese 5-spice
  13. Provide 1 1/2 tbsp szechuan sauce
Steps to make Spicy Pork Asian Dumplings:
  1. Use a food processor to mince all ingredients separately before they are all mixed together.
  2. Cook filling mixture in frying pan with sesame oil
  3. Drain pan and let cooked mixture cool down
  4. Fill the dumplings with 1tbsp of filling, seal with water or egg wash
  5. Finish by frying dumplings in a pan of shallow oil or steam/boil them.
  6. Serve with a chilled sweet chili sauce, houisen sauce, sweet and sour or any other Asian dipping sauce
