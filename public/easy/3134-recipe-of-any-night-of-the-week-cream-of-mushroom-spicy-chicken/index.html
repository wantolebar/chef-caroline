The ingredients needed to cook Cream of mushroom Spicy chicken:
  1. Prepare 26 oz cream of mushroom
  2. Provide 1 medium onion
  3. Use 1 medium bell pepper
  4. Provide 5 pinch creole season
  5. You need 2 pinch red pepper flacks
  6. Provide 1 box chicken wings
Instructions to make Cream of mushroom Spicy chicken:
  1. First season chicken wings with Creole seasoning
  2. Then place chicken into a cooking pan
  3. Then add the medium onion and bell pepper
  4. Next add about 2 tablespoons of water to the pan
  5. Than cover your pan with aluminum foil
  6. Place in a 300 and 50 degree oven to cook for 30 minutes
  7. Once your chicken is done then you will open the 26oz can of cream of mushroom
  8. Pour on top of the chicken make sure the chicken is completely covered
  9. Then placed back in the oven for about 10 to 15 minutes
  10. Now you're ready to serve
