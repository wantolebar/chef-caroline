The ingredients needed to cook Oven Baked Pulled Pork with Apple Cider BBQ Sauce:
  1. Take Rub
  2. Use 7 lb Pork sholder
  3. You need 3 tbsp Cayenne
  4. You need 3 tbsp Dry Mustard
  5. Prepare 4 tbsp Garlic Powder
  6. Provide 1 tbsp Paprika
  7. Prepare 4 tbsp Ground Black Pepper
  8. Get 1/3 cup Salt
  9. You need 2 liquid smoke
  10. Get BBQ Sauce
  11. Take 1 cup Yellow or Brown Mustard
  12. Use 2 cup Ketchup
  13. Take 1 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
  14. Prepare 2 cup Packed Brown Sugar
  15. Provide 3 clove Garlic Crushed
  16. You need 1/4 cup Honey
  17. Get 2 tbsp Molasses
  18. Take 1 tbsp Cayenne
  19. Use 1/4 cup Worcestershire Sauce
  20. Provide To Serve
  21. Get 16 Hoagie or Hamburger Buns
Instructions to make Oven Baked Pulled Pork with Apple Cider BBQ Sauce:
  1. Preheat oven to 250°F
  2. For Rub
  3. Mix all the rub ingredients together in a bowl
  4. Put Rub on Pork shoulder make sure to coat it good all over
  5. Put Pork Shoulder in a Dutch oven and put in oven for 6 hours or until internal temp is between 170 to 180°F with thermometer
  6. While Pork shoulder is baking Using Crock pot mix all the sauce ingredients in Crock Pot and turn on low stirring occasionally
  7. When Pork shoulder is done using two forks seperate meat into shreds and put in crock pot stir it in well
  8. Then let it sit on warm for about 45 minutes stirring occasionally
  9. Then serve on Hoagie buns or Hamburger Buns with Creamy Coleslaw recipe also posted by me
  10. Footnote add drippings and fat with rub to the sauce with the meat adds to the flavor only thing don't use is the bone from the shoulder
  11. I will sometimes soak pork shoulder overnight with liquid smoke and water in large pot
