Instructions to make Easy Restaurant Style Chicken Biryani:
  1. Marinate chicken pieces with salt, lemon juice and turmeric powder for 10 minutes.
  2. Boil 6 cups of water in high heat.When it boils vigorously,add the soaked rice, whole spices and salt.Cook till it's 80% done and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a kadai,add chopped onion and fry until golden brown, take out half quantity of fried onion & set aside.
  4. In remaining fried onion,add ginger garlic paste and mix well.
  5. Add chicken and fry until chicken changes colour.
  6. Add black peppercorns,star anise, cinnamon stick, cloves, black cardamom, green cardamom, cumin seeds,red chilli powder, turmeric powder,garam masala powder, coriander powder, cumin powder,salt,bay leaf and tomato cubes,stir well & cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Now add yogurt and mix well.
  8. Add green chillies and plums,stir well.
  9. Now add green coriander leaves and mint leaves,give a nice stir.
  10. Cover and cook for 6-8 minutes.
  11. Take out half quantity of cooked chicken & gravy and set aside.
  12. Spread fried onion on rest of the gravy.
  13. Now add a layer of cooked rice,place chicken pieces along with little gravy adding 1/2 tsp lemon juice,garam masala, ghee and fried onion over it. This is how first layer biryani done.
  14. Now prepare last layer with rest of the rice. Sprinkle rest of the gravy,fried onions, garam masala powder,yellow food colour,liquid milk,rose water,kewra water and ghee with a spoon.
  15. Now cover the kadai with an aluminium foil and close it with the lid.Let it cook on a low flame for 10 minutes.
  16. Open the lid before serving.
  17. Take some portions of biryani in a plate.
  18. Garnish with some orange and tomato slices.
  19. Chicken biryani is ready to serve.
