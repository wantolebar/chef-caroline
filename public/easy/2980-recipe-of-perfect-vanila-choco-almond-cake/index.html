The ingredients needed to make Vanila Choco Almond Cake:
  1. Get eggs
  2. Use and half cup castor sugar
  3. Provide oil/butter
  4. Prepare maida leveled
  5. Provide baking powder leveled
  6. Provide vanila essence
  7. You need milk
  8. Provide coco powder
  9. Use chopped almonds
  10. Get Any Flavored jam for glazing
Instructions to make Vanila Choco Almond Cake:

  2. Secondly hand whisk ya electric beater se eggs white ko 7-8 minuts ke liey beat karein untill yh foamy sa hojaey or sath sath is mn Castor sugar add krty rahen.
  3. Thirdly is mn butter/oil add karein or isy 2-3 minut ke liey itna beat karein ke yh aik jan hojaey. Sath mn one by one egg yolk b add krty rahen.or vanila essence drops b dal dein.
