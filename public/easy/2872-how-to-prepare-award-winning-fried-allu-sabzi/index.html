The ingredients needed to cook Fried Allu sabzi:
  1. Provide Ingredients for potatoes frying
  2. Get 1/2 kg potatoes
  3. Get Oil for deep fry
  4. Get Ingredients for sabzi
  5. Use 1/3 cup oil
  6. Use 1 small onion
  7. Get 3-4 curry leaves
  8. Prepare 1 tsp cumin seeds
  9. Prepare 2 small tomatoes
  10. You need 1/2 tsp red chilli flakes
  11. You need 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  12. Provide 1 tsp bhuna masala
  13. You need 1/2 tsp Kalonji
  14. Take Pinch sour salt
  15. Prepare Salt to taste
  16. You need Fried potatoes
  17. Prepare For garnish
  18. You need 1 Sliced green chilli
  19. Provide Chopped corriender
Steps to make Fried Allu sabzi:
  1. Peal wash and cut potatoes in jullion shape then deer fry them until done it will take almost 15 mints (don't let them crispy)
  2. Now add oil in a pan then add sliced onions in it then let it golden brown then add cumin seeds and curry leaves..
  3. Then add add tomato slices and simmer for 5 mints then mashed them with spoon then add turmeric powder, red chilli flakes, salt, sour salt, bhuna masala and Kalonji and mix broil it until oil comes upward then add fried potatoes in it
  4. Them mix and further cook for 5 mints then garnish corriender and sliced green chilli and serve…
