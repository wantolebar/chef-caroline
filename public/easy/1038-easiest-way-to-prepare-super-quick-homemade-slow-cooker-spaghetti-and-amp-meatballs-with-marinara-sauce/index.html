Steps to make Slow cooker spaghetti & meatballs with marinara sauce:
  1. Marinara sauce:
  2. Dice onion and mince garlic, basil, and oregano; add to slow cooker.
  3. Add rest of ingredients to slow cooker; cook for 8 hours.
  4. Meatballs:
  5. In a large bowl mix together ground beef, beaten egg, & bread crumbs.
  6. Add rest of ingredients; mix well.
  7. Roll meat mixture into 12 - 2" balls; bake @ 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
  8. When marinara sauce is almost done; add the meatballs to the sauce and finish slow cooking.
  9. Spaghetti:
  10. Cook pasta according to the pkg. directions; adding Olive oil & Salt to the water. When done; drain and serve with sauce & meatballs.
