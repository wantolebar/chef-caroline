The ingredients needed to make Indian Curry:
  1. Use Olive Oil
  2. You need Onion Large
  3. You need Garlic Paste
  4. You need Turmeric
  5. Take Chili Powder
  6. Get Cayenne Powder
  7. Provide Curry Powder
  8. You need Himalayan Salt
  9. Take water
  10. Use Cream Cheese (1oz)
  11. Get Ground Beef
  12. Take Chicken, cubed
Steps to make Indian Curry:
  1. If using ground beef, brown the beef separately and drain.
  2. Saute onions in Olive oil
  3. Mix all ingredients except meat, water and cream cheese, mix well.
  4. Add chicken until cooked. Add browned beef if using ground beef.
  5. Add water, cover and simmer on low heat for 30 mins.
  6. Add cream cheese, simmer another 10 mins and mix until smooth.
