The ingredients needed to cook Blueberry Cinnamon Bread Pudding:
  1. Take 1 loaf brioche, torn into small pieces and left out overnight to dry
  2. Prepare 4 eggs
  3. Prepare 3 cups whole fat milk
  4. Get 2 tsp unsalted butter
  5. Take 1 cup sugar
  6. You need 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  8. Provide 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Take 1/4 tsp lemon zest
  10. Provide 1 cup fresh blueberries
  11. You need 1 large baking pan, generously greased - or if you have faith in your nonstick pan, then go forth and do that
Instructions to make Blueberry Cinnamon Bread Pudding:
  1. Spread the pieces of broiche evenly inside your baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar until pale and frothy, then whisk in the milk, nutmeg, cinnamon, vanilla extract and lemon zest.
  3. Poured this mixture over the bread, and add the blueberries. Mix it all in, making sure that every piece of bread is soaked. I got in there with my hands and mixed it all in, but you can use a spoon if you want. Leave it out in room temperature for about 40 minutes to let the egg mixture soak into the bread. While it's soaking, preheat the over to 300F (148C)
  4. Bake the pudding for 45 minutes at 300F, and then turn it up to 425F (218C) for another 15 minutes after that to get a nice browning.
  5. Let cool, then serve with your favorite toppings - recipes for such coming soon!
