The ingredients needed to make Raven's Cinnamon Rolls with Cream Cheese Icing:
  1. Prepare whole milk (scalded)
  2. Get unsalted butter not margarine
  3. Get granulated sugar
  4. You need active dry yeast
  5. Use eggs, beaten
  6. Prepare salt (if using salted butter, omit salt)
  7. You need all purpose flour plus more for dusting
  8. Provide Filling:
  9. Prepare packed brown sugar
  10. Get cinnamon. Or to taste
  11. Provide softened butter
  12. Take Cream Cheese Icing:
  13. Prepare package softened cream cheese
  14. Take softened butter
  15. Provide confectioner sugar
  16. Provide vanilla
Instructions to make Raven's Cinnamon Rolls with Cream Cheese Icing:
  1. Bring milk to just under a boil. When bubbles form around edge turn off heat and remove.
  2. Add butter and granulated sugar to hot milk. Stir until butter is melted and sugar dissolved.
  3. When cooled to lukewarm, add yeast. Let proof for 10 minutes or until frothy.
  4. After yeast has proofed, add eggs and flour and salt last.
  5. If using bread machine use dough cycle. If using mixer with hook attachment, mix on low speed until combined. Then on medium speed for around 8 to 10 minutes.
  6. Knead until dough is smooth and elastic. It should pull away from sides of bowl. It should be soft and not stick to your finger when touched.
  7. If dough is sticky, add a couple tablespoons of flour until no longer sticky. Add couple drops of water if to dry.
  8. Oil a large bowl and roll dough around to cover. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise until double in size.
  9. Punch down dough. On floured surface, roll out dough into 9x13 rectangle.
  10. Mix softened butter, brown sugar and cinnamon until a creamy paste and well combined.
  11. Spread filling generously onto dough leaving 1 inch edge at one end. This helps to seal edges by leaving off the filling.
  12. Starting with long edge, roll tightly in jelly roll fashion.
  13. When you get to opposite edge, pull dough up and pinch to seal together. Pinch and tuck in ends also.
  14. With serated knife, pizza cutter or dental floss, cut rolls into 12 to 15 pieces.
  15. Place rolls touching each other in oiled 9x13 baking dish.
  16. If you want bigger rolls, cut wider slices and you can also roll out dough bigger to make more rolls before filling.
  17. Cover with plastic wrap or towel and let rise in warm place until double in size.
  18. Heat oven to 350. Bake rolls 15 to 20 minutes or until set in center. Cover with foil if browing to fast after 15 minutes.
  19. While rolls are baking, mix the cream cheese, vanilla, butter and powdered sugar until smooth and creamy. If to thick, add little splash of milk to thin slightly.
  20. Spread half of the icing over hot rolls as soon as they come out of oven. This melts into nooks and crannies and is delicious!
  21. When slightly cooled but still warm, spread the rest of icing over warm rolls.
