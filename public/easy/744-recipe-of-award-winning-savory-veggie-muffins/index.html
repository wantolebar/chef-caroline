The ingredients needed to make Savory Veggie Muffins:
  1. Provide Dry ingredients
  2. Get flour
  3. Take corn meal
  4. Prepare ground hemp seeds
  5. Use nutritional yeast
  6. Prepare coconut sugar
  7. Prepare baking powder
  8. Provide salt
  9. You need Wet ingredients
  10. Get culinary coconut milk
  11. Provide soy milk
  12. Get water
  13. Use ground flax seed
  14. You need Vegetables
  15. Use chopped fresh spinach
  16. Provide beefsteak tomato
  17. Prepare red onion
  18. Take peas
  19. Provide corn
  20. Prepare sliced leek leaves
  21. Provide Celery stalk
Steps to make Savory Veggie Muffins:
  1. Combine dry ingredients. Set aside.
  2. Stir together ground flax seed with 6 tablespoons water to form flax seed gel.
  3. Combine wet ingredients with flax seed gel.
  4. Saute the onions briefly, then toss in the rest of the vegetables, adding the spinach last. cook 1 minute.
  5. Combine pre-cooked vegetables with wet ingredients. Mix together.
  6. Bring wet/vegetable ingredients and dry ingredients together, just until there is no dry flour left in bowl.
  7. Preheat oven to 400°F. Add batter to muffin pan. Cook for 15-20 minutes.
