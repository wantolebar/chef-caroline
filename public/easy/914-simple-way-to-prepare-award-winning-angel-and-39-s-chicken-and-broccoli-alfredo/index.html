The ingredients needed to cook Angel's Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo:
  1. Prepare 1 lb Chicken Breast
  2. Use 1 Onion
  3. Provide 1 packages Frozen Broccoli
  4. Get 1 dash Salt
  5. You need 1 dash pepper
  6. Take 1 Butter
  7. Use 1 box Bowtie Noodles
  8. You need 1 dash garlic
  9. Take 1 Alfrado Sauce
Steps to make Angel's Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo:
  1. Boil water for noodles (add salt and butter before adding noodles ) Don't cook all the way done! drain water off noodles
  2. boil frozen broccoli with butter( not all the way done ) drain water off broccoli
  3. Cut chicken into 1/2 inche pieces season with salt, pepper, and garlic
  4. slice onion
  5. put butter in pan and add chicken and onions to cook until done
  6. combine chicken , broccoli , noodles , and Alfredo sauce together
