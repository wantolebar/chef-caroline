The ingredients needed to cook Mike's Shrimp Cocktail:
  1. You need FOR YOUR PROTEINS
  2. Provide 2 Pounds Large Raw Shrimp [de-veined - shucked - tails removed]
  3. Provide FOR YOUR VEGETABLES
  4. Provide 1/4 Cup Purple Onions [thin sliced]
  5. You need 1/4 Cup Celery [fine chop with leaves]
  6. Get 1/4 Cup Cucumbers [seeds removed - fine chop]
  7. Use 2 LG Jalapeños [fine chop]
  8. Take 2 Roma Tomatoes [small diced]
  9. Get 1/4 Cup Radishes [thin sliced]
  10. Use 2 Pickled Carrots [thin sliced]
  11. Get 1 Bunch Cilantro Leaves [rough chop]
  12. Use 1 Avocado [sliced - added only upon serving]
  13. Use FOR YOUR RED SHRIMP MARINADE SAUCE
  14. Provide 1 Cup Chilled Clamato Juice
  15. Prepare 1 Cup Chilled V8 Juice
  16. Get 2 tbsp Tapitio Hot Sauce [or more - i use much more!]
  17. Get 1/3 Cup Heinz Ketchup
  18. Use 1/2 Cup Fresh Lime Juice
  19. Prepare 1/4 tsp Mexican Oregano
  20. Provide to taste Sea Salt And Black Pepper
  21. Take 1 Dash Worchestershie Sauce
  22. Take 2 tbsp Orange Juice
  23. Prepare SIDES/GARNISHES
  24. You need Tapitio Hot Sauce
  25. Prepare Quality Crackers
  26. Use Lime Wedges
  27. You need Fresh Cilantro
  28. You need Green Onions
Steps to make Mike's Shrimp Cocktail:
  1. Make your RED SAUCE MARINADE. Place your raw shrimp in it and mix. Refrigerate for 10 minutes.
  2. In the meantime, chop all of your chilled vegetables.
  3. Add everything together - except for Avocado. Save that for individual serving. Otherwise you'll have an icky looking, murky red sauce.
  4. Marinate for 10+ minutes longer in fridge.
  5. Garnish with chopped cilantro, green onions, lime wedges and Tapitio Hot Sauce. Serve with quality crackers.
  6. Enjoy!
