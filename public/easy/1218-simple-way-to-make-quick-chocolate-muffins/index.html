The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate muffins:
  1. Get 1 cup apf
  2. Provide 2 tbsp coco powder
  3. You need 1 cup butter milk
  4. Take 1/2 cup butter
  5. You need 1/2 cup sugar
  6. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Provide 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
Steps to make Chocolate muffins:
  1. Mix butter milk baking soda and sugar and keep aside for 5 minutes
  2. Sieve apf baking powder and coco for 3-4 times
  3. Blend the butter till light and fluffy
  4. Mix butter milk butter and essence
  5. Add dry ingredients to wet one
  6. Make a semi thick batter
  7. Preheat the oven on 180 degree for 10 minutes
  8. Pour the batter in muffin moulds till 2/3 and place it to bake on 180 degree for 12-15 minutes
  9. Take a toothpick test
  10. Take them out and let them cool down and then serve
