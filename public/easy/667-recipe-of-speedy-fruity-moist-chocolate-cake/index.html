The ingredients needed to make Fruity moist chocolate cake:
  1. Use cake flour
  2. Use granulated white sugar 3/4
  3. Get unsweetened cocoa powder
  4. Prepare baking soda
  5. You need salt
  6. Provide large eggs
  7. Provide buttermilk or substitute by putting 1 tbsp white vinegar
  8. Prepare in a cup then filling the rest up with milk; let stand 5 minutes
  9. Prepare Until thickened
  10. You need butter melted
  11. Prepare vanilla extract
  12. Take hot coffee or 2 tsp instant coffee in 1 cup boiling water
  13. Get strawberry jam for the fruity glaze
  14. Take Any fruits of your choice
  15. Provide whipping cream
  16. Provide chiller milk for the whipped cream
  17. You need icing sugar
Instructions to make Fruity moist chocolate cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour two 9-inch baking pans (or line with parchment paper circles) and set aside.
  2. In the large bowl of a standing mixer, stir together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt. Add eggs, buttermilk, melted butter and vanilla extract and beat until smooth (about 3 minutes). Remove bowl from mixer and stir in hot coffee with a rubber spatula. Batter will be very runny.
  3. Pour batter evenly between the two pans and bake on middle rack of oven for about 35 minutes, until toothpick inserted in centre comes out clean with just a few moist crumbs attached.
  4. Allow to cool 15 minutes in pans, then run a butter knife around the edges of each cake. Place a wire cooling rack over top of each pan. Wearing oven mitts, use both hands to hold the racks in place while flipping the cakes over onto the racks. Set the racks down and gently thump on the bottom of the pans until the cakes release. Cool completely before handling or frosting.
  5. In a mixing bowl Combine your (powdered whipping cream,icing sugar and milk)beat until the cream holds soft peaks and set aside
  6. For the fruity glaze
  7. Mix the strawberry jam with hot water
  8. Pour over the fruits and mix well
  9. Using a palette knife rub the whipped cream on your cake board
  10. Drizzle the strawberry jam mixture over the first layer and using a piping bag filled with any nozzle of your choice go round the edge of it
  11. And spread then you pour the fruity mixture leaving 1inch boarder for decorating
  12. Using your filled piping bag and make some frosting aroud the edges
  13. Serve right away or keep refrigerated
