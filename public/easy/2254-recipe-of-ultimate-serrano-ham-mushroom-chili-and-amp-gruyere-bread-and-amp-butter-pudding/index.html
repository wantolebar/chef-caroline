The ingredients needed to make Serrano Ham, Mushroom, Chili & Gruy√®re Bread & Butter Pudding:
  1. Get butter
  2. Prepare thick-cut granary bread

  4. Use Serrano ham, in pieces
  5. Prepare vegetable oil
  6. Provide red onion, cut into half-moons and thinly sliced
  7. Provide garlic, chopped
  8. Prepare red chili, with seeds, sliced
  9. Get chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  10. You need very little salt & pepper
  11. Get eggs
  12. Get milk
  13. Get gruy√®re cheese, grated
Steps to make Serrano Ham, Mushroom, Chili & Gruy√®re Bread & Butter Pudding:
  1. Pre-heat oven to Gas Mark 5 or electric equivalent
  2. Butter an oven-proof dish. Generously butter the bread on one side, cut into triangles and place on dish, buttered side up.
  3. Carefully smear a light covering of the mustard over the bread and add the ham on top.
  4. Heat the oil in a frying pan and gently fry the onion for 3 minutes, only stirring if necessary to avoid sticking.
  5. Add the garlic and chili and cook for a further minute, then add the mushrooms and continue cooking for a further 2 minutes or until the ingredients are softened but not burnt, stirring occasionally.
  6. Spread the onion, garlic, chili and mushrooms over the bread and very lightly season.
  7. Beat the eggs and milk together and pour evenly over the rest. Sprinkle the cheese on top.
  8. Bake in the pre-heated oven for 20-25 minutes or until it appears a nice golden-brown.
  9. Enjoy as it is or perhaps with a small salad accompaniment.
