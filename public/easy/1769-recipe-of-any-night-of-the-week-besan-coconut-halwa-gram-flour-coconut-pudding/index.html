The ingredients needed to cook Besan coconut halwa/Gram flour coconut pudding:
  1. Use 1/2 cup besan/gram flour
  2. Get 5 tsp dry coconut powder
  3. Provide 3/4 cup sugar
  4. Take 2 cup milk
  5. Take 1/2 cup ghee
  6. You need 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  7. You need 2 tsp chopped almonds and cashew
  8. You need 1/2 tsp tutti furtti
Steps to make Besan coconut halwa/Gram flour coconut pudding:
  1. Heat ghee in a kadhai, add the garamflour and fry it on a low flame strring continuously till the raw smell disappears.
  2. Once it is lightly browsed, and add the dry coconut powder, sugar and milk.Mix very well and cook.
  3. When the mixture starts leaving the sides and bottom of the Kadhi, add chopped almonds, cashew,tutti furtti and cardamom powder and mix very well.
  4. Switch off the flame and serve hot and warm.
