The ingredients needed to prepare Chicken Curry in Bangladeshi Style:
  1. Take Ingredients :
  2. Get o Chicken (500 gm)
  3. Prepare o Red Chilli powder (1.5 tsp)
  4. Prepare o Soya bean oil (5 tbsp)
  5. Provide o Turmeric powder (1 tsp)
  6. Take o Onion (3 medium size sliced)
  7. Provide o Ginger paste (1 tsp)
  8. Get o Garlic paste (1 tbsp)
  9. Take o Cumin fried powder (1 tsp)
  10. Take o Whole Garam Masala (Cardamom 2 inch 1 pcs, Cinnamon 3 pieces, Cloves 5)
  11. Get o Potato 2 pcs medium size cut into 8 pcs
  12. Get o Coriander powder (1 tsp)
  13. Prepare o Salt (to taste)
  14. Take o Water (5 cup)
