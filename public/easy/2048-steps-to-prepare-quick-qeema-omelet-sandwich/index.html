The ingredients needed to cook Qeema omelet sandwich:
  1. Provide For omelet
  2. Take eggs
  3. Prepare chicken mince
  4. You need small onion chopped
  5. Take red chilli powder
  6. Provide black pepper powder
  7. Get turmeric powder
  8. Provide ginger garlic paste
  9. You need cumin powder
  10. Use chopped fresh coriander
  11. Get green chilli chopped
  12. Provide Salt
  13. Provide For sandwich assembling
  14. Take bread
  15. Provide Salad
  16. Prepare tomato sliced
  17. Take onion sliced
  18. Use sandwich cheese slices
  19. Prepare Green chutney
  20. Get Chilli garlic sauce
  21. Provide Mayonnaise
  22. You need Oil
Instructions to make Qeema omelet sandwich:
  1. Take a wok and heat oil in it
  2. Add finely chopped onion and sautay till soft than add ginger garlic paste and mix little bit
  3. Add chicken mince and mix quickly till colour changes to white
  4. Add salt, turmeric, red chilli powder, black pepper and cumin powder and mix well. Add fresh coriander and chopped green chillies. Cook till mince and spices cook nicely and oil seperated. Keep a side to cool down
  5. Now take a bowl and whisk all the eggs than add chicken mince into it and mix well
  6. Now take a frying pan and some oil in it pour the egg better and make omelettes of required size and thickness. As much as needed. Keep a side
  7. Now cut onions and tomato into slices and keep side
  8. Wash and dried out salad leaves
  9. ASSEMBLING: Toast bread slices from sides in minimum oil on tawa and keep side
  10. Apply chilli garlic sauce on one slice and keep qeema omelet over it. Cover it with 2nd bread slice
  11. On 2nd bread slice apply mayonnaise and keep salad leaves over it than put cheese slice over leaves. Cover it with 3rd bread slice
  12. On 3rd bread slice apply green chutney and put onion rings and tomato slices and cover with 4th bread slice.
  13. Now secure slices with toothpicks and cut into triangle shape. If u want remove corners of bread.
  14. Qeema omelet sandwich is ready to serve. You can use any kind of sauce or chutney whatever u like and serve to your family and friends
