The ingredients needed to cook Cowboy Cookies:
  1. Get Dry ingredients
  2. Get 1 1/3 cup all purpose flour (spooned into measuring cup and levelled)
  3. You need 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Use 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Take 1 cup cooking oats
  7. Use 3/4 cup M&M's
  8. Use 3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  10. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  12. Take Wet ingredients
  13. Get 1/2 cup butter, softened
  14. Take 1 large egg
  15. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
Steps to make Cowboy Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and combine thoroughly.
  4. Roll dough into 1&1/2 inch balls and place on cookie sheet, one inch apart.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes.
