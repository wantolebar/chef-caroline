The ingredients needed to make Hasselback Potatoes:
  1. Get 2 potatoes
  2. Prepare To taste salt
  3. Take To taste pepper
  4. Prepare To taste garlic
  5. Provide To taste rosemary
  6. Take To taste Aromat roast potato spices
  7. Provide As needed butter
  8. You need As needed polony
  9. You need As needed cheese
Instructions to make Hasselback Potatoes:
  1. Slice potatoes but not right to the bottom.. I sprinkled the potatoes: salt, pepper, garlic and rosemary, Aromat roast potato spices. Added a blob of butter on each and baked for 45mins. I then added polony and cheese slices between the slits. Dein baked for another half an hour.
  2. PS: if potatoes are not soft cooking, bake them in a microwave first for a few minutes!
