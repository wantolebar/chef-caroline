The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Love Cheesecake:
  1. Use 1/2 block Philadelphia cheese
  2. Provide 250 ml double cream
  3. Provide 1 punnet strawberries
  4. Get 8 digestive biscuits
  5. Use 1/2 cup butter
Instructions to make Strawberry Love Cheesecake:
  1. Using blender, add biscuits & blend until almost fine crumbs. Can put biscuits into clear plastic bag& use rolling pin to crush biscuits if preferred.
  2. Add melted butter to biscuit ingredients mix and put in a round baking tray, I used heart shaped container. Press down until level.
  3. Leave in fridge too set for half hour or more
  4. Blend strawberries and set aside.
  5. In a mixer or blender blend Philadelphia cheese.
  6. Do the same with the double cream. Then fold cream, Philadelphia cheese & strawberries together.
  7. Place sliced strawberries at side container on top biscuits.
  8. Pour in all ingredients.
  9. Put in fridge overnight to set. Then add cream & decorate the next day.
  10. Serve & enjoy
