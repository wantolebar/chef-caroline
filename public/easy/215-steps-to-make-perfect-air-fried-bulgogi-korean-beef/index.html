The ingredients needed to cook Air Fried Bulgogi Korean Beef:
  1. Provide thinly sliced beef rib-eye
  2. Get thinly sliced onion
  3. Use Korean BBQ Sauce or to taste
  4. You need grated ginger
  5. Get thinly sliced green onion
  6. Prepare sesame seed
Instructions to make Air Fried Bulgogi Korean Beef:
  1. In a large Ziploc bag, combine the meat, onion, Korean BBQ sauce and ginger and mix well. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Line the fryer basket with grill mat or a sheet of lightly greased aluminum foil.
  3. Spread the beef out inside the basket as much as possible and air fry at 380F (190C) for 8-10 minutes, stir once in the middle, until the the meat is cooked through.
  4. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and green onion to serve.
