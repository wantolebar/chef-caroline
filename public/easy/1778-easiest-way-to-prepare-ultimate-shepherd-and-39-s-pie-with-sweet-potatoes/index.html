The ingredients needed to make Shepherd's pie with sweet potatoes:
  1. Use Vegetables
  2. Prepare 1 onion
  3. Provide 1 1/2 cup celery
  4. Use 1 cup fennel
  5. Take 1 medium tomato
  6. Provide 1 small zucchini
  7. Provide 1 packages mushrooms
  8. Use Meat
  9. Take 1 lb ground beef
  10. Use 1/2 lb sausage
  11. Provide 6 1/2 oz can tomato paste
  12. Provide Topping
  13. You need 2 medium sweet potatoes, mashed
Steps to make Shepherd's pie with sweet potatoes:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Peel sweet potatoes and cook in the microwave with one quarter cup of water until done
  3. Brown 1 pound ground beef, 1/2 pound sausage, 1 cup of fennel, 1 1/2 cup of celery, and 1 medium onion
  4. Season the meat mixture to taste
  5. Blend the sweet potatoes until smooth
  6. Cut up remaining vegetables including a summer squash 1 medium tomato and mushrooms
  7. Once the meat mixture is cooked to add the vegetables plus one 6 ounce can tomato paste to it
  8. Cover until heated through then transfer to a cake pan
  9. Bake uncovered in a 350°F oven for about 20 minutes then use the broiler to brown the top
  10. Let sit for about 5 minutes so the goodness congeals together
