  1. Use start
  2. Get creamed corn
  3. Prepare sugar
  4. Get flour
  5. Provide eggs
  6. Use whole milk
  7. Provide white pepper
  8. Use hot sauce / this is optional
  9. Provide plus 2 tablespoons unsalted butter / melted
  10. You need frozen corn, thawed or 8 ounces of fresh corn cut off the cob.
  11. Provide salt
Steps to make Savory Baked Corn Soufle:
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Mix all ingredients together except thawed or fresh corn.
  3. Now just incorporate the thawed corn or fresh corn into mixture.
  4. Grease with softened butter a 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
  5. Place in mixture and bake for 40- 50 minutes. Insert a toothpick in middle and see if it comes out clean.
