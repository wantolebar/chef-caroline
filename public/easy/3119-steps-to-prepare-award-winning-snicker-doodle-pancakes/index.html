The ingredients needed to make Snicker Doodle Pancakes:
  1. You need 2 cups: All purpose flour
  2. You need 16 tsp: Brown sugar
  3. Use 1 tsp: Cinnamon
  4. Get 4 tsp: Baking powder
  5. Use 1/4 tsp: Baking soda
  6. Provide 1/2 tsp: Salt
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cups: Milk
  8. Get 1/4 cup: Butter, melted
  9. Use 2 tsp: Vanilla extract
  10. Use 1 large: egg
Steps to make Snicker Doodle Pancakes:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients together (flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, salt).
  2. Pour dry ingredients into a large bowl and make a well in the middle.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into the well (milk, melted butter, vanilla extract, egg).
  4. Mix slightly with a wired whisk until all ingredients are wet.
  5. Now fold the batter until it is smooth.
  6. Cook the batter on a non-stick surface on low-medium heat until light golden colour.
  7. Top with maple syrup.
