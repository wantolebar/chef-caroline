The ingredients needed to make Ready in 25 Minutes with Pancake Mix Easy Pizza Bread for a Light Lunch:
  1. Prepare Pizza bread dough
  2. Prepare 200 grams Pancake mix
  3. Use 1 Egg
  4. You need 100 ml Milk
  5. Prepare 1/2 Onion
  6. Provide 1 Pizza sauce (store-bought or "Instant easy pizza sauce" )
  7. Prepare Instant easy pizza sauce
  8. Provide 4 tbsp Ketchup
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Olive oil
  10. Take 1 tbsp Minced onion
  11. Take 1/2 tbsp Grated garlic
  12. Get 2 pinch Salt
  13. You need 1 Basil (if available)
  14. You need Pizza topping
  15. Prepare 1 Shredded cheese
  16. Use 1 anything on hand Sausage, bacon, bell pepper, etc.
Instructions to make Ready in 25 Minutes with Pancake Mix Easy Pizza Bread for a Light Lunch:
  1. Gather the ingredients and pre-heat the oven at 190℃ for 15 minutes.
  2. Mince half of the onion. Set aside 1 tablespoon's worth for the pizza sauce.
  3. If you do not have store-bought pizza sauce on hand, mix all the ingredients for the "Instant easy pizza sauce". Use the minced onion you have set aside from Step 2.
  4. Make the pizza dough.
  5. Mix all the ingredients for the pizza dough (Ingredients shown in the Step 4 photo). The batter will be very loose. Gradually add the milk, and adjust.
  6. Line a baking tray with parchment paper. Place the pizza dough on the parchment paper and flatten out. It should be about 1 cm thick. Shape into a small circle so that the dough does not go over the parchment paper.
  7. Pour on the pizza sauce, and spread it out evenly.
  8. Place on your favorite toppings. Slice your toppings very thinly. Only two sausages were used in the photo above!
  9. Top it off with shredded cheese and bake in the pre-heated oven at 190℃ for about 15 minutes.
  10. It would look great if you have chopped parsley to garnish your pizza. The size of the pizza shown in this photo turned out to be little bigger than 24 × 32 cm.
  11. The dough is very loose and fluffy. Somewhat like bread dough.
  12. Slice and serve.
