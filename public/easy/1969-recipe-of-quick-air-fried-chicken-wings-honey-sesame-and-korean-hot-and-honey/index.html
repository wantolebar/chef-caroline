The ingredients needed to make Air Fried Chicken Wings (Honey Sesame and Korean Hot and Honey):
  1. Take Chicken Wings
  2. Use Baking Powder
  3. Provide Garlic Powder
  4. Prepare Salt Pepper
  5. Prepare Chicken Stock
  6. Prepare Honey
  7. You need Soy Sauce
  8. Use White Sugar
  9. Provide Sesame Oil
  10. Provide Corn Starch
  11. Use Water
  12. You need Gochujang
  13. Take Rice Vinegar
  14. Prepare Sesame Seeds
Instructions to make Air Fried Chicken Wings (Honey Sesame and Korean Hot and Honey):
  1. Marinate chicken wings with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and baking powder for 30min-1 hour. The baking powder helps the chicken skin to crisp up in the air fryer.
  2. Prepare the sauces.
  3. Honey Sesame. Add together 1 cup of chicken broth, 2 tbsp of soy sauce, 1/3 cup of honey, 1 tbsp of white sugar, 1 tsp of sesame seeds.
  4. Korean Hot and Honey. Add together 3 tbsp of soy sauce, 2 tbs of honey, 1 tsp of rice vinegar, 1/2 tsp of sesame oil, and 1 tbsp of Gochujang. Stir together.
  5. Bring Air Fryer to heat at 375 F. Oil the Air Fryer and place chicken wings in the air fryer for 12-15 min on each side or until desired crispiness. Let rest on a baking rack.
  6. Add Honey Sesame sauce to a pan and bring to a simmer. Add in corn starch slurry (1 tsp of corn starch and 2 tbsp of water). To thicken until it is a glaze like texture. Throw in half of the wings to sauce and toss together.
  7. Toss the rest of the wings in the Korean Hot and Honey sauce. Finish by topping wings with sesame seeds and green onions. Enjoy!
