The ingredients needed to make My Grandmother's Mashed Sweet Potato with Sweet Chestnuts:
  1. Use 500 grams Sweet potatoes
  2. Prepare 250 grams Candied chestnuts (Weight of chestnuts)
  3. Provide 3/4 cup Candied chestnut syrup
  4. Prepare 1 dash Salt
Steps to make My Grandmother's Mashed Sweet Potato with Sweet Chestnuts:
  1. Peel the sweet potatoes and cut them into 1.5 cm semi-circular shapes. Then put them in water.
  2. Steam the sweet potatoes until soft. When you can easily pierce them with a skewer, they're done.
  3. Mash the steamed potatoes with a masher, then strain until smooth.
  4. Put the strained sweet potato in a pan. Turn on the heat, add the salt and the candied chestnut syrup, and stir over low heat for about 5 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat, add in the candied chestnuts, and mix well. Once it has chilled, it's ready.
