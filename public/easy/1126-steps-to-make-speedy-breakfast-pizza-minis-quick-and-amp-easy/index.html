The ingredients needed to prepare Breakfast Pizza Minis (quick & easy):
  1. Prepare 1 can Libby's Country Sausage Gravy
  2. Provide 8 tbsp Shredded Cheddar Cheese (sharp, mild or whatever cheese u like)
  3. Take 1 can refrigerated biscuits (preferably the layer ones u peel)
  4. Take 3 large eggs or 5 egg whites
  5. Provide 1 large cookie sheet for baking
  6. Get 1 can nonstick cooking spray to keep biscuits from sticking
  7. Use 1 small skillet to cook eggs in
Instructions to make Breakfast Pizza Minis (quick & easy):
  1. Preheat oven to whatever it says on biscuits directions (usually 375°)
  2. Let oven heat up while u prepare the food (at least 15 mins)
  3. Beat eggs with a fork or whisk until scambled
  4. Cook scrambled eggs in skillet w/ cooking spray just until done over med. to med-high heat (don't brown or overcook & no salt)
  5. Set eggs aside, open biscuits, flatten them out w/ hands & fingers (not too much though or it will rip the dough)
  6. Spray cookie sheet with cooking spray & place flattened biscuits on it
  7. Spoon 2-3 teaspoons of the sausage gravy on top & spread around but not too close to edges (leave a space around the edges that's about the thickness of index finger)
  8. Next sprinkle scrambled eggs on top of gravy on all biscuits (do not salt eggs. the cheese & gravy are salty enough)
  9. Sprinkle the tops with about 1 tablespoon each of cheese (don't use too much cheese. the gravy is salty & too much will overpower)
  10. At this point u can sprinkle some pepper or dried parsley on top. Now put in oven & bake until biscuit crusts are golden & done! ENJOY!!
  11. U can also cook & brown some shredded hash browns & maybe onions or peppers & put on top the eggs before the cheese!
  12. Or if u have kids who like bell peppers u can have them use a star or heart or whatever shaped cookie cutter and make shapes out of the peppers & place on top of cheese before baking! Just a couple ideas to make it ur own! :-)
