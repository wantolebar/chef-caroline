The ingredients needed to make Mushroom soup:
  1. You need 4 cups chicken broth 3\4pound mushrooms
  2. Take 1 slice onion
  3. Get 1/4 cup butter
  4. Provide 1/8 cup flour (scant)
  5. You need 1/4 cup cream
  6. Take 1/4 cup half&half
  7. Provide Salt
  8. Take Pepper paprika
Instructions to make Mushroom soup:
  1. Wash chop mushrooms add with onion and cook in stock about 20 minutes
  2. Then rub through sieve
  3. Reheat and add thickening then milk and cream which have been heated in double boiler
  4. Serve very hot with little whipped cream
