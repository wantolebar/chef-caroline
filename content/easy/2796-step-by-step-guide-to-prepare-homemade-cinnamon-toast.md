##### The ingredients needed to cook Cinnamon Toast:

1. Prepare 2 slices white bread
1. Provide  salted butter, softened
1. Provide  white sugar
1. Get  ground cinnamon


##### Instructions to make Cinnamon Toast:

1. Spread butter on one side of bread. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on buttered side. (If you mix the sugar and cinnamon first, the color would be more even)
1. Heat pan. Toast the buttered side first. Toast until the sugar caramelize. (The caramelized sugar will give your toast a nice crunch)
1. Toast the other side until golden brown.
