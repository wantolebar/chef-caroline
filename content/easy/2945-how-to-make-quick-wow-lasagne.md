##### The ingredients needed to make WOW lasagne:

1. Get  red sauce
1. Get 300 grams beef mince
1. Take 2 brown onions
1. Prepare 1/2 can corn kernals drained
1. You need 1 cup sliced mushroom
1. You need 5 eggs
1. Take 1 can crushed tomatoes
1. Get 3 tbsp Italian seasoning spices
1. Prepare 4 tbsp tomato paste
1. Provide 2 cup water
1. Use 1 1/2 tsp basil pesto
1. You need 1 1/2 tsp sundried tomato pesto
1. Get 1 tbsp oregano
1. Prepare 2 tbsp bottled mince garlic
1. Get 2 clove crushed
1. Use 1 sliced carrot
1. Use 1 cup peas
1. Get 1 bottle of dolmio bechamel sauce.
1. You need 1 packages fresh dolmio lasagne sheets
1. Provide 1 handful shredded parmesan


##### Instructions to make WOW lasagne:

1. Preheat oven to 160 fan forced.
1. Cook on high In a large pot drizzle olive oil, combine onions, garlic and meat. Cook till meat is cooked n onions r soft.
1. Put heat on medium and add all the spices plus salt and pepper to taste and the pestos. Stir for a further 2 minutes.
1. Add corn, peas, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste,carrots, mushrooms and water. Stir, cover and cook for 1 hour. Every so often check and stir sauce.
1. In a ovenproof dish drizzle with olive oil or olive spray and lay lasagne sheet down, on top lay sauce down then bechamel and repeat till full. On very top sprinkle parmesan cheese and dry oregano.
1. Place in oven for 1 hr or till lasagne sheets r soft. When finished take out and let stand for 5-10 minutes.
