##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Walleye:

1. You need  butter or extra virgin olive oil
1. Get  whole wheat ritz crackers
1. Use  shredded parmesan cheese (opt.)
1. Use  dried oregano
1. Prepare  dried basil
1. You need  salt
1. Provide  garlic powder
1. Provide  to 1 1/2lbs walleye fillets


##### Steps to make Baked Walleye:

1. Crush crackers and place in flat container.
1. Add season &amp; Parmesan to cracker crumbs &amp; mix
1. In a clean container, coat walleye with oil.
1. Press walleye into cracker crumbs on both sides.
1. Place on wire rack in a shallow pan.
1. Place in oven for 30 min. at 350°F until flakes apart.
