##### The ingredients needed to prepare Cheesy meatballs in tomato sauce:

1. Provide 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1. You need 2 eggs
1. You need 1 cup Italian style bread crumbs
1. Take  Worcestershire sauce
1. Get 1 can diced tomatos
1. Get 2 cups shredded Italian blend cheese
1. Provide  salt, pepper, garlic powder &amp; Italian seasoning


##### Steps to make Cheesy meatballs in tomato sauce:

1. Mix eggs, salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce (like, 2 table spoons) garlic powder &amp; bread crumbs into thawed hamburger meat, use your hands! It&#39;s good for the soul!
1. Roll golf ball size balls of meat into balls and place on a cookie sheet. Sprinkle Italian seasoning on meatballs and place them in the oven and broil them for 5 minutes. ***This is the step you don&#39;t want to forget!!!*** Make sure meatballs are browned on all sides.
1. Pour can of diced tomato into bottom of slow cooker &amp; season with salt and pepper (you can also add a little sugar to cut back acidity) and place a layer of browned meatballs in crock pot.
1. Pour 1 cup of cheese over meatballs and repeat step 2 to make a second layer of meatballs ontop of cheese.
1. Add other cup of cheese on top of second layer of meatballs. Cook in slow cooker/crock pot on low for 8-9 hours or high for 4-5 hours. Serve warm with pasta or potatoes and steamed veggies.
