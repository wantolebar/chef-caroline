##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken spread for sandwiches:

1. Use 250 gm Chicken with bones
1. Take 4 Garlic pods
1. Use 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Prepare 1 cup Water to boil the chicken
1. You need 1 tsp Black pepper
1. Use 1 tsp Oil
1. You need 1 cup Mayonnaise


##### Steps to make Chicken spread for sandwiches:

1. Take chicken add oil water, garlic pods and salt and also ginger garlic paste bring it to boil until all the water is dried up and chicken is slightly stained brown. Now shred the chicken into pieces discard the bones.
1. Now add black pepper to it and mayonnaise. You may keep it into the fridge and can have it till a week.
1. It&#39;s can be spread on the bread slices add cucumber or any salad veggies you like then pan grill or may be grilled and have it in your Breakfast, as a snack or any time on the go.. Taste is just awesome.
