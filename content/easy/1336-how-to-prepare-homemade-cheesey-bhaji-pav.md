##### The ingredients needed to make Cheesey Bhaji Pav:

1. Take 1/4 cup Cheese
1. Prepare 6 nos Pav
1. Use 1/2 cup Ready Bhaji
1. Take 1/4 cup Butter
1. Provide 3 tbsp Oregano


##### Steps to make Cheesey Bhaji Pav:

1. Cut pav in center with the help of cookie cutter. Apply butter.
1. Now fill cheese in it.
1. Now will bhaji, and sprinkle oregano.
1. Again put cheese on it. Keep in microwave for 4 min.
1. Tasty cheese bhaji pav is ready to eat.
