##### The ingredients needed to cook stuffed shells:

1. Take 1 box jumbo shells
1. Take 1 lb ground beef
1. Provide 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1. Provide 15 oz cottage cheese
1. You need 1 egg
1. Take 2 tsp basil--dried
1. Get 1 salt &amp; pepper to taste
1. You need 2 clove garlic (minced)
1. Provide 1/3 cup onion (minced)
1. Get 1 cup mozzarella cheese
1. Provide 24 oz about 2 jars ; spaghetti sauce (any kind you prefer)


##### Steps to make stuffed shells:

1. cook shells according to box directions
1. preheat oven to 350; cook ground beef, onion and garlic until lightly browned.
1. drain excess fat
1. combine mozzarella, cottage and parmesan cheese along with, Basil,salt&amp;pepper, and egg in a bowl. Add the beef, onion and garlic mixture to this
1. in a 13X9 dish spread about 1 cup of the sauce on the bottom. fill the shells and put in the pan. if you need another pan a 8X8 held the extras
1. pour the extra filling over shells then pour the remaining sauce over shells.
1. bake for about 1 hour 15 minutes or until cooked through
