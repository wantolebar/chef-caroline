##### Steps to make Corn salad:

1. Pressure cook the corn cobs in 3 cups water for 2 to 3 whistles.
1. And slice off all the corn kernels from corn cob.
1. Finely chop the onion, tomato, coriander leaves and cucumber.
1. Take a big bowl add the chopped vegetables and add the corn kernels.
1. Add chilli powder, salt,pepper powder and garam masala and nicely mix it.
1. Now add the lemon juice give a nice mix.
1. Serve yummy corn salad.
