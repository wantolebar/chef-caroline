##### The ingredients needed to prepare Spätzle:

1. Use 500 grams flour
1. Provide 3 eggs
1. Use 1/4 liter Pent warm water
1. Take 1 salt


##### Instructions to make Spätzle:

1. Stear the eggs in the flavour and add the water during stearing .
1. Take a big pot but water inside. If the water is cooking put 2 1/2 tablespoons of salt inside.
1. Fill the spätzle with a spätzle sieve in a pot with cocking water.
