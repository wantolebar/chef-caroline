##### The ingredients needed to cook Parmesan Crumb Roasted Potatos:

1. Provide  baby yukon gold potatos, pre cooked for 7 minutes in boiling salted water and drained, then cut in half.
1. You need  bakery croissant pulsed in food processor to make course crumbs
1. Get  minced cloves of garlic
1. Use  fresh grated lemon zest
1. Provide  cajun seasoning
1. Provide  black pepper and salt to taste if needed
1. Get  olive oil
1. You need  butter, melted
1. Get  chopped frrsh parsley
1. Take  grated parmesan cheese
1. Prepare  sliced gree onions


##### Steps to make Parmesan Crumb Roasted Potatos:

1. Preheatvven to 450
1. Combine in a large bowl the croissant crumbs, garlic, lemon zest, pepper, cajun seasoning and oil and butter mix to combine. Add potatos and toss to coat. Place potatos in a single layer, cut side down on baking sheet.Sprinkle tops with the crumbs left in the bowl.
1. Roast 10 minutes, add parmesan cheese and parsley and roast 5 tob10 minutes more or until golden and crispy.Remove to a platter garnish with green onions and serve hot!
