1. Prepare 1 Ginger Slice
1. Use 3 Tablespoons Lemon juice
1. Prepare To Taste salt Black
1. Prepare To Taste Black pepper
1. Provide To Taste Cumin powder


##### Steps to make Red detox juice:

1. Chop tomatoes, beetroot, carrots, ginger and coriander leaves. Add to the food mixer, add the mint leaves, cumin powder, salt, black pepper and lemon juice. Add half cup water and grind well. Pour and drink.
