##### The ingredients needed to cook Amaretto-Praline , (Zante):

1. Prepare 100 grams walnuts
1. Provide 25 grams pistachio nuts
1. Take 250 grams marzipan
1. Prepare 15 grams quality honey
1. You need 100 grams icing (confectioners) sugar
1. Provide 4 tbsp Amaretto(  almond liquor )
1. Get 300 grams dark best chocolate.for baking
1. Use 30 pistachio kernels unsalted for decoration


##### Instructions to make Amaretto-Praline , (Zante):

1. chop the wal and pistachio nuts very very fine.
1. take the marzipan, honey, 60 grams of the icing sugar.the chopped nuts and the liquor and lnead into a dough
1. divide this inti three equal parts and dust with rest of icingsugar. Roll  into three 45 cm long sausages  cut each into 20 equal small pices
1. roll each on off these pieces into a ball then into oval and rest on a bakingpaper lined tray
1. melt the chocolate and brush over one side of each oval
1. let chocolate set .repeat until praline  is evenly covered with  chocolate
1. make a piping bag out of bakingpaper or use? bought one with a very fine nozzle and pipe a swirl or spiral  onto each praline .add half a pistachio  .
1. enjoy with friends or wrap as present
1. this will make about 60 pralines
