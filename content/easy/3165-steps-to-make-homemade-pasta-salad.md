##### The ingredients needed to prepare Pasta Salad:

1. Use 1 (16 oz.) Box Rotini pasta
1. Prepare 1 head broccoli florets
1. Prepare 1 pack grape tomatoes, halved
1. Provide 2 cucumbers, seeds removed, diced fine
1. Use 1 roasted red pepper, diced
1. Get  Green onions, sliced
1. Provide 1 can large black pitted olives, sliced
1. Use 1 bottle, about 10 greek peppers, whole (peppercinis)
1. Provide 1 block colby cheese cubed
1. You need 1 block pepper jack cheese cubed
1. Provide  Or mozzarella pearls or shredded mozzarella
1. Take 1 ham steak cubed
1. You need 1 bag mini pepperonis
1. Take 1 packet Good Seasons Italian seasoning mix
1. Use 2 Tbls honey
1. Prepare 1 (24 oz.) bottle olive garden creamy Italian dressing
1. Provide 3 Tbl pesto


##### Instructions to make Pasta Salad:

1. Boil pasta according to directions and drain then run cold water over pasta to cool, drain again and set aside
1. Put together entire ingredients into large mixing bowl (except the pasta and bottle dressing) turning to mix
1. Add pasta and bottle dressing and turn in bowl with spatula until evenly coated, I also add some juice from the pepper jar
1. Cover and place in fridge...at least 2-3 hours to marry the flavors Overnight is better
