##### The ingredients needed to prepare Easy cake mix snickerdoodle cookies:

1. Use 1 box yellow cake mix
1. Get 1/4 cup vegetable oil
1. You need 2 large eggs
1. Provide 3 tablespoons sugar
1. Take 1 teaspoon sugar


##### Steps to make Easy cake mix snickerdoodle cookies:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Grease baking sheets
1. Mix sugar and cinnamon and set aside
1. In a large bowl mix cake mix, eggs and oil and one tablespoon of cinnamon mixture.
1. Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Roll balls in remaining cinnamon sugar mixture. Place 2-inches apart on prepared pans. If desired, flatten balls with bottom of a glass.
1. Bake 8 to 9 minutes or until set. Cool cookies 1 minute on pan. Cool completely on wire rack.
1. Baking Tip: Use a 1 to 1-1/4-inch scoop to form dough into balls. For best results, bake one baking sheet at a time. If baking two sheets together, rotate pans halfway through baking.
