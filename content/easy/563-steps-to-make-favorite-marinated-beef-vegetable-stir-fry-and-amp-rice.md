##### The ingredients needed to make Marinated Beef Vegetable Stir Fry &amp; Rice:

1. Provide 1/4 onion, chopped
1. Use 2 cup marinated beef
1. Take 1 1/2 bunch cilantro
1. Use 1/2 each bell pepper
1. Prepare 1 1/4 head cauliflower
1. Provide 7 mushrooms
1. Provide 1 cup white rice
1. Get 1/4 stick butter
1. Prepare 1/3 lemon
1. Use 3 dash soy sauce


##### Steps to make Marinated Beef Vegetable Stir Fry &amp; Rice:

1. Add butter to pan and cook sliced marinated beef (short ribs) and stir cook for 3 mins
1. Take beef out and add onions, bell peppers, cauliflower, mushrooms, and any other chopped vegetables to the pan. Stir for 3 mins.
1. Once the vegetables are crunchy but cooked, add the beef back in and stir, adding salt and pepper as needed.
1. Serve with a side of rice and a slice of fresh lemon.
