##### The ingredients needed to prepare Thai red curry:

1. Use 1-2 tablespoons Thai red curry paste
1. Get 3 tablespoons Fish sauce
1. Get 2 tablespoons palm or brown sugar
1. You need 1 x 400ml tin of Coconut milk
1. Prepare 250 ml vegetable stock
1. Provide 1 teaspoon Turmeric
1. Get 2 stalks lemon grass
1. You need 3-4 kaffir lime leaves
1. Provide 500-600 g Pumpkin and diced
1. Prepare  Asian greens
1. Take  Lime juice
1. You need  Coriander as garnish
1. Take  Optional: 500-600g combination of prawns and fish fillets-diced
1. You need  Optional: 500-600g seasonal vegetables


##### Steps to make Thai red curry:

1. Place first nine ingredients into a pan. Slowly bring to the boil, then reduce heat and simmer until pumpkin is just cooked. This is your curry base.
1. For a seafood curry add peeled raw prawns and approx. 400g of salmon- diced. Simmer gently for 3-4 minutes then add Asian greens and cook for a further 1-2 minutes. Add lime juice to taste and sprinkle with coriander before serving with rice or noodles.
1. For a vegetable curry add vegetables of your choice at the same time as the pumpkin, bring to the boil and then simmer gently until cooked. Add Asian greens and cook for a further 1-2 minutes then check flavour and add lime juice to taste and sprinkle with coriander before serving with rice or noodles.
1. I&#39;ve made this curry with firm white fish, such as monkfish or cod, or just fresh seasonal vegetables such as pumpkin,sweet potato, cherry tomatoes and french beans.
