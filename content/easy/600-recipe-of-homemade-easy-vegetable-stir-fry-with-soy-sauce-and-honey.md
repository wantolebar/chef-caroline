##### The ingredients needed to cook easy vegetable stir fry with soy sauce and honey:

1. You need  vegetables
1. Get 1 cup jasmin rice
1. Provide 1 bell pepper
1. Take 1 sweet onion
1. Provide 5 stock asparagus
1. Get 2 tbsp soy sauce
1. Take 2 tbsp honey
1. Provide 1 dash sea salt
1. Prepare 1 clove galic minced
1. You need 1 tsp ground pepper
1. Provide 1 jalepeno pepper
1. Get 1 tbsp canola oil or olive oil


##### Steps to make easy vegetable stir fry with soy sauce and honey:

1. cook rice according to box directions in sauce pan or rice cooker
1. heat up canola or olive oil in fry pan
1. chop up onion and Bell pepper and add to pan
1. while pepper and onion cooks, mince jalapeno and galic and add to pan
1. then chop up asparagus and add to vegetables
1. finally add soy sauce, honey, sea salt, and pepper, let cook until bell pepper is soft and onion is caramelized.
1. scoop rice into a bowl and top with vegetables
