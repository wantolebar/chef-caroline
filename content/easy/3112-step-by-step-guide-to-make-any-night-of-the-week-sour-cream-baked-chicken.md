##### The ingredients needed to cook Sour Cream Baked Chicken:

1. Use 1/2 cup Sour Cream
1. You need 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
1. Get 1 tsp Parsley
1. Use 1/4 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
1. Take 1 pinch of Salt
1. You need 1 lb Chicken Breast


##### Steps to make Sour Cream Baked Chicken:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
1. Mix sour cream, parsley, red pepper flakes, parm cheese.
1. Spread sour cream mixture over top of the chicken.
1. Pop in the oven for 20 minutes.
1. Serve with your fave sides!
