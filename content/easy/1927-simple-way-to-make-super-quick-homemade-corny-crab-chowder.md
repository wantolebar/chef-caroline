##### The ingredients needed to make Corny Crab Chowder:

1. Use 8 oz fresh crab meat or refrigerated canned crab meat
1. Take 2 T olive oil
1. Get 5-6 russet potatoes, peeled and chopped (about 13 oz)
1. Get 1 small onion, chopped
1. Get 48 oz low-sodium chicken broth
1. You need 1-1/2 c frozen corn
1. You need 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
1. You need 1 T dried basil
1. Prepare 1/2 c half and half
1. You need 1/2 tsp salt
1. You need 1/4 tsp pepper
1. You need  chopped fresh parsley or dried oregano to garnish


##### Instructions to make Corny Crab Chowder:

1. In large pot, heat oil.  Add potatoes and onions.  Cook until onion is tender.  Add broth and simmer 20 min.
1. Add remaining ingredients and cook on low 5 min.  Serve with garnish if using.
