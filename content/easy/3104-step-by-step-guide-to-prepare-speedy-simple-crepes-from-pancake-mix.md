##### The ingredients needed to prepare Simple Crepes From Pancake Mix:

1. You need 30 grams Pancake mix
1. Prepare 1 Egg (medium)
1. Take 70 grams Milk or Soy Milk
1. Get 5 grams Sugar


##### Instructions to make Simple Crepes From Pancake Mix:

1. Gather your ingredients.
1. Mix the pancake mix and sugar gently with a whisk.
1. Add the egg and mix well with the whisk.
1. Mix until smooth.
1. Add the milk.
1. Continue mixing.
1. Heat a Teflon frying pan and melt the butter.
1. Take about 80% of a ladleful of batter and pour into the pan.
1. Let the batter spread out in the pan. Now cook it on a low heat to prevent burning.
1. As it cooks and changes color, it will start to peel around the edges. When it starts to look like a lightly fried egg, flip it.
1. All it needs is about 10 seconds on the other side and it&#39;s done!
1. Put the crepe on a plate and cook the rest of the batter. Once it&#39;s all done, wrap and let cool.
1. As for the filling, try ice cream, custard, or fruit as a dessert!
1. You could also fill it with candied chestnuts and whipped cream. All you need is chocolate sauce on top and you&#39;re set!
