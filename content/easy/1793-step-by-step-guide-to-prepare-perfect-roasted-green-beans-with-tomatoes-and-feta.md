##### The ingredients needed to make Roasted Green Beans (with Tomatoes and Feta):

1. You need 4 cups green snap beans, trimmed
1. Take 2 cups grape tomatoes, halved
1. Prepare 1/4 cup fresh basil, chiffonade (divided)
1. Provide 2 garlic cloves, chopped/minced
1. Use 1/2 tsp salt
1. Provide 1/4 tsp black pepper
1. You need 4 oz fat free crumbled feta


##### Instructions to make Roasted Green Beans (with Tomatoes and Feta):

1. Preheat oven to 450F
1. Spray roasting pan with cooking spray, arrange beans in single layer. Roast until desired tenderness, about 10 minutes
1. Mix remaining ingredients (except 1/2 basil) until well coated, set aside
1. Toss beans with tomato mixture and top with remaining basil.
1. Refrigerate prior to serving.
