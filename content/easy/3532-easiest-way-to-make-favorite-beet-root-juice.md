##### The ingredients needed to cook Beet root juice:

1. Use 2 beet root
1. You need 3 tbsp sugar
1. Prepare 2 big lemon
1. Take leaf Fresh mint
1. Take  Strawberry flavour
1. Take cube Ice


##### Steps to make Beet root juice:

1. Peel the beetroot,and cut it into dice shape
1. Squeeze the lemon and keep it aside
1. Put all the ingredients in a blender
1. Add sugar and flavour together with ice cube
1. Pour the lemon juice blend it untill smooth
1. Sieve the beet root juice and pour it in a glass cup or jar,garnish with mint leavesb
