##### The ingredients needed to prepare Marzipan chocolate candy:

1. Prepare 70 g milk chocolate
1. You need 120 g dark chocolate
1. Take 40 g butter
1. Prepare 80 g heavy cream
1. Provide 1 tbsp raspberry liqueur
1. You need 1 vanilla
1. Provide 250 g marzipan
1. Use 300 g dark chocolate (couverture)


##### Instructions to make Marzipan chocolate candy:

1. Chop the chocolate and the butter
1. Heat the cream until simmering and add chocolate, butter and stir constantly.
1. Take away from fire, add the liquer and stir again.
1. Transfer in a bow and let cool at room temperature stirring occasionally. When cool cover with stretch wrap and refrigerate for an hour.
1. Mix the marzipan with vanilla and  roll out to 1cm thick square.
1. Spread the refrigerated ganash on top of the marzipan and refrigerate again for 3-4 hours.
1. Cut the candy in squares and cover in melted (or even better tempered) dark chocolate.
1. Decorate to your taste and serve.
