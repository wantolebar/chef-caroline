##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate mug cake:

1. Get 2 tablespoons all purpose flour
1. Use 1 egg
1. Provide 4 tablespoon dust sugar
1. Take 2 tablespoon butter
1. Use 2 tablespoon cocoa powder
1. Provide 1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence
1. Use 1 pinch baking powder


##### Instructions to make Chocolate mug cake:

1. Mix sugar,butter and egg well
1. Now add the flour mix well
1. Add baking powder and vanilla essence mix well
1. Pour it into a microwave safe cup
1. Bake it on microwave mode for 3 minutes
