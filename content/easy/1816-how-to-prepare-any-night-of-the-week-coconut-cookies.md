##### The ingredients needed to prepare Coconut Cookies:

1. Get 1 1/2 bowl all-purpose Flour/ Maida
1. Use 100 gram butter
1. Take 1/2 cup powdered sugar
1. Take Pinch salt
1. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
1. Get 1/2 cup desiccated coconut


##### Steps to make Coconut Cookies:

1. In a large bowl, beat the butter with sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy.
1. Whisk together the flour, salt, baking powder. Now add some coconut in it and make a soft dough.
1. Give 30 minutes rest to the dough untill it set.
1. Than make a small small balls from the dough and roll it in desiccated coconut. Put it on greased dish.
1. Put cookies in a preheated cooker for 15 minutes at a low flame. Once cookies is cooked than give rest for 30 minutes.
1. Once cooled, serve the cookies immediately or store in an airtight jar or box.
