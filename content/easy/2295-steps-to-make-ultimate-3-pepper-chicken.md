##### The ingredients needed to make 3 Pepper Chicken:

1. Provide 500 grams chicken
1. Get 1 green bellpepper
1. Provide 2-3 green chillies
1. Get 4 jalapenos
1. Provide 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
1. Prepare 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
1. You need 1 tablespoon red chilli powder
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon garam masala powder
1. Take 1 tablespoon coriander powder
1. Get  Whole garam masala:
1. Provide 1 tea spoon cumin seeds
1. Take 1 black cardemom
1. Prepare 2 green cardemom
1. Take 1 &#34;cinnamon piece
1. Take 3 cloves
1. Use To taste Salt
1. You need 1/2 cup curd
1. Take 1/2 cup oil


##### Instructions to make 3 Pepper Chicken:

1. Heat oil and fry sliced onion till golden,add whole garam masala.
1. Add chicken and turmeric powder,cook till water dries.Add ginger-garlic paste and all powdered spices except garam masala powder.Mix well.Cook for 2-3 minutes on medium heat.
1. Chopped green bell pepper,slice jalapenos and green chillies.Keep aside.
1. Add water and boil on high flame.Add salt,simmer the flame and cook till 50%done.Add all 3 peppers and cook on low-medium flame till done.Add curd and stir.Add garam masala powder and stir till oil separates out.serve with boiled rice.
