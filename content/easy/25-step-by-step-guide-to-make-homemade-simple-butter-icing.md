##### The ingredients needed to cook Simple Butter icing:

1. Get 600 g icing sugar
1. Take 300 g unsalted butter
1. You need  Food colouring (few drops)
1. Get  Vanilla extract


##### Steps to make Simple Butter icing:

1. Beat 600g of icing sugar with the 300g butter together with the food colouring and the vanilla extract
1. Add 2-3 tablespoon of boiling water to loosen and beat until smooth
1. Fill into a piping bag with your desired shaped cone and decorate your cake
