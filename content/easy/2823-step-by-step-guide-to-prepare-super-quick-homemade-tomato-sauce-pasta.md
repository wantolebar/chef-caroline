##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato sauce pasta:

1. You need 2 cups boiled pasta any shape
1. Get 1/2 cup tomato paste
1. Take 1 chicken cube
1. Take 1/2 cup water
1. Prepare 1/2 tbsp chopped garlic
1. Provide 1/2 tsp red chilli flakes
1. Use 1/2 tsp black pepper
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
1. You need 1/8 tsp mixed herbs
1. Prepare 1 tbsp any shredded cheese
1. Get 1 tbsp oil


##### Steps to make Tomato sauce pasta:

1. In a pan heat oil and add garlic.
1. Saute it then add tomato paste and all the mentioned spices
1. Add chicken cube and water and let it cook until raw flavour of tomatoes goes away
1. Add boiled pasta mix very well until well coated
1. Now add cheese and mixed herbs.
1. Serve hot
