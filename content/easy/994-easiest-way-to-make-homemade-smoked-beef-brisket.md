##### The ingredients needed to cook Smoked Beef Brisket:

1. Use  12-14 lb. brisket
1. Prepare  Kosher salt
1. Get  course ground black pepper
1. Get  garlic powder


##### Steps to make Smoked Beef Brisket:

1. Combine salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Season brisket liberally, wrap in plastic, and let it set in fridge for a few hours.
1. Start smoker and bring it to 225° using the wood of your choice. I use pecan or oak.
1. Once your smoker has come up to temperature, put the brisket on with a drip pan underneath to catch drippings.
1. Once internal temperature has reached 165°, wrap brisket in foil and return to smoker.
1. Continue cooking until you reach internal temp of 195°-205° then let rest in cooler for at least an hour. Slice and serve.
