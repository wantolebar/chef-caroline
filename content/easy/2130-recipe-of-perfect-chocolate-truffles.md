##### The ingredients needed to prepare Chocolate Truffles:

1. You need 1/3 cup Raisin
1. You need 2 Tbsp Marsala Wine
1. Provide 12 oz Pound Cake (chocolate pound cake is the best.)
1. You need 1/3 cup Granola Cereal
1. Use 2 Tbsp Heavy Cream
1. You need  ...........................................................
1. You need  &lt;Chocolate Ganache&gt;
1. Get 4 oz Chocolate
1. Get 4 oz Heavy Cream


##### Instructions to make Chocolate Truffles:

1. Combine raisins and marsala wine into the small bowl and leave it 5 minutes.
1. Break the pound cake into small pieces.
1. Add raisins, granola cereals, heavy cream and blend it well. (If the mixture is still dry, add more heavy cream.)
1. Make it round shape.
1. Make chocolate ganache: Heat the heavy cream in a small sauce pan. Pour over the chopped chocolate, and whisk until smooth.
1. Spread on the cake while the chocolate ganache is still warm. Allow to set in a cool dry place for at least 1 hour.
