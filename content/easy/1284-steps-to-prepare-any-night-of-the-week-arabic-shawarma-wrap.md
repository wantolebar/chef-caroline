##### Instructions to make Arabic shawarma wrap:

1. Mix all the ingredients of pita bread.make a soft dough by using warm water.
1. Cover it for sometime.Make small balls of dough when it volume rise to it&#39;s dough.
1. Roll it in the shape of roti.It should be 1/4&#34; thick.
1. Roast it on tawa on a low flame.
1. Pita bread is ready.
1. For stuffing--- Marinate the chicken cubes in the stuffing ingredients overnight or for at least 4 hours.
1. Then preheat the oven 200°c and bake the chicken for 10-15 minutes.
1. Then chop into smaller strips.
1. Stuffing is ready
