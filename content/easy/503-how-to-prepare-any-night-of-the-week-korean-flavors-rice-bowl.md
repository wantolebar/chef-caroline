##### The ingredients needed to prepare Korean flavors Rice Bowl:

1. You need  sauce
1. Provide 2 tbsp soy sauce
1. You need 1/3 cup brown sugar
1. Use 3 tbsp water
1. Use 1/2 tsp ground dried ginger
1. Get 1 tbsp sesame oil
1. Provide 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1. Get  main
1. You need 4 clove garlic
1. Prepare 1 lb ground meat (leaner is better)
1. Take 2 piece green onions


##### Steps to make Korean flavors Rice Bowl:

1. Add all sauce ingredients (first 6 ingredients) to a small bowl, whisk together, adjust pepper flakes to your heat level
1. Add a little oil to your pot, heat on medium high
1. Chop garlic, add too hot oil, stir constantly for about 1 minute
1. Add ground meat to pot with garlic, stir and chop into small pieces, until cooked
1. Add sauce to ground meat, keep stirring, about 2 minutes
1. Add green onions, stir for 1 additional minute
1. Serve over prepared hot rice
1. NOTES - be careful with the soy sauce, too much can make it overly salty. I also added a handful of snow peas to this one in the picture. Had some left over. Turned out good if you have some. Next time I&#39;ll try some chopped water chestnuts also, I think the crisp will add a nice touch. I was out of red pepper flakes and used 1 tablespoon of hot sauce instead, worked great.
