##### The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Cookies:

1. Take  melted butter
1. Take  powder sugar
1. Get  all-purpose flour
1. Take  baking powder
1. Use  vanilla essence
1. Get  peanut butter
1. You need  milk
1. Prepare  chopped chocolate


##### Steps to make Peanut Butter Cookies:

1. Mix sugar and melted butter. Then add baking powder and vanilla essence. Mix well.
1. Then add all-purpose flour and make a dough by adding milk in a parts.
1. Once the dough is ready then add chopped chocolates, mix all then freeze it for 1/2 an hour.
1. After that make a small balls from the dough. Then stuffed 1 small scoop pea nut butter into it.
1. Now lightly press with a hand and flattens the dough. Once all done, baked it in a pan by adding salt and separator on top of it. Place all the cookies on a plate and baked for 20 minutes in a low to medium flame.
