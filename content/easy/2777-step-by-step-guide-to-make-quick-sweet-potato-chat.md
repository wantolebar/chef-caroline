##### The ingredients needed to prepare Sweet Potato chat:

1. Take  sweet potato boiled
1. Take  onions finely chopped
1. Take  coriander leaves chopped
1. Prepare  peanuts (roasted and coarsely ground)
1. Provide  black salt
1. You need  plain salt or as per taste
1. Provide  red chilli powder or as per taste
1. Use  chaat masala
1. Take  roasted cumin powder
1. Use  green chutney
1. Use  tamarind sweet chutney
1. Take  lemon juice


##### Instructions to make Sweet Potato chat:

1. First, cut boiled sweet potato into small pieces and keep in a bowl.
1. Now add finely chopped onions, coriander leaves, salt to taste, black salt, roasted cumin powder, red chilli powder, chaat masala, roasted and coarsely ground peanuts and mix them well.
1. Now add chopped sweet potato, green chutney, sweet tamarind chutney and lemon juice, mix well gently.
1. Sweet potato chaat is ready, serve in a serving bowl / plate and garnish it with pomegranate seeds.
