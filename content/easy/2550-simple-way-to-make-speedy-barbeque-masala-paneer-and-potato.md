##### The ingredients needed to prepare Barbeque masala paneer and potato:

1. You need 250 grm Paneer
1. Take 1/2tsp chili powder
1. Get 1/2 tsp corriander powder
1. Take to taste Chaat masala
1. Use To taste Salt
1. Prepare 250 grm potato cubes


##### Instructions to make Barbeque masala paneer and potato:

1. Cut the paneer and potato in cube shape
1. Take a bowl and put 1/2 tsp chili powder,coriender powder and paneer tikka masala, chat masala and salt as for your taste and mix it with water.
1. Coat the paneer and potato with masala. Put it into a grill rod or shewer and grill it and serve it with sauce, chutney and mayonnaise
