##### The ingredients needed to cook 2 minute Carrot Cake:

1. Prepare 1 package Duncan Hines carrot cake size for 1
1. You need 3 packets instant sugar free maple oatmeal
1. Get 3 scoops Genepro flavorless powder
1. You need 1 (4 oz) apllesauce
1. Provide 6 tbsp water


##### Steps to make 2 minute Carrot Cake:

1. Empty one packet of carrot cake mix into bowl
1. Add 3 packs of sugar free oatmeal
1. 3 scoops of genepro
1. One container of applesauce or 1/2 cup out of the jar
1. You will see after combining it will still be pretty dry. So add in 6 tbsp of water
1. Divide into 3 microwave safe containers sprayed with PAM
1. After 2 minutes in the microwave it should slide out
1. Let it cool and add cream cheese frosting. This product is available now in US stores. A frosting already in a sealed bag, genius. I added 1/2 serving to my individual cake
1. I made a few and am freezing them for later.
