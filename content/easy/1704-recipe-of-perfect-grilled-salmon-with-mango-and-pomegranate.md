##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Salmon with Mango and Pomegranate:

##### Steps to make Grilled Salmon with Mango and Pomegranate:

1. Make the Asian style dressing : Mixed all the sauce ingredients together and leave it aside.
1. Brush the salmon fillets lightly with the vegetable oil, and season both sides with salt and pepper.
1. Preheat a gas grill to medium-high or prepare a moderately high charcoal fire. Grill the salmon for 5 to 7 minutes per side until lightly charred and cooked to your desired.
1. Serve the salmon with the mango and pomegranate salad and dressing.
