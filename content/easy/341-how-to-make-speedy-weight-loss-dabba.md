##### The ingredients needed to prepare Weight loss Dabba:

1. You need 100 grams cooked rice
1. You need 2 onions chopped
1. Use 1 Capsicum chopped
1. Take 1/2 carrot grated
1. Take 1 tablespoon soya sauce
1. Provide 1 teaspoon pepper powder
1. Prepare  Salt as per taste
1. Prepare 2 spoons ghee
1. Take 1 tablespoon oil
1. Provide 4-5 cashews


##### Instructions to make Weight loss Dabba:

1. Take oil in kadai add chopped onion and Capsicum Red Pepper powder then cashews following of that add grated carrot
1. Add soya sauce and salt. Add Pinch of sugar then add cooked rice mix well lastly add ghee and covered for 5 minutes. Ready for lunch.
