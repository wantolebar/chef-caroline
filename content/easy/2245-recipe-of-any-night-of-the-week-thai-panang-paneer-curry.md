##### The ingredients needed to make Thai Panang Paneer Curry:

1. Use For Thai Panang Curry Paste -
1. Get 1 small onion, chopped
1. Take 5-6 garlic cloves
1. You need 1&#34; galangal / ginger, sliced
1. Use 8-10 dry red chillies, soaked in water for 10-15 minutes
1. Provide 1/4 cup coriander roots, chopped
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp lime zest
1. You need 2-3 lemon grass (bottom part), sliced
1. You need 4-5 kaffir lime leaves
1. Prepare 2 tbsp roasted peanuts, powdered
1. Provide 1 tsp roasted coriander powder
1. You need 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
1. Provide 1/2 tsp pepper powder
1. Prepare 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
1. Use 1 tsp tamarind paste
1. Provide 1 tsp sugar
1. Take to taste salt
1. Prepare For Thai Panang Curry
1. Take 200 gms paneer, cubed
1. Use 2-3 tbsp oil
1. Get 3/4 cup panang paste
1. You need 1 cup coconut milk
1. Take 2-3 green chilies, slit
1. Provide to taste salt


##### Steps to make Thai Panang Paneer Curry:

1. Thai Panang Curry Paste - Blend all the above mentioned ingredients to a smooth paste by adding some water. Store in an airtight container to be used later.
1. Thai Panang Paneer Curry - Heat oil in a pan / kadhai and add the panang paste. Cover and cook on a medium flame for 2 minutes. Add salt, chilies, paneer cubes and coconut milk.
1. Simmer, covered on a medium flame for 2-3 minutes. Switch off the flame and transfer to a serving dish. Enjoy with plain steamed rice.
