##### The ingredients needed to make Roasted Parmesan Bok-Choy:

1. Prepare 4 baby bok choy
1. Prepare 8 pat butter
1. Get To taste pink Himalayan salt
1. You need 8 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
1. Prepare 8 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese
1. You need 1 tablespoons red peppercorns slightly crushed
1. Provide 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder


##### Steps to make Roasted Parmesan Bok-Choy:

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Wash the bok-choy. Split the bok-choy in half. Season and drizzle with olive oil. Season with salt and garlic.
1. Add the pepper corns and put in the oven for 25 minutes.
1. Take out and add the butter and Parmesan cheese, put back in oven till butter and cheese melts. Put under the broiler till browned.
