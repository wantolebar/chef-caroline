##### The ingredients needed to prepare Yogurt Avocado Salad:

1. Provide 1 medium avocado
1. You need 1/2 iceberg lettuce
1. Prepare 1 small red onion
1. You need 4 tbsp natural yogurt
1. Provide 2 tbsp olive oil
1. You need 1 thick slice of lemon
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp wholegrain mustard
1. Use to taste Salt and pepper


##### Steps to make Yogurt Avocado Salad:

1. Tear the lettuce with your hands, chop the avocado into chunks and slice the onions. Keep all fairly the same size.
1. Mix all of the other ingredients in together. With two forks toss the salad.
