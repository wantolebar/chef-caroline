##### The ingredients needed to make Corned Beef Hash and Eggs:

1. Take 1 each large potato,  diced
1. Provide 4 oz cabbage, chopped
1. You need 2 each eggs
1. Provide 8 oz corned beef,  diced


##### Instructions to make Corned Beef Hash and Eggs:

1. Cook the potatoes,  corned beef, and cabbage in a skillet
1. When potatoes are browned, make two seperate piles of mixture.
1. Make a well in each pile, and crack an egg into well.
1. Cook until egg is cooked to your liking.  Place skillet under broiler for faster cooking of the yolk.
