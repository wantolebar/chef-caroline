##### The ingredients needed to prepare Banana &amp; pineapple smoothie:

1. Prepare 250 ml milk
1. You need 1 banana
1. Use Half a cup pineapple
1. Provide 2 tablespoons peanut butter
1. Provide 3 tablespoons yogurt
1. Use 3 tablespoons raw oats
1. You need 1 teaspoon cinnamon


##### Instructions to make Banana &amp; pineapple smoothie:

1. Pour milk into blender
1. Add pineapple into blender &amp; blend
1. Add rest of ingredients &amp; blend till smooth
1. Serve chilled
