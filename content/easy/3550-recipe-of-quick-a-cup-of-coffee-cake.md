##### Steps to make A cup of coffee cake:

1. Grab two mugs! The smaller it is the more the cake overflows and looks all pretty, if you are like us, and have slightly bigger mugs, you won&#39;t see the effect of how much it can rise! :)
1. Begin by softening the butter in the mugs for about 10-15secs, make sure you don&#39;t melt it. Next stir in 2 tbsps of sugar until its fluffy and creamy - it doesn&#39;t take long probably 30 seconds
1. If you are going with the egg, stir it up in a ramekin and divide it between the two mugs. If you are not using the egg, then go to the next step
1. Add 2 tbsps of sour cream, and a few drops of vanilla (don&#39;t pour lots!). Stir in the 1/4 cup of flour and 1/8 tsp baking powder and mix it all up. You should now have a cake batter type consistency
1. For the crumb topping!: Mix 1 tbsp of butter, 2 tbsps flour, 1 tbsp brown sugar and 1 tsp of cinamon. Smoosh it together with your fingers to form lumps. Add this crumb topping mixture to your cup
1. In the microwave it goes for a minute first, then do 10 or 20 seconds extra and that&#39;s it. It will be hot, so don&#39;t get super excited and dig into it like K did :)
