##### The ingredients needed to make Thai Peanut Curry Noodles:

1. Take 8 oz whole wheat spaghetti noodles (or any long pasta)
1. Prepare 1 broccoli, chopped (I like to use some of the stem as well)
1. Take 1 Red bell pepper, thinly sliced (or any colour)
1. Use  Sauce
1. Use 1/2 cup smooth natural peanut butter
1. Provide 2 1/2 tbsp yellow thai curry paste (red works as well)
1. Provide 2 tsp sesame oil
1. Get 1 1/2 tsp sambal (Sriracha is good as well)
1. Provide 1 tbsp soya sauce (low sodium)
1. Get 1 1/2 tbsp Fresh ginger, minced  (or more, I like more )
1. Get 1/2 cup Or so, of the pasta cooking water


##### Steps to make Thai Peanut Curry Noodles:

1. Boil water and cook pasta according to direction,  add the broccoli in the last 3 min of cook time. Reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta water and drain the rest and set aside.
1. In a large bowl, whisk the ingredients of the sauce, and slowly add the water. (You may not use the full 1/2 cup) you&#39;re looking for a smooth, lump free texture.
1. Toss your noodles, broccoli  and peppers in the sauce to combine.
1. Serve and enjoy (you can add chopped cilantro for garnish, I just didn&#39;t have any ðŸ˜¡)
