##### The ingredients needed to make Rock ledge popovers:

1. Use 2 cups sifted flour
1. You need 1/2 teaspoons salt
1. Use 3 eggs
1. Prepare 2 cups milk
1. Take 2 tablespoons melted butter


##### Instructions to make Rock ledge popovers:

1. Preheat oven to 425
1. Sift flour and salt
1. Put eggs in bowl break with fork just enough to mix in milk and butter
1. Add egg mixture to flour mix
1. Heat greased muffin pan for 5 minutes
1. Remove from oven spoon in the mix
1. Bake for 40 minutes turning oven down to 375 once they pop
