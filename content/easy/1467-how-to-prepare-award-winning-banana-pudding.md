##### The ingredients needed to prepare Banana Pudding:

1. Get  package instant vanilla pudding mix
1. Provide  cold milk
1. Take  can sweetened condensed milk
1. You need  vanilla extract
1. Prepare  container frozen whipped toppings
1. Get  package of vanilla wafers
1. Take  bananas


##### Instructions to make Banana Pudding:

1. In large bowl, mix pudding mix and milk for 2 minutes. Blend in condensed milk until smooth. Stir in vanilla extract.
1. Layer wafers first, then bananas, and last pudding in a bowl. Repeat process until bowl is full. layer top with whip cream
1. Chill for about 45 minutes to an hour and you are ready to serve
