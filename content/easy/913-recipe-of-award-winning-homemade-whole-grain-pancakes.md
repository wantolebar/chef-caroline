##### The ingredients needed to prepare Homemade whole grain pancakes:

1. You need 1 cup flour (whole grain)
1. Prepare 3 tablespoons sugar
1. Use 2 tablespoons cinnamon
1. Get 3/4 cup milk
1. Prepare 1 egg


##### Steps to make Homemade whole grain pancakes:

1. Whisk all ingredients together
1. Bake on a 350 degree pan
1. Makes: about 12
1. Top with berries, powdered sugar, or Maple syrup!
1. Enjoy!
