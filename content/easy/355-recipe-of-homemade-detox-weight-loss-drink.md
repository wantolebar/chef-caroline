##### The ingredients needed to cook Detox weight loss drink:

1. You need 1 small piece of dalchini / innamon
1. You need 1 bay leave
1. Use 2 cloves
1. Use 1 elaichi / cardamom
1. You need 1/2 tsp nigella
1. Take 1/4 lemon juice
1. Provide 1 green tea bag
1. You need 1,1 /4 cup water


##### Instructions to make Detox weight loss drink:

1. Take all ingredients,Take water in a pot, put it on flame.
1. Add bay leave, kalonji in to water.
1. Now crush cloves and elaichi and add in to boiling water.
1. After some boil off the flame.
1. Now add tea bag in it.after 5 minute take it out add lemon juice drink hot like tea.
