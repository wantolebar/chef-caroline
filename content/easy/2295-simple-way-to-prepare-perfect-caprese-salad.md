##### The ingredients needed to prepare Caprese Salad:

1. Prepare 1 packages red grape tomatoes
1. Provide 1 packages yellow grape tomatoes
1. Provide 1 packages fresh mozzarella
1. Use 1 extra virgin olive oil
1. Get 1 salt and pepper
1. Take 1 packages fresh basil


##### Instructions to make Caprese Salad:

1. Combine tomatoes and cheese in a mixing bowl. Toss with good EVOO.
1. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh basil
1. Variations: Balsamic vinegar, Red wine vinegar, goat cheese, fresh oregano
1. Try using larger tomatoes and cheese. Slice them on the bias.
