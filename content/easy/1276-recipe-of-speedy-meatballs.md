##### The ingredients needed to make Meatballs:

1. Get 1/2 pound ground beef
1. Take 1/2 pound italian sausage
1. You need 1 1/2 tbsp dry minced onion
1. Provide 1 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
1. Provide 1 tsp garlic powder
1. Get 1 tsp basil
1. Use 1 egg
1. Provide 8 saltine crackers crushed
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper


##### Instructions to make Meatballs:

1. In a large bowl mix all ingredients well. Put in a baking dish rolled into balls, should get around 20 balls.
1. Bake at 375° for 20-25 min.
1. Good for spaghetti, or bbq, can be frozen until needed.
