##### The ingredients needed to cook Simple pancake:

1. Use 100 g flour
1. Prepare 1 egg
1. Use 1 small cup of milk
1. Use to taste Sugar
1. Take as needed Butter


##### Steps to make Simple pancake:

1. Add the flour in a bowl add the egg, sugar and milk mix well to form a thick paste
1. Add one Teaspoon of butter in a pan and add two spoons full of the mix and fry until golden brown
1. Repeat the same
1. Serve hot
