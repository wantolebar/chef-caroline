##### The ingredients needed to cook Loaded Matzo Ball Soup:

1. Prepare 1 Package Matzo Ball Mix,
1. You need 1 Yellow Onion, Chopped
1. Use 3-4 Carrots, Chopped
1. Prepare 2 Celery Stalks, Chopped
1. Use  White Cabbage, Chopped
1. Take  Fresh Dill
1. Prepare  Chicken or Vegetable Stock
1. Get to Taste Salt and Pepper
1. Prepare 1 Garlic Glove (optional)


##### Instructions to make Loaded Matzo Ball Soup:

1. Prepare the Matzo Ball Mix as per packet instructions, then leave in the refrigerator as you prepare the rest.
1. In a soup pan, sauté onion and garlic (if using) for 5 - 8 minutes, until translucent and lightly brown.
1. Add carrots, celery and cabbage and cook for a further 5-8 minutes, until veggies are softening slightly.
1. Add enough chicken or veggie stock to the pot (normally 1 - 2 L).
1. Bring soup to a boil, then add rolled matzo ball mix (golf ball size).
1. Cover and simmer for about 30 minutes, until matzo balls are cooked through and still bouncy/fluffy.
1. Enjoy with a nice buttered bread on the side (challah or rye ideal!).
