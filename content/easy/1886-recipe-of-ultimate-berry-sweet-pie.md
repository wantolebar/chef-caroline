##### Steps to make Berry Sweet Pie:

1. Be sure to make your crust from scratch for the best results.
1. In bowl mix four, sugar, and cinnimon.
1. Take 1/3 of dry mixture and pour into one of the pie crusts.
1. Add the fruit then pour in the rest of your dry ingredients .
1. Place other crust over the top of the filled pie and fold the sides under the tin. Pinch the outside edge to create a traditional look.
1. As sauce leaks out paint the top of your crust with it to give a more colorful finished presentation.
1. Make 4 slit cuts on the center top of pie.
1. Place on cookie sheet and Bake at 350°F for 45min or until golden brown.
