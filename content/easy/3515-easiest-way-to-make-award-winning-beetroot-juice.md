##### The ingredients needed to make Beetroot juice:

1. Prepare 1/2 watermelon
1. Use 2 pcs beetroot
1. You need 2 pcs carrot
1. Take 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
1. Get  Honey (optional)


##### Steps to make Beetroot juice:

1. Wash and peel beetroot and carrots. Chop them.
1. Scoop the watermelon and blend together with the beetroot and carrots.
1. Add the honey and cinnamon powder.
1. Serve chilled.
