##### The ingredients needed to prepare Chocolate waffles with icecream:

1. Take 140 gm all purpose flour
1. Provide 1/2 tsp salt
1. Get 1 tbsp coco powder
1. Use 1 tsp vanilla essence
1. Take 40 gm caster sugar
1. Use 120 ml buttermilk
1. You need 35 gm+1tbsp butter melted
1. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
1. Get 1/2 tbsp baking powder
1. Prepare As needed Vanilla ice cream
1. Provide Aa needed Chopped nuts
1. Use As needed Fruits (optional)
1. Prepare 1 tbsp honey


##### Instructions to make Chocolate waffles with icecream:

1. Mix all dry ingredients maida, baking soda, baking powder,coco powder in a bowl,seive them 2 times
1. In another bowl whisk buttermilk, butter and vanilla extract,sugar
1. Pour liquid in dry ingredients
1. Stirring continuously to blend,do not remains any lumps
1. Heat waffle iron, lightly brush with melted butter,pour 1 tbsp batter,cook waffles from both sides until brown and crisp
1. You can keep the mixture in fridge,for a day
1. Serve with honey, icecream, chocolate syrup,nuts and fruits
