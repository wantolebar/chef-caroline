##### The ingredients needed to make Caramel chocolate chips cookies:

1. Prepare 1 1/4 cup flour
1. Prepare 1/4 cup brown sugar
1. Prepare 1/4 cup granulated sugar
1. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
1. You need 1 tsp vanilla
1. Take 1 egg
1. You need 1/4 cup Butter
1. Prepare 1 cup Chocolate chips
1. Take  Ceramel candy (I used eclairs)


##### Steps to make Caramel chocolate chips cookies:

1. Add the butter,granulated and brown sugar in a mixer bowl and cream them together
1. Crack ur egg and add vanilla then mix again
1. Combine the dry ingredients together flour,baking powder and baking soda then add it to ur wet ingredients and mix to form a dough
1. And the chocolate chips and mix using a spatula then refrigerate for 30mint
1. After 30mint scoop the dough in you hand then flatten it and put the ceramel candy at the center then rolled it to form a ball
1. Put parchment paper on ur baking tray then arrange the dough
1. Bake in a preheated oven for 15-20mint
1. Done
