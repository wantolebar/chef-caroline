##### The ingredients needed to prepare Pork Tendon Bone Noodle Soup:

1. You need  k Pork bones with meat /tendon
1. Prepare  pork bullion broth cube
1. Use  onion, sliced
1. Get  garlic cloves, crushed
1. Prepare  bay leaves
1. You need  salt
1. Take  pepper
1. Get  water
1. You need  cabbage, quartered
1. Get  green beans
1. Get  marble potatoes
1. Provide  fresh egg noodles


##### Instructions to make Pork Tendon Bone Noodle Soup:

1. In a pot boil the first 8 ingredients together for 1-1.5 hours with lid. If you have a pressure cooker, boil it for less than half the time.
1. Taste the soup and adjust accordingly. Add 1/4-1/2 c water if too salty or soup has reduced a lot. Add the potatoes. Boil another 10 minutes.
1. Add the cabbage and beans, just on top, and cover for another 5 minutes.
1. Drop in the egg noodles and cover for another 3-4 minutes. Turn off heat and serve immediately.
1. If not serving immediately, remove noodles and veggies and place in a separate container. Combine with hot soup when ready to serve.
