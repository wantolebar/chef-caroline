##### The ingredients needed to prepare Egg Noodles for kids:

1. You need 3 eggs
1. Take 1 packet noodle
1. Prepare 3 cups water
1. You need 1/4 tsp soya sauce
1. Provide 1/4 tsp chilli vinegar
1. You need 2 tsp oil
1. Provide 1/4 tsp salt
1. You need 1/4 tsp chilli powder


##### Steps to make Egg Noodles for kids:

1. Cook noodles in 3 cups of water. Drain and keep aside
1. Heat oil in a pan. Break and pour eggs in the pan, add salt and chilli powder
1. For scrambling the eggs,keep stirring. Once scrambled egg is ready, mix the cooked noodles and add the masala given.
1. Mix well and serve hot.
