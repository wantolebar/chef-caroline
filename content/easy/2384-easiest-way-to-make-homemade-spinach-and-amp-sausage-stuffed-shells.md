##### The ingredients needed to prepare spinach &amp; sausage stuffed shells:

1. Take 12 oz box of jumbo shells
1. Get 1/4 tsp parsley
1. Take 1/2 tsp salt &amp; pepper
1. Prepare 15 oz container of ricotta cheese
1. Get 4 cup mozzerella cheese
1. You need 10 oz package of frozen spinach; thawed and drained
1. Take 1 26 ounce jar of spagetti sauce
1. Get 1 lb sausage; cooked, broken up and drained


##### Steps to make spinach &amp; sausage stuffed shells:

1. perpare shells as directed on box; strain and let cool
1. in a medium sized skillet throughly cook and break up sausage; drain; and let cool down
1. In a large bowl mix cheeses, spices, spinach and sausage; mix throughly
1. preheat oven to 350°F
1. add a thin layer of spagetti sauce in a 13&#34; x 8&#34; cassarole pan
1. depending on size scoop about 2 tablespoons in shells or desired amount and arrange in the pan
1. pour some sauce over shells; top with more cheese if desired cook for 30-35mins
