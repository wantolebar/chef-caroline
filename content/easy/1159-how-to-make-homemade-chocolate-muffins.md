##### The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate muffins:

1. Provide 500 g flour
1. Take 250g butter
1. Provide 250g sugar
1. Prepare 50g Chocolate powder
1. You need 3 eggs


##### Instructions to make Chocolate muffins:

1. Preheat the oven
1. Weigh all the ingredients appropriately
1. Mix the eggs and sugar,cream in until its fluffy
1. Add the eggs one at a time following the same direction
1. Fold in the flour until the mixture is in good consistent
1. Butter the baking tin and pour in the mixture Bake at 180°c
