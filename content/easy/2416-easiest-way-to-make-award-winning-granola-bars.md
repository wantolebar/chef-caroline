##### The ingredients needed to prepare Granola bars:

1. Use 1/4 Cup Groundnut
1. Get 1/4 Cup Oatmeal
1. Provide 1 Cup Sugar
1. Use 1/4 Cup Cashew nut


##### Steps to make Granola bars:

1. In a dry mill, roughly blend ur groundnut and cashew but and set aside.
1. In a fryin pan on medium heat, melt ur sugar.
1. Once d sugar has melted, pour in ur nuts and oatmeal and mix thoroughly with a wooden spatula and allow to sit for 1-2 mins.
1. As its still a bit hot, mould to any shape u want.
1. Ur granola bars are ready.
