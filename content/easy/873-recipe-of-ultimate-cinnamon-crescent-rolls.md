##### The ingredients needed to make cinnamon crescent rolls:

1. Take  FILLING
1. Get 1 can crescent rolls
1. You need 5 tbsp margarine (soft-room temp)
1. Use 1/4 cup sugar
1. You need 2 tsp cinnamon
1. Use  GLAZE
1. Provide 2 tbsp melted margarine
1. Take 1/4 cup powder sugar
1. Take 1/2 tsp vanillia


##### Steps to make cinnamon crescent rolls:

1. mix first 3 ingredients together
1. open crescent rolls and spread filling over each one
1. roll crescent rolls up placing on baking sheet with small end down
1. bake in preheated oven at 350°F for 11-14- mins
1. when finished remove and let cool half way
1. while still warm drizzle glaze over then eat
