##### The ingredients needed to make Oreo Chocolate cake:

1. You need 2 cup Oreo biscuits
1. Prepare 1 cup chocolates chip biscuits
1. Provide 2 cup milk
1. Provide  Chocolates balls
1. You need 1 spoon baking powder


##### Instructions to make Oreo Chocolate cake:

1. Separate the cream of oreo from biscuits
1. Powdered the chocolates chip and oreo biscuits
1. Add two cups of milk in finely grounded powder of biscuits
1. Add separated oreo cream in mixture
1. Add one spoon of baking powder
1. Grease the mold with oil and add mixture in it
1. Place it in to oven for 30 mints
1. After decorate it with chocolates balls
