##### The ingredients needed to make Roasted Acorn Squash:

1. Use 2 acorn squash, cut in half
1. Take  Salt and pepper, generously
1. You need  olive oil spray


##### Steps to make Roasted Acorn Squash:

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cut acorn squash in half and scoop out the seeds. Season with salt and pepper generously and spray with olive oil on the squash for 2 seconds each squash.
1. Place on a cookie sheet cut side down. and Bake for 40 minutes. Flip over and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
