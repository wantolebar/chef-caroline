##### The ingredients needed to make Cottage Cheese Stuff:

1. Provide 1 small container Cool Whip
1. Use 1 large size small curd cottage cheese
1. Take 1 pkg strawberry Jell-O
1. Take 1 small container frozen strawberries (thawed)


##### Instructions to make Cottage Cheese Stuff:

1. Dump all ingredients in a bowl and mix it up (Do not add any water to the jello, just add it dry)
1. Cover &amp; place in fridge for at least 2 hours, to firm up. Enjoy :)
