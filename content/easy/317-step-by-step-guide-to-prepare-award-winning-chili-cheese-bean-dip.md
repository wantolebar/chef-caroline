##### The ingredients needed to cook Chili Cheese Bean Dip:

1. Provide 1 block Cream cheese softened
1. Provide 1 can Chili with beans
1. Get 2 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese


##### Steps to make Chili Cheese Bean Dip:

1. In a Microwave safe bowl spread Cream Cheese on the bottom of the bowl.  Next spread Chili with Beans on top of the Cream Cheese. Do not mix together!  Sprinkle Chedder Cheese on top.
1. Depending on your microwave, you would microwave on high for 6-8 minutes. Until everything is hot!
1. Serve with bread, toast &amp;  Tostitos chips - After heating, you could top with a couple of your favorite things. Cut up small tomatoes, black olives &amp; peppers etc. Enjoy!
