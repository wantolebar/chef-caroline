##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Muffins with Tofu and Pancake Mix:

1. Take 1 block Tofu (silken)
1. Take 300 grams Pancake mix


##### Instructions to make Easy Muffins with Tofu and Pancake Mix:

1. Fold in the pancake mix and tofu with a rubber spatula. Pour the batter into the muffin molds.
1. Bake in an oven preheated to 190℃ for 15-20 minutes.
1. Done.
