##### The ingredients needed to make Slow cooker hot spiced apple cider:

1. Prepare  cider
1. Provide 1 gallon apple cider
1. Provide 1 1/4 cup orange juice
1. Use 3/4 cup pineapple juice
1. Get 1 1/2 tsp ground cloves
1. Take 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1. Get 1/2 tsp nutmeg
1. Get 2 tsp ground ginger
1. Take 1/8 cup sugar
1. Get 1/8 cup honey
1. Prepare 2 tsp fresh orange zest
1. Get 12 cinnamon sticks/optional


##### Steps to make Slow cooker hot spiced apple cider:

1. Mix all ingredients in slow cooker.
1. Set to low for 4 hours.
1. Serve with cinnamon sticks in mug.
