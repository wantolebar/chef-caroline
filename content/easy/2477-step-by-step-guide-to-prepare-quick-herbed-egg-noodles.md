##### The ingredients needed to prepare Herbed Egg Noodles:

1. You need 8 oz uncooked wide egg noodles
1. Take 3 tbsp butter
1. Provide 1 garlic clove, minced
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
1. Use 1/4 tsp dill weed
1. Use 1/4 tsp dried thyme


##### Steps to make Herbed Egg Noodles:

1. Cook noodles according to package directions.
1. Meanwhile in a skillet, melt butter. Stir in garlic, salt, dill &amp; thyme.
1. Drain noodles and add butter mixture. Toss to coat.
