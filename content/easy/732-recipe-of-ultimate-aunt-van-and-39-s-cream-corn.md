##### The ingredients needed to prepare Aunt Van&#39;s Cream Corn:

1. Prepare 7 each Corn on the cob
1. You need 2 tbsp Oil
1. Provide 1 cup Flour
1. Take 1 as needed Water


##### Instructions to make Aunt Van&#39;s Cream Corn:

1. Cut corn off from cob 2 times (to get the milk). Add oil to bottom of the pot. Add corn &amp; about 1/2 cup to 1 cup of flour &amp; mix together. Add enough water to cover corn &amp; over.  Add butter, salt &amp; pepper to taste. Cook 35-40 minutes.
1. Or microwave:  25-30 minutes.
