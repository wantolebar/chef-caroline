##### The ingredients needed to cook TX&#39;s Style ~ UnStuffed Bell Pepper Casserole:

1. Get 1 tbsp Olive oil
1. Get 1 Onion, chopped
1. You need 2 Bell Peppers, chopped
1. Prepare 1/2 Poblamo pepper, chopped
1. Prepare 1 lb Ground Round
1. Get 1 tbsp Beef boullion
1. Use 1/2 cup Hot water
1. Get 3 cup Cooked rice
1. Use 1 Monterey Jack Cheese, shredded


##### Instructions to make TX&#39;s Style ~ UnStuffed Bell Pepper Casserole:

1. Saute onion and peppers in oil
