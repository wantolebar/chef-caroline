##### Instructions to make Chicken and Kale Orzo:

1. Spray a large pan with frylight and sautè the peppers and kale. Add the garlic, onion salt and chilli and cook for 5 mins. In the meantime put the orzo in a separate pan to cook.
1. After 5 mins add the chicken to frying pan and fry until the chicken is cooked through. Stir in the chicken stock.
