##### The ingredients needed to prepare Banana, Pumpkin seeds Mango smoothie:

1. You need  ripe bananas
1. Use  Pumpkin seeds soaked in water
1. Provide  water Melon seeds
1. Get  ripe mango medium peeled, deseeded and chopped
1. Provide  almond milk
1. Provide  honey
1. Take  ice cubes
1. You need  nutmeg powder
1. Use  pumpkin seeds and watermelon seeds for the garnish


##### Steps to make Banana, Pumpkin seeds Mango smoothie:

1. Soak pumpkin seeds in water for 1 hour
1. Peel and chop the bananas and mango
1. Add chopped fruits in a blender and add soaked pumpkin seeds, water melon seeds, nut meg powder, almond milk, honey and ice cubes.
1. Blend well, until you get a smooth mixture.
1. Delicious and healthy banana pumpkin seeds, mango smoothie is ready.
1. Pour into chilled serving glasses, and garnish with pumpkin seeds and watermelon seeds.
