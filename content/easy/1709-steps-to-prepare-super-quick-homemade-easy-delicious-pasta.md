1. Prepare  of pepper
1. Provide  mixed vegetables (zucchini, spinich, onions, mushrooms etc)

1. Take  of spaghetti sauce
1. Get  of cheddar cheese (divided)


##### Instructions to make easy delicious pasta:

1. boil water on high, after boiled add noodles. reduce heat to medium amd stir occasionally.
1. while noodles are cooking; in another pan, saute mixed vegetables. Cut garlic into big chunks. let cook til softened.
1. after vegetables are cooked add in spaghetti sauce. stir until all mixed together, add milk then cheese wiz &amp; half of cheddar cheese. mix in pepper for seasoning.
1. drain noodles after cooked, add them to sauce mixture. Stir together,  add remaining cheese over top.
