##### Instructions to make Barbeque style Cajun potatoes:

1. Mix mayonnaise and tomato sauce and cajun spice mix.Set aside. Wash the potatoes nicely to remove dirt. Boil them in salt water. Remove and allow them to cool.don&#39;t remove skin.Gently flatten in your palm.Do Not break it into pieces.
1. In a pan,heat oil and shallow fry the potatoes. Lay the fried potatoes in a plate. Drizzle the mayonnaise mixture on top of it.sprinkle Onions and spring Onions on it.serve hot.
