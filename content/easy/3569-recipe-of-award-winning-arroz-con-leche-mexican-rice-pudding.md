##### The ingredients needed to prepare Arroz con Leche (Mexican Rice Pudding):

1. Provide 3 cup Whole Milk
1. Take 1 1/4 cup Water
1. Use 1 cup Medium grain rice
1. Get 2 Cinnamon sticks
1. Get 1/4 tsp Salt
1. Use 14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1. You need 1/2 cup Raisins (soaked in warm water to soften)
1. Use 2 tsp Vanilla


##### Steps to make Arroz con Leche (Mexican Rice Pudding):

1. Bring milk, and water to a slew simmer in a large pot, over medium-low heat
1. Sir in rice and add cinnamon sticks. Barely simmer uncovered until rice is soft approx. 20-30 min stirring occasionally.
1. When rice is soft, remove cinnamon sticks and stir in condensed milk, vanilla, salt and raisins.
1. Return to a slight simmer for another 10-15 min or until most of the liquid is absorbed and rice has a pudding like consistency.
1. Serve hot with a sprinkle of cinnamon.
