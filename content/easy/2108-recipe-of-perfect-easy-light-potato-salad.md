##### The ingredients needed to make Easy light potato salad:

1. Prepare  small unpealed potatoes
1. You need  hardboiled eggs
1. Provide  small onion (home grown), sliced
1. Take  ripe tomato (3 colour home grown variety), diced
1. Use  flat parsley (from the my herb garden)
1. Take  rosemary (from my herb garden)
1. You need  thyme (from my herb garden)
1. You need  unfiltered virgin olive oil
1. Take  white wine vinegar infused with estragon
1. Prepare  rough salt
1. Use  freshly ground black pepper
1. Use  honey


##### Instructions to make Easy light potato salad:

1. Boil potatoes untill soft
1. Hardboil your organic eggs (mine is from my chickens in the back yard) normally 10 min from cold water to ready.
1. Cut your potatoes in two, and dice your eggs after.
1. Add our sliced onion, diced tomato and herbs and give it a gentle stir as not to break the potatoes
1. Add your olive oil and vinegar. let it stand for two hours so that the potatoes absorbs all the flavours of the herbs.
1. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste
