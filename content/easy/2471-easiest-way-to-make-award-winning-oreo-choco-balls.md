##### The ingredients needed to prepare Oreo choco balls:

1. Provide 15 Oreo biscuits
1. Take 60 gms cheese
1. You need 1 Cadbury dairy milk
1. Take 6 pieces white chocolate
1. Prepare as required Cashew
1. Prepare as required Pesto


##### Steps to make Oreo choco balls:

1. Firstly blended biscuits &amp; made finely crushed biscuit.
1. Then stirred the cheese nicely &amp; mixed the crushed biscuit in it.
1. Made a dough &amp; kept it 30 mins for refrigeration. Took 1/2 bowl of hot water &amp; dipped the white chocolate filled glass in it for melting.
1. White chocolate melted. After 30 mins of refrigeration mixed crushed cashew &amp; almond nuts.
1. Mixed nicely. Made Oreo chocco balls. Then dipped into white &amp; dark chocolate.
1. Dipped into dark &amp; white chocolate.
1. After garnishing chocco balls are looking like that.
