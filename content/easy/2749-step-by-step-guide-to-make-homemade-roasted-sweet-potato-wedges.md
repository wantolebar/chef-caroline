##### The ingredients needed to prepare Roasted sweet potato wedges:

1. You need 2 sweet potatoes
1. Use 1 tsp garlic puree
1. Prepare 1 tsp cider vinegar
1. Take 1 tbsp olive or avocado oil
1. Take 2 tsp paprika
1. Take 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1. Provide 1 tsp lime juice (or lemon)
1. Get  Season with salt and pepper


##### Steps to make Roasted sweet potato wedges:

1. Wash and quarter sweet potatoes. In a large bowl mix spices, oil, garlic, lime juice, vinegar, salt and pepper.
1. Toss potatoes in mixture to cover. Place onto a lightly oiled baking tray in the centre of a preheated oven 220C for 15 minutes. Turn oven and return to oven for another 15 minutes until golden on the outside and fluffy on the inside.
