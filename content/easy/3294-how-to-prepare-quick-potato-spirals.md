##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Spirals:

1. Prepare 500 kg small potatoes or fingerlings
1. Take 1/4 cup Olive oil
1. Take 1 tsp dried dill/thyme leaves
1. You need 1 tsp garlic powder
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Take as needed Oil for deep frying
1. Prepare 10 Wooden skewers


##### Steps to make Potato Spirals:

1. Soak skewers in water for 30 minutes.
1. Wash and scrub potatoes (with skin on if preferred) thoroughly.
1. Cut small ends off the potatoes. Insert the skewer into the potato making sure it in the center of the potato.
1. With a sharp paring knife, start cutting the potato keeping the knife at an angle. Keep the knife close to the cuts for getting thinner slices of potato.
1. For flavored oil: Combine olive oil with dried herbs, garlic powder, and salt.
1. Heat oil a deep thick bottomed pot. Deep fry the potatoes without overcrowding them.
1. Remove them to a plate lined with paper napkins to drain excess oil.
1. Spread the potato slices gently to avoid breaking them apart.
1. Brush the potatoes with prepared flavoured oil and serve hot with ketchup or any dip of your choice.
