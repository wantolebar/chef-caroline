##### The ingredients needed to cook Apple smoothie:

1. Take 2 Apples
1. Use 2 tablespoons sugar
1. Provide 1 glass milk
1. Take as required Dry fruits (Almond Cashew Makhana Coconut Sawdust)


##### Steps to make Apple smoothie:

1. Clean the apple, Peel off and cut it into small pieces.
1. Pour apple milk and all the makhane dry fruits in a large pot and keep it for 5 minutes.
1. Grind in grinder.
1. Remove from glass and garnish with dry fruit and serve for drinking.
