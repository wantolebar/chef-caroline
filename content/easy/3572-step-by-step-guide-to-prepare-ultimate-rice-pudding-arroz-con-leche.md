##### The ingredients needed to prepare Rice pudding/arroz con leche:

1. Get  Ingredients
1. Take 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
1. Prepare 4 tbsp sugar
1. Use 1 cup rice
1. Use 4 cup milk


##### Steps to make Rice pudding/arroz con leche:

1. Grab a large pot to make your rice pudding in
1. mix in 2 cups of milk, tablespoon of ground cinnamon, 4 tablespoons of sugar into the pot and bring to a boil
1. pour in the cup of rice and let simmer mixing every 2-5 min.
1. as the rice and milk thicken add in another cup of milk. Do this only when the rice thickens until you get it to your liking
1. Rice pudding complete! Set aside to cool or serve instantly if you can&#39;t wait
