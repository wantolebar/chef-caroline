##### The ingredients needed to cook Sweet and Sour Ribs:

1. Take 1 bell pepper cut in diced
1. Prepare Half onion diced
1. Prepare 5 cherry tomatoes
1. Get 1 kg ribs
1. Get  Sauce:sugar vinegar ketchup water
1. Use  Flour for coating
1. Take  Black pepper
1. Use  Egg white


##### Instructions to make Sweet and Sour Ribs:

1. Mix flour salt white pepper and egg white then coat all the ribs.Heat a wok and add oil deep fry one by one the ribs until golden brown.Remove and set aside
1. Saute onion until translucent then add bell pepper.Pout the ribs and the sauce.Cook it until the sauce evaporates and absorbs by the meat.Serve
