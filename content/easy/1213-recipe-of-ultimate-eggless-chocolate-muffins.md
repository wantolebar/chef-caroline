##### The ingredients needed to cook Eggless chocolate muffins:

1. Use 3/4 cup All purpose flour
1. Prepare 2 1/2  tablespoon Cocoa powder
1. Get 1/2 cup sugar
1. Prepare 1/2  teaspoon baking soda
1. Get 1/4 teaspoon salt
1. You need 1/4 cup oil
1. You need 1/2 cup cold water
1. You need 1teaspoon vanilla essence
1. Provide 1 teaspoon lemon juice/vinegar


##### Instructions to make Eggless chocolate muffins:

1. Take flour, cocoa powder and baking soda sieve together.
1. Add salt and sugar in it
1. In another bowl take oil, cold water and essence and mix well
1. Reserve lemon juice aside in a separate bowl
1. Now mix both dry and wet ingredients mix till sugar gets dissolved well.
1. Pre heat micro on high for 1 minute.
1. Just before starting baking add lemon juice in the batter and mix well.
1. Fill muffins liners in baking tray fill 3/4
1. Bake on high for 4 to 5 minutes or till toothpick inserted gets out clean.
1. After gets baked; put Muffins on wire rack to allow them cool.
1. Yummy eggless chocolate muffins are ready.
1. Happy Cooking!!!
