##### The ingredients needed to make 2 Ingredients Pancakes:

1. You need 1 Banana (ripe)
1. Take 2 Eggs
1. Get 1 cooking spray, for pan
1. Get 1 tbsp Peanut Butter (optional)


##### Steps to make 2 Ingredients Pancakes:

1. Mash ripe banana well (and peanut butter), add in whisk eggs.
1. Warm non stick or cast iron pan to medium, spray with oil. Cook silver dollar size pancakes 30 seconds on each side and serve.
