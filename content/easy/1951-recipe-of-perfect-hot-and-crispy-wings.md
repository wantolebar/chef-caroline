1. Provide 2 cup Flour (mada)
1. Provide 1/2 tsp Salt

1. Take 1 tsp Chicken powder
1. Use  For frying
1. You need 2 cup Oil


##### Steps to make Hot and crispy wings:

1. In mixing bowl marinate chicken wings with black pepper, salt, ginger garlic paste,,for 30 min
1. Add black pepper and salt mix in flour and divide 2 part,1part add 1 glass water and make paste - 2nd leave dry flour
1. Take 1 bowl chill water
1. Take a pan and heat oil
1. Now roll each chicken -  Wings in flour mixture and deep in chill water and last deep dry flour
1. Derp fry till light golden and crispy
