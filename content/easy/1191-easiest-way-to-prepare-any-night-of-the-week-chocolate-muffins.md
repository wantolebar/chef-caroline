##### The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate Muffins:

1. Get 60 grams Margarine
1. Get 50 grams (Cane) sugar
1. You need 1 Egg
1. Prepare 35 grams ○Milk
1. Take 5 grams ○Rum (or milk)
1. Get 85 grams ★White flour
1. Provide 15 grams ★Cocoa powder
1. Take 3 grams ★Baking powder
1. Provide 40 grams Chocolate chips (or chopped chocolate bar)


##### Steps to make Chocolate Muffins:

1. Sift the ★ dry ingredients.  Bring margarine, egg, and milk to room temperature. Combine the ○ ingredients.
1. Put margarine into a bowl, and mix until soft. Add sugar little by little, and mix well with a whisk until white and fluffy.
1. Add a beaten egg little by little, and mix well with the whisk.
1. Alternate adding the ingredients: 1/3 of ★, 1/2 of ○, 1/3 of ★, the remaining ○,  the remaining ★, and chocolate chips in that order, and fold in until the batter is no longer floury!
1. Pour the batter into muffin cups about 70 ~ 80% full with a spoon. Sprinkle sliced almonds or walnuts as toppings if you like.
1. Bake in the preheated oven at 180℃ (350℉) for about 25 ~ 30 minutes, then it&#39;s done.
