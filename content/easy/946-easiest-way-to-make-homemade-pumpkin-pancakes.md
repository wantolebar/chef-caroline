##### The ingredients needed to cook Pumpkin Pancakes:

1. You need 2-3 Cups Baking Mix
1. You need 1 Can Pumpkin Puree
1. Use 5 tbsps Ground Cinnamon
1. Get 1/4 Cup Granulated Sugar
1. Take 2 Cups Milk (more if needed)


##### Instructions to make Pumpkin Pancakes:

1. Mix everything in a large mixing bowl. Add enough milk to make it thin enough to pour into skillet.
1. Heat a large skillet on medium low heat. Add a Tablespoon or two of butter to coat skillet. Once melted add batter to skillet to make the size of pancake desired.
1. When small bubbles start to form around edges flip pancake over and finish cooking for 2 minutes longer or til cooked through. Once done serve immediately with your favorite syrup and toppings ðŸ˜‹
