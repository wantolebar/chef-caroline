##### The ingredients needed to make South India Famous Tomato Jam:

1. Provide  Big Tomatoes (cut the tomatoes in chunks)
1. You need  Sugar
1. Use  Cashew nuts (break in half)
1. Take  Raisins
1. Prepare  Ghee
1. Get  Cardamom
1. Get  Water


##### Steps to make South India Famous Tomato Jam:

1. Heat a frying pan add ghee and fry cashewnuts till golden brown and keep aside,now fry the kishmish.
1. Take a pan add sugar and water along the Cardamom and bring to boil until sugar is melt down.
1. Now add the tomato chunks and let it cook until all the tomatoes have cook and melted out,reduce the flame and let become thick consistency.
1. Add the cashewnuts and kishmish,mix well.
1. Can be eaten with anything of your choice
1. P.S:-Can be refrigerated up to 2 to 3 weeks
