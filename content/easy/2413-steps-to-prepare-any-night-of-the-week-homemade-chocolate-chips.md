##### The ingredients needed to cook Homemade chocolate chips:

1. Provide  dark compound chocolate
1. Get  white compound chocolate
1. Take  butter
1. Get  icing sugar
1. Get  butter paper/parchment paper


##### Instructions to make Homemade chocolate chips:

1. Melt the chocolate in double boiler or in oven.
1. Rest for few min and check the consistency of chocolate, it should be in folding texture and not pouring texture.
1. Mix 1/2tsp butter and icing sugar while chocolate is warm. Make a butter piping and pour the chocolate.
1. Cut the nozzle edge and drop the chocolate on butter paper like chips. Rest for 5mins untill chocolate is bit hard.
1. Place in refrigerator for 3mins and remove the chocolate chips. Store in airtight container for future use.
