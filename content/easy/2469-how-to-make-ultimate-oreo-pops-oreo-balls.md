##### The ingredients needed to cook Oreo Pops/Oreo balls:

1. Provide 1 packet Oreo Biscuit
1. Prepare 3 tsp Honey
1. Get 1 tsp milk powder
1. Prepare 1 cup Dark compound
1. Provide 1/4 cup white compound
1. Use as needed colour full sprinkles
1. Provide as needed chopped cashew nuts
1. Get as needed chocos chopped


##### Steps to make Oreo Pops/Oreo balls:

1. First take Oreo Biscuits remove cream part and powder all biscuits properly.
1. Now melt chocolate in microwave or double boiler process.
1. Now mix Oreo Biscuit powder and honey make a dough and make small balls set it in freezer 10 minutes.
1. After 10 minutes take 1 by 1 Oreo balls set lollypo sticks and coat melted chocolate sprincle cashew nuts, sprincles and set it in a freezer in 20 minutes
