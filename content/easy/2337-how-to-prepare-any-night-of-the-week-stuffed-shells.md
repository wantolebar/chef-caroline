##### The ingredients needed to make Stuffed shells:

1. Use 1.5-2 lbs chicken
1. Prepare 15 oz cream cheese with chives
1. Provide 2 cups shredded cheese
1. Provide 4 oz parmesan cheese
1. Take 24 oz jar of spaghetti sauce
1. Prepare 1 (12 oz) box of jumbo shells


##### Instructions to make Stuffed shells:

1. Cook chicken in crock pot in chicken broth
1. Shred chicken
1. Melt 1 cup of shredded cheese, 4 oz of parmesan cheese, 15 oz of cream cheese with chives and 1/2 of a jar of spaghetti sauce. Then add to shredded chicken.
1. Cook jumbo shells for 8 min. Then drain and rinse in cold water.
1. Pre heat oven to 400 degrees. Stuff shells. Put the rest of spaghetti sauce on top of the shells then top off with the rest of the shredded cheese.
1. Cook for 25-30 min
