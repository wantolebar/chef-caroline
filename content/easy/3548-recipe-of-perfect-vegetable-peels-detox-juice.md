##### The ingredients needed to cook Vegetable Peels Detox Juice:

1. Prepare 1 cup mix Vegetable Peels of (Carrot, cucumber, beetroot, bottle gourd)
1. Use to taste salt and Red chilli powder
1. Provide 1/2 tsp black pepper Powder
1. Use 1/2 tsp Chaat masala to sprinkle
1. You need 2 cups Hot water


##### Steps to make Vegetable Peels Detox Juice:

1. Wash all the veggies. Peel them and collect the peels in a glass bowl.
1. Heat water without boiling. Pour over the peels.
1. Cover and keep them soaked overnight.
1. Strain them in the morning, add the desired spices and enjoy this healthy detox water.
