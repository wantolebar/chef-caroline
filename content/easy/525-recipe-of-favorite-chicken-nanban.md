##### The ingredients needed to cook Chicken Nanban:

1. Take 2 Chicken breast
1. You need 1 Cake flour
1. Use 2 Beaten egg
1. Take 1 dash less than needed for deep frying Vegetable oil
1. You need  For the sauce:
1. You need 3 tbsp Rice vinegar
1. You need 3 tbsp Soy sauce
1. Get 2 tbsp Raw cane sugar (or regular sugar)
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Mirin
1. Take 1 tbsp Cooking sake
1. You need  Tartar Sauce:
1. You need 2 Boiled egg
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Mayonnaise (I used light mayo)
1. Use 1 tbsp Ketchup
1. Provide 1 tbsp Milk
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Finely chopped onions
1. Get 1 tbsp Dried parsley
1. Use 1 dash Salt and pepper
1. Prepare 1 tsp Lemon juice


##### Steps to make Chicken Nanban:

1. Thinly slice the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces and season with salt and pepper.
1. Add the ingredients for the marinade into a small saucepan and let it simmer gently. Combine all of the ingredients for the tartar sauce in a separate bowl.
1. Coat the chicken from Step 1 in a layer of flour and then a layer of beaten egg and fry in a little oil.
1. While the chicken from Step 3 is still hot, place it in the marinade and leave for a while for the flavours to soak in.
1. To finish, simply arrange on a plate with the tartar sauce.
