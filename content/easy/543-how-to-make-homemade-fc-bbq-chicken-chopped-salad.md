##### The ingredients needed to prepare FC BBQ Chicken Chopped Salad:

1. Take  mixed salad greens (2 bags)
1. You need  corn (boiled, steamed, roasted, or grilled are all good), kernels cut off
1. You need  cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
1. You need  small red onion, thinly sliced
1. Provide  an apple, cored and diced
1. Get  precooked chicken breast halves (baked, roasted, grilled are all fine), cut into 1/2&#34; cubes
1. You need  grated cheese (I had mild cheddar. I think the original has Jack.)
1. Take  basil depending on how much you like, chopped
1. You need  BBQ sauce
1. Prepare  Ranch dressing
1. You need  fresh lime


##### Instructions to make FC BBQ Chicken Chopped Salad:

1. Your ingredients.
1. Put salad greens in a large mixing bowl
1. Throw all your other ingredients on top then dress with Ranch and BBQ sauce and a healthy squeeze or two of fresh lime juice.
1. Toss thoroughly and enjoy!
