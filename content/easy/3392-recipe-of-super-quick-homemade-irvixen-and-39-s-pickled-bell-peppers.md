##### The ingredients needed to make Irvixen&#39;s Pickled Bell Peppers:

1. Use 2 each Medium sweet red, yellow and green bell peppers
1. Provide 1 large Red onion, halved and thinly sliced
1. Provide 2 tsp Mixed pickling spices
1. Take 1 tsp Celery seed
1. You need 1 cup Sugar
1. Use 1 cup White vinegar
1. Prepare 1/3 cup Water


##### Steps to make Irvixen&#39;s Pickled Bell Peppers:

1. In large glass bowl, combine peppers and onion.
1. In a small saucepan, combine remaining ingredients. Bring to boil, boil for 1 minute.
1. Pour mixture into bowl through a fine mesh sieve. Discard spices.
1. Refrigerate, covered, 24 hours, stirring occasionally.
1. Can be refrigerated up to one month.
1. Great as a hot dog relish!
