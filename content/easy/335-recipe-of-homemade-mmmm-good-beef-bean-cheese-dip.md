##### The ingredients needed to make Mm..Mm Good Beef Bean/cheese dip:

1. Take 1 lb Ground beef/round
1. Use 1 can Refried beans
1. Prepare 1 can Small can Green chiles
1. Get 1 packages Taco seasoning
1. Use 1 cup Sour cream
1. Use 1 packages Mixed mexican cheese
1. Take 1 bag of Tortilla chips
1. Take 1 cup Chopped green onion
1. You need 1/2 cup Water


##### Instructions to make Mm..Mm Good Beef Bean/cheese dip:

1. Brown ground beef/drain/add seasoning along with water...let simmer 10min
1. Open refried beans/mix with seasoned beef..sprinkle a few green onion in beef mix
1. Put mixed beef/beans in dish..spread sour cream ontop
1. Layer sour cream with chiles/cover next with cheese....bake for 15 on 350..take out sprinkle last bit of green onion on finished dip
1. Eat by dipping tortilla chips/No double dipping...
