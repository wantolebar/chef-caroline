##### The ingredients needed to cook Mexican street corn pasta:

1. You need  cobs of corn grilled and cut (or one can of corn)
1. Provide  pasta cooked and drained
1. Use  pasta water (for sauce)
1. Get  heavy or lite cream
1. Provide  a cooked veggies (optional- I used green beans)
1. Use  Grilled chicken or ground turkey cooked (optional)
1. Get  Chopped fresh parsley
1. Prepare  Parmesan cheese
1. Use  Queso fresco crumbled
1. Use  Sprinkle of pepper flakes
1. Take  Salt and pepper


##### Steps to make Mexican street corn pasta:

1. Boil pasta and set aside. Grill corn and cut up.
1. Add heavy cream and some pasta water to pan. Heat through and added the 2 cheeses, veggies, pasta, and seasoning.
1. Add chicken and anything else you would want.
