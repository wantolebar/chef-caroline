##### The ingredients needed to cook Beetroot Juice:

1. Prepare 2 small beetroots
1. Prepare 6 tsp jaggery
1. Prepare To taste black salt
1. You need 2 tsp honey
1. Prepare 1 lemon juice
1. Get 1 ltr cold water
1. You need As required ice cubes (optional)
1. Get As required sugar (optional)


##### Instructions to make Beetroot Juice:

1. Peel off beetroot. Chop into pieces. Grind it. Transfer it to the bowl. Add water.
1. Or you can also grate it, add water &amp; keep aside for half an hour.
1. Now add all ingredients &amp; mix it well.
1. You can add more salt &amp; sugar as per your taste. Sieve the juice &amp; serve it.
