##### Steps to make Cinnamon roll bites:

1. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt.
1. Cut the cold butter into cubes and add to the bowl. Cut in the butter with a fork until they are in very small pieces.
1. Stir in milk gradually, adding just enough so that the dough comes together. Bring together in a ball with your hands (if you accidentally add too much milk and it becomes sticky, just add a little more flour). You may not need all the milk.
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease the sides of a 9-10&#34; pie plate.
1. In a medium bowl, combine brown sugar, granulated sugar and cinnamon. Roll dough into 1&#34; balls and dip in cinnamon sugar mixture and place in the pie plate.
1. Drizzle the melted butter over the dough balls in the pan. Bake for 23-26 minutes until biscuits are cooked and butter/sugar mixture is bubbling in the bottom of the pan.
1. While the dough is baking combine cream cheese, powder sugar, butter, and vanilla in a small bowl. Using electric mixer, beat until smooth. Spread glaze on rolls. Serve warm or at room temperature.
