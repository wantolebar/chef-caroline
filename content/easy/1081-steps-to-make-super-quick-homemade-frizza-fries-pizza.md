##### The ingredients needed to cook Frizza (Fries +Pizza):

1. You need As needed Ready made french fries(can make at home too)
1. Use As needed Ready made pizza sauce (can make at home too)
1. Get As needed Satay sticks soaked in water
1. You need 2 tablespoons chopped onion
1. Provide 2 tablespoons chopped red capsicum
1. Provide 2 tablespoons chopped green capsicum
1. Prepare 2 tablespoons boiled corn kernels
1. Provide 1 teaspoon olive oil
1. Provide Pinch salt,
1. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
1. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon oregano
1. Provide 2-3 tablespoons grated mozerrella cheese


##### Instructions to make Frizza (Fries +Pizza):

1. Deep fry about 20 french fries.
1. Mix all the chopped vegetables,oil,salt chilli flakes and oregano in a bowl.
1. Put about ten french fries in each satay stick,apply pizza sauce on top of fries and spread the vegetables on pizza sauce,top it with grated mozerrella cheese.
1. Bake it in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for 5 to 7 minutes or until cheese melts. Serve hot with tomato ketchup.
