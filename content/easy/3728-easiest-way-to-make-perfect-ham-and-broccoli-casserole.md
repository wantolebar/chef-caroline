##### The ingredients needed to prepare Ham and broccoli casserole:

1. Use 2 cups spiral noodles
1. Take 2 cups diced ham
1. Take 2 cups fresh broccoli chopped into small pieces
1. Use 1 jar alfredo sauce
1. Get 1/2 cup 2% milk
1. Get 1 tbsp vegetable oil
1. Use 1/2 onion
1. Get 2 cups grated cheese
1. Take  Ground Pepper


##### Steps to make Ham and broccoli casserole:

1. In a med saucepan over med heat add oil and cook ham until lightly browned
1. Add onion &amp; broccoli simmer until broccoli soft.
1. Pour alfredo sauce and milk and mix well
1. In a lightly greased cassarole pour all ingredients cover with grated cheese and bake at 350° for 30min or until cheese melted turning slightly brown.
