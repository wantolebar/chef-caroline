##### The ingredients needed to make Love Your Husband Dip (Beer and Cheese Dip):

1. Take  cream cheese
1. Use  shredded cheddar cheese(I prefer sharp)
1. Use  beer ( I prefer a dark beer because it gives the dip a stronger beer taste  but go with what you have on hand)
1. Take  hidden valley ranch salad dressing mix
1. Take  dijon mustard


##### Steps to make Love Your Husband Dip (Beer and Cheese Dip):

1. In a microwavable bowl place the cream cheese and heat for 45 seconds. Stir it a little and replace it in the microwave for another 30 seconds.
1. Add all remaining ingredients and give a good stir. Back in the microwave for 45 seconds. Remove and mix it well again. Back in the microwave for 45 seconds.
1. At this point it should be a creamy consistency where all ingredients are mixed well and cheese is completely melted.
1. Serve with pretzels, tortilla chips, crackers(Triscuits are great with this),bread slices, carrots and celery.....whatever you like to dip!!
