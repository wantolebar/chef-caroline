##### The ingredients needed to prepare Carrot cake:

1. Provide 4 eggs
1. Provide 1 1/4 cups vegetable oil
1. Get 2 cups sugar
1. You need 2 tea vanilla
1. Prepare 2 cups flour
1. You need 2 tea baking soda
1. Prepare 2 tea baking powder
1. Take 1/2 tea salt
1. Prepare 2 tea ground cinnamon
1. Prepare 3 cups grated carrots
1. Provide  Pecans and raisins optional


##### Steps to make Carrot cake:

1. Pre heat oven to 350°C
1. In large bowel beat together eggs, oil, sugar and vanilla
1. Mix in flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and cinnamon
1. Fold in carrots opt also pecans and raisins
1. Put in 9×13 baking dish for 40 to 50 min.
1. When cool frost with cream cheese frosting
