##### The ingredients needed to make Crunchy and Delicious!  Pan-Fried Sugar Snap Peas:

1. Take  Sugar snap peas
1. Prepare  Olive oil
1. Prepare  Salt and pepper


##### Steps to make Crunchy and Delicious!  Pan-Fried Sugar Snap Peas:

1. Remove the fibrous parts of the snap peas, rinse with water and drain.
1. Add the snap peas, olive oil, salt and pepper to a plastic bag and shake it.
1. Spread the snap peas from Step 2 into a heated skillet, cover and cook over medium heat for 2 minutes.
1. Turn the snap peas over and cook another 1.5 minutes uncovered, then serve.
1. Check out an easy toaster oven version. -  - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/172000-easy-sugar-snap-peas-baked-in-the-toaster-oven
1. Check and adjust the cooking time at Step 3 and 4.
1. I used to pan-fry the peas over medium high heat at Step 3, but medium heat seems better.
