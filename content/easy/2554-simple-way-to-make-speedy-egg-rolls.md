##### The ingredients needed to cook Egg rolls:

1. Use  for the dough
1. Use 1 n half cup of flour
1. Use 1/2 tspn Baking powder
1. Take Pinch salt
1. Prepare 50 g butter
1. Take 2 tblspn v.oil
1. You need 1/2 cup cold water
1. Take  V.oil for frying
1. Prepare  Filling
1. Get 4 Boiled eggs


##### Steps to make Egg rolls:

1. In a clean bowl put flour, Baking powder,salt,oil and butter mix very well until everything is well combine
1. Add cold water to foam the dough crack the eggs and remove the shell,divide the Dough into 4equal halves
1. Flatten each dough put 1egg in the center and roll until the dough covered all part of the egg
1. Place pan on fire add the vegetable oil Allow to get hot then you reduce the fire,put in the egg dough rolls and fry until golden brown
1. Serve
