##### The ingredients needed to cook Chiles Rellenos:

1. Provide 12 Chile poblanos
1. You need 2.5 LBS Ground beef
1. Use 4 Large Carrots
1. Prepare 4 Medium Potatoes
1. Take 1 Medium Yellow Onion
1. Use 2 Jalapeños
1. Provide 1 Cup All Purpose Flour
1. You need 12 Eggs Seperated
1. Get 4 Cloves Garlic
1. You need 1 Tbsp Tomato Buillon
1. Provide 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
1. Prepare 1 Tsp Pepper
1. Provide 2 Medium Tomatoes
1. Provide  Oil For Frying


##### Steps to make Chiles Rellenos:

1. Once ground beef is sauteed a little, add water. Mix in cubed carrots then potatoes when it&#39;s almost done add onion, Jalapeños, And Tomatoes.
1. Drain food when it&#39;s done. Add 1/4 cup of water.
1. Return food to pan then add Tomato Buillon, salt, garlic powder, and pepper.
1. Cut a line in the middle of each poblano Pepper, and take out all of the seeds
1. Char Chile poblanos, then peel skin off.
1. Stuff each pepper with food that you made before. Close up with toothpicks.
1. Throw poblano Pepper in flour, make sure it is well coated.
1. Whisk egg whites untill you stuff peaks, then add yolks (I do two at a time, because they deflate quickly)
1. Coat floured pepper in the egg
1. Fry stuffed coated pepper. Enjoy !
1. Enjoy!
