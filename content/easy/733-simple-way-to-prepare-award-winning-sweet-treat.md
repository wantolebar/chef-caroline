##### The ingredients needed to cook Sweet Treat:

1. Get 5 cake slices (cut in squares)
1. Provide 5 apple slices (cut in squares)
1. Use 1 tbsp chopped mango wadi (ambawadi)
1. You need 1 tsp cardamom powder
1. Provide 5 rasgulla slices
1. You need 2 tbsp sugar
1. Prepare 2 tbsp homemade ghee
1. You need Pinch salt
1. Get 2 tbsp sugar


##### Steps to make Sweet Treat:

1. Melt 2 tbsp homemade ghee in a pan &amp; roast the cake slices. Once roasted remove from pan &amp; let it cool.
1. Add 2 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp cardamom powder &amp; pinch of salt to a pan &amp; let the sugar melt. Once the mixture turns brown, add the apple slices &amp; let them caramelize. Once done, remove the slices &amp; let them cool.
1. To arrange in the serving plate, place a slice of roasted cake, then a slice of caramelized apple, a slice of rasgulla &amp; some chopped mango wadi. Serve.
