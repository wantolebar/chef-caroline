##### The ingredients needed to cook Carrot Cake Mug Cake #mugcakeweek:

1. Use 6 Tablespoon all Purpose Flour
1. Provide 3 Tablespoon grated Carrots
1. Provide 2 Tablespoon Sugar
1. Take 1/4 Teaspoon Vanilla essence
1. Prepare 1/4 Teaspoon Baking Powder
1. Prepare 2 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
1. Provide 1/8 Teaspoon Salt
1. Take 1/8 Teaspoon Cinnamon
1. Prepare 4 Tablespoon Milk


##### Steps to make Carrot Cake Mug Cake #mugcakeweek:

1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and cinnamon until combined
1. Add vegetable oil, milk vanilla Essence, whisk to combine
1. Add grated carrots and fold them in
1. Set microwave to high and put the mug with cake batter. Cook 2 minutes, check if done by lightly tapping the middle, it should be firm, if not add an extra minute
1. Serve immediately
