##### The ingredients needed to prepare Corned beef hash:

1. Prepare 1 tin corned beef
1. You need 6 floury potatoes
1. Take  Salt and pepper knob butter
1. Get Dash milk
1. Take 75 g mature cheddar
1. Prepare 2 tomatoes
1. Get Dash Worcestershire sauce


##### Instructions to make Corned beef hash:

1. Boil the potatoes, drain and mash.  Add the butter, salt and pepper and mash well.
1. Add the tin of corned beef and mix well, empty into a casserole dish.
1. Grate the cheese, sprinkle on top of the corned beef hash.  Slice the tomatoes and lay on top. Sprinkle over the Worcestershire sauce
1. Bake for 30 minutes in the oven
