##### The ingredients needed to prepare Vegetable Daliya:

1. Get  Daliya
1. Prepare  water
1. Use  Salt
1. Use  large onion (thin-sliced)
1. Get  Mix Vegetables(1 cup)
1. Provide  Heeng
1. Use  Cumin seeds
1. Use  Red chilli powder, turmeric, coriander powder
1. You need  mustard oil


##### Steps to make Vegetable Daliya:

1. Dry roast the dalia and now keep it aside. Now in the same cooker add 1 tbsp of mustard oil and a tsp of cumin seeds and roast for 1 minute.
1. Now add sliced onion fry for 1 min now add veggies (cabbage peas, potato,capsicum,carrot)and fry for further 3 minutes.
1. Now add salt, coriander powder, turmeric powder,red chilli powder and fry for 2 minutes. Add daliya in it stir properly and close the lid of pressure cooker and allow it to whistles twice on medium flame and cook for 3 minutes on low flame.
1. Now yummy healthy daliya is ready serve it hot and enjoy.
