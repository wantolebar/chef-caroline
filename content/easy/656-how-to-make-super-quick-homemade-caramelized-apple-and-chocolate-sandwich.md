##### The ingredients needed to make Caramelized Apple and Chocolate Sandwich:

1. You need  Apple
1. Prepare  sugar
1. Prepare  Cardamom powder
1. Prepare  lemon juice
1. Provide  Dairy milk (23 grms)
1. Take  Chocolate syrup
1. Take  Whole Wheat Bread (Homemade bread is used here)


##### Instructions to make Caramelized Apple and Chocolate Sandwich:

1. Collect all the ingredients. Peel and grate the Apple
1. Now transfer the grated Apple into a pan and put it on low flame. Cook the Apple for 5-7 min untill all moisture absorb. Than add sugar in it and further cook until it become thick. Add cardamom powder and switch off the flame. When it cool down completely add lemon juice in it. Transfer it into a bowl.
1. Now take 2 bread slices. Apply butter and grated chocolate on one slice. Spread prepared caramelized Apple on other slice.
1. Apply butter on griller and put the sandwich on it and cook it until crisp. Take it out in plate.
1. Garnish with chocolate syrup and serve hot..
