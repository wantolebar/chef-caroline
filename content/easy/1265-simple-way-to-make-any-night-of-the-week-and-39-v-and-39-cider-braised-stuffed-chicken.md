##### The ingredients needed to make &#39;V&#39; cider braised stuffed chicken:

1. Prepare  x150 g skinless boneless chicken breasts
1. Use  low fat cooking spray
1. Get  red onion, cut into thin slices
1. Use  mls dry cider
1. Provide  freshly ground black pepper
1. Provide  FOR THE STUFFING.....50 grams wholemeal breadcrumbs
1. Get  capers in brine
1. Get  fresh sage leaves, chopped
1. Take  garlic clove diced
1. Get  low fat spread


##### Instructions to make &#39;V&#39; cider braised stuffed chicken:

1. Place the chicken breasts between two sheets of  cling film on a chopping board and bash gently with the end of  a rolling pin to flatten slightly.  Use a Sharp knife to cut into the meat to form a pocket.
1. Mix together the ingredients for the stuffing and season with some black pepper. push the stuffing into the pocket, pulling the chicken over. keep back  about a tablespoon of the mixture.
1. present the oven to 180c/ fan oven 160c Spray a non stick frying pan with the cooking spray and heat until hot. Add the onion and cook, stirring occasionally for 5 minutes until softened. Spoon into an ovenproof dish which will hold the chicken snuggly.
1. Place the chicken on top of the onions and pour over the cider. Scatter over remaining stuffing. Spray a sheet of foil with the cooking spray and cover the dish. bake for 20 minutes then remove the foil and cook for a further 10 minutes until the chicken is cooked through serve immediately with green veg or rice....enjoy
