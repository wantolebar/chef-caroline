##### Steps to make Egg noodles:

1. Boil the noodles. Strain the water when done. Mix the noodles with some oil.
1. Heat oil. Add finely chopped garlic and green chillies followed by carrots and capsicum.
1. Add the noodles. Stir well.
1. Now add soya sauce, green chilli sauce l, salt and vinegar. Mix everything well.
1. Before turning the flame add onions and pepper powder.
1. Now in a different pan, heat some oil, add the eggs. Add salt and pepper. Scramble it nicely.
1. Add the scrambled eggs into the noodles. Serve it hot!
