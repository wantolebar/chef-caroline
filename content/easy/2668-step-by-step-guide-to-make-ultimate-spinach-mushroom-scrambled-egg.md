##### The ingredients needed to cook Spinach mushroom scrambled egg:

1. Prepare 2 eggs
1. Provide 4 tbsp milk
1. You need 1 salt
1. You need 1 tsp ground black pepper
1. Take 1 pinch ground nutmeg
1. You need 1 pinch ground ginger
1. Prepare 1 pinch garlic powder
1. Prepare 1/2 cup spinach
1. Use 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
1. Use 2 tbsp unsalted butter


##### Steps to make Spinach mushroom scrambled egg:

1. In a skillet add 1 teaspoon of butter. When it melts add the mushroom, sprinkle a pinch of salt and ground pepper and close the lid.
1. Add spinach to the skillet n close the lid after giving a stir. Keep it for another 3 to 4 mintues
1. Meanwhile in a bowl add eggs, milk, pinch of salt, ground pepper, nutmeg, ginger powder and garlic powder and beat it.
1. Heat a pan with medium heat and add the remaining butter and let it melt.
1. Now add the spinach n mushroom in the egg mixture and give it a beat once. Pour it in the pan. Now when the egg starts coming with a spatula stay scratching it from the outer end of towards the center. Repeat this for the entire pan. Repeat this until the egg mixture has entirely cooked.
1. Serve it with toasted bread.
