##### The ingredients needed to cook Lemon Cheesecake tartlets:

1. Use 8 Tablespoons Butter Cold    ,  cold
1. Prepare 3 Tablespoons Sugar
1. Get 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
1. Prepare 1   1/4 Cups All purpose flour
1. Take 6 Tablespoons Philadelphia Cream Cheese
1. You need 3 Tablespoons Sugar
1. You need 2 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
1. Provide 1   1/2 Cups Lemon Zest
1. Prepare 1 Egg
1. Prepare  Cream Whipped
1. Get  Strawberry Preserve


##### Instructions to make Lemon Cheesecake tartlets:

1. Mix to together butter (cut it out into cubes first) with flour until it forms a bread crumb like consistency.
1. Add cold water, one tsp at a time until it forms a workable dough. Now wrap it in cling film and refrigerate it for about 30 minutes.
1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees.
1. Roll out the dough onto a greased pie dish. Prick the base with a fork and bake for 10 minutes.
1. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese with an electric mixer until creamy. Add sugar; beat until smooth.
1. Add egg and lemon juice; beat until combined. Stir in lemon zest.
1. Divide batter among crusts. Bake until centers are just set, 20 to 22 minutes.
1. Place tarts on a wire rack. Cool completely. Refrigerate until thoroughly chilled, about 2 hours.
