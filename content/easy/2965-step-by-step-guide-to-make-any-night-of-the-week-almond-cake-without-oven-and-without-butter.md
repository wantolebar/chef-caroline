##### The ingredients needed to cook Almond cake without oven and without butter:

1. Use 1 cup or 10 tbspn all-purpose flour
1. You need 3 eggs room temperature
1. Provide 6 tbsp oil
1. Use Half cup or 6 tbsp of sugar
1. You need Half tbspn baking powder not soda
1. Take 3 tbspn room temperature milk
1. Provide Half cup chopped almond
1. You need 1/4 cup almond cutted into half
1. You need 1 tbspn vanilla essence or lemon essence
1. Get 2 tbsp apple jam for glaze
1. Use Half cup of water for glaze making
1. You need 2 tbsp ghee


##### Steps to make Almond cake without oven and without butter:

1. First of all mix all dry ingredients in a bowl sieve flour and baking powder together keep it aside,Sieve process is very important it gives airation and your cake will rise nicely
1. Now in a separate bowl add 3 eggs it should be room temperature not cold add sugar, vanilla essence and beat it for 2 minutes or until light in colour
1. Then add 6 tbsp of oil and 3 tbspn of room temperature milk then beat it again
1. Now add one by one the flour mixture into egg mixture and beat it again gradually make sure there should be no lumps in the batter, in the end add chopped almonds on top of the batter and mix it
1. Now grease the cake mould properly with 2 tbsp of ghee and decorate the surface with half cutted almonds make flower
1. Now carefully add the cake batter into the mould and put in the preheat pot for 1 hour in slow flame or you can bake it in the oven at 180° Celsius for 40 to 45 minutes
1. When the cake is done check with the skewer if it&#39;s clean then it&#39;s done now take it out from the pot and cool it down completely and cover with clean cloth don&#39;t take it out immediately from the mould otherwise your cake will break in the meanwhile make glaze
1. For glaze add 2 tbsp of apple jam in a pot add half cup of water and cook it until the jam is dissolved
1. Now pour the glaze on top of the cake and enjoy with tea !
