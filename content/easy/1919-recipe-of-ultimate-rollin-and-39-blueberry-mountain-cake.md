##### The ingredients needed to prepare Rollin&#39; Blueberry mountain cake.:

1. Use  cinnamon rolls
1. Use  blueberry pie filling
1. You need  melted butter
1. Take  chopped pecans (optional)
1. Get  cinnamon
1. Take  brown sugar( light or dark)
1. Use  nutmeg
1. Prepare  vanilla extract


##### Instructions to make Rollin&#39; Blueberry mountain cake.:

1. Preheat oven to 320° Cut cinnamon rolls in bite size pieces . set icing aside till end.
1. In mixing bowl put blueberry filling ,melted butter,nutmeg,cinnamon,brown sugar and vanilla extract. Mix well.
1. add cinnamon rolls to mixture. Coat them with mix.
1. add chopped pecans give it a rough mix.(optional)
1. scoop mixture into greased bundt pan.
1. bake an hour
1. when finished baking turn cake upside down on cake platter or plate. Remove cake from mold. Take icing from both cans of cinnamon rolls and drizzle icing over top of cake.
