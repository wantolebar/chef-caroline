##### The ingredients needed to prepare Deep Fried Stuffed Avocado:

1. You need 2 Avocados, cut in half and hollowed out some for filling.
1. Take 4 Fresh crab cakes
1. Use 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese
1. Use 3 tbsp Parmesan cheese
1. Prepare 1 Fried mushroom batter to coat.
1. You need 1/4 tsp Garlic salt


##### Steps to make Deep Fried Stuffed Avocado:

1. Cut avocados in half and hollow out a little to fill.
1. Mix fresh crab cakes, cheeses, and garlic salt.
1. Fill each half slightly less than full and put back together.
1. Carefully roll avocado in batter and immediately deep fry.
