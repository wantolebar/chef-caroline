##### The ingredients needed to cook Dark chocolate muffin:

1. Take 200 gms wheat (atta)
1. Get 180 ml milk
1. Provide Pinch salt
1. Prepare 4 tablespoon cocoa powder
1. Take 4 tablespoon low fat cream or you can use malai
1. Use 1 cup brown sugar
1. Use 1 tablespoon baking powder and soda. You can add 1more tsp
1. Take 1/2 cup whipping milk
1. Take 1 tablespoon cake gel
1. Provide 3 tablespoon chocolate syrup
1. Get 3 tablespoon warm butter
1. Prepare 2 tablespoon coconut powder


##### Steps to make Dark chocolate muffin:

1. In a bowl whisk butter and cream smoothly.now in another bowl mix ata with milk make a centre in a middle.add cocoa powder,brown sugar,whipping cream,cake gel and coconut powder.it gives yummy flavour.and whisk the batter to a thick consistency. Add the batter of cream and butter together. Bit the batter for 5 min.
1. Now heat the cooker in a medium flame for 10 min.greased the baking panwith oil spread some dry flour over it and and pour the batter in the pan and bake for 35 min.
1. You can use small size baking pan for making muffin.
1. Check meanwhile whether it was finely bake or not with toothpick or fork.and switch off the flame.
1. Your muffin is ready to serve with tea.
