##### The ingredients needed to make Italian chicken broccoli and yellow rice:

1. Prepare 3 cup minute rice
1. Provide 1 lb boneless skinless chicken tenders cut into bite size pieces
1. Get 16 oz zesty Italian salad dressing
1. Take 14 1/2 oz chicken broth
1. You need 1 dash salt
1. Get 3 packages yellow rice seasoning
1. Get 3 cup broccoli florets


##### Steps to make Italian chicken broccoli and yellow rice:

1. in a pot high heat put in chicken broth Italian dressing and seasonings
1. let boil for 10 minutes add broccoli and rice cook till liquid is not visible cover and remove from heat wait 10 minutes and serve if you don&#39;t like twangyness of dressing reduce amount by half add 8 ounces of broth. you can substitute zesty for creamy Italian dressing
