##### The ingredients needed to make Low Carb Egg Noodles💛🍝:

1. Take 2 eggs
1. Use to taste salt
1. You need to taste black pepper powder
1. Use 1 tsp butter/oil


##### Instructions to make Low Carb Egg Noodles💛🍝:

1. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl and add salt and black pepper
1. Heat up a pan add butter then pour this egg mixture and spread roundly
1. Keep the flame low and cook it for 1 minute then flip it over and turn off the flame
1. Cut this egg into strips with the help of pizza cutter or knife.
1. Enjoy with toast and tea.😍
