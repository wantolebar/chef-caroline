1. Prepare 2 Tbsp butter
1. Prepare 1/2 onion, minced
1. Prepare 200 gr corned beef
1. Use 1 medium carrot, small diced
1. You need 100 gr green beans, cut into small pieces
1. Provide 100 gr frozen corn
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
1. Provide 1/4 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
1. You need 1/2 tsp nutmeg powder
1. Take 2 stalks green onions, thinly sliced
