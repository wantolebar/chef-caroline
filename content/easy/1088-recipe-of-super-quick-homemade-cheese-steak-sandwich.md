##### The ingredients needed to prepare Cheese steak sandwich:

1. Prepare 1 lb Top Round Steak
1. You need 1 loaf olive oil ciabatta bread
1. Take 1 packages velveeta cheese
1. Take 1 chopped onion
1. Provide 1 chopped green pepper
1. Take 2 tsp salt
1. Get 2 tsp ground black pepper
1. You need 2 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
1. Get 1/4 tbsp cayenne pepper
1. You need 1/2 cup mayonnaise


##### Instructions to make Cheese steak sandwich:

1. Slice the top round steak, the onions and the peppers
1. Sautee the onions and peppers with olive oil for 5 mins then add the steak, cook steak to taste
1. Add the cayenne pepper to the mayo
1. Spread the mayo on the bread, add the steak and the cheese to your liking
1. Enjoy :)
