##### The ingredients needed to make Bhutta Masala / Indian Style Spiced Roasted Corn On The Cob:

1. Prepare  Sweet corns
1. Get  Indian chaat masala
1. Provide  Red chilli powder
1. Prepare  Paprika powder
1. Prepare  Lime  cut into small wedges
1. Get  Salt


##### Steps to make Bhutta Masala / Indian Style Spiced Roasted Corn On The Cob:

1. In a small bowl, mix together the chaat masala, chilli powder, paprika, &amp; salt. Keep this aside. Remove the corn husk and strings from the corn. Roast the corn on the heated grill, turning it on all sides occasionally so that the corn gets roasted evenly.
1. Once the corns are all ready, take a lemon wedge, dip it in the prepared spice mix, and rub it on the hot corn. Sprinkle a little lemon juice if desired. Repeat the same process for the remaining corns. Enjoy the hot tangy corns with a medley of spices!
