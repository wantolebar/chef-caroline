##### The ingredients needed to prepare Fresh Chilled Sweet Corn Soup:

1. Get  Corn on the cob
1. Get  Milk
1. Use  Heavy cream
1. Prepare  Soft brown sugar
1. Provide  Salt
1. Use  Sweet white wine, or kuei hua chen chiew (liquor flavored with sweet osmanthus flowers)


##### Instructions to make Fresh Chilled Sweet Corn Soup:

1. Using a knife, shave the corn off the cob and into a pot. Add a pinch of salt and some cold milk, and turn the heat on. When it boils, simmer for a minute over low heat. Turn the heat off, cover the pot and steam for 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Use an electric mixer (or a food processor or a juicer) and process the corn until it&#39;s very fine.
1. When you get corn juice, use a strainer and strain. Scoop the remaining corn on the strainer with a gauze or clean cloth, and squeeze well to get all the remaining umami-rich flavor of the corn.
1. Put the strained soup into a pot and cook over low heat. Season with sugar until it reaches the preferred sweetness. Strain the soup again, and chill the pot in a bowl of iced water. When it&#39;s chilled enough, add heavy cream and wine. Mix well, and chill in the refrigerator before serving.
1. My family even eats the strained ingredients by mixing it into cookies or bread. Here&#39;s the corn bread made with strained lees - it&#39;s delightful!
