##### The ingredients needed to prepare Kata chorchori(fish bones and mix vegetable stir fry):

1. Get 250 gm fish bones
1. Use 1 bowl chopped vegetables
1. You need 1 onion chopped
1. Provide 1 tsp ginger paste
1. Prepare 1 tsp garlic paste
1. Provide 1 tsp nigella seeds
1. Provide 1 tsp red chilli Powder
1. Prepare 1 tsp turmeric powder
1. Provide To taste salt and sugar
1. Get As needed oil


##### Steps to make Kata chorchori(fish bones and mix vegetable stir fry):

1. Heat oil and fry the fish bones with salt and turmeric powder and keep aside
1. Now temper nigella seeds and green chillies,fry onions
1. Add vegetables and saute well adding ginger garlic paste
1. Add red chilli Powder and bones
1. Saute well till oil separates
1. Serve it with steaming rice
