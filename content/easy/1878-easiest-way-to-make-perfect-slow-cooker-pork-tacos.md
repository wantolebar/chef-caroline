##### The ingredients needed to prepare Slow Cooker Pork Tacos:

1. Use 5-6 lbs Pork Shoulder (bone in preferred)
1. Use 4 cups Beef Broth
1. Use 2 cups water
1. Use 2 Large Onions (quartered or julienne)
1. Provide 4 Poblano peppers (julienne)
1. Provide 2 Jalapenos (diced)
1. Take 2 tbsp Parsley
1. Take 2 tbsp Basil
1. Provide 4 tbsp Knorr Chicken Bouillon
1. Get  Queso fresco
1. Take  Mexican sour cream
1. Use  Corn Tortillas (flour if preferred)


##### Steps to make Slow Cooker Pork Tacos:

1. Put pork shoulder in roaster. Add all other ingredients. Set roaster to 225 degrees.
1. After cooking for 12.5 hours shred for tacos.
1. When plating put meat first followed by Onions and Poblanos. Gently squeeze taco at this point to remove juice (trust me even after that you will still bathe in it). Then add Queso Fresco. Drizzle Mexican Sour Cream. Top with Parsley
