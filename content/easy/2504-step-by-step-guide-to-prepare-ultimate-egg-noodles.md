##### The ingredients needed to make Egg noodles:

1. Provide 2 packets indomie noodles
1. Take 1 fresh pepper
1. Get 3 eggs
1. Provide 1 tablespoon Vegetable oil


##### Instructions to make Egg noodles:

1. Break the eggs into a bowl then add small salt
1. Boil the noodles and add the sliced fresh pepper and oil and seasoning
1. Let it almost dryup then add the eggs
1. Stir and mix it and wait until it is completely dried
