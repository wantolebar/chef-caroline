##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Squash Cake:

1. Use 3 slices squash (grated already)
1. Get 1/2 cup white sugar
1. Get 1 cup brown sugar
1. Provide 1 whole butter
1. You need 5 pcs eggs
1. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
1. Take 2 cups flour
1. You need 2 tsp baking soda
1. Provide 1/2 powdered milk (i used birch tree)


##### Steps to make Easy Squash Cake:

1. 1. Combine all the ingredients together. Mix well. Set aside. - 2. Put a little oil into the bread or cake pan. - 3. Put all the mix ingredients in the Pan. - 4. Heat for about 45 mins or 1hr in a 180 C. (but check it after 30mins. You already knew its cook if color looks brown)  - 5. Let it cool And then Served.
1. Take note: the Ingredients and Procedure are the same when I bake Banana cake. The white sugar supposed to be 1 cup but I make it 1/2 so that its not too sweet.
