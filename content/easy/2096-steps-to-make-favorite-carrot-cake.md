##### The ingredients needed to cook Carrot Cake:

1. Provide 2 cup sugar
1. Take 1 1/2 cup oil
1. Prepare 4 eggs
1. Provide 2 1/4 cup flour
1. Use 2 tsp salt
1. Prepare 2 tsp baking soda
1. Take 2 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1. You need 3 cup finely grated carrots


##### Steps to make Carrot Cake:

1. Preheat oven to 300°F
1. Grease and flour two 9 inch round cake pans
1. Combine sugar, oil and eggs in stand mixer. Mix at medium speed for 2 minutes
1. Add dry ingredients, mix at low speed for 1 minute
1. Add carrots, mix until incorporated, about 1 minute
1. Divide batter evenly between the 2 cake pans. Use spatula to move batter from center of pans to the edges
1. Bake for 1 hour or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean
1. Cool on rack for 1 hour, then frost with Duncan Hines cream cheese frosting
