##### The ingredients needed to cook Crab Bake:

1. Use  crab bake
1. You need 16 oz crab
1. Provide 1/2 cup sour cream
1. You need 1 tsp garlic powder
1. Prepare 8 oz cream cheese
1. Get 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
1. Take 1 tbsp milk
1. Use 1 tbsp sugar
1. Provide 1 1/2 cup cheddar cheese


##### Instructions to make Crab Bake:

1. Preheat oven to 375°
1. Mix all ingredients (except 1 cup cheese) together
1. Spread in pan
1. Top with remaining cheese
1. Bake for 20-30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly
