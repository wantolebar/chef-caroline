##### The ingredients needed to make Bacon Chicken Corn Chowder:

1. Provide 2 tbsp butter
1. Use 1 medium yellow onion chopped very well
1. Prepare 1 can of diced tomatoes with green chiles(14.5 oz)
1. Take 1 cup corn(canned or frozen)
1. Use 4 oz cream cheese
1. Provide 2 1/2 cup chicken stock
1. Use 1 1/2 cup half and half
1. You need 1 tsp ground cumin
1. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1. Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste
1. Prepare 3 slices of bacon cooked to crispiness and diced into small pieces
1. Take 2 1/2 cup chicken that had been cooked and diced
1. Provide 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1. Use 1 tortilla chips to break into soup


##### Steps to make Bacon Chicken Corn Chowder:

1. Melt butter and sautee onion for 5-7 minutes in a large soup/stock pot. Add next 8 ingredients and simmer over low-medium heat until cream cheese is thoroughly melted.
1. Add chicken, bacon and cheese to pot and continue to simmer another 5 minutes. Serve with crushed tortilla chips.
