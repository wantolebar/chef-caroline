##### The ingredients needed to prepare Best Apple Cake:

1. You need 110 grams butter
1. Prepare 200 grams caster sugar
1. Get 1 egg
1. You need 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1. Get 200 grams self-raising flour
1. Take 1/2 tsp salt
1. Provide 175 ml milk
1. Use 2 apples - peeled, cored &amp; cut into eighths
1. You need 4 tbsp caster sugar
1. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon


##### Instructions to make Best Apple Cake:

1. Preheat oven to 180°C &amp; grease 23cm cake tin
1. Cream butter &amp; 200g sugar. Beat in egg and vanilla
1. Add flour &amp; salt alternately with milk
1. Spread into tin. Add apples then sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over
1. Bake for 50-60 mind or until apples are tender and cake is cooked
