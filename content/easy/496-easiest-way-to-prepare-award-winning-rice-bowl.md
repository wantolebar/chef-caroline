##### Instructions to make Rice Bowl:

1. Rinse rice 2 times
1. Add rice to rice cooker
1. Add water to the 2 1/2 line in cooker bowl
1. Add steamer Baskett cooker
1. Add vegetables
1. Put lid down, plug in, turn switch to cook
