##### The ingredients needed to cook Easy Muffins with Pancake Mix:

1. Get 50 grams Unsalted butter
1. Prepare 30 grams Sugar
1. You need 1 Egg
1. Prepare 150 grams Pancake mix
1. Use 80 ml Milk
1. You need 3 tbsp Maple syrup


##### Instructions to make Easy Muffins with Pancake Mix:

1. Melt the butter in the microwave. Mix it evenly with the sugar and egg.
1. Add the pancake mix, milk, and maple syrup. Stir until smooth.
1. Pour the mixture into baking cups and bake for about 20 minutes in an oven preheated to 180℃. They&#39;re done.
