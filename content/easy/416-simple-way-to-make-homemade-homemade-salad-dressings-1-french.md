##### The ingredients needed to cook Homemade Salad Dressings - #1 French:

1. Prepare 3/4 cup oil
1. Prepare 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1. Take 1/4 cup ketchup (organic, if possible)
1. Prepare 2 tbsp brown or turbinado sugar
1. Use 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1. Use 1 garlic clove, pressed


##### Steps to make Homemade Salad Dressings - #1 French:

1. Combine all ingredients in an airtight jar, screw the lid on tight and dance the conga with it! It should emulsify nicely and give you a dark-orange thick fluid which will make any lettuce go &#34;oh la lá&#34;!
1. Refrigerate until used, shake it vigorously if necessary before serving.
