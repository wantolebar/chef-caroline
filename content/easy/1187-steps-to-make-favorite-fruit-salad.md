##### Steps to make Fruit Salad:

1. Place the oatmeal, coconut oil, shredded coconut, honey, cinnamon, and salt on a baking sheet
1. Mix well and bake 10-15 minutes at 300°F.
1. Mix together the apple, banana, and Greek yogurt
1. Toss dried cherries and chocolate chips into the granola when it comes out. Top the fruit and yogurt with the granola.
