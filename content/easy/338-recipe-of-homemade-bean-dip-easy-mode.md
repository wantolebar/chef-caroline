##### The ingredients needed to prepare Bean Dip (Easy Mode):

1. Take 1 can Old El Paso Traditional Refried beans
1. Take 1 Onion
1. You need 1/4 cup Sour Cream
1. Use 1 cup Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese


##### Instructions to make Bean Dip (Easy Mode):

1. Chop up your onion into small dices.
1. Pour the beans into the pan and turn the stove on to medium heat. You will want to stir occasionally.
1. Add a 1/4 cup of sour cream and mix it in!
1. Add 1 cup of cheese and begin to stir it until it&#39;s all melted in. Reduce heat to low.
1. (Optional) Add 1/2 cup more cheese for even more flavor.
1. Add your onion and mix!
1. Pour into a party bowl and dip!
