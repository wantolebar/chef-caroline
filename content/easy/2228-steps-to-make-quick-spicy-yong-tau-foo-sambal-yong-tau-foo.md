##### The ingredients needed to cook Spicy Yong Tau Foo / Sambal Yong Tau Foo:

1. Provide  STORE BOUGHT READY TO EAT YONG TAU FOO
1. Get 10 fried bean curd  stuff with fish paste
1. Take 15 fried fish ball
1. Use 4 tbsp spicy sauce ( sambal sauce)or any sambal oelek
1. Prepare 2 slice light soy sauce
1. You need 1 sugar
1. Take 1 lime juice
1. Provide 20 sugar snap pea


##### Instructions to make Spicy Yong Tau Foo / Sambal Yong Tau Foo:

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil then add in some salt and sugar snap pea and simmer for a minutes then drain and set aside
1. In pan with oil saute shallot or onion then add in the ready to eat store bought fried beancurd and fish ball together with the sugar snap pea
1. Add lime juice and Stir fry to mix well then off heat and serve
