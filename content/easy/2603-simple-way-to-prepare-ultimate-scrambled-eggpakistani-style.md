##### The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Egg(pakistani style):

1. Prepare 8 eggs
1. Take Half cup Cooking oil
1. You need  Onions 2 chopped
1. You need  Tomato 1 chopped
1. Use 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste
1. Get 1 tsp Salt
1. Provide 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
1. Get 1 tsp Cumin powder
1. Get 1 tsp Coriander powder
1. You need  For garnishing
1. Prepare  Green chilli
1. Prepare  Green coriander


##### Steps to make Scrambled Egg(pakistani style):

1. Fry onions in oil, add tomatoes and ginger garlic paste.
1. Add all spices, fry it for 2 mins.
1. Beat eggs then pour into masala.
1. With the help of spoon smashed it. On medium heat. Let the oil release.
1. Off the flame, garnish it then ready to serve.
