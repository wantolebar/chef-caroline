##### The ingredients needed to prepare Kickin Hot wings:

1. Use  chicken wings
1. Prepare  franks buffalo hot sauce
1. Take  lemon juice
1. Provide  chicken marinade


##### Steps to make Kickin Hot wings:

1. Marinade everything together, (use only half of the bottle of franks hot sauce and also use the tray you are going to cook in to marinade the chicken). Marinade it 2 hours before, or overnight to get a much better flavour. I chose not to add salt because both the hot suace and chicken marinade have salt in them.
1. Preheat the oven to 180c.
1. Transfer the dish to the preheated oven. Cook for 45-60 minutes. Turn the chicken half way through the cooking time.
1. Remove the wings from the tray onto another dish. Coat the wings with the remaining of franks hot sauce.
1. And enjoy
