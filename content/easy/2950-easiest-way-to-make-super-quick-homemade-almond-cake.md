##### The ingredients needed to make Almond Cake:

1. Get  Almonds (Cut in halves for garnishing, as u required)
1. Provide  Oil
1. Prepare  Almond Powder
1. Provide  Milk
1. You need  eggs
1. You need  +4 tbsp Plain Flour
1. Provide  + 4 tbsp Powdered Sugar
1. Get  Vanilla Essence
1. Use  Baking Powder
1. Take  For Shining
1. Provide  Jam (Any Flavour)
1. Provide  Water


##### Steps to make Almond Cake:

1. Sab sy pehly baking pan mein butter paper lagakar usay butter sy grease kar lain achi tarah aur apni mrzi sy almonds kat kar decorate kar lain aur thori dair ke liye fridge mein rakh dain.
1. Ek bowl mein plain flour,baking powder aur almond powder ko chan kar achi tarah mix kar lain.
1. Ab ek or bowl mein oil daalen aur thori thori sugar dal kar hand whisk sy achi tarah mix kar lain.
1. Jab sugar achi tarah mix ho jaye to is mein ek egg dalen achi tarah mix karein,isi tarah baqi dono b add kar dain.
1. Ab is mein milk aur vanilla essence add kar dain aur mix karein.
1. Ab thora thora kar ke plain flour wala mixture dalty jayen aur mix krty jayen. Jab achi tarah mix ho jaye to baking pan mein daal dain.
1. Daichky ko 10 minute pre heat kar lain pehly sy. Ab is mein stand ke ooper koi carboard rakh kar us ke ooper baking pan rkhen kyun ke cake upside down hoga islye wo jalay ga nahi.
1. 20 minutes medium to low aur phr 10-15 mints low heat py bake karein.
1. Shining ke liye ek pan mein water aur jam dalen. Jab bubbles anay lgen to flame off kar dain. Thanda honay sy pehly he isay cake py brush ki madad sy laga dain.
1. Yummy Almond Cake ready hai.
