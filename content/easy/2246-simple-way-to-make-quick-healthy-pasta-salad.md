##### The ingredients needed to cook Healthy Pasta Salad:

1. You need 1 cup- Boil whole wheat pasta/ semolina pasta
1. Get 1 1/2 cup- Julienne cut of carrot, cabbage and capsicum
1. Prepare 4-5 tbsp- Yogurt
1. Get 1/2 tbsp- Oregano
1. Prepare 1/4 tbsp- red chili flakes
1. Get to taste Black pepper according
1. Use to taste Salt


##### Steps to make Healthy Pasta Salad:

1. First of all properly wash to all the vegetable.
1. Now put to a yogurt in large bowl, then add salt, black pepper, oregano, red chili flake and vegetables and whisk all together.
1. Now add boil pasta in dressing and mix it and toss together.
1. Our healthy pasta salad is ready for the serve.
1. Serve salad in chilled or normal temperature as your wish and enjoy it.
