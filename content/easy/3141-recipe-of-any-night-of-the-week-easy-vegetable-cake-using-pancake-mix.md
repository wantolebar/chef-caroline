##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Vegetable Cake using Pancake Mix:

1. You need 150 ml Vegetable juice
1. You need 200 grams Pancake mix
1. Provide 2 tbsp Vegetable oil
1. Take 2 tbsp Sugar


##### Steps to make Easy Vegetable Cake using Pancake Mix:

1. Preheat the oven to 360°F/180°C. Mix the ingredients until well incorporated.
1. Pour the batter into a mold lined with parchment paper and bake for 40-45 minutes.
1. I used this 100% mixed vegetable juice made by Ito-en, but you can use tomato juice or any kind of vegetable juice.
1. It has a strong and bright orange colour.
