##### The ingredients needed to prepare Beef and broccoli with hazelnuts:

1. Use  small broccoli
1. Provide  medium ny or flank steak
1. Get  hazelnuts
1. Provide  garlic cloves
1. Provide  avocado oil (3-4 tbsp for cooking, rest for sauce
1. Take  chili flakes (optional but recommended!)
1. Take  salt
1. Get  ground black pepper
1. Get  minced ginger (optional)


##### Instructions to make Beef and broccoli with hazelnuts:

1. Heat your pan on low with the separate oil kept for cooking (really just personal preference here)
1. Slice steak into thin strips
1. Add steak, up temperature to medium and sautee for about 3 mins and then add your hazelnuts. Cook for 2 mins.
1. Add garlic and ginger (if using) and continue to stir often.
1. Now throw in the chopped broccoli, sautee till it reaches your preferred consistency (I like crisp veggies, about 5 -6 mins)
1. When you are close to your end result, add the remaining oil, chili flakes, salt and pepper
1. Serve hot with or without rice!
