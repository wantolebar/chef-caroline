##### The ingredients needed to cook Green tomatoes atop sausage:

1. Get 1 large green tomato
1. You need 1/4 lb country ( bulk ) sausage I used Odoms Tennessee Pride
1. Get 1/4 tsp basil
1. Prepare 1/4 cup sliced colby jack cheese
1. Take 4 tsp grated parmesan cheese
1. You need  salt
1. You need  ground black pepper, fresh
1. You need 1/4 cup vegetable oil


##### Instructions to make Green tomatoes atop sausage:

1. Heat oil in a pan
1. Add sausage form into patties large ones. Have small pieces to crisp on side.
1. When sausage is turned add sliced cheese after you turned and cooking for 1 and a half minutes.
1. After cheese melts let cook 3 minutes more remove to a paper towel
1. Slice the tomato thick and add to oil.  Fry about 3 minutes.  Turn and add parmesan cheese salt and pepper fry 2 minutes.  Remoe to sausage patties 2 on each patty. Add sausage crisp, and basil.
1. If you have any tomato left over dice and add to your plate add salt and pepper to taste.
1. Serve hope you enjoy
