##### The ingredients needed to prepare Cheese Arancini:

1. Use  low-sodium chicken broth
1. Take  Kosher salt
1. Provide  arborio rice
1. You need  pine nuts
1. Take  shredded mozzarella cheese
1. You need  chopped fresh parsley
1. Take  large eggs
1. You need  grated parmesan cheese
1. Get  breadcrumbs
1. Prepare  Vegetable oil, for frying


##### Instructions to make Cheese Arancini:

1. Bring the broth and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan over medium heat,Stir in the rice, reduce the heat to low and simmer until tender, about 15minutes. Spread on a parchment-lined baking sheet and let cool completely. - Combine the pine nuts, mozzarella,and parsley in a bowl set aside.Beat the eggs in a large bowl, then stir in the cooled rice, the parmesan and breadcrumbs,Shape the mixture into sixteen 1 inch balls, fry till golden brown, cut from middle and garnish according to your choice.
