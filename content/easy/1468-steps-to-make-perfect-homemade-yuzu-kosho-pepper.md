##### The ingredients needed to prepare Homemade Yuzu Kosho Pepper:

1. Take  to 10 Green yuzu citrus
1. Use  Hot green chili peppers (fresh)
1. Use  Salt


##### Instructions to make Homemade Yuzu Kosho Pepper:

1. Rinse the yuzu and pat dry. Remove any damaged parts with a knife.
1. Grate the green rind. Try to leave the white pith on the fruit, because it&#39;s bitter.
1. Like this!
1. Rinse the green chili peppers throughly and pat dry. Remove stem and blend in a food processor or blender. Add in the seeds for extra spice.
1. Thoroughly mix all the ingredients together.
1. Chill in the fridge for a week to 10 days to improve the taste, and it&#39;s done! The seeds will absorb the green color. If you freeze them, they&#39;ll last for a year.
1. Bottle them up to give away. Sterilize the container in boiling water.
1. Pack them into small bottles. They really make nice gifts!
