##### The ingredients needed to cook Spaghetti and Hash:

1. Provide 1 1/2 lb Spaghetti (cooked/cooled)
1. Use 2 can Corned Beef Hash
1. Prepare 1/4 lb American Cheese
1. Use 2/3 cup Milk
1. Provide 1/2 cup Italian Style Bread Crumbs
1. You need 1/2 stick Margerine or Butter


##### Steps to make Spaghetti and Hash:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F
1. Spray 9x13 inch pan with cooking spray
1. Layer 1/3 of pasta in bottom of pan
1. Evenly spread 1 can of hash over pasta
1. Place even layer of cheese over hash
1. Repeat steps using remaining ingredients, should end with last part of pasta on top
1. Once complete, pour milk around top
1. Sprinkle bread crumbs to cover pasta
1. Place slices of margerine around top
1. Cover and bake for 45 minutes, uncover and continue to bake for another 15 minutes or until top is golden brown
1. When done, let rest 10 minutes to set
