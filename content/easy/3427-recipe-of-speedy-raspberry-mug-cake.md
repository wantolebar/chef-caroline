##### The ingredients needed to cook Raspberry mug cake:

1. Use 1/2 cup bisquick
1. Use 8 tbsp milk, creamer
1. Use 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1. You need 1 handful raspberrires, to coat bottom of mug
1. You need 2 tbsp brown sugar, unpacked
1. Get 1/2 tbsp cinnamon


##### Steps to make Raspberry mug cake:

1. Load berries into bottom of mug and sprinkle with brown sugar.
1. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over berries.
1. Microwave for 2 minutes. Cool before serving. Serving suggestions include cool whip, caramel sauce, or ice cream.
