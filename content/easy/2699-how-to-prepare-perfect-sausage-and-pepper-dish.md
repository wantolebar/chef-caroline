##### The ingredients needed to make Sausage and pepper dish:

1. Prepare 2 lb sausage links
1. Use 1 each Green bell pepper
1. Prepare 1 each red bell pepper
1. You need 1 each Orange bell pepper
1. Use 1 each yellow bell pepper
1. Provide 1/2 each sliced yellow onion
1. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil


##### Instructions to make Sausage and pepper dish:

1. Slice sausage and bell peppers
1. Heat skillet on medium heat with olive oil
1. Add sausage and cook for about 3 minutes.
1. Add bell peppers and onion and cook until translucent
1. Remove from heat and serve
