##### The ingredients needed to cook Slow Cooker BBQ Pulled Pork:

1. Use 2 lb boneless pork roast or chops
1. Provide  olive oil
1. Prepare  season salt
1. Take  ground black pepper
1. Provide  garlic powder
1. Prepare  Rosemary leaves
1. Get 1 water
1. You need  BBQ sauce


##### Instructions to make Slow Cooker BBQ Pulled Pork:

1. Baste pork with oil.
1. Season with season salt, pepper, garlic, and rosemary.
1. Place in slow cooker.
1. Add about 1/2-3/4 inch of water.
1. Cover and cook on low until meat easily shreds with forks. About 8 hours.
1. Once meat is shredded, add BBQ sauce and mix well. Heat on high until heated through.
