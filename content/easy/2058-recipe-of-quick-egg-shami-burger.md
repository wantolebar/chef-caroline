##### The ingredients needed to prepare Egg Shami Burger:

1. Get  Bun or 2 Bread pieces for Sandwich
1. Prepare  Shami Kebabs (Recipe given in notes)
1. Provide  Mayonnaise
1. Take  Egg
1. Get  Onion Cut in Ring shape
1. Provide  Tomato Finely Sliced and dipped in vinegar+lemon juice
1. You need  Salad Leaves
1. Provide  Cucumber Thinly sliced and dipped in vinegar


##### Steps to make Egg Shami Burger:

1. Cut the bun into half and cook the inner sides with oil till slightly brown
1. On one half add salad leaves, onions, tomatoes and cucumbe
1. Apply Mayonnaise over it
1. Fry an omelet with chaat masala and place it over the stuffing
1. Spread shami kebabs on the other half of bun
1. Place it over the other half
1. Cook well the both sides on pan or tawa with oil. Press the burger with spatula
