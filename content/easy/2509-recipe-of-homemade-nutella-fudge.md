##### The ingredients needed to prepare Nutella Fudge:

1. You need 1-2 scoops Vanilla ice cream
1. Take 1 cup Oreo biscuits
1. Take 1/2 cup Cocoa powder
1. Use 1 cup Maida
1. Provide 1 cup Sugar
1. Provide 1 cup Butter
1. Get as required Almonds and Choco chip
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Amul fresh Cream
1. Use as required Chocolate balls
1. Provide as required Chocolate syrup


##### Steps to make Nutella Fudge:

1. Take a bowl and add maida to it. Then add sugar, baking powder and baking soda to it.
1. Whisk it properly for about 15minutes. Now add cocoa powder and half cup of water to the mixture and mix it until the batter smoothens.U can add Amul cream to it to give it a sweet flavour.
1. Add almonds and choco-chip to it.
1. Now take a baking tray and dust flour on it.
1. Put the batter into the tin and bake it for about 25-30 minutes.
1. The chocolate cake is ready.
1. Now for making nutella fudge firstly take a piece of chocolate cake and place a scoop of vanilla ice cream on it.
1. Now place chocolate balls on the sides of the cake to make it tempting
1. Atlast put choco syrup(nutella) on the ice cream for making it presentable.
1. Fudge is ready.
