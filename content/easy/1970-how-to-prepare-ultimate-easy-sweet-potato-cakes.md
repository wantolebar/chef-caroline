##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Sweet Potato Cakes:

1. Get 1 Japanese sweet potato (medium)
1. Provide 1 tbsp ●Sugar
1. You need 2 pieces ●Butter
1. Get 20 ml ●Milk
1. Use 1 Egg yolk


##### Instructions to make Easy Sweet Potato Cakes:

1. Steam the sweet potato. (Of course you can boil or microwave it too!)  ②Mash the sweet potato. ③Add the ● marked ingredients while hot and knead. ④Put the mixture in aluminum cups,  adjust the shape, and brush the surface with the egg yolk.
1. Bake in the oven (or toaster oven) for about 5 minutes. When the surfaces are golden brown, they are done.
