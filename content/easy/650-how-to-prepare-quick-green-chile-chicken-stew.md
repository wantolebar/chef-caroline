##### The ingredients needed to make Green Chile Chicken Stew:

1. Prepare  chicken breasts
1. Provide  cilantro, finely chopped
1. Use  onions, chopped, separated
1. You need  garlic, peeled and chopped
1. Use  apple cider vinegar
1. Prepare  cumin powder
1. You need  honey
1. Provide  kosher salt
1. You need  Black pepper
1. Take  tomatillos or tomato, diced
1. Get  diced green chiles
1. Use  diced jalapeños


##### Steps to make Green Chile Chicken Stew:

1. preheat oven to 350
1. combine first 9 ingredients (only one onion) in a medium pot. cover ingredients with water and stir. bring to simmer over medium heat. cook for 25 minutes.
1. remove chicken and shred into bite sized pieces. set aside broth.
1. meanwhile combine tomatillos or tomato, chiles, and peppers into a single layer on a rimmed baking sheet. Stirring occasionally, roast in oven for 20 minutes.
1. puree roasted chile mixture in a food processor gently until it is chunky. set aside.
1. add remaining 1 onion to medium pot with a tablespoon of broth and caramelize the onion.  About 15 minutes. add broth and bring to simmer. add chicken and take off heat. serve over long grain white or brown rice.
