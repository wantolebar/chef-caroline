##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate mug cake:

1. Take 1/4 cup wheat flour
1. Prepare 1 tbsp cocoa powder
1. Get 1/4 cup powdered sugar
1. Use 1/2 tspn baking powder
1. Use 1/4 tspn baking soda
1. You need 2 tbsp oil
1. Take 1 tbsp curd
1. Prepare 3-4 drops vanilla essence
1. Provide 1/4 cup milk


##### Steps to make Chocolate mug cake:

1. Seive and mix dry ingredients.
1. Mix all wet ingredients in a bowl.
1. Mix both dry and wet ingredients into smooth batter.
1. Pour in a mug (microwave safe) and microwave for 3 mins.
1. Ready to serve with any topping.
