##### The ingredients needed to prepare Egg rolls:

1. You need 5 large eggs
1. Take  Maggi seasoning
1. Use  Black pepper
1. Provide  Ground coriander
1. You need  Red bell pepper
1. Prepare  Chiilies
1. Prepare  Parsley
1. Provide  Onions
1. Prepare  Garlic


##### Instructions to make Egg rolls:

1. Break egg in a bowl. Add in spices and mix together
1. Dice bell peppers and add
1. Cut onions,Ground coriander,maggi, and garlic and add. Mix everything together
1. Cut chilles and parsley and add to the mixture
1. Give it one last final mix and set aside
1. In a non stick pan, drizzle a little oil pour some of the egg mixture and let it cook on low heat
1. Then start folding from an end to the middle
1. Shift it to the end of the pan and add more mixture and let it cook
1. It will stick to the other. Fold again to the middle and pour more mixture
1. Continue this process until all your egg mixture has finished
1. Fold it to the end and take it off the heat
1. Slice the egg and serve
