1. Provide  sweet potatoes

##### Steps to make Beef Shoulder steaks with veggies:

1. Cut meet into 2 centimetre steaks
1. Pound steak in a plastic bag.

1. Cut broccoli into individual florets
1. Steam sweet potato and carrots for about 5 minutes.
1. After about 5 minutes, when you feel softness with the carrots, add broccoli. Cook for 5 more minutes.
1. Place large frypan on medium high heat.
1. Add minced garlic.
1. Add steaks and cook until brown on the outside, about 5 minutes.
1. Turn heat to low, cover and cook for 3 more minutes.
