##### The ingredients needed to prepare Simple Microwave Steamed Bread with Yogurt &amp; Pancake Mix:

1. You need 100 grams Pancake mix
1. Get 80 grams Yogurt
1. Use 1 Egg
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Vegetable oil


##### Instructions to make Simple Microwave Steamed Bread with Yogurt &amp; Pancake Mix:

1. Add the yogurt, egg, and vegetable oil to a bowl and mix well.
1. Add the pancake mix, and mix until the batter becomes smooth.
1. Put the batter into a microwaveable dish and microwave without plastic wrap at 500 W for 5 minutes, and it&#39;s complete.
1. This is also delicious if eaten after being freeze-stored. I wrapped it up without cutting it, then defrosted it in the microwave at 500 W for 30 seconds.
1. The above picture was made by just putting the bowl in which I mixed everything as-is in the microwave, as per everyone&#39;s suggestions. I used my homemade pancake mix with brown sugar, and strawberry yogurt.
