##### The ingredients needed to make Mini - Garden:

1. Get  wafer biscuits to make borders
1. Prepare 12 pcs Chocolate cupcakes
1. Provide  Marzipan made carrots and fruits
1. Prepare  Mint leaves few
1. You need  Cupcakes:
1. Take 1 cup Sugar
1. Get 1 cup flour
1. Provide 1/2 cup cocoa powder
1. Get 3/4 tsp baking powder
1. You need 3/4 tsp baking soda
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Provide  Egg 1 large
1. Get 1/2 cup milk
1. Take 1/3 cup oil
1. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
1. Provide 1/2 cup boiling water


##### Steps to make Mini - Garden:

1. Place sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder and soda, salt in a bowl.
1. In this add in egg, oil, milk, vanilla essence and mix it well.
1. Finally add in boiling water and mix everything well till smooth batter is formed.
1. Pour into muffin tray lined with liners and bake it at 170 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes till done.
1. Cool and utilize it.
1. For garden arrange the wafers as shown in pic, In the middle crumble the cupcakes.
1. Top with marzipan made carrots and fruits alongwith mint leaves.
