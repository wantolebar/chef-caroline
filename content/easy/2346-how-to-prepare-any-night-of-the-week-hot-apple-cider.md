##### The ingredients needed to cook Hot Apple Cider:

1. Get 2 quart apple cider
1. Provide 1 orange
1. Take 1 apple
1. Prepare 2 stick cinnamon
1. Take 1/4 tsp ginger
1. Prepare 1 tsp whole cloves


##### Steps to make Hot Apple Cider:

1. Cut the orange into slices (unpeeled)
1. Slice the apple, and discard the core
1. Add all ingredients together into a crock pot
1. Set crockpot on high for 2 to 5 hours
1. After several hours have passed, set the crock pot to low and serve with a ladle.
