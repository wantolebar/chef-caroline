##### The ingredients needed to prepare Oats and egg mix casserole:

1. Take  oats
1. You need  eggs beaten

1. Take  baby spinach finely chopped
1. Get  Black salt
1. You need  Black pepper
1. Get  Handful of coriander chopped
1. Provide  green chilli chopped
1. Use  medium size chopped onion
1. Provide  butter
1. Prepare  milk


##### Instructions to make Oats and egg mix casserole:

1. First grind the oats in powdered paste
1. Next in a mixing bowl,mix oats,chopped onion,grated carrot,chilli,coriander leaves,chopped spinach,black salt,and black pepper,and milk.mix properly with a spoon.
1. Next add eggs beaten in a oats mixing bowl, and mix finely in a smooth batter.add 1tsp butter. And mix again
1. Now pour the batter in a greased pan.and cut the tomato slices over the top of the batter,and preheat the oven for 10 min.and bake for 10 min in 180 degree c
1. Healthy and fast breakfast oats is ready to serve season it with boiled egg.
