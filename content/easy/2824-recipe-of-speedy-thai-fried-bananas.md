##### The ingredients needed to cook Thai Fried Bananas:

1. Take 1 ripe banana
1. Take 3/4 Cup rice flour
1. Use 1/4 Cup sugar
1. Provide 1/2 tsp baking powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
1. Take 1/3 Cup fresh grated coconut
1. Get 1 1/2 tsp toasted white sesame seeds
1. Get 1/3 Cup water
1. Get As needed Oil to Deep fry


##### Instructions to make Thai Fried Bananas:

1. Take ripe banana.Peel it and Cut in three equal parts.Now make 3-4 thin slices of each part.In this way Cut all banana slices.Now in a mixing bowl,add rice Flour, sugar, secret ingredient fresh grated coconut, salt and toasted sesame seeds.Mix all dry Ingredients.Rice Flour gives crispiness.
1. Add 1/3 Cup water and make semi thick batter.Add baking powder and mix well.Now put banana slices in batter and coat well.Heat oil on Medium flame and When heated sufficiently, lower the flame.Slide in batter coated Bananas.Deep fry till they turn dark brown in colour.
1. Take out the crisp fried Bananas in a serving plate and serve hot.This crunchy dessert is delicious but not too sweet.AWESOME taste!!
