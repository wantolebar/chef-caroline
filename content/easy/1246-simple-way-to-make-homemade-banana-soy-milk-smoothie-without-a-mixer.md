##### The ingredients needed to make Banana Soy Milk Smoothie without a Mixer:

1. Get 1 Banana
1. Get 300 ml Processed soy milk
1. Get 1/2 to 1 teaspoon Lemon juice (See Helpful Hints)
1. Provide 2 to 3 drops Vanilla extract
1. Take 1 tsp Sugar (Lakanto S sweetner) (See Helpful Hints)


##### Instructions to make Banana Soy Milk Smoothie without a Mixer:

1. Break up the banana into a large bowl. Sprinkle on lemon juice right away to prevent discoloring.
1. Mash with a whisk.
1. After 2 to 3 minutes, the bananas should have a paste-like consistency.
1. Add in the soy milk!
1. If you incorporate air into the mixture while whisking, it will become thick and creamy in about 3 minutes. When it reaches your desired consistency, add 2 to 3 drops of vanilla essence.
1. Pour into a cup filled with ice, then drink up.
