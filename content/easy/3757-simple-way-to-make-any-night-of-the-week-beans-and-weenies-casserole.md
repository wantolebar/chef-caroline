##### The ingredients needed to cook Beans and Weenies Casserole:

1. Use 1 package corn bread mix
1. Take 1 large can baked beans
1. Provide 1 package hotdogs


##### Steps to make Beans and Weenies Casserole:

1. Prepare the corn bread mix as instructed on box.
1. Pour into a greased casserole dish.
1. Chop up hotdogs and layer into the batter.
1. Spoon baked beans on top.
1. Bake at 400 for 25 minutes.
