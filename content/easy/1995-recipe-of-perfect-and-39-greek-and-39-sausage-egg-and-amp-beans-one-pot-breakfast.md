##### The ingredients needed to cook &#39;Greek&#39; Sausage, Egg &amp; Beans One Pot Breakfast:

1. Get 1/2 (1 tin) vegetarian sausages with baked beans,
1. Take 1 medium egg,
1. You need 1/2 a medium red onion cut into slices,
1. Prepare 1 medium vine tomato, cut into chunks,
1. Get  Around 35g reduced fat feta, cut into small cubes,
1. You need 1/4 tsp garlic granules,
1. Get  Dried chives to season and garnish (optional),
1. Prepare  Salt and pepper to season
1. You need 1/2 tbsp vegetable oil
1. Provide  Sides:
1. Prepare  Buttered toast of your choice


##### Instructions to make &#39;Greek&#39; Sausage, Egg &amp; Beans One Pot Breakfast:

1. Heat a large frying pan or wok over a medium to high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once nice and hot add the red onions and fry for a couple of minutes to soften then add the tomato.
1. Next pour in the beans with veggie sausages plus the garlic granules and stir together gently. Spread the mixture out in the pan and make a space to crack in the egg near the center. Turn the heat down to medium. Crack in the egg and then season the surrounding ingredients with some salt and pepper.
1. Scatter the feta around over the tomato, sausage, onions and beans section. Turn the heat down a little lower and allow everything to gently sizzle away as the egg cooks through in the centre.
1. Sprinkle over the chives and make up some buttered toast to go on the side. Remove pan from the heat once the egg white is cooked and allow to cool for a minute before serving. Perfect spooned over the hot buttery toast, and the runny yolk is lovely to dunk in! :)
