##### The ingredients needed to prepare Vanilla almond cake with three layers:

1. Get 1 1/2 cup all purpose fine flour(maida)
1. Take 1 cup sugar fine
1. Prepare 1 cup milk
1. Provide 1/2 cup refined oil
1. Take 1 tsp baking powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
1. You need 1 tsp vanilla essence
1. Use as needed Dry fruits almond and cashewnuts finely chopped for garnishing


##### Steps to make Vanilla almond cake with three layers:

1. Preheat the microwave
1. Grease the all three heart shape cake tin with oil
1. Take a bowl add oil sugar vanilla essence and mix well
1. Take a sieve add all purpose flour baking powdee and baking soda strain them well in a bowl
1. Mix well by adding milkto it with a cut fold method
1. Pour the cake batter in cake tin and tap it well to release air bubbles and garnish with dry fruits
1. Now bake the cake in a microwave for 25-30 min on convection mode 180 degree celcius
1. Once baked let the cake cool for 1 hour and serve it
