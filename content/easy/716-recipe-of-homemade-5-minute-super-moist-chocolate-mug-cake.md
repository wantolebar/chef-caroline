##### Instructions to make 5-minute Super Moist Chocolate Mug Cake:

1. Sieve the flour, baking powder and cocoa powder into a microwaveable mug. Make sure the mug is at least 1/3 empty so that it has enough space to fluff up.
1. Add sugar, semolina and salt. Stir well.
1. Add oil to the dry mix and stir until there are no lumps. Add milk and mix well.
1. Top it with your chocolate chips or chunks. I used bits of cooking chocolate because I love bites of gooey, almost melting bits in my cake.
1. Heat this mix for 1.30 minutes.
1. Take the mug out. Eat cake.
