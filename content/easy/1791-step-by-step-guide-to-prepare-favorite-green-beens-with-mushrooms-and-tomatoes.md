##### The ingredients needed to cook Green beens with mushrooms and tomatoes:

1. Get 2 cup diced tomatoes
1. Use 1/3 cup sautéed white mushrooms
1. Take 1/2 tsp salt
1. Take 31 oz canned green beans
1. Take 1/2 tsp red wine vinegar
1. You need 1 tbsp butter


##### Steps to make Green beens with mushrooms and tomatoes:

1. Sautee the tomatoes and mushrooms in butter 15 minutes. Add green beans and mix.
1. Bring to a boil add salt and vinegar.  Turn heat down to a simmer. Simmer 3 minutes
1. Serve hope you enjoy!
