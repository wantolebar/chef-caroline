##### The ingredients needed to cook Zesty White Bean Dip:

1. Take 1 1/2 cup white beans (cannellini, navy, etc.)
1. Use 6 clove garlic
1. Provide 6 large leaves fresh basil
1. You need 1 large lemon (juiced)
1. Prepare 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
1. You need 1/2 tsp salt (or more to taste)
1. Use 1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper


##### Steps to make Zesty White Bean Dip:

1. Place the beans in a colander and rinse well.
1. Add all the ingredients, except the basil, to a food processor or blender and puree until smooth.
1. Add basil leaves and blend until basil is well incorporated.
1. Enjoy!
