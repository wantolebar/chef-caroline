##### The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Milk Pudding:

1. Take  whole milk
1. You need  brown sugar
1. Take  powdered gelatin
1. Take  hot water
1. Use  drops of vanilla essence


##### Instructions to make Vanilla Milk Pudding:

1. Combine sugar with milk in a pot over low to medium heat. Stir until the sugar has melted but don&#39;t let the mixture boil
1. Dissolve powdered gelatin in hot water and add to the pot. Stir the mixture and heat for about 5 minutes
1. Remove from heat and stir in a few drops of vanilla essence to taste
1. Pour the mixture evenly into glass or ceramic containers and let it cool to room temperature
1. Chill the pudding in the refrigerator for 3-4 hours to solidify.
