##### Instructions to make Grilled Corn Salad:

1. Roast the corn on the cob on a grill or on the gas burner.
1. Remove the corn kernels using a knife. Hold the corn upright and push down with a sharp knife, the kernels will come off easily.
1. In a wok, heat some oil. Add the grilled corn kernels and toss to coat.
1. Add the onions and tomatoes. Mix well. Season with salt, red chilli powder and cumin powder. Cover and cook on a low flame for a few minutes just to allow the flavours to blend.
1. Remove from the flame and add the coriander and mint leaves. Mix.
1. Add the lemon juice and mix.
1. Serve warm or chilled.
