##### The ingredients needed to make Butternut squash &amp; apple baby food:

1. You need  butternut squash
1. Prepare  apples
1. Get  cinnamon
1. You need  Around 100ml breast milk or formula


##### Steps to make Butternut squash &amp; apple baby food:

1. Get ingredients
1. Chop/peel squash &amp; apple and distribute on a baking tray
1. Bake in oven at 175 degrees c - remember NOT to add oil as its for babies. Bake until soft &amp; tender. Add cinammon during baking.
1. When ready remove and leave to cool before blending. You can see in the picture that when it’s ready it’s really mushy.
1. Blending with breast/formula milk - add as required to make a purée appropriate for your baby. Younger babies may want a more watery consistency. I used about 100ml here.
1. Storage wise: because it contains milk - for breast milk it can be in the fridge for 3 days from the day the milk was pumped, or freezer for 6 months. For formula- follow the instructions for the provider.
1. I made about 6 meals for my baby from this recipe but you may make more/less depending on milk added &amp; appetite of baby
