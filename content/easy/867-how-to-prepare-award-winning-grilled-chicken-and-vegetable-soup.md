##### The ingredients needed to cook Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Soup:

1. You need 1 Tbsp olive oil
1. You need 1/2 cup chopped sweet onion
1. You need 1 large clove chopped garlic
1. Provide 1/2 tsp dried basil
1. You need 1/4 tsp dried oregano
1. Use 8 cups chicken broth
1. Get 14 ounce can diced tomatoes
1. Provide 3 medium potatoes chopped
1. Provide 1/2 cup diced carrots
1. Take 1/2 cup diced celery
1. Prepare 8 ounces grilled chicken breast chopped
1. Use 1/2 cup chopped green beans


##### Instructions to make Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Soup:

1. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil in a large saucepan.
1. Add basil, oregano and chicken broth and bring to a boil.
1. Add tomatoes, potatoes, carrots, celery and chicken breast. Lower heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
1. Add green beans and simmer for another 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
1. Serve and enjoy!
