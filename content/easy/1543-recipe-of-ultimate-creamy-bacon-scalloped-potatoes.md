##### The ingredients needed to prepare Creamy Bacon Scalloped Potatoes:

1. Use  Coarsely chopped bacon
1. Take  Sliced thinly russet potatoes
1. Use  Thinly sliced onion
1. Take  Sea salt
1. Prepare  Fresh ground pepper, or less if you prefer.
1. Provide  Light cream
1. Take  Flour
1. You need  Chicken stock
1. Take  Grated Parmesan


##### Instructions to make Creamy Bacon Scalloped Potatoes:

1. Preheat oven to 350°
1. On the stovetop over medium heat, sauté bacon until lightly crisp. Remove bacon with a slotted spoon; save rendered grease in pan.
1. Brush some bacon grease on bottom and sides of a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.Arrange one layer of sliced potatoes over bottom of baking dish.
1. Scatter a handful of sliced onions. Sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper to taste.Drizzle 1 teaspoon bacon grease over onions; then sprinkle a handful of chopped bacon on top. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon flour over layer.Add another layer of potatoes and onions; repeat, seasoning, bacon grease, bacon, and flour.
1. Add one final layer of potatoes; sprinkle remaining salt and pepper.Pour cream and stock over potatoes to just cover the top layer. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake until potatoes are tender, takes about an hour.
