##### The ingredients needed to prepare Cake Pops:

1. Provide 1 cup Chocolate Cake in crumbled form
1. You need As needed Chocolate Frosting (amount depends on moistness of cake
1. Take 2 tbsp I used
1. Take 100 grams White chocolate compound
1. You need 100 grams Dark chocolate compound–50
1. Use Aa needed cake pop sticks
1. Get As needed Sprinkles


##### Steps to make Cake Pops:

1. Add cake to a large bowl.
1. Crumble until it resembles fine crumbs - Add in frosting a little bit at a time until cake is moist and can hold a ball shape, yet still slightly crumbly.
1. Use your hands to incorporate the frosting into the cake crumbs.
1. Use a mini ice cream scoop and scoop out two balls of cake mixture.
1. Roll the mixture into a tight ball and place on a plate.
1. Repeat until all the cake mixture has been rolled into balls.
1. Melt 2-4 ounces of white chocolate in the microwave. - Dip the tip of the cake pop sticks into the white chocolate and insert into the cake balls about half-way.
1. Refrigerate for about 20 minutes. - Meanwhile prepare all of your decorating supplies.
1. Melt the remaining chocolate in a large cup. Make sure you have enough chocolate to completely submerge the cake ball. - Remove cake balls from freezer. - Dip cake balls carefully into the chocolate until covered.
1. Let the excess chocolate drip off. Swirl and tap gently if needed. - Add the sprinkles while the chocolate is still wet. It will harden quickly.
1. Stick the decorated cake pop into a styrofoam block to finish setting. - Place into the freezer to speed up setting time. - Enjoy!
1. Note_For marble look i put some melted dark compound in centre of melted white compound and dip cake balls and swirl carefully.
