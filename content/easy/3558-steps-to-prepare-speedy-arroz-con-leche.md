##### The ingredients needed to cook Arroz con leche:

1. You need 3 1/4 whole milk
1. Prepare 1 1/4 water
1. Take 1 can sweetend consensed milk
1. Get 1 cup uncooked rice (can use more is desired)
1. You need 2 tsp vanilla extract
1. Take 2 cinnamon sticks
1. Prepare 1 ground cinnamon (for garnishing)
1. Provide 1 sugar to taste
1. You need 1/3 cup raisins


##### Steps to make Arroz con leche:

1. Place rice, water and cinnamon sticks in a heavy pot and soak for an hour.
1. When the milk is boiling, lower heat and wait for the rice to cook over low heat.
1. Pour milk in the pot of rice and bring to a boil.
1. When rice is very well cooked add the 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract and sweet condensed milk or some sugar to taste.(You don&#39;t have to put the condensed milk its alright only with milk).
1. Stir the mixture constantly until it reaches the desired consistency.
1. Let it cool
1. Finally, serve it with a dusting of ground cinnamon. Add raisins too, if using.
