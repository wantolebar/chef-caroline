##### The ingredients needed to cook Cream cheese chocolate cake:

1. Prepare 2 cup all purpose flour
1. Take 2 cups icing sugar
1. Provide 3/4 cup cocoa powder
1. Take 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
1. Provide 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda
1. You need 1 tsp salt
1. Prepare 2 eggs
1. You need 1 cup milk
1. Take 1/2 cup oil
1. Take 2 tsp vanilla essence
1. Take 1 cup boiling water
1. Provide  Chocolate cream cheese frosting
1. Take 3/4 cup coco powder
1. Get 240 gms cow cheese
1. Take 200 gms butter unsalted
1. Use 3 cups icing sugar
1. Prepare 500 gms whipping cream
1. You need 1 tsp vanilla essence
1. You need  For moist
1. Prepare 1/2 cup milk


##### Instructions to make Cream cheese chocolate cake:

1. Heat oven to 180 degree.Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
1. Stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt in large bowl. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; beat on medium speed of mixer 2 minutes. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.

Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely.
1. Just beat butter and cream cheese and sugar then other side beat cream and coco powder together Then mix both and beat add little milk if frosting is too thick.
1. Now moist 1st layer with milk and apply frosting on it then moist above layer both aside with milk and cover the whole cake with frosting Decorate with marshmallow flowers and chocolate chips
