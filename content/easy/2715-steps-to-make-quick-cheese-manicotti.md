##### The ingredients needed to cook Cheese Manicotti:

1. Use 18 Jumbo Shells
1. Take 1 lb Ricotta Cheese (or cottage cheese)
1. Get 1 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese
1. You need 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
1. Take 1 Egg
1. Use 1 1/2 tsp Parsley
1. Provide 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Use 1 jar Spaghetti Sauce


##### Instructions to make Cheese Manicotti:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F
1. Boil Jumbo Shells
1. Mix Cheeses, egg, parsley &amp; salt
1. Pour half of the sauce into bottom of baking dish
1. Fill shells with cheese mix
1. Top with rest of sauce
1. Bake for 45 min
