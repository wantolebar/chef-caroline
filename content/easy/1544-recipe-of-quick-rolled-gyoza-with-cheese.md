##### The ingredients needed to cook Rolled Gyoza with Cheese:

1. Provide 25 gyoza dumpling skins
1. Provide 4 sliced cheese
1. Get 1 small bunch of Chinese chives
1. You need 2 table spoons sauce for barbecued meat(sauce for Yakiniku)
1. Take 2 table spoons vegetable oil


##### Instructions to make Rolled Gyoza with Cheese:

1. Cut Chinese chives to 3cm, add Yakiniku sauce and mix.
1. Fold sliced cheese to separate them into 6 parts, so that it can be rolled in Gyoza sheets
1. Roll up Chinese chives and sliced cheese with Gyoza sheets. Seal the seams with water.
1. Put oil into a heated frying pan and lay the gyoza, with the seam sides facing heated pan.
1. Once they are brown, flip them over. When both sides are nicely brown, they are done.
