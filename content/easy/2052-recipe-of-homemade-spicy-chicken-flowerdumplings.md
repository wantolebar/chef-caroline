##### Steps to make Spicy Chicken FlowerðŸŒ¹Dumplings:

1. Take minced chicken, add 1tsp of salt, 1tbsp black pepper powder, 1tbsp adrak lasan paste, 1tbsp soya sauce, 1 Tbsp oil, 1 medium sized onion, 2 green chillies and crush them all in a grinder.
1. After grinding, add 1/2 cup fresh coriander, and mix it well.
1. Oiled your hands and make small balls of mixture using your palm.
1. Refrigerate the balls for 15 minutes.
1. Take 1/4 cup of rice and soak them in water for 5 minutes
1. Divide the rice into 2 plates and add 2 to 3 drops of red colour in 1 plate of rice.
1. After refregirating for 15 minutes, covered the chicken balls with uncooked red and white rice
1. Steam the balls for 15 to 20 minutes
1. Spicy Chicken FlowerðŸŒ¹Dumplings are ready
