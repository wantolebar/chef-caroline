##### The ingredients needed to prepare Tossed Caprese Salad:

1. Take 1 Arugula
1. Prepare 6 basil, chiffoned
1. Provide 3 tbsp mozzarella ball, cubed
1. Take 1/4 cup chopped tomato
1. Use 1/4 cup aged balsamic vinegar
1. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste
1. Provide 1 croutons


##### Steps to make Tossed Caprese Salad:

1. Layer as follows:  Arugula,  tomatoes,  mozzarella, basil,  croutons.
1. Add vinegar, oil and salt and pepper to mason jar.  Shake.
1. Serve dressing over salad.
