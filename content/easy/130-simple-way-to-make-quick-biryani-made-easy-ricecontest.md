##### The ingredients needed to make Biryani made easy #ricecontest:

1. Prepare  rice
1. Prepare  grade chicken marinated
1. Provide  ground cumin, paprika, tandoori masala
1. Use  cinnamon(optional)
1. Get  large potatoes and 1 pinch of salt and sunset yellow food color
1. Provide  large onions
1. You need  natural yoghurt or Mala
1. You need  large tomatoes
1. You need  salt
1. You need  Cooking oil for deep frying the potatoes&amp;frying chicken &amp; onion
1. Use  small piece of raw pawpaw
1. Prepare  cinnamon sticks or a safron stick- optional


##### Steps to make Biryani made easy #ricecontest:

1. Shallow fry the marinated chicken. I marinated mine using lime juice,coriander and 1 teaspoon of mixed spice..until tender but not too soft.
1. Shallow fry your onions.
1. I slightly boiled my potatoes and added a pinch of salt then coated with the food colour and deep fried them.
1. Blended the tomatoes, yoghurt and diced pawpaw.
1. Stir fry the spices then add the onions and the yoghurt mixture.once hot. add in your chicken and bring it to boil
1. When almost boiled add in your fried potatoes for 2-3min.
1. The rice cook it separately, I boiled 4 cups of water then added a pinch of salt and my washed rice. So we used 1 1/2cups normally white rice and added 1/2cup of multi grain japanese rice for a twist but that&#39;s optional and the cinnamon sticks/safron stick.
