##### The ingredients needed to prepare Strawberry cheesecake!!!!:

1. Prepare 250 g digestive biscuits
1. Prepare 100 g butter, melted
1. Get 1 vanilla essence
1. Get 600 g cream cheese
1. You need 100 g icing sugar
1. Prepare 284 ml pot of double cream
1. Get  For the topping
1. Take 400 g punnet of strawberries, halved
1. Prepare 25 g icing sugar
1. Get  For the strawberry coulis
1. Provide  Add remaining strawberries to pan with tablespoon of caster sugar and a teaspoon of water, bring to boil


##### Steps to make Strawberry cheesecake!!!!:

1. Melt butter
1. Crush biscuits
1. Mix butter in with biscuits
1. Add to 20cm square or round tin and chill in fridge for 30 minutes.
1. Mix soft cheese, icing sugar and vanilla in a separate bowl until smooth.
1. Add cream and mix throughly.
1. Put the mixture on the biscuit base and chill for 4 hours or over night
1. Cut strawberries in half and save some to make strawberry sauce.
1. To make strawberry sauce, add caster sugar and strawberries in a pan with a teaspoon of water and bring to boil. Take off heat and let it cool.
