##### The ingredients needed to prepare Unstuffed Bell Peppers:

1. Prepare 1 chopped onion
1. Get 2 clove garlic minced or sliced
1. Get 1 bell pepper chopped
1. Take 2 lb ground meat
1. Get 1 season salt
1. Take 1 garlic powder
1. Use 1 onion powder
1. Get 2 can diced tomatoes or rotel
1. Take 1 cup cooked rice
1. Take 4 tbsp taco sauce
1. Provide 3 bell peppers sliced thin
1. Get 1 salt
1. Use 1 shredded cheese


##### Instructions to make Unstuffed Bell Peppers:

1. Sauté onions, garlic, and 1 bell pepper
1. Cook ground meat adding seasonings.
1. Drain grease from ground meat.
1. Combine sautéd vegetables &amp; ground meat.
1. Add rice, cans of tomatoes, &amp; taco sauce to meat mixture.
1. Layer bottom of casserole dish with half of the sliced bell peppers.
1. Sprinkle with salt
1. Spread 1/2 meat mixture over sliced bell peppers.
1. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
1. Repeat layers using remaining ingredients.
1. Cover and bake in 400° preheated oven for 45 minutes to an hour.
