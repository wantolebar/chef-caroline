##### The ingredients needed to make Jalapenos Cheese Ball:

1. Prepare 500 gms potatoes
1. Use 1 cup white Cheddar
1. Prepare 1/4 th cup sliced jalapenos
1. Use 1/4 th cup All purpose flour
1. Use 1 Egg lightly beaten(pure vegetarian guys can use cream)
1. Prepare 1 cups panko bread crumbs
1. You need For frying Vegetable/corn oil
1. Get  Ranch dressing
1. Provide 1/4 th cup mayonnaise
1. Take 1/4 th cup Hung curd
1. Get 1/2 tsp Chopped parsley
1. Get 2 tsp Garlic powder
1. Prepare 1tsp salt
1. Use 1/2 tsp onion powder
1. Take 1 tsp Dry parsley
1. You need 1 tsp white pepper powder


##### Instructions to make Jalapenos Cheese Ball:

1. Have oil heated to 325 * f -  - Pick the boiled potato discard the skin. mash the potato in a large bowl add jalapenos,salt, pepper, cheese and mix to combine,Roll the mixture about the size of a golf ball,place the flour, eggs, and bread crumbs in 3 separate bowls. Roll each ball in the flour, then the egg and then the bread crumb set aside,when the oil is hot fry the balls in batches. Cook for about 5 minutes per batch, serve along with ranch dressing.
1. Ranch dressing - place all the ingredients together mix well and serve with sprinkle of paprika powder if like some spiciness in it
