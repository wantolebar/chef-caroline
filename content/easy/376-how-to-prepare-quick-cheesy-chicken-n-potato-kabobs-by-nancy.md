##### The ingredients needed to prepare Cheesy chicken n potato kabobs by Nancy:

1. Provide 5 potatoes boiled and mashed
1. Take 1 cup small pieces of boiled chicken
1. Get 1/2 cup Any cheese or according to your taste
1. Get 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder
1. Get 1 teaspoon Red crushed chillies
1. Prepare to taste salt
1. Get 1 teaspoon cumin seeds powder roasted
1. Prepare 2 tablespoons cornstarch
1. Use  Bread crumbs as required
1. Provide  oil for frying


##### Steps to make Cheesy chicken n potato kabobs by Nancy:

1. Mix all ingredients together except bread crumbs
1. Take small quantity and give them shape now coat one bye one with bread crumbs and fry in pan at low heat with less quantity of oil.
1. And serve with ketchup or any dip you want.
