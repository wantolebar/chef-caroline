##### Steps to make Healthy Pasta Salad:

1. First of all properly wash to all the vegetable.
1. Now put to a yogurt in large bowl, then add salt, black pepper, oregano, red chili flake and vegetables and whisk all together.
1. Now add boil pasta in dressing and mix it and toss together.
