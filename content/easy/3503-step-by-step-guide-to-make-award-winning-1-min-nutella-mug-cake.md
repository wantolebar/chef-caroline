##### The ingredients needed to make 1 Min Nutella Mug cake:

1. Get 4 T nutella
1. Take 4 T flour
1. Prepare Drop vanilla essence
1. Take 3 T milk
1. Use 1/4 t baking powder


##### Instructions to make 1 Min Nutella Mug cake:

1. Beat the nutella and milk till smooth
1. Add in the other ingredients (flour, vanilla essence and baking powder) and mix well. Mug or cup should be 3/4 full.
1. Cook in the microwave for 70 seconds
1. Top with ice cream, nutella and a dusting of icing sugar
