##### The ingredients needed to cook Pav bhaji:

1. Prepare 300 gms Pumpkin(your choice)
1. Use 3 cups Potato diced
1. Use 1 cup Cauliflower
1. Prepare 1 medium Onion chopped
1. Get 4 medium Tomato chopped
1. Take 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
1. Provide 1/2 tbsp Salt
1. Take 3 tbsp butter
1. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
1. Prepare to taste Red chilli
1. Get 2 Pav bhaji masala
1. Get 1/2 tbsp turmeric
1. Use 1 cup water
1. Use For PAV BHAJI GARNISH-
1. You need 1 Onion chopped spring slices
1. You need 2 lemon
1. Prepare as required Butter
1. Get as required coriander leaves
1. Get some Paneer


##### Instructions to make Pav bhaji:

1. Start instant pot, add oil &amp; butter in it, add onion and saute it for 3 minS.
1. After add ginger, garlic paste saute 2 mins., after that add tomatoes saute all 5 mins.
1. Add Pav bhaji masala, red chill, Salt, turmeric &amp; add all chopped veggies &amp; water mix well press the cancel and close lid with vent in sealing position, cook on high pressure for 8 mins.
1. Turn off heat, let the pressure release naturally.
1. Put on medium flame and let bhaji boil for a minute. Bhaji mash with hand blinder &amp; potatoes masher. Boil for 2 mins. Add coriander leaves &amp; paneer.
1. When u serving add lemon juice, spring onion,butter.
1. Also try bhaji with bread &amp; Pav.
