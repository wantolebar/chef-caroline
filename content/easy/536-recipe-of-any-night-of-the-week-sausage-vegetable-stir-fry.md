##### Instructions to make Sausage vegetable stir fry:

1. Cut all veggies in similar shape and set aside
1. Boil sausage for 3 mins and dice
1. In a pan, saute the cooked diced sausage,add in onions followed by green beans and carrot
1. Add boiled potatoes
1. Add tomatoes and peppers,add spices and seasoning.
1. Stir for a while and add soy sauce
1. Stir fry until everything us well combined
1. Serve with white rice or cous cous
