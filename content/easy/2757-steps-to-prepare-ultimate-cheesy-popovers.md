##### The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Popovers:

1. Get 1 cup gluten-free all purpose flour (or regular flour)
1. Get 1 cup almond milk (or milk of your choice)
1. Provide 1/2 tsp garlic salt
1. Get 3 eggs
1. Take 1 tbsp butter
1. Use 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese


##### Instructions to make Cheesy Popovers:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Spray muffin tin with cooking spray.
1. In food processor, add flour, milk, salt, eggs, &amp; butter.  Cover &amp; process for 2 1/2 minutes continuously. Add cheese &amp; pulse 2 times to mix.
1. Pour into prepared cups, filling each about 3/4 full. Bake about 45 to 50 minutes or until golden. Serve immediately.
