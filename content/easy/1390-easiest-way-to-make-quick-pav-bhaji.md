##### The ingredients needed to cook Pav bhaji:

1. Use 1 tbsp butter
1. Prepare 3 tomato, finely chopped
1. Prepare 1/4 cup peas / matar
1. Provide 1/2 capsicum, finely chopped
1. Prepare 1 tsp salt
1. You need 1 1/2 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder / lal mirch powder
1. Take 1/4 tsp turmeric / haldi
1. You need 11/2 tsp pav bhaji masala
1. Take 2 tsp kasuri methi / dry fenugreek leaves
1. Take 3 tbsp coriander leaves, finely chop ped
1. Take 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
1. Take 1 onion, finely chopped
1. Use 1/2 lemon juice
1. Take 3 drops red food colour, optional
1. You need As needed water to adjust consistency
1. Use 8 pav / bread roll
1. Take 4 tsp butter
1. Provide 1/2 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder / lal mirch powder
1. Take 1/2 tsp pav bhaji masala
1. Use 4 tsp coriander leaves, finely chopped


##### Steps to make Pav bhaji:

1. Firstly, in a large kadai heat 1 tbsp butter and add vegetables. Cook and mash well.
1. Now add 1 tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp pav bhaji masala, 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp coriander leaves.
1. Heat a tbsp of butter and add ¼ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp pav bhaji masala, 1 tsp kasuri methi.
1. Also add 1 tbsp coriander leaves, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, 1 onion and ½ lemon juice. Saute well.
1. Now add 3 drops of red food colour and mix well.
1. Boil and mash for 5 minutes adjusting consistency.
1. Finally, serve pav and bhaji as pav bhaji.
