##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Carrot Cake:

1. You need  cake ingredients
1. Provide 100 grams chopped walnuts
1. Use 4 eggs
1. Prepare 250 grams self raising flour
1. Provide 350 grams caster sugar
1. Get 350 grams vegetable oil
1. You need 1 tsp cinnermon
1. You need 1 tsp nutmeg
1. Get 1 dash vallina extract
1. Get  icing
1. Get 400 grams icing sugar
1. Get 300 grams cream cheese
1. Use 110 grams room temp butter
1. Provide 1 dash lemon juice


##### Instructions to make Simple Carrot Cake:

1. preheat oven 180°F
1. grease two round cake tins
1. mix flour and spices together
1. blend together all the other wet ingredients
1. add carrots and walnuts and mix together
1. put mixture in cake tins, even amounts
1. put in the middle of the oven for 35-45 mins
1. cool down on a cooling rack
1. for icing mix butter and cream cheese together
1. mix in all the other ingredients
1. spread imbetween cake layers and on top
1. please note: walnuts are optional and can be replaced with raisins
