##### The ingredients needed to prepare Apple Basket:

1. Use  tarts
1. You need  sugar
1. Provide  cinnamon powder
1. Get  sliced apples
1. Use  Walnut pieces
1. Provide  butterscotch sauce(readily available)


##### Instructions to make Apple Basket:

1. Heat sugar in a pan and caramelize.
1. When it melts add Cinnamon powder and 3/4 th of sliced apples and mix well.
1. Add Walnut pieces and mix and off the flame and cool.
1. Take the tarts fill with 1/2 tbsp butterscotch sauce.
1. Then put the apple stuffing up to 3/4 th of the tart.
1. Arrange the Apple slices overlapping each other in a circular form to give a shape of a rose.
1. Repeat to make more tarts and serve immediately.
