1. You need 3/4 cup sugar
1. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
1. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
1. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
1. You need 4 tbsp melted butter
1. Use  For caramel sauce
1. Provide 2 tbsp sugar
1. Provide 1 tbsp butter
1. Use 1/4 cup cream
1. Use  Other ingredients
1. Use 8 sliced almonds


##### Instructions to make Honey almond cake:

1. For vanilla cake - 1. Pre-heat the oven at 180 degrees celsius for 10 mins. - 2. Sieve together maida,baking powder and baking soda in a bowl.  - 3. Now mix together curd,butter and sugar in another bowl.
1. 4. Now gradually add wet ingredients(2) into dry ingredients(1). - 5. Beat them with a blender and add vanilla essence. - 6. Grease the baking dish with butter and dust some flour, it will help the cake to come out easily.
1. For caramel sauce  - 1. Take a thick bottom base pan and add sugar to it. - 2. Heat the sugar on low flame till it gets melted. - 3. Add the butter. - 4. As the butter melts, add amul cream and keep stirring.
1. How to arrange  - Put the caramel sauce on cake and garnish with sliced almonds.
