##### The ingredients needed to cook Pork Rice Bowl:

1. Prepare 200 g thin-cut pork loin chops (preferably from the shoulder end, or belly or ham)
1. Prepare 3~4 leaves cabbage (washed, thinly shredded and soak in cold water) or lettuce (washed and torn into pieces)
1. Use 1/2 to 1 onions (chopped)
1. You need 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
1. Take  Sauce (mix in advance):
1. You need * 2 Tbsp sugar
1. Take * 3 Tbsp sake
1. Prepare * 3 Tbsp soy sauce


##### Instructions to make Pork Rice Bowl:

1. Heat the oil in a frying pan over medium heat and sear the pork on both sides. Take them out from the pan.
1. Cook the onion in the same frying pan until lightly browned. Add the sauce, pork and simmer thoroughly.
1. Arrange the rice in a bowl and place the cabbage or lettuce over the rice, then add the pork and sauce.
