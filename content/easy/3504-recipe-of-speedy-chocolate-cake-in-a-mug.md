##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cake in a mug:

1. Get 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1. Use 1/4 cup white sugar
1. You need 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
1. Use 1/8 tsp baking soda
1. Use 1/8 tsp salt
1. Use 3 tbsp milk
1. You need 2 tbsp canola oil
1. Provide 1 tbsp water
1. You need 1/4 tsp vanilla extract


##### Steps to make Chocolate cake in a mug:

1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a large microwave-safe mug; stir in milk, canola oil, water, and vanilla extract.
1. Cook in microwave until cake is done in the middle, about 1 minute 45 seconds.
