##### The ingredients needed to make Pav bhaji:

1. Get 5-6 Potatoes boiled
1. You need 1 Capsicum
1. Use 1 handful Peas -
1. Use 3-4 Cauliflower florets
1. Take 3-4 Onions-finely chopped
1. Get 4-5 Tomatoes puréed-
1. Prepare 2-3 tsp Ginger garlic paste-
1. Take as per taste Salt-
1. You need 2tsp Red chilli powder-
1. Prepare 1tsp Garam masala-
1. Get 2 tsp Coriander powder
1. Provide 2-3 tsp Pav bhaji masala-
1. Use as needed Fresh coriander leaves- to garnish
1. Prepare 2-3 Lemons-
1. Get 2 Finely chopped onions-
1. You need 1 pack Pav buns
1. Get 100 gms Butter
1. You need 3-4 tbsp Oil-


##### Instructions to make Pav bhaji:

1. Boil and mashed potatoes.
1. Roughly chop the veggies, boil them and mash them.
1. Heata pan put oil, half of butter,chopped onions and fry till golden brown, add ginger garlic paste and further cook for few minutes.
1. Add above-mentioned spices and mix well,
1. Add mashed potatoes and mashed vegetables, mix well add water as required, cover with lid and cook for 10 minutes.
1. Mash the cooked bhaji with a masher and cook till the pan leaves oil.
1. Heat a tawa. Cut the pav in the centre and put butter on tawa, sprinkle red chilli powder and toast the pav.
1. Transfer the hot bhaji in a serving bowl. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves top up with finely chopped onions.serve with buns.
