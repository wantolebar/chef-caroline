##### The ingredients needed to prepare Porcupine Meatballs:

1. Use  Meatballs
1. Provide 1/2 C Long Grain Rice, uncooked
1. Prepare 1/2 C Water
1. You need 1 Medium White Onion, chopped
1. You need 1 tsp Sea Salt
1. Provide 1/2 tsp Celery Seed
1. Prepare 1/8 tsp Ground Black Pepper
1. Provide 1 pound Ground Beef
1. Prepare 2 Tblsp Cooking Oil
1. Provide  Sauce
1. Provide 15 oz can Tomato Sauce
1. Use 1 C water
1. You need 2 Tblsp Light Brown Sugar
1. Use 2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce


##### Instructions to make Porcupine Meatballs:

1. In a large mixing bowl: ground beef, 1/2 C water, onion, salt celery seed, black pepper, and garlic powder. Combine with hands until blended.
1. Heat oil in large frying pan. I love using my cast iron skillet, or chicken fryer.
1. Form 1 1/2 inch meatballs and place in heated skillet. As balls brown roll them in the skillet to cook evenly. Drain grease in container safe for hot grease.
1. While meatballs are cooking mix sauce in a large mixing bowl: can tomato sauce, 1 cup water, brown sugar, worcestershire sauce.
1. Once meatballs have been gently browned and firmed up pour sauce directly over meatballs in the skillet. Heat to a simmer. Cover and simmer gently about one hour. Checking to make sure they heat evenly. Don&#39;t cut the time or the rice will be under cooked.
1. Serve meatballs with sauce. I like to have mine with mashed potatoes and a vegetable.
