##### The ingredients needed to cook Banana ice cream with chocolate chips:

1. Provide  frozen bananas
1. Provide  cap of vanilla extract
1. You need  plain yogurt (optional)


##### Steps to make Banana ice cream with chocolate chips:

1. Peel frozen bananas.
1. Put bananas, vanilla and yogurt in blender.
1. Blend til smooth.
1. Pour mix into a freezer bag.
1. Add chocolate chips and mix gently. Close bag.
1. Put in freezer.
1. Leave for a few hours. Depending on how hard you like you ice cream. The pic is the last of the ice cream. Everyone has been spooning out a little here and there.
