1. You need 1 tsp Ginger paste
1. Use 1 tbsp Chilli powder
1. You need 1 tsp Tenderiser
1. Provide 1 tsp Salt
1. Take  Veggies
1. Provide 1 Capsicum (jullienes)
1. Get 1 Zucchini (jullienes)

1. Prepare 1/4 tsp Blackpepper (crushed)


##### Steps to make Whosaynas Veggies and Steak Stirfry:

1. Marinate the fillet slices and rest it for three hours
1. Then fry in four tablespoons of oil
1. Fry on high flame till it&#39;s browned up, lower flame to cook
1. Remove on platter and cut strips
1. Stirfry veggies with salt, black pepper and garlic in a tablespoon of oil on high flame for ten minutes
1. Add steak strips and mix well and pour on casserole
