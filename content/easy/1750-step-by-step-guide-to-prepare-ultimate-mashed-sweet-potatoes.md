##### The ingredients needed to prepare Mashed sweet potatoes:

1. Take 2 medium sweet potatoes
1. Use 1 large Irish potato
1. You need  I table spoon blue band


##### Instructions to make Mashed sweet potatoes:

1. Peel the potatoes
1. Clean them thoroughly
1. Put the sweet potatoes in a sufuria,add water and bring them to boil for 20 minutes.
1. Then add the Irish potatoes bring them to boil together. For the remaining 25 mins.
1. When ready, put them in a bowl and mash well till smooth.
1. Add the blue band and mix well till smooth.
1. Serve with beef stew and some vegetables.
