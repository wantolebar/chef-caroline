##### The ingredients needed to prepare Chinese qeema:

1. Get 2 tbs oil
1. Use 1 lb keema
1. Use 1 tbsp soya sauce
1. Provide 1 ts crush pepper
1. Provide 1 ts black pepper
1. You need 1 ts salt
1. Prepare 1 whole green chilli
1. Prepare 1 capsium
1. Take 2 small onion
1. Take 1 ts garlic paste


##### Steps to make Chinese qeema:

1. Aap pan me oil dale oil garam ho to is me garlic paste daal de phir few minutes gumai phir is me keema daal de.
1. Keema ke under black pepper, salt, soysauce, crush pepper,and green chilli ko two piece kar ke dalna he.
1. Keeme ko 15 minute cook karna he. Jab is ka paani bilkul Thora reh jaye
1. Is me yellow green pepper ke square cubes and onion ke small square cubes kar ke daalna he pir is ko cover kar ke 5 to 10 minutes medium to high par cook hone de bas itna dhiyan rakhe vegetable boht soft na ho.phir is ko dhania daal kar serve karein.
