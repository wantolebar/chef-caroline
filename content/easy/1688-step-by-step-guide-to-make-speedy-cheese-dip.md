##### The ingredients needed to prepare Cheese Dip:

1. Provide  Mayonnaise
1. Use  Grated cheese
1. Get  Cream
1. Get  Garlic grated
1. Prepare  Salt
1. Use  Black pepper powder


##### Instructions to make Cheese Dip:

1. Take Mayonnaise in a bowl.
1. Mix cheese in the Mayonnaise.Whisk.
1. Add cream,salt,garlic and black pepper into mayo mixture.Mix it well.
1. Micro the dip for 30 sec.and then mix the dip.
1. Your aromatic tasty dip is ready to serve.This dip will go very well with french fries, potato wedges and garlic bread.
