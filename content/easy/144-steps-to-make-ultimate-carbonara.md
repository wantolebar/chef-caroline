##### The ingredients needed to cook Carbonara:

1. Take 200 kg Penne Pasta
1. Prepare 1 cup quick melt cheese
1. Provide 1 cup fresh milk
1. You need 1/2 cup cooked bacon
1. Provide 1/2 cup all purpose cream
1. Provide 1/2 cup sliced button mushroom
1. Take to taste Salt and Pepper
1. Prepare  Garnish with chopped parsley


##### Steps to make Carbonara:

1. Put milk and all purpose cream to boil
1. Put quick melt and wait it to melt then set aside
1. Cook pasta for 10 mins
1. Pour the sauce into the cooked pasta and put the bacon bits and garnish with parsley
