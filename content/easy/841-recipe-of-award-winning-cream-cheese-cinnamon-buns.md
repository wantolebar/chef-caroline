##### Instructions to make Cream cheese cinnamon buns:

1. Per heat oven 350
1. Cream together 1 8oz Cream cheese and 1cup confectioners set aside
1. Melt butter  in long dish
1. Mix 1cup sugar with 2tsp ground cinnamon
1. Roll 1 biscuit with rolling pin flat spread 1tbs cream cheese evenly roll up and then roll in butter. ....  roll in cinnamon sugar.....  tie in a knot...and..place on baking sheet
1. Repeat until all biscuit are finished. About an inch apart. Cover with the remaining cinnamon sugar
1. Bake 20 to 25 minutes
1. Cream together remaining ingredients for frosting. Cover with a blanket of cream cheese frosting and enjoy
