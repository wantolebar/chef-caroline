##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet corn puree:

1. Get  corn (chopped off the cob)
1. Take  butter
1. Get  fresh cream


##### Steps to make Sweet corn puree:

1. Boil the corn until cooked. About 4 minutes
1. Strain the water and add butter and stir while on medium heat
1. Blend with a hand blender or normal blender until lumpy
1. Push corn mix through a sieve
1. Add cream if desired and serve
