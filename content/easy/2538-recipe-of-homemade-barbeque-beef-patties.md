##### The ingredients needed to prepare Barbeque Beef Patties:

1. Prepare 1 cup Soft Bread Crums
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Milk
1. Use 1 lb Ground Beef
1. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
1. Use 1 Ground Black Pepper
1. Get 1/2 cup Chopped Onion
1. Provide 1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
1. Get 1/4 cup Vinegar
1. Get 3 tbsp Sugar
1. Provide 1/2 cup Ketchup
1. Use 1/2 cup Water


##### Steps to make Barbeque Beef Patties:

1. Preheat oven to 350℉
1. In a large mixing bowl, add Bread Crumbs and Milk. Combine to moisten crumbs.
1. Add in Ground Beef, Salt, and Pepper. Combine together.
1. Add Chopped Onion and combine with mixture.
1. Shape into patties. (Usually comes out to 6 patties) Place into prepared 8 inch baking dish.
1. Mix together Worcestershire Sauce, Vinegar, Sugar, Ketchup, and Water in a medium bowl.
1. Pour sauce over patties and baste with spoonfuls of sauce until patties are covered.
1. Bake for 45 minutes.
