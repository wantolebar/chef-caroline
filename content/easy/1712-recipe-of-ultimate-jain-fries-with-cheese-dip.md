##### The ingredients needed to prepare Jain Fries with Cheese Dip:

1. Prepare  For fries-
1. Prepare  raw bananas
1. Get  Salt
1. Get  as per taste Pepper
1. Use  as required Oil for frying
1. Provide  For Cheese Dip-
1. You need  butter
1. Provide  milk
1. You need  cheese cubes/slice


##### Instructions to make Jain Fries with Cheese Dip:

1. For the dip, in a bowl take butter, add milk and grated cheese or cheese slice and microwave for 30 seconds. After 30 seconds remove the bowl and stir. Again microwave for 30 seconds. Do this 3 times that is 1 minute and 30 seconds.
1. After that blend it for 10-15 seconds using a hand blender to get the smooth consistency. The dip is ready. You can store the dip in an air tight container for a week in refrigerator.
1. Alternatively, you can also make the dip in the double boiler method until the cheese and butter melt completely. Then, blend it for 15-20 seconds.
1. For fries, Peel the bananas and cut them into thin sticks. Soak them into cold water for 15 minutes.
1. Drain off the water and spread the banana sticks into a kitchen towel.
1. Now fry them on medium flame until they turn golden.
1. Immediately after removing the fries from oil, sprinkle some salt and pepper.
1. Serve hot with cheese dip.
