##### The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Soup:

1. Get 200 grams Mushroom
1. Prepare 1/2 liter Meat soup
1. Provide 200 ml Fresh milk
1. Prepare 3 clove Grinded garlic
1. You need 1 small Onion
1. Get 3 tbsp Flour
1. Provide 3 tbsp Butter
1. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
1. Provide 150 ml Fresh cream


##### Steps to make Mushroom Soup:

1. Put butter in a suitable ban on the stove,
1. add onion&amp;grinded garlic , stirr them well untill turning into yellow
1. Add the flour , stirr well till it becomes creamy , then add the mashrooms slices and stirr them
1. Add fresh milk, meat soup , salt ,fresh cream with quickly &amp; continuous stirring untill it turns into creamy
1. Mix it with the mixer till getting a suitable strength
1. Decorate it with some mushroom slices
