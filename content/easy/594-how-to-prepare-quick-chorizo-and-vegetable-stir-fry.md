##### The ingredients needed to cook Chorizo And Vegetable Stir fry:

1. Use 2 tbsp oil
1. Provide 1 large dice onion
1. Provide 1 tbsp dice garlic
1. Prepare 3 slice chorizo cut into strip
1. Provide 2 tbsp Campbells condensed oxtail soup
1. Get 4 tbsp water
1. Provide 4 cup choi sum or any green vegetable
1. Take 1 tsp salt
1. Get 1/2 tbsp light soy sauce
1. You need 2 small red chili pepper
1. Use 1 tbsp sliced ginger


##### Instructions to make Chorizo And Vegetable Stir fry:

1. With oil saute the onion, ginger  and garlic with chorizo cut into strip for 2 minutes then (add oxtail condensed soup with water and mix well) as gravy
1. Add choi sum or and green leaf vegetable that you have and stir fry to mix then cover lid for another 2 minutes
1. Add seasoning and 2 red small chili (optional ) let it simmer for another 2 minutes then off heat and serve
