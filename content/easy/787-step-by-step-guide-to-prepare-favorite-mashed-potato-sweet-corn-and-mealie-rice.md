##### The ingredients needed to cook Mashed potato,sweet corn and mealie rice:

1. Use 5 potatoes(peel and cut into dices)
1. Get 250 g green beans,(cut into small strips)
1. Provide 1 teaspoon aromat
1. Get 100 g butter
1. Use 4 sweetcorn on the cob
1. Take 300 g rice


##### Steps to make Mashed potato,sweet corn and mealie rice:

1. Boil potatoes and green beans until soft then drain water and add margarine and aromat
1. Boil sweetcorn for 45 minutes then remove from heat, rub with margarine then oven grill for 15 minutes
1. Rinse rice and boil till it&#39;s soft
1. Served with fried hake and relish
