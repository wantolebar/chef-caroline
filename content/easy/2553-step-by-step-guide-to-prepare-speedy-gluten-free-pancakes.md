##### The ingredients needed to prepare Gluten-free Pancakes:

1. You need 1/2 cup coconut flour
1. Take 1 ripened banana
1. Provide 1/2 tbsp baking powder
1. Take 1/2 tbsp vanilla essence
1. Use 1-2 spoon dried berries optional
1. Provide 1/2 cup milk
1. Use 1 pinch salt


##### Instructions to make Gluten-free Pancakes:

1. Mix all the ingredients and make a pancake batter
1. Heat the pan on medium flame and coat it with lil bit of oil or butter.
1. Add the 1 scoop of batter in a pan, cook until edges starts to brown and flip. Cook it for 1- 2 minutes.
1. Voilla!!! pancakes are ready, top them with honey and fruits of your choices.
