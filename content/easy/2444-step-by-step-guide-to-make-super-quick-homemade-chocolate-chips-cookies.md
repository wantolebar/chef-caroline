##### The ingredients needed to cook 🍭🍩Chocolate Chips Cookies 🍩🍭:

1. Use 125 gram soft Unsalted butter(room temp)
1. Provide 1/2 cup white sugar
1. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1. You need 1 egg (lightly beaten)
1. Use 1 &amp; 3/4 cups of self-raising flour
1. Get 250 grams milk chocolate chips


##### Steps to make 🍭🍩Chocolate Chips Cookies 🍩🍭:

1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees. Cream butter until smooth add sugars, and beat until smooth. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Into a large bowl, Slowly add the flour into wet mixture. Fold in chocolate chips.
1. Drop 2 to 3 tablespoons dough per cookie onto ungreased baking sheets; space dough at least 2 inches apart to allow for spreading. Bake until golden, 8 to 10 minutes. Remove cookies from baking sheets, and allow to cool on baking racks.
