##### The ingredients needed to make Pandan Pancake:

1. Provide 2 eggs
1. Prepare 50 gr sugar
1. Use 100 gr all-purpose flour
1. You need 10 gr milk powder
1. Take pinch salt
1. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
1. Provide 50 ml milk
1. Provide  Pandan juice / pandan paste
1. Provide  (If you wish to make a chocolate one, add 10gr cocoa powder &amp; reduce 10gr flour)
1. Provide  Bit of butter (~3gr, melted)
1. Provide  Vanilla (optional)
1. Take  Filling
1. Get  Chocolate spread / any jams / etc


##### Steps to make Pandan Pancake:

1. Whisk eggs and sugar until well combined then add dry ingredients
1. Pour milk (i combined milk and pandan juice before pouring it) or simply add drops of pandan paste
1. Cook pancake and fill as you wish
1. Fold and enjoy!
