1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. You need 2 large eggs

1. Get 1 cup or to taste fresh blueberrys..you can use frozen but I&#39;d let them thaw a bit before using

1. In a separate dish mix eggs  milk  and butter
1. Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients and fold in your blueberrys
