- "Mix the vermicelli evenly covered with seasoning, keep stirring it for 6-7 minutes. Tambahkan seledri dan daun bawang, aduk perlahan selama 2 menit, dan bihun goreng udang siap."

##### The ingredients needed to cook Shrimp Fried Vermicelli/Bihun Goreng Udang:

1. Use 1 packet vermicelli (450 gr)
1. You need 200 gr shrimp
1. Use 1 onion (big size)
1. Use 4 cloves garlic
1. Get 1 carrot
1. You need 1/4 cabbage from whole
1. Take 4-5 stems celery leaves
1. Prepare 4-5 stalks spring onions
1. Prepare 5 tbsp oil
1. Provide 1.5 tsp salt
1. Get 1.5 tsp black pepper (you can put less if doesn&#39;t like spicy)
1. Use 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce
1. You need 4-5 tbsp chili sauce/siracha (depends on how spicy you want)

Biarkan dan tiriskan.
