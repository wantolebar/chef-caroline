##### Instructions to make Rajgira fruit tart:

1. Mix rajgira atta jaggery and ghee,make a dough (when we bind the dough it easily bind) - Dough keep in fridge for 1hrs - Preheat the oven at 160C for 10minutes.
1. Grease the tart mould with ghee and then press the dough properly at tart mould - Bake at 160C for 25 minutes  - Then keep the tart at room temperature till it will be cold
1. Demould the tart
1. Fruit raita: mix all the fruits and salt in curd.
1. Assembly: fill the tart with fruit raita
