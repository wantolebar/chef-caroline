##### The ingredients needed to make For Breakfast and Sweets Easy Kabocha Cake with Pancake Mix:

1. You need 150 grams Pancake mix
1. Get 150 grams with skin  Kabocha squash
1. Take 50 ml Soy milk or milk
1. Prepare 2 Eggs
1. Provide 80 grams Butter
1. Provide 80 grams Sugar
1. Take 1 dash Vanilla oil


##### Instructions to make For Breakfast and Sweets Easy Kabocha Cake with Pancake Mix:

1. Slice the kabocha with skin on. Put into a heatproof container, cover with a plastic wrap and microwave for 4-5 minutes at 600W.
1. Mash with a folk, add soy milk to mix.
1. Put butter into a deep heatproof container without covering. Heat for 30 seconds or more in microwave. Preheat the oven at 340°F/170°C.
1. Beat the egg into a bowl. Add sugar to mix. Then add butter, kabocha paste and vanilla oil, mixing after each addition.
1. Sieve the pancake mix and mix quickly. Stop mixing when it&#39;s no floury texture.
1. Use a spatula and mix the batter roughly until smooth.
1. Pour the batter into each muffin cup with a spoon, fill to about 70%. Bake for 20-30 minutes.
1. It&#39;s done if the muffins have browned like the photo and nothing comes out with a skewer when poked through. Let it cool in cooling rack.
1. When cooled, keep the muffins in a plastic bag. It&#39;ll become moist and soft.
1. A cross section. It&#39;s fluffy!
1. Friends will love this kabocha cake as gift. You can use rum instead of vanilla oil for adults.
1. Serve with vanilla ice cream for dessert.
