##### The ingredients needed to prepare Simple Sticky Rice Flour Crepe:

1. Prepare 100 grams Shiratamako
1. Prepare 150 ml Milk
1. Use 1 Egg
1. Take 1 Margarine (butter also OK)
1. Take 1 Pancake syrup
1. Provide 1 Powdered sugar


##### Instructions to make Simple Sticky Rice Flour Crepe:

1. Add the milk to the shiratamako a little at a time and expand. Next, add the egg and mix well.
1. Apply a thin coating of oil to a frying pan and pour in the batter in a thin rounded shape. Cook both sides.
1. Transfer your cooked crepes to a plate and top with margarine and syrup. Create layers of crepe -&gt; margarine -&gt; syrup and repeat.
1. Dust with sifted powdered sugar and it&#39;s complete!
