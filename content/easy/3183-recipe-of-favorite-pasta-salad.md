##### The ingredients needed to make Pasta salad:

1. Use 3 green onions
1. Take 1/2 green bell pepper
1. You need 1/2 cup sliced black olive
1. You need 1/2 cup sliced green olive
1. Take 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1. You need 1 envelope a good seasons Italian salad dressing mix
1. You need 1/4 cup canola oil
1. Use 3 tbsp of red wine vinegar
1. You need 1 box of bow tie pasta
1. Get 1/4 cup of McCormicks salad supreme


##### Steps to make Pasta salad:

1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and rinse in cold water set aside
1. Chop onion and bell pepper into small pieces.  Place in mixing bowl add black and green olives, oil, vinegar and good seasons envelope. Mix well.
1. Add pasta and mix until pasta is coated with vegetables and seasonings.
1. Once pasta is well coated add the salad supreme and grated Parmesan cheese and lightly mix.  Refrigerate and serve once cold.
