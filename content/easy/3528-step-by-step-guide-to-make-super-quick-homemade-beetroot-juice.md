##### The ingredients needed to cook Beetroot juice:

1. Provide 1 small red Beetroot
1. Provide 1 large Apple
1. Take 1-2 Carrots
1. Provide 1/2 Lemon or Lime
1. Get 1/2 inch Ginger, peeled


##### Steps to make Beetroot juice:

1. Wash all vegetables and fruits in running water and pat dry them.
1. Peel and cut beetroot into long slices. Cut apple into long pieces and remove the core. Peel and cut carrot into long pieces.
1. Put glass or container under the nozzle of a juicer and turn it on.
1. Process all ingredients (beetroot, apple, carrot, ginger) except lemon alternatively through juicer.
1. Squeeze the half lemon into prepared juice and stir well. Pour it into chilled serving glass and serve. Drink it immediately.
