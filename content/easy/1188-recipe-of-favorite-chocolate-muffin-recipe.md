##### The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate Muffin Recipe:

1. You need 85 grams Cake flour
1. Get 15 grams Cocoa powder
1. Take 2 grams Baking powder
1. Use 65 grams Butter
1. Take 60 grams Granulated sugar
1. Provide 1 Egg (medium)
1. You need 50 grams Heavy cream
1. Use 30 grams Caramel chocolate chips
1. Get 1 Almond slices


##### Steps to make Chocolate Muffin Recipe:

1. Sift the cake flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder together. Warm the heavy cream.
1. Add the softened butter and granulated sugar into a bowl, and mix well. Preheat the oven to 170℃.
1. Add the beaten egg to Step 2 little by little, and mix (until the mixture becomes fluffy and creamy).
1. Alternately add the sifted dry ingredients and heavy cream to Step 3 in 3 batches, and fold in.
1. Add 30g  of your preferred chocolate chips to Step 4, and briefly mix. Scoop the batter into the mold with a spoon.
1. Put almond slices, and chocolate chips (not listed) on top, then bake in the oven preheated to 170℃ for 25 ~ 30 minutes.
1. This is one I baked in a muffin cup. I used 5 g of black cocoa powder, and 10 g of cocoa powder.
