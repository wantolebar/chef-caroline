##### The ingredients needed to cook Halloumi sandwich:

1. Take 125 gm halloumi cheese
1. Provide 30 gm arugula leaves
1. You need 80 gm fresh tomato
1. Provide 1 pc panini bread
1. You need 330 gm sundried tomato pesto
1. You need 80 gm mixed salad or french fries


##### Instructions to make Halloumi sandwich:

1. Grill the halloumi cheese then cut the panini bread, toast under salamander then and sundried tomato pesto and the halloumi cheese.
1. Finish it in the panini machine and serve it with mixed green salad
