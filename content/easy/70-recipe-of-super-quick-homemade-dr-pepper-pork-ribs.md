##### The ingredients needed to prepare Dr pepper pork ribs:

1. Provide 1 full rack of pork ribs
1. Prepare 1/2 cup bbq rub
1. Take 1 can dr pepper
1. Use  Bbq sauce


##### Instructions to make Dr pepper pork ribs:

1. Wash ribs in sink and pat dry with paper towels
1. Sprinkle ribs liberally with rub on both sides and place in a deep foil lined pan
1. Pour one can of dr pepper on the ribs and top the ribs with one more sprinkle of rub.
1. Bake in the oven at 350° for 30 min
1. Remove from the oven and place on the grill.
1. Brush on bbq sauce and cook for 12 min on medium heat. Flip after 6 min. Bbq both sides and again at the end.
