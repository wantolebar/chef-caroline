##### The ingredients needed to make Pulled pork:

1. Take  pork tenderloin
1. Use  garlic cloves
1. Use  onions
1. Prepare  bbq sauce
1. Use  Smoked paprika powder


##### Steps to make Pulled pork:

1. Place your pork tenderloin in an oven proof pot.
1. Grate your garlic cloves and rub it into the pork tenderloin.
1. Rub smoked paprika powder into the pork tenderloin.
1. Dice the onions and place them in the pot with the pork tenderloin.
1. Cover with bbq sauce. Put on the lid and make sure it is closed.
1. Cook in the oven at 125°F celsius/255F for 5,5 hours.
1. Take out your pot and remove the lid. Using to forks, pull the meat apart.
1. Serve with tortillas, vegetables and a sauce of your chosing.
