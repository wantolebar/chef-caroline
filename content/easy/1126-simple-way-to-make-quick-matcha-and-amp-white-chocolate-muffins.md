##### The ingredients needed to cook Matcha &amp; White Chocolate Muffins:

1. Get 2 cups Self-Raising Flour
1. Take 1/2 cup Caster Sugar
1. You need 1/4 teaspoon Salt
1. Prepare 1-2 tablespoons Matcha Powder *Today I added 2 tablespoons
1. Get 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil
1. Provide 1 cup Milk
1. Get 1 Egg *lightly whisked
1. Provide 100 g White Chocolate *coarsely chopped


##### Steps to make Matcha &amp; White Chocolate Muffins:

1. Preheat oven to 180℃. Line a 12-hole muffin pan with cases.
1. Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix to combine. Spoon the mixture into each case. Bake for 20 minutes or until cooked through. Cool on a wire rack.
