##### The ingredients needed to make Apple cider Vinegar and lemon tea:

1. Use 250 ml Water
1. Prepare 4 Ice cubes
1. Get 2 tbsp Apple cider vinegar
1. Take 1/2 A Lemon


##### Instructions to make Apple cider Vinegar and lemon tea:

1. In clean glass place ice cubes
1. Pour water in to glass
1. Squeeze in lemon
1. Add the vinegar and stir it
1. If the taste is to strong for you, you can add 1 tablespoon of honey and a dash of cinnamon before you put the ice cubes in :)
