##### The ingredients needed to make Rice Bowl:

1. Use 2 cup Cooked Brown Rice
1. Take 1 tsp Red Wine Vinegar
1. Take  Salt
1. Use 3 tbsp Olive Oil
1. Get 2 large Eggs
1. Get 1/2 Avocado
1. Provide 1 Hot Sauce
1. Use 1 Green onion/Scallions
1. Take  Ground Black Pepper


##### Instructions to make Rice Bowl:

1. Mix onions, cooked rice and vinegar in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper to taste
1. Cook eggs in Olive Oil on both sides
1. Top rice bowls with egg, avocado and hot sauce
