##### The ingredients needed to prepare Stuffed Pepper Soup:

1. Use  Italian sausage, casting removed
1. Take  onion, chopped
1. Get  bell peppers, chopped
1. Get  pablano pepper, chopped
1. Prepare  garlic cloves, chopped
1. Provide  fire roasted tomatoes
1. You need  tomato sauce
1. Use  chicken broth
1. Get  brown rice
1. Provide  worcestershire sauce


##### Instructions to make Stuffed Pepper Soup:

1. In large dutch oven brown sausage on high heat.
1. Drain fat, then add onions, garlic, and peppers.
1. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Add tomatoes, sauce, worcestershire, 2 cups of the chicken broth, and season with salt and pepper.
1. Cover and simmer on low heat for 30 minutes.
1. In separate pot cook rice with remaining chicken stock according to directions.
1. Add rice to soup and serve.
