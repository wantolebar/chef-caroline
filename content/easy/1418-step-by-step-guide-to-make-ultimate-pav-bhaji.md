##### The ingredients needed to prepare Pav bhaji:

1. You need  fresh peas
1. You need  potatoes
1. Use  tomatoes
1. Prepare  onions
1. Prepare  garlic
1. Take  ginger chilli paste
1. Use  Butter
1. Prepare  Oil
1. Take  Pav bhaji masala
1. Use  Kashmiri red chilli powder
1. Get  coriander powder


##### Steps to make Pav bhaji:

1. Boil potatoes, peas in the pressure cooker for 5 whistles and let it cool. Then in another pan cut onion, tomato and grind it in a mixer jar to make a puree of it.
1. Now chop garlic, then heat a pan and add oil then add butter, add chopped garlic and ginger chilli paste then stir it, after that add onion paste, stir it until it&#39;s raw smell goes then add chopped capsicums, stir it add Pav bhaji masala, red chilli powder, garam masala then add tomato puree saute them all, until the oil leaves the pan.
1. Now open the lid and mash potatoes, peas then add tomato gravy and mix well, let it cook for 5 mins then add salt, dhaniya powder and mix it.
1. Now heat another pan add butter then add the 2nd tadka, add garlic and red chilli powder, pav bhaji masala and pour into bhaji with Kashmiri red chilli powder.
1. Its is ready to serve.
