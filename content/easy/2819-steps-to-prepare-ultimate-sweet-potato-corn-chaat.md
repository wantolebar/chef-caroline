##### Steps to make Sweet potato corn chaat:

1. To make the nutritious sweet potato and corn chaat, first boil the sweet potatoes and sweet corn separately and cool. Peel the sweet potatoes and cut into medium cubes.
1. Finely chop the onions, tomatoes green chilli and the coriander leaves.
1. Add all mentioned ingredients for chaat in a mixing bowl and mix well. Check seasoning and add if more is required.
1. Serve this nutritious and delicious chaat garnished with sev, roasted puffed barley and some crushed khakraa.for breakfast, evening snack or a light dinner with soup!
