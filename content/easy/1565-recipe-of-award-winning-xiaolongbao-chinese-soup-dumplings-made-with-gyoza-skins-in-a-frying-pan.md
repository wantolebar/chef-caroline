##### The ingredients needed to prepare Xiaolongbao (Chinese Soup Dumplings) Made with Gyoza Skins in a Frying Pan:

1. Get  Filling:
1. You need 100 grams Ground pork
1. You need 1/8 Japanese leek (finely chopped)
1. Get 1 tsp Garlic (grated)
1. You need 1 tsp Ginger (grated)
1. Get 1 tbsp Soy sauce
1. Provide 1 tbsp Cooking sake (Shaoxing wine)
1. Provide 1 tsp Sesame oil
1. You need 1 tbsp Katakuriko
1. Provide  Soup
1. You need 60 ml Water
1. Get 3 grams Gelatin
1. Provide 1 tbsp Chicken soup stock granules
1. Get 20 Gyoza skins
1. Take 1 Chinese cabbage


##### Instructions to make Xiaolongbao (Chinese Soup Dumplings) Made with Gyoza Skins in a Frying Pan:

1. Make the soup: Boil some water in a pot.  Add the chicken soup stock granules and gelatin, then mix.  Transfer to a bowl and chill in the refrigerator.
1. When it gets thick, break it up with a fork.  Chill again in the refrigerator.
1. Make the filling:  In a bowl, add all the ingredients to make the filing and knead well until it becomes sticky.
1. Add the mixture from Step 2 and quickly mix together.
1. Wrap the filling with gyoza skin.  Place a small portion of filling into the middle of each skin.  Bring the edges to the top and pinch the edges in a pleated fashion.
1. Choose a plate that can fit inside your frying pan when you&#39;re holding it with both hands. Line the plate with leaves from a Chinese cabbage and place the dumplings on top of it. *Make sure you leave some space between the dumplings.
1. Line the bottom of your frying pan with parchment paper, add some water and put the small plate over it. Place the plate with the dumplings on top. Cover with a lid, steam-fry for 5 minutes and serve.
