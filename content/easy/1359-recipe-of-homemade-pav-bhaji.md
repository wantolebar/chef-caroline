1. You need  Pav bhaji masala(homemade)
1. Use  Butter oil


##### Steps to make Pav bhaji:

1. Boiled in cooker all the vegetables for three whistles and at that make a rough paste of crushed tomato and onion.
1. Then in a pan, put three big spoons of oil and add mustard, jeera, bay leaf, curry leaves.Tadka is ready.
1. Then mix the paste of onion and tomatoes with all the masalas and then cook it for five minutes and then mix mashed vegetables and cook it for sometimes and again heat oil and add half spoon of kashmiri red chilli powder and then cover it with a lid after adding pav bhaji masala.
1. In a non-stick pan, roasted the pav with butter and serve it with salad,(if you like you can serve with buttermilk and roasted papad).
