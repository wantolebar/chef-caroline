##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Butter icing:

1. Take 1 simas pack
1. Prepare 1 pack icing sugar
1. Provide  Food colour (pink)
1. Provide  Flavour of your choice
1. Provide  Sprinkles
1. Use  Milk optional


##### Steps to make Simple Butter icing:

1. Sieve the icing sugar
1. In a mixer add in the icing sugar and butter and give it a good mix til cream and fluffy
1. Add in your flavour and food colour. mix it very well
1. Pour it in a pipping bag and decorate your cup cake the way you want and enjoy.
