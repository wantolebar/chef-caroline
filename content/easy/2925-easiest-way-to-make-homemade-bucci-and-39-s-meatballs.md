##### The ingredients needed to prepare Bucci&#39;s Meatballs:

1. Take 1 lb ground beef
1. Get 1/2 lb Hot sausage, removed from casing
1. Get 2 eggs
1. You need 1 cup bread crumbs
1. Provide 2 tsp minced garlic
1. Prepare 1 tbsp minced onion
1. Prepare 33/100 cup chopped fresh parsley
1. Use 1/2 cup grated parmesan
1. Provide 2 tbsp Chopped sundried tomatoes
1. Use 1 tsp salt
1. Take 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1. Get 2 tbsp olive oil


##### Steps to make Bucci&#39;s Meatballs:

1. preheat oven to 450°F
1. in a large bowl, combine all ingredients, except oil. mix, knead, and squeeze until very well blended
1. wet hands in water. roll the mixture into 1 1/2&#34; meat balls
1. oil a shallow roasting pan and brown the balls in the oven, about 20 minutes
