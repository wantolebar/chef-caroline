##### The ingredients needed to prepare Mexican Bean Dip:

1. You need 1 can refried beans (16 oz)
1. You need 1 can or jar of jalapeño cheddar dip (9 or 15 oz)
1. Use 1 cup mayonnaise
1. Get 1 cup sour cream
1. Prepare 1 package taco seasoning
1. Get 1 bag shredded cheese (Mexican blend)
1. Use 1 bag tortilla chips


##### Instructions to make Mexican Bean Dip:

1. In a 8x10 inch dish, layer the refried beans evenly and then layer the cheese dip evenly.
1. In a separate bowl, blend the mayonnaise, sour cream, and taco seasoning to form the 3rd layer. Then layer the blended ingredients evenly on top of the beans and dip.
1. Top the dip with the bag of cheese evenly. Refrigerate the dip and serve with chips.
