##### The ingredients needed to prepare Shrimp, Edamame, and Corn Tempura Fritters:

1. Get 1 Vegetable oil
1. Prepare 100 grams Peeled shrimp
1. Use 50 grams Edamame or fava beans
1. Use 40 grams Corn kernels
1. Prepare 50 grams Tempura flour
1. Use 75 ml Water


##### Instructions to make Shrimp, Edamame, and Corn Tempura Fritters:

1. Prep the peeled shrimp, edamame, and corn.
1. Heat the frying oil. Mix up the tempura flour following the package instructions. Add the shrimp, edamame and corn, and deep fry tablespoons of the batter.
