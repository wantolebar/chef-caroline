##### The ingredients needed to prepare Tabouli Salad:

1. You need  parsley finely chopped
1. Take  Roma tomatoes finely chopped
1. Get  fresh mint finely chopped
1. Get  garlic cloves, minced (optional)
1. Use  extra fine bulgar
1. Prepare  Some flaxseed (optional)
1. Provide  Onions (1 white and 3 green onions very finely chopped
1. Take  cucumber finely chopped
1. Provide  Small to medium heads of radish finely chopped
1. Take  lemons juice
1. Get  extra virgin olive oil
1. Get  black pepper and cinnamon
1. Get  Romain lettuce leaves washed and dried to serve/decorate with
1. You need  Salt to your taste
1. Use  voilà, bon appétit


##### Steps to make Tabouli Salad:

1. Wash the bulgur wheat and soak it in water for 5-7 minutes till it drain well.
1. Chop all the vegetables, herbs, green onions and tomatoes draining excess juice if needed.
1. Place all in a large bowl and toss or mix all gently; parsley, mint, tomatoes, lemon juice, garlic, radish, peppers, cinamon, salt and at the end add the olive oil and flaxseed (optional)
1. Taste and adjust seasonings as you like while mixing; serve with lettuce leaves.
