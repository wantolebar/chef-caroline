##### The ingredients needed to make Bak Choy And Dumpling In Pork Broth:

1. Provide 4 cup bak choy
1. Provide 1 liter water
1. Prepare 1/2 cup spring onion
1. Provide 20 slice papper thin pork lean  belly
1. Provide 8 slice dumpling  skin
1. Provide  seasoning
1. Use 1/2 dash black pepper
1. Get 1/2 tsp light soy sauce
1. Take 1/2 tsp salt


##### Steps to make Bak Choy And Dumpling In Pork Broth:

1. Bring water to a boil then add thinly sliced pork for 3 minute then simmer and add bak choy
1. simmer down and add seasoning then add dumpling skin (DONT STIR ) as dumpling skin breaks easily
1. simmer for another 3 minutes then add spring onion and off heat
