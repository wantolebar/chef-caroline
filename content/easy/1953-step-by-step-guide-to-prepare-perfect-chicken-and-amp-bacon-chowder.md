##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken &amp; Bacon Chowder:

1. Use 1 lb bacon, chopped
1. Get 2 med shallots, finely chopped
1. You need 1/2 tsp chili powder
1. You need 3/4 tsp salt, divided
1. You need 1 quart chicken broth
1. Prepare 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
1. Prepare 1 lb red potatoes, cut into 1/2 inch chunks
1. Use 2 cup whole milk
1. Get 1 cup fresh corn
1. Take 1 thinly sliced basil for garnish
1. Use 1 oyster crackers, for serving


##### Steps to make Chicken &amp; Bacon Chowder:

1. In a 6 quart sauce pot, cook bacon on med until crispy and brown. Remove bacon and set aside. Save about 3-4 tablespoons of the bacon in the pot.
1. To the pot add your shallots, chili powder and 1/4 of salt. Cook until shallots are tender 5-8 minutes.
1. Slowly stir in broth. Heat to a simmer on high.
1. Add chicken, 1/2 of the bacon and potatoes. Reduce heat to med.
1. Cook 12-15 minutes or until chicken is done. Remove chicken, shred and return to your pot.
1. Stir in milk, corn and 1/2 salt (more If desired). Cook as additional 3-5 minutes until corn is hot.
1. Garnish with basil and rest of bacon. Serve with oyster crackers if desired.
