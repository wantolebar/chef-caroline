##### The ingredients needed to cook 10 Minute Sugar-Cinnamon Bread:

1. Use 6 Bread Slice
1. Prepare 4 tbsp Melted butter
1. Use 1 Cinnamon
1. You need 1 Sugar
1. You need 1 OPTIONAL  fruit or spread


##### Instructions to make 10 Minute Sugar-Cinnamon Bread:

1. Preheat oven to 350°            370° for those in a higher elevation.
1. Lay as many slices of bread as you want on on a pan side to side.
1. Melt butter for 20 seconds, and brush evenly on one side of the bread.
1. Sprinkle even mix of sugar and cinnamon onto the buttered bread.
1. Place in oven for 5 minutes, 7 for those who like a cruchy bite.
1. Once removed from the oven, you may want to put on a bit of Chocolate syrup, or even your favorite fruit!
