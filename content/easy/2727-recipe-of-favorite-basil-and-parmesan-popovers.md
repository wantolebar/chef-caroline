##### The ingredients needed to prepare Basil and Parmesan Popovers:

1. Get 1 cup all purpose flour
1. Use 7 oz Whole milk
1. Get 1/2 tsp salt
1. Prepare 2 eggs
1. Take 1 Tblsp dry basil
1. Provide 1 Tblsp Parmesan Cheese
1. Get 1/2 tsp melted butter


##### Instructions to make Basil and Parmesan Popovers:

1. In a blender add milk butter eggs, flour and butter mix till blended.
1. Butter or grease pop over pan or muffin pan, pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
1. Pour batter in pan sprinkle basil and cheese on top,set aside for 10 minutes. When oven is ready put pan on middle rack and bake for 45 mins till they pop. Make sure inside is hollow, Serve immediately you dpread butter or jam
