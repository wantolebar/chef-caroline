##### The ingredients needed to cook Roasted Fingerling Potatoes:

1. Get 1 1/2 lb fingerling potatoes
1. Use 1 tsp tablespoon dried rosemary
1. Provide 14 oz chicken broth
1. Provide 1 tbsp butter
1. Provide 1 tsp salt
1. Provide 1 tbsp olive oil


##### Steps to make Roasted Fingerling Potatoes:

1. Wash potatoes and place in large skillet.
1. Add rosemary, broth, oil,  butter and salt.
1. Bring to a boil over high heat.
1. Reduce to medium heat and boil until potatoes are fork tender, about 20 minutes, covered but leaving lid aside to let some steam escape.
1. Press down on potatoes with fork or potato masher until slightly cracked.
1. Return to medium heat and cook uncovered until remaining liquid evaporates and potatoes brown on bottom. (About 10 minutes).
1. Turn potatoes and brown on other side for about 4-5 minutes.
