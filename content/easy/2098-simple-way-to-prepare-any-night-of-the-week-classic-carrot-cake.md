##### The ingredients needed to prepare classic carrot cake:

1. Prepare 2 cup flour
1. You need 1 cup sugar
1. You need 1 cup brown sugar
1. Use 2 tsp baking soda
1. Provide 2 tsp cinnamon
1. Get 1/4 tsp salt
1. Use 4 eggs
1. Provide 1 cup vegetable oil
1. Take 2 cup shredded carrots
1. Take 3/4 cup chopped walnuts


##### Instructions to make classic carrot cake:

1. preheat oven to 350°F.
1. drain pineapple reseveing 2 tbsps of the juice. you may discard the rest or save for another use
1. in a large bowl combine all dry ingredients.
1. add eggs, oil, pineapple, carrots and reserved juice blend with a mixer on med speed until combined.
1. stir in walnuts
1. transfer to a greased and floured 13x9 baking dish or to 9 inch baking pans bake 35 to 40 minuted
1. cool on wire racks leave in pan 10 minutes the remove if using the round pans
1. I top this cake off with cream cheese frosting and then press more chopped walnut on the top.
