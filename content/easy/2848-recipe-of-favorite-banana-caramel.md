##### The ingredients needed to make Banana Caramel:

1. Get 3-4 Ripe bananas
1. Get as per taste Powered sugar
1. You need 2-3 tbs water


##### Steps to make Banana Caramel:

1. Roughly chop bananas
1. Add banana and powdered sugar in a pan and cook on medium flame till bananas are soft
1. After bananas are soft add 2 to 3 tbs of water and mash it using spatula.
1. Cook till it changes colour and are soft and mushy.
1. Let it cool down and then grind it in mixture jar and store in a jar in fridge.
