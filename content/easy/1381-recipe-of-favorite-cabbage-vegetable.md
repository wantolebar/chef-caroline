##### The ingredients needed to cook Cabbage vegetable:

1. You need 250 grams cabbage
1. Use 4-5 curry leaves
1. Use 1 green chilli powder
1. Use 1/4 spoon cumin seeds
1. Provide 1/4 spoon turmeric powder
1. You need as required chopped coriander leaves


##### Steps to make Cabbage vegetable:

1. Heat oil in a pan or kado add cumin seeds and saute.
1. Then add green chili, curry leaves 10 add shredded cabbage, turmeric powder, salt lemon juice and saute for 2 minutes more or till the cabbage likely softened add coriander leaves and serve.
