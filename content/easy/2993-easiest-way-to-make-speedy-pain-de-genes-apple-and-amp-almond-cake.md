##### The ingredients needed to prepare Pain de Genes - Apple &amp; Almond Cake:

1. Get 4-5 apple
1. Provide 20 g butter (for the apples)
1. Get 20 g granulated sugar
1. Take 100 g almond powder
1. Get 150 g sugar
1. Get 3 eggs
1. Use 50 g plain flour
1. Use 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1. You need 130 g unsalted butter


##### Instructions to make Pain de Genes - Apple &amp; Almond Cake:

1. Heat the oven to 180°C
1. Peel, core the apples and cut into 12s
1. Heat the butter in a pan and sauté the apples till mostly cooked(5-6min). Add granulated sugar and lightly caramelize the apples.
