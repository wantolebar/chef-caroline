##### The ingredients needed to cook Bitter Gourd Omelette:

1. You need 3 eggs
1. Get 2 small red chili
1. Get 1 tbsp oil
1. Provide 2 cup bitter gourd
1. You need 1 dash turmeric powder and Chili powder
1. Get  season
1. Use 1 dash salt and pepper


##### Steps to make Bitter Gourd Omelette:

1. Slice bitter gourd and remove the seed
1. In pan with oil layer bitter gourd for a minutes then turn over
1. Add in the beaten eggs and let it set for half a minute then add seasoning and cover for a minute then off heat and enjoy
1. Slice up and enjoy
