##### The ingredients needed to make Tater Tot Pulled Pork Casserole:

1. Use 16 oz Bag of frozen Tater Tots
1. You need 1 lb Pre-cooked Pulled Pork (no sauce)
1. Take  Shredded Cheese
1. You need  Season salt


##### Instructions to make Tater Tot Pulled Pork Casserole:

1. Preheat oven to 425° and Lightly oil or spray casserole dish
1. Spread Tater Tots one layer deep in the casserole dish and sprinkle Season Salt, to your discretion, over Tots
1. Break Pulled Pork apart and lay over Tots
1. Sprinkle Cheese over the top (I used a mix of Extra Sharp Cheddar and Sharp White Cheddar)
1. Cook for 30mins
1. Serve with array of BBQ sauces and enjoy!
