##### The ingredients needed to cook Lose Weight the Healthy Way! Vegetable Smoothies:

1. Prepare 1 Banana
1. You need 1/4 Apple
1. Take 1 Lemon juice
1. Use 1 Mandarine or other type of orange
1. Take 1 to 3 plants or clusters Spinach (or komatsuna greens)
1. You need 100 ml Water


##### Steps to make Lose Weight the Healthy Way! Vegetable Smoothies:

1. Peel and core the apple and slice 1 cm thick. Slice the banana 1 cm thick too. Sprinkle with lemon juice, arrange in a single layer in a plastic bag, seal and freeze.
1. Divide the mandarine orange into segments and cut up the spinach roughly. Put them with the frozen fruit and water in a blender, blend until smooth, and it&#39;s done.
1. If you are a vegetable juice novice, try using milk or yogurt instead of the water, or add 1-2 teaspoons of honey, to make the smoothie easier to drink.
