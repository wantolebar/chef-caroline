##### Instructions to make Hungarian Mushroom Soup:

1. Sauté diced onion and garlic in butter for five minutes.
1. Add sliced mushrooms. Sauté on medium heat for ten minutes,  adding the dill weed and paprika in the last 7 minutes.
1. Add flour. Cook for five minutes.
1. Add broth. Then milk. Cook for five minutes. Add sour cream. Cook 3-5 minutes more.
1. Serve with crisp sliced turkey bacon. - Enjoy!
