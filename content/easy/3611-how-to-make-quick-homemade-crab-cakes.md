##### The ingredients needed to prepare Homemade crab cakes:

1. Take 16 oz crab meat
1. Provide 2 eggs
1. Get 2 tbsp mustard
1. Prepare 1/4 cup mayonnaise
1. You need 1/2 tsp salt
1. Provide 1/2 tsp pepper
1. Prepare 1/2 loaf bread
1. Get 1 pinch garlic


##### Steps to make Homemade crab cakes:

1. Preheat oven to 375
1. In a bowl break crab meat down to smaller pieces
1. Add in eggs, mustard, mayonnaise, salt, pepper, garlic, mix (i use my hands)
1. Break down bread to small pieces (i have used both white and wheat bread) add to crab mixture.
1. Shape into ball&#39;s in your hand (whatever size you like)
1. Put in oven for about 30 min or until they start to brown.
