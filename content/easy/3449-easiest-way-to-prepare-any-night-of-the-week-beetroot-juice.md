1. You need  For topping:
1. Get leaves As required Fresh mint
1. Prepare slices Few lemon


##### Instructions to make Beetroot juice:

1. Peel the beetroots and grind in mix grinder.
1. Now take one bowl. Take out beet crush in it.
1. Add sugar, chat masala and lemon juice in it and stir it till sugar desolve.
1. Now take a sieve to pour beet juice and drain the juice in glasses. The juice is ready to serve. You can top it with fresh mint leaves and a lemon slice.
