##### The ingredients needed to make Pazham Pori ripe banana fritters:

1. You need 1 cup idli batter
1. You need 3 ripe bananas
1. You need to taste Salt
1. Use 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1. You need 1 tsp brown sugar
1. Provide 1-1/2 cup oil for deep frying
1. Provide 1 tsp dessicated coconut for garnish


##### Instructions to make Pazham Pori ripe banana fritters:

1. Slice bananas length wise.
1. Add salt, brown sugar and turmeric to the idli batter and mix well.
1. Dip banana slices in the batter.
1. Evenly coat bananas in the batter.
1. Heat oil and fry the bananas one by one.
1. Until they are golden.
1. Sprinkle dessicated coconut or icing sugar and serve immediately
