##### The ingredients needed to make Keto strawberry cheesecake smoothie:

1. Provide 2 oz cream cheese (full fat)
1. Prepare 4 medium-sized strawberries frozen
1. You need 2 tbsp heavy cream
1. You need 1 tbsp sweetener
1. Use 1 cup cold water


##### Instructions to make Keto strawberry cheesecake smoothie:

1. Put all ingredients into blender and blend until everything has been combined and smooth.
1. If you find you smoothie too rummy, add some ice cubes to make it thicker.
