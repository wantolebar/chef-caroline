##### The ingredients needed to prepare Short crust fruit tart pastry:

1. Prepare  refined flour
1. Use  Sugar

##### Steps to make Short crust fruit tart pastry:

1. Put the flour into the bowl and add butter and pinch of salt and sugar
1. Mix the mixture together
1. Add a 1 tsp of milk and mix it well.with the creaming method.
1. Add mixture of egg
1. Make a soft dough and cool down for 30 minutes
1. Make a sheet of a dough and cut it and place it in molds
1. After that bake it for 30 minutes
1. After the baking cool it down and decorate with whipped cream and fresh fruites like pinnapple and strawberries
