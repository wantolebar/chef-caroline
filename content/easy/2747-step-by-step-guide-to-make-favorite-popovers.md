##### The ingredients needed to prepare Popovers:

1. You need 1 1/4 cup All purpose flour
1. Provide 1 1/4 cup Cold milk
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Provide 3 large Eggs


##### Instructions to make Popovers:

1. Preheat oven to 425.
1. Beat all ingredients until well incorporated.
1. Pour into a greased popover pan. Filling each cup 2/3 full.
1. Bake at 425 for the first 15-20 minutes. Until they have completely risen in the center.
1. Reduce heat to 350 and bake another 15-20 minutes until dark golden brown.
1. Serve piping hot! Great with whipped orange honey butter (recipe in my profile).
