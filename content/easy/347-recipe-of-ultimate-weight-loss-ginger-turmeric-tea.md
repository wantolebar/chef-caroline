##### Steps to make Weight loss Ginger turmeric tea:

1. First in a pot take water,now add ginger pieces and lemon slices.
1. Then add 1 tsp turmeric powder. Let it boil.
1. Now take black pepper corns and crush it.Add crushed black pepper into ginger water.when water starts boiling.Turn off the burner. Let it cool for 2 minutes. Tea is ready.
1. Now sieve this tea into cup and add honey.Mix well.Serve hot.
