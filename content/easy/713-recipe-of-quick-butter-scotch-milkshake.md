##### The ingredients needed to cook Butter scotch milkshake:

1. Take  Caramalise
1. Use  For caramalise: sugar
1. Take  Gher
1. Get  Almonds or walnuts


##### Instructions to make Butter scotch milkshake:

1. For caramelized: In a pan, add ghee, walnuts and almonds. Saute it well and keep it aside in another bowl.
1. Melt sugar on slow flame. Add walnuts and almonds. Stir for few seconds untill rich brown colour.
1. Spread mixture on greased plate. When cool, crush into small pieces.
1. Now add milk, ice cream, peeled apple pieces, caramelized nuts in a mixer jar and churn it well till it becomes smooth. Garnish with chopped almonds and enjoy the milkshake.
