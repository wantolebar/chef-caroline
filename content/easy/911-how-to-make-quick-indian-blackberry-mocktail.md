##### The ingredients needed to cook Indian Blackberry Mocktail:

1. You need 6-7 Indian Blackberry, Kala Jamun
1. Take 2 ice cubes
1. Use 1/8 tsp black salt
1. You need 1/8 tsp chat masala
1. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
1. Use 1/2 cup soda water
1. Provide 2-3 mint leaves
1. You need 1/2 lemon juice


##### Steps to make Indian Blackberry Mocktail:

1. First remove seeds of java Plum, chop it.
1. Now take a mixar jar and add chopped java plum, ice cubes, chat masala, black salt, lemon juice, sugar and grind it.
1. Now take this puree in glass, add some mint leaves and add chilled soda water. Serve it. I garnished it with fresh cherry.
1. Enjoy.
