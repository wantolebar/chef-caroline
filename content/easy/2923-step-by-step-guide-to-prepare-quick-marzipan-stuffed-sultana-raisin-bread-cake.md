##### The ingredients needed to prepare Marzipan Stuffed Sultana (Raisin) Bread Cake:

1. You need 1/2-1 cup Rum Steeped Sultanas OR Raisins           (see recipe)
1. You need  Zest of 1/2 Lemon *grated
1. You need 20 g Butter *to spread over the baked cake
1. You need  Icing Sugar for dusting
1. Provide  Pastry
1. You need 2 teaspoons Dry Yeast
1. Provide 1 cup Bread Flour
1. Prepare 1 cup Plain Flour *plus extra for kneading
1. Prepare 1/4 cup Sugar
1. Use 1/2 teaspoon Salt
1. Use 1 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
1. Take 1/4 cup Milk
1. You need 2 Eggs *room temperature, lightly whisked
1. Use 60 g Butter *softened
1. Get  Marzipan
1. Prepare 1 cup Almond Meal
1. Get 1 cup Icing Sugar (Powdered Sugar)
1. Prepare 2 tablespoons Milk


##### Steps to make Marzipan Stuffed Sultana (Raisin) Bread Cake:

1. Combine all the Pastry ingredients in a large bowl. Mix well to form a soft dough. Add Rum Steeped Sultanas and Lemon Zest, and knead to combine, gradually adding extra Flour as required, until smooth and elastic.
1. Cover with a plate or plastic wrap, and set aside in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
1. To make Marzipan, combine Almond Meal and Icing Sugar in a bowl, add Milk and mix well. Then knead until required texture is achieved. If it is too wet, add extra Almond Meal.
1. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough into 1cm thick square, that will be about 30cm x 30cm square.
1. Roll Marzipan into a long sausage shape and slightly flatten it. Place Marzipan in centre, then fold the dough in thirds.
1. Place the log on a baking tray lined with baking paper, cover with tea towel or plastic wrap, and set aside in a warm place for 1 hour.
1. Preheat oven to 180℃. Bake for 30 minutes or until nicely browned and cooked through. When the top gets too dark, cover with a sheet of foil.
1. Spread Butter on top and transfer to a wire rack to cool. When it is cooled, dust with Icing Sugar.
