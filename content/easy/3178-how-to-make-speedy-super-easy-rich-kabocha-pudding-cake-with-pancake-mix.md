##### The ingredients needed to prepare Super Easy Rich Kabocha Pudding Cake with Pancake Mix:

1. You need 300 ml Milk
1. Provide 150 grams Cooked kabocha
1. Use 80 grams Sugar
1. Prepare 50 grams Pancake mix
1. Get 50 grams Margarine
1. You need 2 Eggs


##### Instructions to make Super Easy Rich Kabocha Pudding Cake with Pancake Mix:

1. 【Prep】① Preheat the oven to 355F/180℃. ② Coat the mold with oil and sprinkle flour or line with parchment paper.
1. Combine all the ingredients with a electric mixer or a food processor. Pour the batter into the mold.
1. Bake in the oven for 45~50 minutes.☆ Chill in the refrigerator well and enjoy.♪
1. This is cooked in a rice cooker. Run the rice cooker twice. It&#39;s nicely done.
