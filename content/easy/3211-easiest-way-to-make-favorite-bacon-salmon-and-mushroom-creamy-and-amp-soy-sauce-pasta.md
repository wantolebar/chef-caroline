##### The ingredients needed to prepare Bacon, salmon and mushroom creamy &amp; soy sauce pasta:

1. Get 200 g pasta
1. You need 5 strips bacon
1. Take 1/2 pack enoki mushroom
1. Provide 1/2 pack maitake mushroom
1. You need 2 TBSP salmon flakes
1. Prepare 2 TBSP butter
1. Provide 1 TBSP soy sauce
1. Prepare 1 clove garlic
1. Prepare 2 TBSP cooking sake
1. Use 1 tsp flour
1. Get 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
1. Prepare 1/4 cup pasta water
1. Use 1/4 sheet seaweed


##### Instructions to make Bacon, salmon and mushroom creamy &amp; soy sauce pasta:

1. Cook pasta al dente in a large pot with salted water. Save 1/4 cup of pasta water.
1. Cut the stem of enoki and maitake. Tear them apart small.
1. Mince garlic.
1. Cut bacon into bite size.
1. In a large pan, without any heat, drizzle olive oil and pour minced garlic. Saut√© with low heat until you get the aroma.
1. Add bacon, enoki and maitake mushrooms into the pan and saut√© with medium high heat for 5 minutes.
1. Add cooking sake and saut√© until the sake is evaporated. Sprinkle flour over at the end.
1. Add heavy whipping cream and cook for 3 minutes. Add soy sauce and butter at the end and turn off the heat when the butter is melted.
1. In the same pan, medium high heat, add pasta and saved pasta water and mixed them very well. Turn off the heat.
1. Add salmon flakes and mix it together.
1. Sprinkle cut seaweed over and enjoyüòç
