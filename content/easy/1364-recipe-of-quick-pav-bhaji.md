##### The ingredients needed to make Pav bhaji:

1. You need 2 pav
1. Use 2 tsp oil and butter
1. Take 2 chopped tomatoes
1. Provide 2 chopped potatoes
1. Use 1 chopped onion
1. Get 1 bowl boiled peas
1. Take 1 chopped brinjal
1. Get 500 gm pumpkin
1. Prepare to taste Salt
1. Use 1 tsp Red chilli powder
1. Take 1/2 tsp garam masala
1. You need 1 tsp pav bhaji masala
1. Get 1/2 tsp cumin seeds


##### Steps to make Pav bhaji:

1. At first, boil all veggies and mashed it. Now heat a pan add oil and butter, cumin seeds, onion, tomato and mashed.
1. Now add turmeric powder, salt, red chilli powder, pav bhaji masala, garam masala and wait for 5 minutes.
1. Now add lemon juice and turn on the gas flame and heat the nonstick pan and roast the paav and serve it with butter.
