1. Use 1 tbsp Olive oil
1. Provide 1 dash Salt
1. Provide 1 dash Black pepper
1. Use 1 dash Dried basil


##### Steps to make Caprese Salad:

1. Cut tomato, avocado and mozzarella cheese into the same size.
1. Sprinkle salt on the tomato and layer with mozzarella cheese and avocado. Wrap with cured ham.
1. Arrange neatly on a plate.
