##### The ingredients needed to cook Vegan Cornbread Muffins:

1. Get  A
1. Use  Corn meal
1. You need  Whole wheat flour (cake flour)
1. You need  Salt
1. Provide  Baking powder
1. Prepare  B
1. Prepare  Soy milk
1. Use  Vegetable oil
1. You need  Molasses syrup (light)


##### Instructions to make Vegan Cornbread Muffins:

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a muffin tray with muffin cups.
1. Add the A ingredients to bowl and mix together with a whisk.
1. Add the B ingredients to a separate bowl and combine them with a whisk as well.
1. Pour the contents of the bowl from Step 3 into the bowl from Step 2 and lightly mix together with a spatula.
1. Pour the batter into some muffin cups that have been greased with a little oil and bake in the oven for 20 minutes. If you are using a pound cake tin then please bake for 30-35 minutes.
1. I recommend either eating these hot out of the oven or re-heating them in a microwave. Try serving them with butter and syrup or jam.
1. In total these muffins should contain 1558 kcal and 2.7 g sodium.
