##### The ingredients needed to make Tabbouleh:

1. Provide  Wheat Bulgar
1. Get  Extra virgin Olive oil
1. Use  Lemon juice
1. Provide  Salt onion Parsley &amp;  PepperSpring      bunchCucumber ,  peeled ,  finely   diced    ,  chopped


##### Instructions to make Tabbouleh:

1. Soak the bulgar wheat in boiling water for 30 minutes. Drain well and add olive oil, lemon juice and plenty of seasoning. Do this immediately while the wheat is hot so that it absorbs the maximum flavor.
1. Add finely chopped spring onion, cucumber and freshly chopped herb to the bowl. Stir well and check the seasoning. Chill before serving.
