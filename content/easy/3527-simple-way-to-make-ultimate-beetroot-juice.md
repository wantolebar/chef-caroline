##### The ingredients needed to make Beetroot juice:

1. Prepare 1 beetroot
1. Use 2 carrots
1. Prepare 2 oranges
1. Get 2 tsp pomegranate seeds
1. Provide To taste Black Salt


##### Instructions to make Beetroot juice:

1. Cut the carrots, beetroot and peel the orange and pomegranate.
1. Pour or grind all in a mixer jar
1. Now Sieve it.
1. Mix black salt and serve healthy drink.
