##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Balls/:

1. You need  For Chocolate Balls
1. You need 4 Tbsp Marie Biscuits (Crushed)
1. Get 2 Tbsp Cocoa Powder
1. Get 1/2 Cup Condensed Milk
1. Get 100 Gms Chocolate
1. Provide 4 Tbsp Butter
1. You need  For White Chocolate Oreo Balls
1. Use 150 gms White Chocolate
1. Get 2 Big Packets of Oreo Cookies
1. Take 2 Tbsp Choco Syrup
1. Take  As Required Milk (For binding the dough)
1. Get 150 gms White Chocolate


##### Steps to make Chocolate Balls/:

1. For Chocolate Balls, Mix Cocoa Powder and Crushed Marie Biscuits in a Bowl. Add Condensed Milk and Mix. (The Consistency will be like Dough Type).
1. Shape with your Hands and Form Balls.
1. Mix Chocolate with Butter and Mix until Creamy. Dip the Balls in the Chocolate and Coat Evenly. Place on a Butter Paper and Freeze for 30 Minutes or until Chocolate Hardens.
1. For White Chocolate Oreo Balls, Crush Oreo biscuits in a Grinder.
1. Now knead a Dough by adding Chocolate Syrup in the Crushed Oreo Cookies.
1. Add some Milk and Knead the Dough nicely. Add Milk as per your Requirement.
1. Take small Portions of the Dough. Make Small Balls.
1. Keep Mixing the White Chocolate Until it melts. Coat the Balls evenly in the Chocolate. Refrigerate the Balls until Chocolate fully Hardens
1. Choco and White Choco Oreo Balls are now Ready. Sprinkle your favourite toppings.
1. Enjoy Your meal.
