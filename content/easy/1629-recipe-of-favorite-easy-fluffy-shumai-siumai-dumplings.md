##### The ingredients needed to prepare Easy Fluffy Shumai (Siumai) Dumplings:

1. Provide 200 grams Thinly sliced pork, coarsely chopped (offcuts; or use sliced pork)
1. Use 1 medium Onion
1. Get 1 1/2 packets Shumai (siumai) skins
1. Prepare 1/3 tsp Salt
1. Use 2 tsp Soy sauce
1. Prepare 1 tsp Sugar
1. You need 1 tbsp Sake
1. Use 1 tsp Sesame oil
1. You need 2 tbsp Katakuriko
1. Prepare 1 dash Grated ginger


##### Instructions to make Easy Fluffy Shumai (Siumai) Dumplings:

1. Put all the ingredients except for the skins in a food processor, and pulse (Don&#39;t process too much or the onion will become too watery. Stop when it&#39;s still pretty coarse.)
1. When it&#39;s about this mixed, it&#39;s good! (The photo shows a double batch or 400 g of ground pork.)
1. Cut up the shumai skins. Cut in half, then shred into 5 mm strips and separate the pieces into a bowl.
1. Form balls from the meat mixture with 2 spoons, and put into the bowl with the shumai skins. Roll around to coat with the skin strips.
1. Heat a steamer in the meantime.
1. Oil the bottom of a steamer, and put in the shumai dumplings, leaving space in between. Steam over low to medium heat for about 10 minutes. When the skins turn translucent, they&#39;re done.
