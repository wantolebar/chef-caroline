##### The ingredients needed to cook Green Breakfast Smoothie:

1. Provide 1 Handful spinach [about a stuffed cup]
1. Take 3 Tbsp Ground Flax Seed
1. Prepare 1 Well Ripened Banana
1. Take 6 Strawberries
1. Provide 1 Small Apple
1. Provide 1 Cup Water (more or less to make it your desired consistency)


##### Instructions to make Green Breakfast Smoothie:

1. Add spinach, banana, ground flaxseed, sliced strawberries, cut apple, and water to a blender.
1. Blend on high for 45 to 60 seconds.
1. Pour into a glass and enjoy.
