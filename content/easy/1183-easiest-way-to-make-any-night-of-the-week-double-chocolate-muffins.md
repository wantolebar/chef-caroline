##### The ingredients needed to cook Double Chocolate Muffins:

1. Take 100 grams Cake flour
1. Get 20 grams Cocoa powder
1. Get 1 tsp Baking powder
1. Use 70 grams Sugar
1. Prepare 1 pinch Salt
1. Provide 40 ml Vegetable oil
1. Provide 1 large Egg
1. Get 40 ml Milk
1. Use 40 grams Chocolate chips
1. Take 1 Sliced almonds


##### Instructions to make Double Chocolate Muffins:

1. Beat the egg in a bowl, add the vegetable oil and mix well with a whisk.
1. Add the milk and mix. Then add the sugar and salt and mix well.
1. Sift the cake flour, cocoa powder and baking powder together. Add 1/3 of the flour mixture into the bowl from Step 2, and mix well with a whisk.
1. Sift in the rest of the flour mixture and fold in. Add the chocolate chips before everything mixes completely, and mix gently.
1. Put the muffin cups in the muffin molds, and pour in the batter from Step 4 with a spoon. Fill about 70% of each mold with the batter. Sprinkle sliced almonds on top.
1. Bake in a preheated oven at 180℃ for 15 minutes. When baked, cool on a rack and they are done.
