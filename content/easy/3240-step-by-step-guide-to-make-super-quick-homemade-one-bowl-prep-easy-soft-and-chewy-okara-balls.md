##### The ingredients needed to cook One-Bowl Prep Easy, Soft and Chewy Okara Balls:

1. Take 150 grams Fresh okara
1. Get 100 grams Pancake mix
1. Take 50 ml Milk
1. Provide 15 grams Sugar


##### Steps to make One-Bowl Prep Easy, Soft and Chewy Okara Balls:

1. Preheat the oven to 360F/180C. Combine all of the ingredients evenly.
1. Roll into ping‐pong sized balls. Place on a baking tray covered with parchment paper.
1. Bake for about 30-35 minutes, and they&#39;re done!
