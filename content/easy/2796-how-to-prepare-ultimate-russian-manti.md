##### The ingredients needed to cook Russian Manti:

1. You need  Flour
1. Take  Water
1. Take  Egg
1. You need 2 Onions
1. Prepare 400 g beef
1. Provide 400 g pork or turkey
1. Get  Butter


##### Instructions to make Russian Manti:

1. Cut meat and onion in small pieces and mix
1. Prepare the dough. Mix Egg and half teaspoon of salt. Add one glass of water. Add flour slowly while mixing until dough is flexible and not sticky
1. Roll dough and get small pieces. Fill them with meat and leave a hole on top when closing the dumpling and put a bit of butter in the hole
1. Steam for 1 hour and eat with sauce of Aceto balsamico and soy sauce
