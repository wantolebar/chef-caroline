##### The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate chips:

1. Take  grm dark chocolate
1. Provide  chamach malai
1. Prepare  Plastic sit ya butter peper


##### Instructions to make Chocolate chips:

1. Micro proof bowl me chocolate leker use 1 mins ke liye micro kar lo.
1. Bahar nikal kar malai dalker mix kar ke phir se 1 mins ke liye micro kar lo.
1. Ab 5 mins ke liye thanda hone per ek cone me bhar lo.
1. Plastic sit ya butter peper ke uper chote chote drops banaye.
1. Freeze me 30 mins ke liye rakh ke nikalker airtight container me leker freeze me rakh lo.
1. 6 months ke liye rakh sakte ho.
