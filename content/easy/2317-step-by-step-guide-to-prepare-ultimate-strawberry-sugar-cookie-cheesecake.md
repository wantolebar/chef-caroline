##### The ingredients needed to prepare Strawberry Sugar Cookie Cheesecake:

1. Get 1 packages sugar cookie dough
1. You need 1 packages cream cheese
1. You need 1 cup powder sugar
1. Get 1/2 stick butter
1. Take 1 box strawberries


##### Steps to make Strawberry Sugar Cookie Cheesecake:

1. Spread your cookie dough in a pan and make sure the bottom is all cover and bake the cookie dough at 350 for 18 minutes
1. After its done baking let it cool for 15 minutes while its cooling you fan start in your filling
1. In a bowl mix together your cream cheese powdered sugar and butter until smooth then slice your strawberries and place them on top
