##### The ingredients needed to prepare Instant pot bbq ribs by SG:

1. Take 1.5 kg marinated ribs (find this in your local supermarket)
1. Use 300 ml apple juice
1. Prepare  Bbq sauce


##### Steps to make Instant pot bbq ribs by SG:

1. Place the ribs and the apple juice (I’ve used a slow juicer for the juice) in the Instant pot and set it to 30 mins on high pressure.
1. When the IP is done, do a QR and place the ribs on a pan. Be careful, as the ribs are hot and they will be tender. Add bbq sauce on the ribs and place them in the oven at 220 degrees Celsius for 10 mins with fan.
