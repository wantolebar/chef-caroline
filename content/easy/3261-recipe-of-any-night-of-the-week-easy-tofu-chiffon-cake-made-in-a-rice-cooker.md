##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Tofu Chiffon Cake Made in a Rice Cooker:

1. You need 150 grams Silken tofu
1. Get 2 Eggs
1. Take 200 grams Pancake mix
1. Get 100 ml Milk
1. Prepare 30 grams Sugar
1. Get 3 drops Vanilla extract (optional)


##### Instructions to make Easy Tofu Chiffon Cake Made in a Rice Cooker:

1. Put the tofu into the inner pot of the rice cooker. You don&#39;t need to drain the tofu.
1. Stir the tofu with a whisk.
1. Add the eggs, milk, and sugar.
1. Mix well.
1. Add the pancake mix and mix well. Finally, add a few drops of vanilla extract (optional).
1. Set the pot in the rice cooker and push start.
1. When a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, it&#39;s done.
1. The cake comes out of the rice cooker pot easily.
