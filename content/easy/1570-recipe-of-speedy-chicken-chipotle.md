##### The ingredients needed to cook Chicken Chipotle:

1. Use 1 tbsp ground cumin
1. Take 1 Chipotle in adobo
1. You need  salt and pepper


##### Steps to make Chicken Chipotle:

1. Add ingredients in a bowl and let the chicken marinate 30 min. Or longer (I left it over night for best flavor)
1. Grill the chicken (I did it indoors)
1. Enjoy! (Now i diced my chicken up and made into buritos with some refried beans, Pico de gallo, guacamole, sour cream)
