##### The ingredients needed to prepare Stuffed Baby Bell Peppers:

1. Take 1 small bag miniature bell peppers
1. Provide 1/4 lb Ground beef
1. Provide 2 clove Garlic chopped finely
1. Get 1 Leftover chopped button mushrooms &amp; onions
1. Take 1 cup Leftover rice
1. You need 1 tsp Oil for drying beef &amp; vegetables
1. Use 1 Spray for baking pan
1. Use 1 Egg - beaten (to bind ingredients)


##### Steps to make Stuffed Baby Bell Peppers:

1. Take one teaspoon oil &amp; sauté the burger, garlic.  Add onion &amp; much room &amp; cup of rice.  When everything is heated through set aside to cool
1. When the stuffing ingredients are cooling - cut opened each small pepper &amp; clean the insides
1. Stuff each pepper to the top &amp; place each into a pan which has been sprayed with coconut oil or cooking spray
1. Bake the peppers in the oven at 350°F for 30 minutes
