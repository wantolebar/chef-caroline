##### The ingredients needed to make Hasselback potatoes:

1. Provide 4 peeled whole potatoes
1. Use  Ground pepper &amp; other spices (of choice)
1. You need 5 tbsp Butter
1. You need 1 tbsp veg. Oil
1. Take 1 teaspoon lemon zest (optional)
1. You need  Mayonnaise/salad cream for ganishing


##### Steps to make Hasselback potatoes:

1. Mix butter, oil and spices to create a paste, add in lemon zest if using.
1. Cut through potatoes creating thin slices without going all the way to the bottom. You can use wooden sticks by each side to prevent slicing in too deep.
1. Smear paste all over the potatoes and ensure it enters through all the slices.
1. Bake for about 50 minutes or until tender and crisp.
1. Serve plain, with cream or sauce of choice.
