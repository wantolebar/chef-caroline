##### The ingredients needed to cook Eggless Coconut Flour Choco chip cookies:

1. Provide 1/2 cup - Coconut flour
1. Use 1/2 cup - Flax gel
1. You need 1 table spoon - Ghee or Oil
1. Take 1/4 cup - Choco chip
1. You need 1/2 cup - Organic Natural brown sugar (you can use normal sugar as well)
1. You need 1/2 tea spoon -Sea salt
1. Take 1 tea spoon - Vanilla


##### Steps to make Eggless Coconut Flour Choco chip cookies:

1. How to make flax gel: Take 2 tsp of flax seed, make powder. Put this powder in thick bottomed small pan, add one cup of water and boil until it is thick, slimy and reduced to almost half.
1. Pre heat oven at 170 °C. Line the baking tray with the butter paper.
1. Take one glass bowl, put flour, sugar, salt crystals, ghee, flax gel, vanilla and mix. Dough will be little wet. Mix in Choco chip and mix once more.
1. Scoop out the mix by using ice cream scooper and arrange in a lined baking tray. - -Bake this in a pre-heated oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. Don’t touch while removing, it will be very soft. Remove the tray and cool as it is. - After cooling, it will harden a bit. After it cools down completely, store it in an air tight container.
1. These cookies are slightly crunchier at outer circle, soft and chewy at the centre. - It becomes a little more harder and firm the next day.
