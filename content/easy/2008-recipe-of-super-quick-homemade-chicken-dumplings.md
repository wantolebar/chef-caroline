##### The ingredients needed to cook Chicken dumplings:

1. Provide 1 large frozen chicken breast
1. Use 1 can cream of chicken
1. You need 1 vegetable oil
1. Use 1 can biscuits


##### Instructions to make Chicken dumplings:

1. cook chicken by boil of you can buy chicken already cooked.
1. cut chicken in little pieces
1. preheat oven to 350
1. flatten biscuits till flat. do not make holes
1. add chicken in the middle and close up the biscuit
1. oil the glass or any type on pan.
1. put dumplings in pan.
1. put in oven for around 40 minutes
