##### The ingredients needed to make Quick Veggie Stirfry:

1. Get 1 tofurkey sausage, sliced
1. Take Handful broccoli florets, chopped roughly
1. You need 1 clove garlic, sliced
1. Prepare 1 tbsp flaked almonds
1. You need 1 tbsp sesame oil
1. Use 2 tbsp soy sauce
1. Use  Sprinkling cayenne
1. Use 1 small onion, diced


##### Instructions to make Quick Veggie Stirfry:

1. Fry the sausage slices in the oil for a few minutes then add the garlic, onions and soy.
1. When the onions have softened add the broccoli and stif fry for 5 mins or so until the broccoli is cooked as you like it. Season a little with pepper to your taste.
1. Serve in a bowl topped with a sprinkling of almonds and a dusting of the cayenne.
