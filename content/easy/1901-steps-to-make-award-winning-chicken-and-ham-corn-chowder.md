##### The ingredients needed to cook Chicken and ham corn chowder:

1. You need 1 onion, diced
1. You need 1 Jalepeno, minced
1. Take 1/4 cup Cilantro
1. Provide 1/4 cup green onion, Chopped
1. Get 6 small red potatoes, diced
1. Provide 3 chicken breasts, precooked
1. Prepare 2 (8 oz) bags diced ham
1. Prepare 1 (32 oz) can corn
1. Use 1 (12 oz) can diced tomatoes
1. You need 6 cups Chicken broth
1. Prepare 2 bay leaves
1. Provide 1 Tbsp garlic powder
1. Provide 1 Tbsp Thyme
1. Provide 1 Tbsp basil
1. You need Pinch salt and pepper
1. Provide 16 oz half and half
1. Prepare 2 cups shredded cheese


##### Steps to make Chicken and ham corn chowder:

1. Put all ingredients in crockpot except cheese and milk. Cook on high 4 hours or low for 8 hours
1. In the last hour, pour cream and Shredded cheese into crockpot.
1. Top with your favorite toppings. Green onions, cooked bacon, avocado
