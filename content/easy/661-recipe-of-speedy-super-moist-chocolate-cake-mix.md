##### The ingredients needed to make Super moist chocolate cake mix:

1. Get 220 g Betty crocker chocolate cake mix
1. Use 1 1/5 cup water
1. You need 1/3 cup oil
1. Provide 3 eggs


##### Steps to make Super moist chocolate cake mix:

1. Here is the Cake mix procedure
1. In bowl,empty the content,add water,oil,and eggs and mix thoroughly
1. Pour in a greased pan and bake for 40-45 minutes
1. Allow to cool down completely then divide and add icing or chocolate frosting on the cake
1. Enjoy the taste of chocolate!
1. I added dark chocolate by grating it on top
