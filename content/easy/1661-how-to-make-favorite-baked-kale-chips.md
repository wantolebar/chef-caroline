##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Kale Chips:

1. Prepare 1 bunch kale
1. Get  Drizzle of olive oil
1. Get  Drizzle of red wine vinegar (optional)
1. Provide  Shake of Kosher salt
1. You need  Shake of garlic &amp; herb seasoning


##### Steps to make Baked Kale Chips:

1. Preheat oven to 350. Pull kale off stem, wash &amp; pat dry. Place on baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt &amp; seasonings. Bake 10-15 minutes. Once out of oven, toss then eat.
