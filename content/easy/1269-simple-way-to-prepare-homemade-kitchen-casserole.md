##### Steps to make Kitchen Casserole:

1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
1. Chop the potatoes
1. Peel and chop the carrots
1. Heat oil and cube the chicken
1. Add the cubed chicken to the hot oil
1. Add celery salt, garlic and pepper to the chicken and sauté till no longer pink
1. When no longer pink remove chicken from the liquids add to a hot oven safe dish. And continue on low heat.
1. Heat the liquids
1. Add the potatoes and carrots to the liquids and simmer
1. Add the salt, onion powder and green onions. Simmer covered for 20 minutes
1. To the chicken add the allspice and pasta and mix
1. Add the Alfredo sauce and stove top stuffing mix well.
1. When the vegetables are tender add to the chicken and Alfredo sauce
1. Simmer and mix well
1. Mix the melted butter, breadcrumbs, parsley, and dill weed, and parmesan cheese
1. Add to top of the casserole
1. Bake till the topping is browned
1. Serve I hope you enjoy!
