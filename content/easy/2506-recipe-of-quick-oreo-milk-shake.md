##### The ingredients needed to cook Oreo milk shake:

1. Prepare 2 scoop Vanilla ice cream -
1. Get As needed Chocolate syrup for decoration
1. Take 2 Cherry -

1. Get 2 Tablespoon Sugar -


##### Steps to make Oreo milk shake:

1. Take a mixer jar and grind the biscuits into fine powder. Add chilled milk in the jar.
1. Now add sugar, ice cream and ice cubes in it. Blend all the ingredients.
1. Pour chocolate syrup on the sides of the glass/tumbler.
1. Now put 1 scoop of ice cream (optional) in a glass.
1. Now pour the milkshake into the glass.
1. Your Oreo Milkshake is ready to be served. Garnish it with in silver balls, cherry and tuti fruty and serve chilled.
