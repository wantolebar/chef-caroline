##### The ingredients needed to make Butter Corn:

1. Provide  fresh corn off the cob(boiled)
1. Get  butter
1. You need  whole cumin
1. You need  salt
1. Provide  coarse black Pepper
1. You need  lemon juice
1. Provide  some fresh chopped dhania


##### Steps to make Butter Corn:

1. Boil the maize, remove from the cob
1. In a frying pan,add butter
1. Once melted add the cumin
1. Saute for 30 secs. Add the maize,salt,black pepper.Mix well &amp; cook for 5-10 mins
1. Keep stirring as no liquid is added
1. Now add fresh dhania &amp; lemon juice. Serve hot
