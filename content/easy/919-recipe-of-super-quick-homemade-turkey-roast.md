##### The ingredients needed to prepare Turkey Roast:

1. Provide  turkey thawed
1. You need  butter room temperature
1. Prepare  parsley chopped
1. Take  garlic minced
1. Take  lemon zest
1. Take  Mirepoix


##### Instructions to make Turkey Roast:

1. Dry off the turkey with kitchen towels
1. Mix the butter, parsley, garlic, lemon zest and season with salt and pepper
1. Chop up the mirepoix and line the baking pan with it. Add turkey innards, if any. Season with salt and pepper.
1. Stuff the butter mix underneath the turkey skin and distribute it evenly
1. Smear remaining butter on the outside if the turkey. Season the turkey with salt and pepper. Lay on top of mirepoix in the baking pan. Cover with foil.
1. Bake in an oven preheated to 375 F (190 C) for 3 hours (aprox 35 min per kg). Paste every 30 min with pan drippings. Remove foil last hour of cooking and start pasting every 15 min
1. For best results, use a thermometer until the internal temperature reaches 163 F (73 C)
1. Let rest for at least 2 hours.
1. Enjoy!
