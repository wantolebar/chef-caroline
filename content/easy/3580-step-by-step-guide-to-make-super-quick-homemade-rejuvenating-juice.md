##### The ingredients needed to cook Rejuvenating Juice:

1. Use 1 Beetroot
1. Use 1 Carrot
1. Use 1 Small Cucumber
1. Prepare 1 Apple
1. Prepare 1 Inch Ginger
1. Take 1 Lemon
1. Provide 1 Bunch Coriander Leaves
1. Get Leaves Some Mint


##### Instructions to make Rejuvenating Juice:

1. Clean and dice or finely scrap all the vegetables
1. Skin the apple and grate it
1. Skin the ginger and grate
1. Squeeze the lemon
1. Clean and chop the leaves
1. Mix all in a bowl and grind all together
1. Fresh refreshing juice drink instantly not to be kept in the fridge
1. PS:- Good for Overall from TOP to TOE (hair tip to toe nail)
