##### The ingredients needed to cook Strawberry cheesecake:

1. Take 3 cups Strawberries
1. Provide 1 cup Creamcheese
1. Prepare 200 ml Cream
1. Provide 1/2 cup Sugar
1. Get 2cup Biscuits  crushed
1. Take 1/2 cup Butter


##### Instructions to make Strawberry cheesecake:

1. Mix crushed biscuits with melted butter and set in a spring form pan.refrigerate it for 15 minutes
1. In a blender add strawberries and sugar and chop them coarsly.
1. Now add creamcheese and cream mix well untill they are fluffy in texture.
1. Pour this mixture on to biscuit layer.
1. Place it in Refrigerator for overnight to let it set.
1. Garnish with fresh strawberries.
