##### The ingredients needed to make Popovers:

1. Get 1 cup flour
1. Get 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
1. Get 1 cup milk
1. You need 2 each eggs
1. Take 2 tbsp unsalted butter


##### Instructions to make Popovers:

1. Bring all cold ingredients to room temperature.
1. Heat the oven to 400°F.
1. Grease a 6-cup popover pan with the 1 teaspoon of butter.
1. Combine the 1 tablespoon of butter, the flour, salt, eggs and milk in a food processor or blender and process for 30 seconds.
1. Divide the batter evenly among the cups of the popover pan, filling each one-third to one-half full. Bake on the middle rack of the oven for 40 minutes, taking care not to open the oven door. Remove the popovers to a cooling rack and pierce each one in the top with a knife to allow steam to escape. Serve warm.
