##### The ingredients needed to prepare Simple pancake:

1. Use  All purpose flour
1. Use  Sugar
1. Use  Milk
1. Prepare  Egg
1. You need  Vegetable oil
1. Prepare  Onion


##### Steps to make Simple pancake:

1. Sieve flour into a bowl to remove particle
1. Add sugar, milk and break in egg with water mix together well to form batter. Add clean chopped onion
1. Heat non stick pan on low fire with little oil and pour in small quantity of the batter (what the pan can take)
1. Allow to fry and turn the side
1. Remove from pan, continue till the batter is finish. Serve with sauce of your choice.
1. Mine with cabbage sauce
