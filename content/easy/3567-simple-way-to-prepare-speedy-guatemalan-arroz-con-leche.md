##### The ingredients needed to cook Guatemalan Arroz con leche:

1. Use 1/2 cup Rice
1. Take 1 cup Milk
1. Get 1 can Evaporated Milk
1. Use 1 stick Cinnamon
1. Use 1/2 cup Sugar (or to taste)
1. You need 2 cup Water
1. Take 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Use 1/2 cup Raisins (to taste)


##### Steps to make Guatemalan Arroz con leche:

1. Pour 2 cups of water into a medium size pot. Allow the water to boil.
1. Add rice and the cinnamon stick  to the boiling water. (For best results, break the cinnamon stick outside of the pot )
1. Cook rice until the consistency is soft and &#34;spongy&#34;
1. Add milk, evaporated milk, salt, sugar and raisins stirring  occasionally to ensure even flavor distribution
1. Continue to cook the rice pudding on LOW heat until most of the milk has been consumed by the rice.
1. Serve and Enjoy! :)
