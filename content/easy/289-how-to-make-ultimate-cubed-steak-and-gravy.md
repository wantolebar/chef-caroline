##### The ingredients needed to cook Cubed Steak and Gravy:

1. Prepare  cubed steak
1. Get  flour
1. You need  garlic salt
1. You need  black pepper
1. Use  vegetable oil
1. Take  large onion sliced
1. Take  can beef broth
1. Provide  dry onion mix soup


##### Steps to make Cubed Steak and Gravy:

1. Combine flour, garlic salt and black pepper in shallow pie plate or baking dish. Dredge both sides of cubed steak in flour mix.
1. Heat oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Add steak cooking for 5 min per side. Remove steak from skillet and set aside.
1. If you want to thicken the gravy some, add a few teaspoons of leftover flour from dredging the steak to the drippings of the pan.
1. Add onion, broth and onion soup mix to the drippings in the pan. Use a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits stuck to the bottom of the pan.
1. Bring to a simmer and cook until onions are soft.
1. Return steak to pan to warm. Serve with mashed potatoes.
