##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Potato Paratha:

1. You need 250 gm Sweet potatoes boied &amp; smashed
1. Prepare 1 cup multigrain flour
1. Use 1 tbsp mix dryfruits powder
1. Use 1 tbsp black sesame seeds
1. Take 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
1. Provide 1 tbsp oil
1. Take 1 tsp salt
1. Provide as needed Water to knead a soft dough


##### Steps to make Sweet Potato Paratha:

1. Mix smashed sweet potatoes, multigrain flour, oil,dryfruits powder, black sesame seeds &amp; salt in a bowl and knead a soft dough with water.
1. Knead dough again with little oil to soften. Make equal portion from the dough and make round balls &amp; roll paraths cut in ❤ shapes cook both the brown colour spreading ghee on both sides on medium flame.
1. Serve hot ready Healthy, yummy Sweet potato Paratha in a plate with Chutney, ketchup.
