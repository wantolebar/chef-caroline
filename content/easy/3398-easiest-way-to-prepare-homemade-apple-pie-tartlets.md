##### The ingredients needed to prepare Apple Pie Tartlets:

1. Provide 1 cup finely chopped apple
1. Take 1/4 cup lemon curd
1. Use  Topping....
1. Take 1/2 cup All purposes flour
1. You need 3 tablespoons sugar
1. Take 1/2 teaspoon Ground cinnamon
1. Prepare 1/4 cup cold butter
1. Take as needed Icing sugar for dusting


##### Instructions to make Apple Pie Tartlets:

1. In a small bowl, combine apples and lemon curd. Spoon into turt shells.
1. In another bowl, combine the flour, sugar and cinnamon, cut in butter until mixture resembles fine crumbs. Spoon over apple mixture. Place on an ungread baking sheet.
1. Preheated the oven 180°C for 10 minutes.
1. Bake at 18 - 20 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on wire racks for 5 minutes. Dust with icing sugar.
1. Serve warm or at room temperature.
