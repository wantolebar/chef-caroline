##### The ingredients needed to prepare Strawberry Fluff Salad:

1. You need 1 (48 oz) Cottage Cheese
1. Take 16 oz Heavy Whipping Cream
1. You need 1 tsp Sweeter (we mix our own)
1. Provide 2 Sugar Free Strawberry Jello
1. Take 193 g Frozen Strawberries sliced


##### Instructions to make Strawberry Fluff Salad:

1. Put 2 cups of heavy cream and 1 tsp sweetner in large bowl and whisk untill stiff peaks form.
1. Add in all other ingredients and mix thoroughly. Refrigerate overnight.
