##### The ingredients needed to prepare Great Grandmas Chicken And Dumplings:

1. Prepare  soup
1. Provide 1 Cooked and shredded chicken breast
1. You need 1 can Campbell&#39;s cream of chicken soup
1. You need 1 can Campbell&#39;s chicken noodle soup
1. You need 1 can Milk
1. Use 1 can Water
1. Prepare 2 Chicken bouillon cubes
1. Get 1 Lemon pepper
1. Prepare  Dumplings
1. Take 2 cup Bisquick
1. Get 1 cup Milk
1. You need 1 Cilantro


##### Instructions to make Great Grandmas Chicken And Dumplings:

1. In a large pot bring water to a boil.
1. Boil the chicken breast, pepper and salt.
1. Once cooked, dry chicken and shred finely.
1. In a separate pot.
1. Combine soups, water, and milk.
1. Whisk together, and bring to a boil.
1. Add the bouillon cubes
1. While waiting for soup to come to a boil make the dumplings.
1. Simply fork the bisquick, milk, and optional cilantro in a small bowl.
1. Make sure to make dough right before soup boils. Do not over mix the batter.
1. Once soup comes to a boil, drop spoonfuls of dough into boiling soup.
1. Cook uncovered for 10 minutes, cook for another 10 minutes covered.
