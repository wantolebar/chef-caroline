##### The ingredients needed to make Homemade delicious chili:

1. Get  ground turkey, extra lean
1. Use  olive oil, extra virgin
1. Prepare  large onion, chopped
1. Use  medium bell pepper, chopped
1. Take  garlic, chopped
1. You need  tomatoes, crushed or diced
1. Get  tomato sauce
1. Get  kidney or pinto beans, rinsed
1. Get  chili powder (or more to taste)
1. You need  salt and pepper
1. You need  hot sauce if you would like


##### Steps to make Homemade delicious chili:

1. In a large saucepan brown ground turkey in 2 tablespoon in olive oil. Spoon out any excess fat.
1. Add onion, garlic and bell pepper and sauté until veggies are soft (about 5 minutes)
1. Add remaining ingredients and simmer 10 to 15 minutes.
1. And you are done! Chili can be served with hot sauce to taste!
