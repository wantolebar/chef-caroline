##### The ingredients needed to cook Stuffed Shells:

1. Get 1 lb mild sausage
1. Take 1 lb bacon
1. Use 1 box jumbo stuffing shells
1. Take 15 oz ricotta cheese
1. You need 5 oz bread crumbs
1. Provide 10 oz cheese (grated)
1. Provide 16 oz marinara of choice


##### Instructions to make Stuffed Shells:

1. Cook sausage and bacon
1. Boil noodles almost till there done. Do not over cook!
1. In a medium bowl add ricotta half a jar of sauce and mix together
1. Drain sausage and bacon till there is no more grease.
1. Drain noodles.
1. Add sausage and bacon into the mixing bowl and stir together the ingredients
1. Preheat oven to 400°F
1. Put the other half of the jar on the bottom of the baking dish
1. Stuff the shells full about 2 tbls and place them in the dish single laired
1. After stuffing the wheels put the other jar if sauce over the shells
1. Sprinkle cheese over the top and cover with bread crumbs and seasonings to your liking
1. Place in oven for 35 minutes and let cool for 5 and serve
