##### The ingredients needed to prepare Leftover Chicken Enchiladas with Goat Cheese #2:

1. Prepare 1 rotisserie chicken; shredded
1. Take 1 large can green enchilada sauce
1. Provide 2 jars tomatillo salsa
1. Use 1 roasted poblano peppers; medium dice
1. Provide 1 bunch cilantro
1. Provide 1/2 cup sweet corn
1. Prepare 1/2 cup diced green chiles
1. Take 1 packages flour or corn tortillas
1. Provide 1 log goat cheese
1. Use 1 handful crumbled cotija cheese


##### Instructions to make Leftover Chicken Enchiladas with Goat Cheese #2:

1. Combine chicken, salsa, cilantro, poblano, corn, chiles, and goat cheese in a large mixing bowl. Add a large pinch of salt. Mix.
1. Cover and bake at 350° for approximately 45 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly reheated.
1. Garnish with crumbled cotija.
1. Variations; Tequila, roasted bell peppers/onion/tomatoes, potato, roasted corn, habanero, lime
