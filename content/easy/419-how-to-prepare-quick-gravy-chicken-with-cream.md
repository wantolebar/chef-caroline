##### Instructions to make Gravy chicken with cream:

1. Wash chicken properly. Then cut the onions, garlic, ginger and make paste with turmeric powder, chilli powder. mix this paste with chicken. Marinate the chicken for 20 minutes.
1. Heat oil in a frying pan.first put the chicken pieces in the pan,shallow fry the chicken pieces. Then pour the gravy on it.Then add poppy seed paste and cashew nut paste.add salt and sugar.cook around 10 minutes. Then add one cup of warm water and covering the pan in low flame. cook for 10 minutes and add gram masala.
1. Garnish with fresh cream.
