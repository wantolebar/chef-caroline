##### The ingredients needed to prepare Beetroot juice drink:

1. You need 2 small sized Carrots
1. Provide 1 medium sized Apple
1. You need 3 big Beetroot

##### Instructions to make Beetroot juice drink:

1. Cut and rinse the beetroot,carrots and apple.
1. Blend smooth pour in the sieve and sieve out the juice.
1. Add ice cubes to the juice and enjoy.
