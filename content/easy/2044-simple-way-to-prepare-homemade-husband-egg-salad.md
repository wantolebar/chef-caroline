##### The ingredients needed to cook Husband Egg Salad:

1. Get  Boiled eggs
1. Get  Bread
1. Provide  Bacon
1. You need  Mayo
1. Prepare  Finely chopped Celery
1. Use  Onion
1. Prepare  Cream Cheese (whipped)
1. Get  Garlic Powder
1. Get  Yellow Mustard
1. You need  Salt and Pepper to taste
1. Provide  Optional: 2tsp dill/sweet relish, tomato, lettuce, or other favorite sandwich toppings.


##### Steps to make Husband Egg Salad:

1. Boil and peel Eggs
1. Finely chop Eggs, Onion, and  Celery
1. Cook Bacon (I microwave 2-3 slices for. 2 1/2 min)
1. Chop Bacon in small pieces
1. Mix Egg, Onion, Celery, and Bacon. Add the Mayo, Mustard, Cream Cheese, Salt, Pepper, Garlic, and Relish (if desired) MIX thoroughly w/ a fork till blended.
1. Put Egg Salad on top of Sandwich toppings and serve. This is good room temp, or chilled.
