##### Steps to make Broccoli &amp; Ground Beef Stir-fry:

1. Cut the broccoli into bite-sized pieces and boil until it is slightly hard. Cut the onion into wedges and julienne the carrot.
1. Heat oil in a frying pan and cook the ground beef until it changes color. Don&#39;t break it apart too much, the point is to leave small clumps of meat!
1. Season with salt and pepper and then transfer to a plate. Stir-fry the vegetables. When they have cooked, mix with the meat.
1. Flavor with the sugar and the soy sauce, arrange on a dish, and top with the boiled broccoli and ground sesame to finish!
