##### The ingredients needed to cook Choco Almond Cake:

1. Prepare  marigold biscuits or 10 oreo/bourbon biscuits
1. Use  baking soda
1. You need  milk
1. You need  powdered sugar
1. Use  powdered Almond
1. Provide  Nutella/ Dairy milk chocolate


##### Steps to make Choco Almond Cake:

1. Crush the biscuits to crumbs and sieve it
1. Add milk and whisk well to make a lump-free batter.
1. Take a small sized cooker and a pan. Grease the pan with butter/oil completely if you don&#39;t have butter/parchment paper.
1. Now Add baking soda, powdered sugar, powdered almonds, 1tspn chocolate to the batter and whisk it. Pour the batter inside a pan,keep it inside the cooker and close the lid.
1. Keep the flame low and bake it for 20 mins.
1. Wait for it to cool and Remove the cake slowly and transfer it to a plate. Apply Nutella/melted dairy milk for icing.
1. Cut the cake and enjoy your dessert.
