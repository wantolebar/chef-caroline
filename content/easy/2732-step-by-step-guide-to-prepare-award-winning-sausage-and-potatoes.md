##### The ingredients needed to prepare Sausage and potatoes:

1. Provide  Cheesy smoked sausage
1. Prepare 5 red potatoes(medium size)
1. Provide 1 onion
1. Take 1 red bell pepper
1. Prepare 1 orange bell pepper
1. You need 1 yellow bell pepper


##### Instructions to make Sausage and potatoes:

1. Cut potatoes in cubes fry in olive oil with the onion
1. Cut peppers in strips add to potatoes cook over medium heat heat till potatoes are browned and soft
1. Cut up sausage add to mixture cook till sausage is done
1. Serve and enjoy
