##### The ingredients needed to cook Baby Food: Vegetable Soup:

1. Take  Carrot
1. Take  Onion
1. Provide  Cabbage
1. Use  Water, enough to cover the vegetables
1. Take  Daikon radish, 5 cm


##### Instructions to make Baby Food: Vegetable Soup:

1. Peel the vegetables, and chop up roughly.
1. Put the vegetables and water in a pan, and bring to a boil. When it comes to a boil, turn down the heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes.
1. Let the boiled vegetables cool down, strain with a sieve, and it&#39;s finished.
1. Pour the soup into an ice cube tray, and freeze. You can defrost the cubes whenever you want. Cut the vegetables into shapes and mash to a consistency suitable for your child&#39;s age.
