##### The ingredients needed to make Arroz con leche:

1. Take 2 cup whole milk
1. Provide 1 1/4 cup Jasmine white rice
1. Prepare 3 stick cinnamon
1. Use 1 cup sugar
1. You need 1 1/3 cup water


##### Instructions to make Arroz con leche:

1. You will need milk water rice sugar cinnamon and Jasmine white rice
1. Mix all ingredients together in a med pot
1. Cook on med heat
1. Stir occasionally to prevent rice from getting stuck
1. Enjoy hot or cold
