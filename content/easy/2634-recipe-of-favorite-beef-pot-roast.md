##### The ingredients needed to cook Beef Pot Roast:

1. Provide 2 Pounds Beef Round   Roast
1. Take 15 Cloves Garlic
1. Provide 1 Teaspoon Salt
1. Use 1 Teaspoon Black Pepper Cracked
1. Take 1 Cup Water
1. Take 1 Cup Red Wine
1. Get 1 Onion Sliced   Large
1. Take 2 Tablespoons Cilantro
1. Take 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1. Prepare 2 Sprigs Thyme


##### Instructions to make Beef Pot Roast:

1. Cut the Roast Beef into 1&#34;thick slices and season with salt and cracked black pepper. Sear on both sides for 3 mts. Let it rest for 5 minutes. Cut beef into 1 1/2&#34; cubes.
1. Use a heavy bottom Pot. Add Olive Oil, add Onions and carmalize onions. Add Beef, Garlic, Salt and Cracked black pepper. Cook on slow heat for 1 hour. Check to make sure it is not dry.
1. Now Add wine and keep simmering until beef is fork tender. This may take upto 1 hour or more.
