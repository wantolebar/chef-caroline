##### The ingredients needed to prepare Italian Pasta Salad:

1. Provide 2 boxes of tri color pasta
1. Provide 1 bottle of olive garden Italian dressing (or any really good brand)
1. You need 1 bag mild shredded cheddar cheese (any brand)
1. Take 1 small bag of pepperoni
1. You need 2 small cucumbers (cut into bite size)
1. Prepare 1 tomato diced


##### Instructions to make Italian Pasta Salad:

1. Fully cook pasta, drain and set aside.
1. Cut cucumbers into small bite size pieces and put into a medium size glass or plastic bowl and set aside.
1. Once pasta has cooled mix pasta and cucumbers together.
1. Dice one tomato add it, dressing, cheese, and pepperoni.
1. Serves 4 to 6 .
