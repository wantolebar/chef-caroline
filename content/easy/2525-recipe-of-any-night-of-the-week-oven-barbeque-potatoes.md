##### The ingredients needed to prepare Oven Barbeque Potatoes:

1. You need 6 medium potatoes
1. Provide 1 small onion
1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. Use 1/2 tbsp butter
1. Take 1/2 sweet pepper (optional)
1. Get 2 dash tabasco sauce (optional)
1. You need 1 cup honey barbeque sauce


##### Instructions to make Oven Barbeque Potatoes:

1. preheat oven to 350°F
1. boil salt and water
1. peel, wash and cut up potatoes in your preferred style and add them to boiling water
1. cook potatoes until partially. Don&#39;t cook them too soft
1. pour off water and add to a baking pan or dish
1. slice onion and sweet pepper and add to potatoes
1. add butter, tabasco sauce (for all you hot sauce lovers) and add barbeque sauce
1. mix everything together and cover with foil. put it in the oven for twenty minutes
1. remove from oven and remove foil and put back in the oven for an additional fifteen minutes
1. remove. let cool and enjoy
