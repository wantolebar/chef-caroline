##### The ingredients needed to prepare Pepper and sausage kabobs:

1. You need 2 packages Beef smoked sausage
1. Take 10 Kabob sticks
1. Use 1 packages mini sweet peppers
1. Prepare 1 Bottle of Swert Bourbon Glaze


##### Steps to make Pepper and sausage kabobs:

1. Soak sticks in water for about an hour
1. Slice sausage diagnal into 2 inch pieces
1. Assemble whole mini sweet peppers and sausage on kabob sticks
1. Cook on grill turning to ypur liking of crispness coating kabobs with Bourbon glaze
1. Remove from grill and pour glaze on top of kabobs and enjoy
