##### The ingredients needed to make Oven Baked Beef Back Ribs:

1. You need  Slab of beef backed ribs
1. You need  Rosemary Garlic
1. Use  Smokehouse maple seasoning
1. Take  Crushed red pepper flakes
1. You need  Your favorite BBQ sauce


##### Steps to make Oven Baked Beef Back Ribs:

1. Preheat oven to 350, mix dry rub, using 2 sheets of foil on the bottom,shiny side up, rub the meat down and place meat side down. Add 2 more sheets of foil on top and fold and crimp all edges.
1. Bake at 350 for 1 hr 30 min
1. Remove from oven open foil cut them into about 2-3 ribs per person and place bone side down on broiler,add your BBQ sauce and broil about 5 mins. Flip over and add BBQ sauce to the other side and broil another 5 mins.
1. Serve and enjoy
