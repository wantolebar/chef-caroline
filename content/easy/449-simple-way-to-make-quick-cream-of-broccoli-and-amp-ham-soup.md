##### Instructions to make cream of broccoli &amp; ham soup:

1. add 6tbs of flour stir until smooth
1. remove from heat and slowly add 1 cup chicken broth and 1 cup water stir until smooth
1. return to heat bring to boil stirring constantly until nice and thick
1. add 1 cup of water and 1 cup of milk
1. stir in all the cheeses
1. after all cheeses are melted drain corn and green beans add them along with the bag of broccoli
1. add in Ham and let cook for 15-20 minutes
