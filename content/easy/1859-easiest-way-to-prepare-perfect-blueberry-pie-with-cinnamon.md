##### The ingredients needed to cook Blueberry pie with cinnamon:

1. Prepare  Filling part
1. Prepare 500 grams frozen blueberry
1. Get 1 tbsp sugar
1. You need 1 tbsp potato starch
1. Use 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
1. Use  dough
1. Use 260 grams flour
1. Prepare 140 grams butter
1. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
1. You need 1/4 tsp salt
1. Use 6 tbsp cold water


##### Instructions to make Blueberry pie with cinnamon:

1. dough: cold butter cut into small pieces and mix with flour, sugar and salt.
1. add water to the dough and mix everything fast. cover the dough with foil or film and put into fridge for no less than 30min
1. mix together the ingredients for filling part and heat them on the very small fire till thickened
1. cool down the filling part to room temperature
1. add cinnamon to the mixture before putting it into the pie form
1. when the dough is cooled down from 2/3 of it make the base for the pie. Put the berries inside and cover it with the remaining 1/3 of the dough.
1. make small holes in the top layer of the pie and bake in the oven for 30 -35 min
