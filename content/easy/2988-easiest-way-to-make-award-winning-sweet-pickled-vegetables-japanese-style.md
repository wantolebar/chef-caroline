##### The ingredients needed to cook Sweet pickled vegetables Japanese style:

1. Provide 5 Cucumber
1. Take 100 g Carrots
1. Provide 200 g Bell pepper
1. Get 300 g Daikon radish
1. You need 30 g Ginger
1. Provide 2 Chili pepper
1. Take  (seasoned)
1. Provide 500 cc Water
1. Take 50 g Salt
1. Provide  (Pickling sauce)
1. You need 1 L Water
1. You need 500 g Sugar
1. Get 200 cc Vinegar


##### Instructions to make Sweet pickled vegetables Japanese style:

1. Cut vegetables with bite size pieces. Peel and shred ginger, take seeds out and slice round chili pepper
1. Place all the vegetables except ginger and chili pepper in a bowl with seasoned liquid to take out extra liquid from vegetables, 2 hours later drain them in a strainer, wash them lightly under running water to take out an extra salt and drain again
1. Boil 1L of water and 500g sugar (2:1 ratio) in a pot, let it cool and add vinegar, this is a sauce for pickles
1. Mix well drained vegetables, ginger and chili pepper and pour pickling sauce, leave it in a fridge. It can be eaten from next day to 5 days
1. If tomatoes are used, prick them so they can be picked well. I made capellini with left over pickles and it was delicious.
