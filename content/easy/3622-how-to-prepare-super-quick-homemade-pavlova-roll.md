##### The ingredients needed to prepare Pavlova roll:

1. You need  pavlova roll
1. Prepare 4 egg whites
1. Take 3/4 cup sugar
1. Get 1 1/2 tsp vinegar
1. Take 1 tsp vanilla essence
1. Use 1 pinch salt


##### Steps to make Pavlova roll:

1. Beat egg whites and salt until quite stiff,add sugar and beat until dissolved.
1. Fold in vanilla cornflour and vinegar.
1. Put in lamington tray 23x30cm lined with baking paper
1. Sprinkle with one teaspoon of cinnamon and one of sugar
1. Cook 10min on 375°F or 190c then let cool.
1. Fill with whipped cream and fruit or a peppermint crisp and cream  and roll up.
