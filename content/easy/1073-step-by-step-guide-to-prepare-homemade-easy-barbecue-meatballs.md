##### The ingredients needed to cook Easy Barbecue Meatballs:

1. Get 1 packages frozen meatballs (regular or Italian)
1. Prepare 2 1/2 cup ketchup
1. Use 1 tbsp prepared yellow mustard
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup brown sugar
1. You need 1 small onion, chopped
1. Prepare 1 tsp siracha (optional)


##### Steps to make Easy Barbecue Meatballs:

1. Mix all ingredients, except for meatballs, in crock pot making sure brown sugar mixed through. Add meatballs and stir to coat.Set crock pot on low and heat for 4 hours. Serve as appetizer or with mashed potatoes.
