##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate muffins:

1. You need 1 egg
1. Get 35 caster sugar
1. Use 50 gm butter
1. Prepare 50 gm flour
1. Prepare 1 gm bp
1. You need 1 pinch bs
1. Provide 1 tsp vanilla


##### Instructions to make Chocolate muffins:

1. In a bowl add butter, sugar,beat it like a cream.things should be at the room temperature.
1. Add whisked egg with the help of spoon in little amount many times.
1. Add vanilla essence &amp; mix it.
1. Now add maida, baking powder, and baking soda.
1. We can use other things like chochip,etc.
1. Mix all things,&amp; keep it in the greased mould.
1. Keep it in the preheated oven 180 for 20 mins.
1. After 20 mins take it out &amp; serve it hot or cold.
