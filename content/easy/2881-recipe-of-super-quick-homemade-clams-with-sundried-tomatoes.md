##### The ingredients needed to cook clams with sundried tomatoes:

1. Take  clams
1. Get 2 tbsp unsalted butter
1. Provide 1 tbsp fresh basil
1. Get 2 tbsp sliced sundried tomatoes
1. Provide 1 pepper
1. Get 1 red pepper flake
1. Provide 1 salt
1. You need 3/4 cup white wine
1. Use 2 tbsp minced garlic
1. Provide 2 lb little neck clams


##### Instructions to make clams with sundried tomatoes:

1. saute butter, clams,tomatoes pinch of red pepper flake and garlic till garlic starts to brown
1. cooking over med heat add wine and reduce till all clams open and broth is reduced by half
1. finish with chopped fresh basil and also use as garnish
