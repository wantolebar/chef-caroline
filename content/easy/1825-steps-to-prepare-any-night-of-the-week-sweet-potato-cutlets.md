##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet potato cutlets:

1. Get 200 gms steam boiled sweet potatoes (mashed)
1. Use 1 tbsp corn flour
1. Provide 1 tbsp rice flour
1. Take 1 tsp chopped green chillies
1. Get 1 tsp grated ginger
1. Use 1 tsp red chilli powder
1. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1. Provide 1 tsp dry mango powder (amchur)
1. You need 1 tsp chat masala
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Take  Oil for frying
1. You need leaves Freshly chopped coriander
1. Prepare  Chat masala for sprinkling
1. Use 8-9 Cashew halves


##### Instructions to make Sweet potato cutlets:

1. Be ready with all the ingredients.
1. Mix all the ingredients except oil.
1. Divide the mixture into small portions. Make round cutlets or tikkis from these portions.
1. Press the cashew halves on the cutlets.
1. Deep fry them on medium flame till golden brown and crisp.
1. Drain on a tissue paper.
1. Sprinkle chat masala and freshly chopped coriander leaves. Serve immediately.
