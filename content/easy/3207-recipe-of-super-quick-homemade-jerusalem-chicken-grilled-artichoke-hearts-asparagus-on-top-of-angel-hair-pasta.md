##### The ingredients needed to cook Jerusalem chicken, grilled artichoke hearts, Asparagus on top of angel hair pasta:

1. Use to taste Salt and pepper
1. Get 1/4 Lemon juice for chicken
1. Use 1 handful pasta
1. Provide 1/4 cup olive oil
