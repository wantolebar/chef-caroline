##### The ingredients needed to cook Three Cheese Dip:

1. Use 1/3 cup Mild Cheddar (shredded)
1. Get 1/3 cup Vermont Cheddar (shredded)
1. Use 1/3 cup Monterey Jack (shredded)
1. Provide 1/2 cup milk


##### Instructions to make Three Cheese Dip:

1. Mix all cheeses.
1. Microwave for 90 seconds
1. Add milk.
