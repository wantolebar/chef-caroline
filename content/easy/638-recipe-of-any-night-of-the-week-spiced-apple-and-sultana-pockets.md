##### The ingredients needed to cook Spiced Apple and Sultana Pockets:

1. Take  apples
1. Provide  butter
1. Prepare  maple syrup
1. You need  caster sugar
1. Provide  mixed spice
1. You need  sultanas
1. Prepare  slivered almonds (optional)
1. Use  sheets butter puff pastry
1. Prepare  egg lightly beaten
1. You need  icing sugar to dust


##### Steps to make Spiced Apple and Sultana Pockets:

1. Peel and core apples, then finely chop flesh. melt butter in large frying pan and cook apple, stirring occasionally for about 15mins, or until browned lightly.
1. Add maple syrup, sugar and spice. Cook, stirring for about 5 mins, or until liquid boils and caramelizes. Transfer mixture to a medium heatproof bowl then stir in sultanas and nuts. Cool for 20 mins.
1. Meanwhile preheat oven to 220deg Celsius (or 200deg fan)
1. Divide pastry sheets into 6 squares and spoon approx 1/4 cup apple and sultana mixture onto each sheet. With each square, fold pastry from corner to corner to create a triangle shape and press edges together with a fork to seal. When done, brush the top of pastries with beaten egg using a pastry brush.
1. Bake parcels for about 20 mins or until golden. Dust with sifted icing sugar (confectionery sugar) and serve with ice cream
