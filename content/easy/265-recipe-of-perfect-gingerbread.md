##### Steps to make Gingerbread:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
1. Grease bottom only of 9&#34; round pan or spray muffin tin with pan spray
1. In large bowl combine flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and ginger; mix well.
1. Add remaining ingredients; blend well. Pour into greased pan, or fill muffin tins 2/3 full.
1. Bake at 350°F for 25-35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
