##### The ingredients needed to prepare Carrot cake:

1. You need 150 gr flour
1. You need 100 gr brown sugar
1. Provide 2 eggs
1. You need 120 gr olive oil
1. Use 10 gr baking powder
1. Take 200 gr grated carrots (3-4 carrots)
1. You need 1 lemon and grated skin
1. Prepare  for the syrup:
1. Take 1 lemon
1. Prepare 80 gr brown sugar
1. You need 25 ml water


##### Instructions to make Carrot cake:

1. Mix eggs and sugar and stir
1. Mix with lemon, lemon skin, baking powder, oil and fluour
1. Bake for 40 minutes (180C)
1. 10 minutes before cook, prepare the syrup
1. Cook in small fire all ingredients for the syrup
1. When the cake done, stick all around with fork and put the syrup on the cake
1. Ready to serve!!
