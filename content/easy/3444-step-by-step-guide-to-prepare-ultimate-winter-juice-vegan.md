##### The ingredients needed to prepare Winter Juice (Vegan):

1. Take 15 grams Ingwer
1. You need 120 grams Beetroot (cooked)
1. Provide 3 Oranges
1. Take 400 ml Apple-Carrot Juice
1. Provide 1 tbsp Linseed Oil
1. Provide 1 tbsp Maple Syrup


##### Instructions to make Winter Juice (Vegan):

1. Peel ingwer and grate finely. Slice beetroot and peel oranges. Remove white skin completely and slice orange into pieces.
1. Puree oranges, beetroot and ingwer. Add apple-carrot juice, linseed oil and maple syrup to taste.
1. Serve cool.
