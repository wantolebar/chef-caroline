##### The ingredients needed to make Oven Roasted Potatoes:

1. Use 1 pound new white potatoes
1. Provide As needed kosher salt
1. Take As needed ground black pepper
1. Prepare As needed granulated garlic powder
1. You need 1 tablespoon extra Virgin olive oil


##### Steps to make Oven Roasted Potatoes:

1. Preheat the oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Heat an oven safe skillet with nonstick spray in it.
1. Wash and dry the potatoes. Cut them in half.
1. Don&#39;t let them sit long in the air.
1. Coat each piece well with olive oil.
1. Gently sprinkle the seasonings over the top.
1. Sear the potatoes in the skillet on the cut side down for 2 minutes.
1. Turn them cut side up.
1. Put them in the oven for 10 minutes or till fork tender.
1. Serve with the pork cop sauce from my recipe Salt and Pepper Porkchops.
