##### The ingredients needed to prepare Rosemary asiago chicken kabobs:

1. Provide  olive oil
1. Prepare  Annie’s creamy Asiago cheese dressing
1. Use  Worcestershire sauce
1. Get  fresh rosemary, leaves finely chopped
1. Take  salt
1. Take  lemon juice
1. Provide  white vinegar
1. You need  fresh ground black pepper
1. Use  white sugar
1. Provide  boneless skinless chicken breasts, cubed


##### Instructions to make Rosemary asiago chicken kabobs:

1. In a bowl stir together all ingredients except chicken until well blended. Let stand 5 minutes.
1. Add sauce to gallon size zip lock bag, then add cubes chicken. Zip shut.
1. Refrigerate and let marinade for 30 mins to 2-3 hours.
1. Thread chicken onto skewers and discard marinade.
1. Either cook kabobs on grill or on flat iron over stove until chicken is no longer pink in center.
1. All done!
