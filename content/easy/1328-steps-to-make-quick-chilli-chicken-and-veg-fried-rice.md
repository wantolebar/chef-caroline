##### Instructions to make Chilli chicken and veg fried rice:

1. FRY the Marinated chicken deep fry untill cooked
1. Now prepare sauce take red chilli dry, chopped garlic saute it i a pan in oil.
1. Put soy sauce, red chilli sauce, vinegar, an black pepper and the cooked chicken give a good toss and cook it
1. Add the cornflour slurry to make the sauce thickened check out the salt, add if needed.
1. For fried rice : boil the rice and cook it keep ready
1. In a walk take oil, chopped garlic and green chilli to the pot and saute it
1. Add onion, carrots, capsicum sliced into the walk on high flame for 2 mins.
1. Put soy sauce 1tbsp vinegar 2tbp, red chilli auce 2tbsp black pepper powder salt if needed and add the boiled rice mix it well
1. Sprinkle the chopped spring onion andcook for 5 min on low flame untill rice is cooked to it&#39;s perfection.
