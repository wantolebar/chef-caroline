##### The ingredients needed to make Buns with ovacado,beetrootpaste n cucumber and fruit juice:

1. Provide 150 g all purpose flour
1. Use 4 ginstant yeast
1. Get 15 g sugar
1. Use 3 tbs oil
1. Use  Warm water
1. Prepare 1 egg york
1. Get Pinch salt
1. Get  For the paste
1. You need  Ovacado
1. Use  Beetroot
1. Provide  Cucumber


##### Instructions to make Buns with ovacado,beetrootpaste n cucumber and fruit juice:

1. Measures your ingredients, and mix dry ingredients apart from water in a bowl.then mix them and add water slowly until it forms a dough knead it for about 5 to 10 min.
1. Oil your bowl,place your dough and place it in a warm place for it to rise,
1. After it rise take it and divide into four balls and roll a little the place in a baking tin then leave them to rise once more then wash the upper part with the egg york and bake them till golden brown
1. So make your ovacado paste grate your beetroot slice the cucumber and mash the ovacado add salt and mix then and prepared them as in the pictures and enjoy.
