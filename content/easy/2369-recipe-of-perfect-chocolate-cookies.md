##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Cookies:

1. Get  granulated sugar
1. Take  + 1/2 cups flour
1. You need  large egg (cold)
1. Provide  cocoa powder
1. Get  chopped chocolate (milk or bitter)
1. Get  cold and chopped butter
1. Provide  baking powder
1. Prepare  Icing sugar (for decoration)


##### Instructions to make Chocolate Cookies:

1. Mix sugar and butter until they get soft. Add egg, flour, cocoa powder and baking powder. Mix well.
1. Add chopped chocolates and keep mixing.
1. Preheat oven 180°c and make circle shapes with your hand. Bake for 10 mins. Sprinkle icing sugar on tops.
