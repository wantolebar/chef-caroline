1. Prepare 2 teaspoons coca powder

1. Provide 2 teaspoons sugar
1. You need 1/4 cup milk
1. Use  cashew nut chopped


##### Steps to make Chocolate Cup cakes:

1. Sieve the flour, baking powder, soda-bi-carbonate, brown color and coca powder.
1. Mix sugar and butter, till light and fluffy. Add the condensed milk and beat well, Mix milk and vanilla essence.
1. Add the flour mixture in parts to the butter-sugar mixture alternately with milk. Beat the batter until it is smooth after each addition.
1. Fold them lightly into the cake batter. Pour into prepared molds or paper till thirds full and bake at 190degree for 15minutes.
1. Cool on a wire rack before DE-molding. cups.
1. For sauce Mix all ingredients of sauce, beat till mixture become smooth, now dip prepared cake and remove, sprinkle some chopped cashew nut.
1. Serve it.
