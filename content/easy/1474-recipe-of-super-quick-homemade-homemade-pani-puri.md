##### The ingredients needed to make Homemade Pani puri:

1. Use 1 packages puri (uncooked)
1. Prepare  mashed potatoes
1. You need 4 potatoes (boiled)
1. You need 2 medium onion, chopped
1. Prepare 1/2 grams peanuts gridded
1. Use 1 bunch coriander leaves chopped
1. Prepare  salt n pepper
1. Get 1/2 tsp chat masala
1. Take 1/2 tbsp ground cumin
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp chili powder
1. Prepare  dipping puri
1. Prepare 1 liter water
1. Take 3 tbsp everest pani puri masala


##### Steps to make Homemade Pani puri:

1. Fry those small puri until it becomes round.
1. Mash the boiled potatoes and add these ingredients.
1. In a bowl, pour water and put pani puri masala and stir well. .
1. Break upper part of puri with ur finger to make a hole and stuff mashed potatoes.  Dip it into the liquid and have it.
