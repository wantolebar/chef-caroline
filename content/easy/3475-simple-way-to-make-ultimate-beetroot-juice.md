##### The ingredients needed to cook Beetroot juice:

1. Get 1 small beetroot
1. Use 1/2 apple
1. Use 1/2 carrot
1. Take 1/2 tomato
1. Use 1/2 inches ginger
1. You need to taste Salt
1. You need 1/4 tsp black salt
1. Take 1/2 tsp chaat masala
1. You need 1/4 tsp roasted cumin powder


##### Instructions to make Beetroot juice:

1. Wash all carrot, beetroot, apple, tomato, ginger nicely. Now peel the beetroot and cut it in small pieces. And cut the other things also.
1. Now add these all in the mixer jar. Then mix salt, black salt, and chaat masala in it. Now put 1 1/2 glass water in it. Then mix them nicely.
1. Then sieve it nicely.
1. Then add little chaat masala on top of the juice.
1. Healthy beetroot juice is ready.
