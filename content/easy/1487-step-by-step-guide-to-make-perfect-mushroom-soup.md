##### The ingredients needed to make Mushroom soup:

1. Take 1 cup slices mushrooms
1. You need 1/2 tbsp cream
1. You need 1 tbsp butter
1. Get as needed Water
1. Provide to taste Salt
1. Use 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
1. Take as needed Thyme herb
1. Provide 2 garlic cloves


##### Steps to make Mushroom soup:

1. Take a pan add butter than add garlic cook for 3 minutes than add mushrooms than add mushrooms cook well till mushrooms get softened than add water cook whole mix for 10 minutes
1. Now blend whole mix now seive whole mix than add salt,pepper and thyme mix well and cook it for 5 more minutes
1. Serve hot
1. You can serve soup with mushroom bread
