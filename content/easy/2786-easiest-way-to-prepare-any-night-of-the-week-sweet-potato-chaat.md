##### The ingredients needed to cook Sweet potato Chaat:

1. Take 4 Sweet potato (boiled / baked)
1. You need 1 Finely chopped onion
1. You need 1 Finely chopped tomato
1. Use 1 Finely chopped green chilli
1. You need 2 tablespoons Beaten curd
1. You need 1/4 teaspoon Roasted cumin powder
1. You need 1 pinch Black salt
1. Take to taste Rock salt
1. You need 1/4 Teaspoon Dry Mint Powder
1. Prepare 1-1 1/2 teaspoons Tamarind sauce


##### Instructions to make Sweet potato Chaat:

1. Boil sweet potato in the cooker or bake in the oven, then cool it down. - peel and cut it into a bowl.
1. Now add onions, tomatoes, green chilies, curd, tamarind sauce and all dried spices and mix them with the help of a spoon.
1. Tasty and healthy sweet potato chaat is ready to eat.
