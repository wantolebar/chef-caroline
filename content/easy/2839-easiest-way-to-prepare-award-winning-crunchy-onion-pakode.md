##### The ingredients needed to make Crunchy onion pakode:

1. Take 2 onions roughly chopped
1. Prepare 1 cup gram flour(besan)
1. Get 2 tablespoons rice flour
1. Get 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
1. Take 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri red chilli powder
1. Provide 1/2 tsp salt
1. Take  Oil for frying
1. Take  Water as required


##### Steps to make Crunchy onion pakode:

1. Take all the ingredients except and onions the oil in a bowl and make a batter by adding water as required.
1. Batter should be lump free and not of running consistency.
1. Heat oil for frying
1. Add chopped onions in the prepared batter and mix well
1. Take little portions and drop them into the hot oil.  -  Keep the heat on medium
1. When slightly cooked, turn over and increase the heat
1. Fry the pakodas till crisp and then take it out
1. Serve hot onion pakore with chutney or sauce
