##### The ingredients needed to make Chickpea “tuna” salad:

1. Use 1 can cooked chickpeas/garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
1. Get 1/2 cup celery
1. Use 1/2 cup onion
1. Prepare 2 Tbs capers
1. You need 2 tbs green onion
1. Provide 1/4 cup mayo (I use avocado oil mayo)
1. Provide Squeeze lime or lemon juice to taste
1. Provide to taste Salt pepper


##### Instructions to make Chickpea “tuna” salad:

1. Pulse beans twice in pulser or blender. You Want it to have some texture still and not super smooth
1. Mix all ingredients and serve on toast or saltines!
1. Yum!
