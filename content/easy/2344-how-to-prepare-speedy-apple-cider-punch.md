##### The ingredients needed to cook Apple Cider Punch:

1. Provide 1 quart Apple cider, chilled
1. Use 1 1/4 cup Spiced Rum
1. Provide 2 tbsp Fresh lemon juice
1. Get 1 Red apple, thinly sliced


##### Instructions to make Apple Cider Punch:

1. In a large pitcher or punch bowl, combine the apple cider, rum and lemon juice. Chill for up to 12 hours. Serve over ice and garnish with the apple.
