##### The ingredients needed to make potato and ham soup:

1. Use 2 medium red potatoe
1. Provide 2 medium russet potatoe
1. Prepare 1 large baking potatoe ( leftovers if you have one )
1. Get 6 slice thick cut bacon
1. Use 2 medium slices of country ham ( bone in )
1. Provide 1/2 cup frozen onion mix
1. Use 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Take 4 cup chicken broth
1. Get 1/2 cup half &amp; half
1. Provide 1/2 cup water


##### Steps to make potato and ham soup:

1. heat up 1 tablespoon of olive oil in stock pot, cut  bacon and ham into chunks  cook in stock pot until brown add onion mix and cook until tender. add chicken stock and simmer cut up red potatoes in bite size pieces. cut up russet  potatoes in medium size pieces. add to chicken broth and simmer until tender
1. add water and half and half and baking potato ( chopped into chunks )  in to soup mash down soup mixture with a potato masher about 3 or 4 times leaving plenty of chunks season  with your favorite seasoning. simmer for about 20 to 25 minutes.
