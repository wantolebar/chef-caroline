##### The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate muffin 🍮:

1. Get 1 1/2 cup maida
1. Prepare 1 cup sugar
1. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
1. Use 1/3 cup oil
1. Use 1/2 tsp baking soda
1. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
1. Prepare 1 tsp coffee powder
1. Prepare 1 tbsp cocoa powder
1. Provide  Choco chips as per need
1. Provide 1 tbsp vanilla essence
1. You need 1 tbsp vinegar


##### Instructions to make Chocolate muffin 🍮:

1. Ek bowl mein milk, oil, vanilla essence aur sugar ko Mila le
1. Ab Ek dusre bowl mein maida, coffee powder, cocoa powder, baking soda aur baking powder ko Mila le
1. Ab doodh wale aur Maida wale mixture ko apas mein Mila le
1. Ab isme vinegar daale aur 5 min ke lie rakh de
1. Oven ko 180°C par 10 min ke lie preheat karein
1. Ab muffin moulds mein paper cups rakhe aur isme tayaar Kiya Mixture daale ab isme upar se choco chips sprinkle Karein.
1. Ab inko 25 mins ke lie 180°C par bake karein
