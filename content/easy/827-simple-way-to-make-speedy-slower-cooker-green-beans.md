##### The ingredients needed to cook Slower Cooker Green Beans:

1. Take 3-4 (14.5 oz) Cans of Green Beans or (fresh Green Beans)
1. Take 3-4 slices uncooked bacon
1. Get 1/3 Cup Onions chopped
1. Take to taste Salt and Pepper
1. Take 1/2 Stick butter


##### Steps to make Slower Cooker Green Beans:

1. If using Fresh Green Beans destring and clean Green Beans.
1. Put Green Beans (fresh or canned)(Do not drain Green Beans if using canned) in Slow Cooker. Add salt, pepper, onions, Bacon, and butter. If using Fresh green beans add 2 Cups of water.
1. Cover and cook on High for 2 Hours.
