##### Instructions to make Cinnamon Rolls:

1. preheat milk to 115°F, then wisk in yeast.
1. add butter once yeast is active.
1. combine all dry ingredients, then add slowly to mixer on low speed until all items form a smooth dough.
1. cover with a towel, and set aside in a warm place for an 1 1/2hrs
1. combine filling, and mix into a smooth paste.
1. roll dough out on a flowered surface (do not fold dough)
1. spread paste in a smooth even layer on dough and remainder in 2 9×13 baking pans
1. preheat oven at 400°F
1. roll dough into pinwheels adding flour as needed. stretch into even log.
1. cut into 1&#34; sections and place evenly into baking pans. ( makes 24 )
1. bake for 17-20 min at 400
1. should be a light golden brown
1. flip over on wax paper
