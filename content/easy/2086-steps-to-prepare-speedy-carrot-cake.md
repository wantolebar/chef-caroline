##### The ingredients needed to cook Carrot Cake:

1. Get 1/2 cup unsalted butter
1. Use 1/3 cup sugar
1. Take 1/2 cup brown sugar or demerara
1. Prepare 2 medium eggs
1. Provide 1 tsp bakin powder
1. Use 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1. Take 1 cup wholemeal self-raising flour
1. You need 1/2 cup self-raising flour
1. You need 1 orange or satsuma juice and zest
1. Use 1 -2 cups finely grated carrot


##### Instructions to make Carrot Cake:

1. pre-heat oven to 180°c
1. cream butter and sugars in a bowl till soft
1. mix in eggs one at a time then add the orange juice and zest
1. then add the baking powder and cinnamon and stir in
1. stir in both flours a bit at a time and then add the carrot
1. pour into a cake tin or cases for individual cakes. put into pre-heated oven and bake till risen and springs bake when touched or till scewer comes out clean
