##### The ingredients needed to prepare Ginger Tea with Apple Cider Vinegar:

1. Get 1 Black tea (tea bag)

1. You need 1/2 tsp ☆ Grated ginger
1. Provide 2 tsp ☆ Honey or sugar
1. Take 2 tsp ☆ Apple cider vinegar


##### Instructions to make Ginger Tea with Apple Cider Vinegar:

1. Put hot water and tea bag in a mug, cover, then steep for about 1 minute.
1. Add the ☆ ingredients, mix well, then serve.
