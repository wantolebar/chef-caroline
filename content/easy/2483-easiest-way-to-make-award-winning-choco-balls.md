##### The ingredients needed to prepare Choco balls:

1. Provide 1 cup Oreo biscuits
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Chocolate
1. Get as needed Milk
1. Prepare handful crushed gems


##### Steps to make Choco balls:

1. Grind all the oreos
1. Add 1 tsp chocolate syrup add little little milk and knead the dough
1. Make balls of them
1. Melt the chocolate and dip the balls in the chocolate
1. Put them in a freezer for 15 minutes
1. Now decorate them With crushed gems your balls are ready To serve
