##### The ingredients needed to cook Chicken and Eggs Bowl (Oyakodon):

1. Use 1 piece chicken thigh
1. Use 4 eggs
1. Prepare 1/2 onion
1. Get 160 mL Dashi broth (otherwise chicken soup) : (A)
1. You need 2 tbsp soy sauce : (A)
1. Take 3 tbsp Sake (otherwise white wine) : (A)
1. Get 2 tbsp sugar : (A)
1. Use 400 g freshly cooked rice


##### Instructions to make Chicken and Eggs Bowl (Oyakodon):

1. Thinly cut the onion into wedges. Cut the chicken into bite-size chunks.
1. Put the condiments (A) in a pan. Heat it until it comes to a boil.
1. Add the onion and simmer over medium heat for 2 minutes. Then, add the chicken and simmer over medium heat again for about 3 minutes.
1. Beat eggs lightly. Then add 2/3 of them to the pan. Turn the heat down and simmer for 2 minutes with a lid. Use two pairs of chopsticks, and don&#39;t beat too much.
1. Add the rest of beaten eggs and boil at medium heat for 10 seconds.
1. Put them over the freshly cooked rice.
