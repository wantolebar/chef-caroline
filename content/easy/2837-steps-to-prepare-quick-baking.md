##### Instructions to make Baking:

1. In a bowl,add your butter and sugar,cream until fluffy and double in size
1. In another ball,you sift and mix all your dry ingredients
1. After creaming butter and sugar,you add your egg one after the other and mix
1. Add your dry ingredients to the wet ingredients bit by bit
1. Do not over mix
1. Rub butter on your cake mould and make sure your oven is preheated for 15mins
1. Put the cake inside oven and bake at 175°C for 30mins
