##### The ingredients needed to cook Cranberry-Jalapeno Cream Cheese Dip:

1. Use  Dip
1. Prepare 12 oz fresh cranberries
1. Take 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1. Get 1 each jalapeno, seeded &amp; finely diced
1. Prepare 1 cup sugar (more/less to taste)
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp cumin
1. Prepare 2 tbsp lemon juice
1. Take 4 each (or 5) diced green onions
1. You need 1 tsp (1/8) salt
1. Take 2 packages cream cheese (softened)
1. You need 1 packages Ritz Crackers


##### Instructions to make Cranberry-Jalapeno Cream Cheese Dip:

1. Pulse cranberries until coarsely chopped
1. Add other ingredients and pulse until well combined and finely chopped
1. Transfer to covered bowl and refrigerate at least 4 hours
1. Spread cream cheese on serving plate or pie dish. Top with Dip.
1. Refrigerate for ~1 hour before serving
