##### The ingredients needed to prepare Airfryer Roasted Potatoes:

##### Steps to make Airfryer Roasted Potatoes:

1. Rinse potatoes and dry them. Cut each potato in quarters.
1. Place potatoes in a plastic bag. Add olive oil, salt and Italian seasoning. Shake bag to coat potatoes.
1. Preheat airfryer 5 minutes at 390째 Place potatoes in airfryer single layer. Cook for 25 minutes at 390째 During cooking time turn potatoes over every 5 minutes. Serve with ranch dressing on the side. Enjoy!
