##### The ingredients needed to prepare Cottage cheese and spinach pie:

1. Provide 400 grams cottage cheese
1. Take 300 grams spinach
1. You need 5 eggs
1. Get 180 grams flour self raising
1. Prepare 40 grams butter
1. Prepare 1 dill
1. Provide 1 spring onions


##### Steps to make Cottage cheese and spinach pie:

1. Beat ONE egg with softened butter with mixer, add flour and mix untill it falls into crumbles. Put more flour if there are no crumbles.
1. Pour the crumbles onto the greased pan. Pat the crumbles slightly so they are not falling everywhere. Then put spinach over it. I used frozen spinach, poured boiling water over them to soften first then soaked the water.
1. I blended cottage cheese with FOUR eggs, dill and spring onions in a food processor and poured the mix over spinach, smooth the surface with a spoon and  bake in the oven for 30-40 min.
1. Serve with fromage frache, Greek or Turkish yogurt.
