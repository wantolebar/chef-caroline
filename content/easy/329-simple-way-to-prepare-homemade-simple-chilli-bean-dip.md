##### The ingredients needed to prepare Simple chilli bean dip:

1. Prepare 1 can chilli w/ beans
1. Use 1 packages cream cheese
1. Get 2 cup shredded chedder
1. Take 1 jar salsa


##### Instructions to make Simple chilli bean dip:

1. preheat oven to 350°
1. spread entire can of chilli on the bottom of the baking dish
1. put cream cheese on a safe plate and microwave until soft
1. spread soften cream cheese over chilli
1. add a half of your chedder
1. for the next layer put your desired amount of salsa
1. top with the rest of the chedder - bake for 20 minutes
