##### The ingredients needed to prepare Oops! Mexican Pepper Cheese Dip:

1. Provide 6 oz cream cheese (spreadable)
1. Take 1 cup extra sharp Vermont white cheddar, finely grated
1. Take 1/3 cup mayo
1. Prepare 2 tbs pickled jalapeño, diced
1. Use 1 tbs fresh jalapeño, diced
1. Prepare 1/2 tbs fresh habanero, diced
1. You need 1/2 tbs fresh serrano, diced
1. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
1. Get 1 tsp onion powder
1. Provide 1 tsp paprika
1. Use 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1. You need 1/2 tsp salt


##### Instructions to make Oops! Mexican Pepper Cheese Dip:

1. Mix all ingredients in a mixing bowl and refrigerate for about an hour. Serve with crackers or tortilla chips and ENJOY!
