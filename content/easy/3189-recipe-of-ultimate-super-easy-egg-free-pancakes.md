##### The ingredients needed to prepare Super Easy Egg-free Pancakes:

1. Use 200 grams Pancake mix
1. Provide 180 ml Milk


##### Steps to make Super Easy Egg-free Pancakes:

1. Put the pancake mix and milk into a bowl. Use a whisk to mix the ingredients together.
1. Warm a nonstick frying pan. Let it cool atop a damp cloth for a short time, then return it to the heat set to medium.
1. Ladle 1 scoop of the pancake batter from Step 1 into the frying pan from Step 2. When the batter begins to bubble, flip over and cook for about 2 minutes.
1. Done.
