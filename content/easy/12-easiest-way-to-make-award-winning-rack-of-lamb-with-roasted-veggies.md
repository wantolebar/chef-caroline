##### The ingredients needed to make Rack of Lamb with roasted veggies:

1. Provide  lamb, 600gr each
1. Use  floury potato
1. Take  small onions
1. Use  garlic
1. Provide  rosemary
1. Provide  Olive oil, salt and pepper, mustard


##### Instructions to make Rack of Lamb with roasted veggies:

1. Preheat the sous vide machine to 57*c. Vacuum two racks per bag with a clove of garlic, teaspoon of mustard and rosemary. Cook for 1-2h (until vegetables are done)
1. Cut potato in wedges and cook in water on low heat for 15min
1. Drain potatoes well, put in a roasting pan with quarteres onions and remaining unpeeled garlic. Cover all in olive oil, season and roast on 220*c for 30min
