##### Instructions to make Caramel Pudding:

1. Mix all the ingredients together for the pudding together. Do not whisk.
1. For caramel, put sugar into a pan. On low heat, mix continuously so the sugar doesn’t burn.
1. Sieve the pudding on top of the caramel. Do not mix.
1. Steam for 45 minutes.
1. Chill in the fridge. Once chilled, put a plate on top, &amp; flip so that the caramel will be on top.
