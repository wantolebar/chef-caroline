##### The ingredients needed to make Marigold biscuits cake (without baking):

1. Prepare 8-10 Marie gold biscuits (1packet)
1. Take 1 tbsp Cocoa powder
1. Use As required powdered sugar
1. Take 1 tspn coffee powder
1. Prepare 1 cup Cold milk
1. You need 5 tsp Ghee


##### Steps to make Marigold biscuits cake (without baking):

1. In a bowl, add 1cup milk, Add 2 tsp sugar powder,1/2 tsp coffee mix it well.
1. Dip the biscuits and stack it one by one (keep one upon another make like a pillar)10 biscuits in 1 stack.
1. Add 8 tsp powder sugar in a bowl,4tspn Cocoa powder, mix it well.
1. Add melted ghee slowly and mix it.
1. Now coat of this mixture in biscuits stack.
1. Put in refrigerator for 1 hr, after 1 hr take out from the refrigerator and cut in slices.
