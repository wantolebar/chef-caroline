##### The ingredients needed to cook Burmese Curry:

1. Take  pork

1. Take  garlic
1. Use  shallots
1. Provide  gingers,sliced

1. Provide  fish sauce
1. Provide  tamarind puree
1. Get  dark or sweet soya sauce
1. Take  sugar cane
1. You need  water


##### Instructions to make Burmese Curry:

1. Chop pork and pork belly into 3 inches pices,marinate 1 hour with chilli paste and hinley powder
1. You need to find a large stew pot ,or 2 medium size ,..set on low heat,brown marinate pork and pork belly until no longer pink,keep stir to the bottom ,to protect from burning and get good aroma from herbs in hinley powder
1. Add water ,only enough to cover all pork,may be not use all 4 litres,cook it on medium heat for 30 mins,add peanuts,gingers,lower heat ..and let it simmer about 1 and half hour
1. Seasoning with tamarind puree,dark soya sauce,soya sauce,fish sauce,sugar cane,simmer 20 more mins..now taste it,this must be little spicy,sweet and sour,stew like textures,serve hot with steamed jasmine rice :)
