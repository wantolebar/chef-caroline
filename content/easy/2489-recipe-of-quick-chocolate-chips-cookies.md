##### The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate chips cookies:

1. Use  maida
1. Use  Sugar
1. Use  Vanilla extract
1. Provide  Cocoa powder
1. Provide  Choco chips
1. Provide  Salt
1. Prepare  butter


##### Steps to make Chocolate chips cookies:

1. In a medium bowl sift together maida cocoa and salt.
1. In a large bowl add butter and beat untill it becomes fluffy then add sugar and beat again for 5 min.
1. Add flour mixture vanilla extract and combine together on low speed untill all ingredients mix them properly.
1. Wrap mixture in plastic bag and cool completely for 1 hr.
1. Preheat oven 325f for 15 min.line 2 baking sheet with parchament paper grease with butter.
1. Usa a spoon to form a dough in to balls and placed it on a prepared baking sheets.pinch them slightly with palm so that choco chips stick with them.
1. Bake them until they firm, 20 to 25 min.
1. After removing from baking sheets.Cool completely on a wire wrack.
1. Serve chocolate chips cookies.
