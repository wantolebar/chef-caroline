##### The ingredients needed to cook Leftovers Chicken Stock:

1. Prepare 1 chicken carcass, stripped of meat
1. Provide 2 stalks frozen celery
1. Get 2 frozen spring onions (or use fresh or half an onion)
1. Prepare 5 peppercorns
1. Get Pinch salt
1. Take 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar           (see tip)
1. Get  Baking tray from your roast chicken, with fats and juices saved


##### Steps to make Leftovers Chicken Stock:

1. Break up the carcass with your hands and put it into your stock pot.
1. If you’ve saved your roasting tray, pour boiling water into the tray and use a spatula to scrape the base. Tip it all into your stock pot.
