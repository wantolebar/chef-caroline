##### The ingredients needed to make Apple pie:

1. Provide  full of plain flour
1. Take  margarine
1. Use  Cold water
1. You need  apples
1. Prepare  cinnamon powder


##### Steps to make Apple pie:

1. Mix flour,margarine and sugar rub till well mixed. Add water slowly to make a soft dough, let the dough rest while you work on the apples
1. Cut apples in cubes add 1tspn margarine in a pan, add sugar and cinnamon powder and let it cook till the mixture is dry and caramelized
1. Cut the pastry dough into half, roll the first half and place the apple mixture, roll the second half and place on top. Use a fork to prick and join the sides
1. Bake till browned and let it cool or stay overnight then serve
