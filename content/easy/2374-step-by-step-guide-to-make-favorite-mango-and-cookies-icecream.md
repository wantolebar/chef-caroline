##### The ingredients needed to cook Mango and cookies icecream:

1. Prepare  cream
1. Take  icing sugar
1. Prepare  mango puree
1. You need  condensed milk
1. You need  Oreo cookies


##### Steps to make Mango and cookies icecream:

1. In a bowl take cold cream and whip with icing sugar till soft peaks.
1. Add condensed milk to mango puree and mix well.
1. Fold in the mix of mango puree and condensed milk into the cream slowly.
1. Crush the Oreo cookies roughly and fold these into the cream mix.
1. Pour in a container and keep in freezer for 6-7 hours or until frozen.
1. Serve cold with more cookies.
