##### Instructions to make Indian Style Pasta:

1. Take a bowl, add water and boil it. After boiling water add some oil and salt then mix it.
1. After one boiling add pasta. Now cook the paste until it becomes soft.
1. If your pasta becomes soft take out and wash it with cold water.
1. Now take a pan add some oil and butter. After melting butter add garlic and green chillies and cook for 2 minutes in low flame.
1. Now add onions and corns cook it for 5 minutes after cook onions add tomatoes, turmeric, salt, black pepper, coriander powder and mix it. And roast your Veggies for 10 minutes.
1. Now add capsicum and roast for 2 minutes. Make sure capsicum not to soft.
1. After roasted your Veggies add your pasta and some salt then mix well.
1. Now your indian style pasta is ready. Its also too good.
