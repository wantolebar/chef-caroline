##### The ingredients needed to make Carrot beetroot juice:

1. Take 1 carrot
1. Take 1 beetroot
1. Use 1/2 inch cinnamon stick
1. Prepare 1 tsp ginger
1. Use 4 tsp sugar
1. Use Pinch salt


##### Instructions to make Carrot beetroot juice:

1. Cut the veggies in small pieces.
1. Grind it into a fine juice.
1. Boil 1 cup of water with fresh ginger, sugar and cinnamon. Now let it cool. In a glass mix the juice and the sugar syrup. It is ready to serve.
