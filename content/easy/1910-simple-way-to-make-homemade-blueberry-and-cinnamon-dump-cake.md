##### The ingredients needed to make Blueberry And Cinnamon Dump Cake:

1. Prepare 1 can Blueberry pie filling
1. Take 1 1/4 stick Butter
1. Take 1/4 cup Hershey cinnamon chips
1. Take 1 box DRY yellow cake mix


##### Instructions to make Blueberry And Cinnamon Dump Cake:

1. Preheat oven to 325, if using a glass dish.
1. Spread the blueberry filling on bottom of dish, sprinkle cinnamon chips all over, sprinkle DRY cake mix on top, then layer diced butter all over. Then I sprinkled some cinnamon sugar on top.
1. Baked at 325 for about 45 min. Served with vanilla ice cream and cinnamon sprinkles:)
