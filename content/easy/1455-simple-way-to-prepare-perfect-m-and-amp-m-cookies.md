##### The ingredients needed to prepare M&amp;M Cookies:

1. Provide 100 g unsalted butter
1. Get 50 g granulated sugar
1. Get 100 g brown sugar
1. Provide 1 pinch salt
1. Get 1 Egg
1. Get 150 g cake flour  (1 1/5 U.S. cup)
1. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
1. Use 50 g M&amp;M chocolate
1. You need 3 Tbsp mixed nuts
1. Take to taste Vanilla oil,


##### Instructions to make M&amp;M Cookies:

1. Preheat the oven to 330F. Blend flour and baking soda together with a whisk. Crush the nuts.
1. Microwave butter until it melts. Combine the sugar and butter together. Add the egg, salt and vanilla oil then mix.
1. Combine all powder ingredients with a spatula. Add the nuts.
1. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of cookie dough onto the parchment paper. Put 5 M&amp;M chocolates for each cookie.
1. Bake 6 to 8 min. Don&#39;t over bake! Be careful, it&#39;s very soft.
1. Happy Holiday Baking!
