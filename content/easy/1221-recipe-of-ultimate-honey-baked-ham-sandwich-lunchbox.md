##### The ingredients needed to cook Honey Baked Ham Sandwich Lunchbox:

1. Take 2 slices wholemeal bread
1. Use 2-4 slices honey baked ham (your choice)
1. Take 2 slices cheese (I use cheddar)
1. Use  Some lettuce
1. You need  Grapes
1. Get  Cherry Tomatoes
1. Provide  Mixed nuts


##### Instructions to make Honey Baked Ham Sandwich Lunchbox:

1. Cut bread into halves. Cut ham, lettuce and cheese into slices of similar size to cut bread.
1. Roll ham, lettuce and cheese in a slice of cut bread and hold in place with a toothpick.
1. Wash grapes and cherry tomatoes.
1. Portion nuts and dried fruit in cupcake cup.
1. Arrange everything to fit together in a lunchbox
1. Have a good lunch!!
