##### Instructions to make Home fries:

1. Peel the potatoes and then cut them as well as the broccoli and bacon.
1. Boil the potatoes and broccoli until they can be pierced by a fork.
1. Drain the water.
1. Fry the potatoes with bacon, corn, and cheese using mayonnaise instead of oil in a frying pan, seasoning them with salt, pepper, and consomm?.
1. After that, put them in a heat-resistant plate.
1. Sprinkle cheese on the dish and place it into a microwave oven to melt (about 5 min).
