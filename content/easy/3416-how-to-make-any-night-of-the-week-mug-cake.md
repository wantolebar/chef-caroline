1. Provide 1/4-1/3 cup milk
1. Take 1 cup bakery mix
1. Provide 1/2 tablespoon vanilla
1. Use 1/2 tablespoon sugar
1. Provide 1 tablespoon butter
1. You need 1-1 1/2 tablespoon pancake syrup
1. Take 1/8-1/4 cup farina


##### Instructions to make Mug cake:

1. Put ingredients in mug
1. Mix it together
1. If you need another mug cuz there’s too much then get another mug

1. Put knife in cake to make sure it’s all done. It should come out all clean.
