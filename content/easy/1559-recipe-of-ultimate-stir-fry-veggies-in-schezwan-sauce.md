##### The ingredients needed to prepare Stir fry veggies in schezwan sauce:

1. Provide  olive oil
1. Use  garlic


##### Instructions to make Stir fry veggies in schezwan sauce:

1. Cut all the vegetables as mentioned or as per liking - Steam them in boiling hot water for 4-5 minutes  - Don’t overdo - They shouldn’t lose the crunch
1. Now strain vegetables in a colander and keep in ice-cold water. This will help in maintaining the colour.
1. Take oil in a pan add chopped garlic and sauté.
1. Add vegetable and toss - Add salt to taste and schezwan sauce too.  - Keep tossing to avoid sticking to the bottom
1. In the last add sesame seeds and toss one more time - Serve hot
