##### The ingredients needed to prepare Club sandwich:

1. Get  chicken boneless

1. Prepare  Red chilli powder
1. Get  Salt as per taste
1. Take  Soya sauce
1. Provide  Wosterchire sauce
1. Get  Garlic powder
1. Take  Paprika
1. Take  Egg
1. Use  Milk
1. Get  Salad
1. Take  Tomato
1. Take  Cucumber
1. Prepare  Bread slices 1 bread
1. Prepare  Butter to toast bread
1. Use  Oil
1. Provide  Mayonaise for spreading


##### Steps to make Club sandwich:

1. Boil chicken with salt and pepper. Once boiled, shred chicken and saute in little oil, add garlic powder, paprika, salt, red chilli powder and mix all. Now add soya sauce, wosterchire sauce, mayonaise and ketchup
1. Apply butter on bread and toast till crispy.
1. Whisk egg with salt pepper and 1tbsp milk and make omlette.
1. For assembling spread mayonaise on bread than spread chicken filling, add another bread and spread mayonaise, add egg cucumber and onion then add another bread. Your sandwich is ready. Serve with french fries and coleslaw.
