##### The ingredients needed to cook Grilled Fish:

1. Prepare 2 Fish Filet  (Indian Salmon, Sea Bass, or Seer)
1. Take 2 Teaspoons White wine vinegar
1. Get 1/2 Teaspoon Gingergarlic paste
1. Provide 1/2 Teaspoon Thyme Dried
1. Get 1/2 Teaspoon Rosemary Dried
1. Take 1/2 Teaspoon Basil Dried
1. You need To Taste Salt
1. Use 1 Teaspoon Olive oil to   brush   on   the   grill   surface


##### Steps to make Grilled Fish:

1. Lightly mix all the spices above.
1. Marinate the fish filet with the mix and let it rest for 1/2 an hour.
1. Switch the grill ON and let it heat at the default pre-heat setting. (3 mins, approx.)
1. Grease the grill surface (both base and lid) lightly with a brush dipped in olive oil.
1. Place the fish on the base and close the lid ensuring that it doesn&#39;t touch the top of the filet. Your filet should be grilled in 3-4 mins.
1. (Optional) If your grill doesn&#39;t have this provision in step 5, keep the lid open. Flip the filet after 3-4 mins and grill for 2-3 mins longer. Et, voila! Ready to eat with lightly buttered, mixed-herb rice or pasta!
