##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Cutlets:

1. Prepare 1.5 Tbsp oil
1. Take 1.5 Tsp mustard seeds
1. Get 15-20 Curry Leaves
1. Take 2 Dried red chillies
1. Provide Pinch asafoetida
1. Provide 1 Finely chopped onion
1. Provide 1 Tbsp sambhar masala
1. Prepare 500 gm chopped Pumpkin
1. Prepare  Salt as per your taste
1. Provide 3/4-1 Cup bread crumbs
1. You need  Semolina for coating
1. Use  Oil for shallow fry


##### Instructions to make Pumpkin Cutlets:

1. Let’s take the pumpkin, peel it and chopped it and boil it until it get soft.
1. Now take the oil in a pan. Once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, dry red chillies, pinch of asafoetida and sauté for a while. Now add finely chopped onion and cook it until onion get soft. Now add boiled pumpkin, samber masala, salt and mix it well.
1. Now mesh the pumpkin and transfer into a bowl. When it get cold the add bread crumbs and make a cutlet shape as per your choice. Now coat cutlet with the semolina and shallow fry them until golden brown.
1. Finally the cutlets are ready and you can serve it with tamarind chutney, red chilli Chutney or green chilli Chutney.
