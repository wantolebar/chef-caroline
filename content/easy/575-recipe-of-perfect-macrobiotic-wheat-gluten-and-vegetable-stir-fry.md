##### The ingredients needed to make Macrobiotic Wheat Gluten and Vegetable Stir-fry:

1. You need 1 medium Eggplant (slim Japanese type)
1. Provide 1/2 Carrot
1. You need 20 cm Burdock root
1. You need 1 Green pepper
1. You need 15 Fu
1. Get 2 tbsp Olive oil
1. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon, minced Garlic
1. Use 1/2 teaspoon, minced Ginger
1. Prepare 1 Salt and pepper
1. Get 1 1/2 tbsp Soy sauce


##### Instructions to make Macrobiotic Wheat Gluten and Vegetable Stir-fry:

1. Roughly chop up the vegetables without peeling. Lightly rehydrate the fu in water. Stir-fry both in olive oil.
1. Adjust the flavor with grated garlic and ginger, a little salt and pepper, and about 1 tablespoon of soy sauce to finish, and it&#39;s complete.
