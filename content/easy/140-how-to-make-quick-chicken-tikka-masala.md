##### The ingredients needed to cook Chicken Tikka Masala:

1. Take  garlic, finely grated
1. Provide  finely grated peeled ginger
1. You need  ground turmeric
1. Provide  garam masala
1. Provide  ground coriander
1. Prepare  ground cumin
1. Provide  whole milk yoghurt
1. You need  salt
1. Prepare  boneless chicken breast, halved lengthwise
1. Provide  vegetable oil
1. You need  onion, thinly sliced
1. You need  tomato paste
1. Use  can whole peeled tomatoes
1. Get  heavy cream
1. Provide  fresh cilantro
1. Get  steamed basmati rice


##### Steps to make Chicken Tikka Masala:

1. Combine garlic, ginger, turmeric, garam masala, coriander and cumin in a small bowl. Whisk yoghurt and half of spice mixture in a medium bowl. Add chicken and turn to coat. Cover and chill for 4-6 hours. Cover and chill remaining spice mixture.
1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, tomato paste, and cook, stirring often until tomato paste has darkened and onion is soft. About 5 minutes. Add remaining half of spice mixture and cook stirring often until bottom of pot begins to brown. About 4 min.
1. Add tomatoes with juices. Bring to a boil,reduce heat and simmer stirring often and scraping up browned bit from bottom of pot until sauce thickens about 8-10 minutes.
1. Add cream and chopped cilantro. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until sauce thickens for about 30-40 minutes.
1. Cut the chicken into bike size pieces and than fry it.
1. Add it to the sauce and simmer for about 8-10 minutes. Serve with rice and cilantro springs.
