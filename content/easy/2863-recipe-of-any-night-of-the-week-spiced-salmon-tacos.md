##### The ingredients needed to make Spiced Salmon Tacos:

1. Prepare 2 salmon steaks
1. You need 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Use 1 tsp fajita seasoning
1. You need 1 teaspoon oregano
1. Get 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1. Take 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1. Prepare 1 teaspoon sugar
1. Get 1 tablespoon lime juice
1. Provide 1 onion diced
1. Provide 1 pepper diced
1. You need  To serve
1. You need 4 Corn taco shells
1. Use 8 Cherry tomatoes halved
1. Provide 2 tbsps coleslaw
1. Provide 2 tbsps guacamole
1. Get 40 g dairy free cheese cubed


##### Steps to make Spiced Salmon Tacos:

1. Preheat the oven to 180 oC - Stir together the olive oil and fajita seasoning - Place the salmon steaks into a casserole dish, season with salt and pepper and drizzle with the fajita oil - Bake in the oven for 10 minutes or until the salmon is cooked
1. Heat a little olive oil in a shallow pan - Place the sliced onion and peppers in a bowl with the oregano, cayenne pepper, sugar, cinnamon and lime juice - Cook for eight minutes on a medium heat
1. Serve with the salmon with the vegetables, taco shells, gauacamole, tomatoes, coleslaw, tomatoes and dairy free cheese
