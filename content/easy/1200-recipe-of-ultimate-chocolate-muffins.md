##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Muffins:

1. Take 250 g self-raising flour
1. Provide 25 g cocoa powder
1. Get 2 tsp baking powder
1. Take 175 g caster sugar
1. Get 175 ml Milk
1. Get 2 large eggs beaten
1. Take 100 ml olive oil
1. Use 75 chocolate chunks
1. You need 1-2 tbsp vanilla extract


##### Steps to make Chocolate Muffins:

1. Preheat the oven to 170 degrees,gas 5. Line a 12-hole deep muffin tray with paper muffin cases. Sift together the flour, cocoa and baking powder in a bowl. Stir in the sugar. Add the remaining ingredients and mix lightly together.
1. Bake for approximately 20 mins or until golden and springy to touch. Cool for 10 mins and transfer to wire rack and leave to cool completely.
1. Your Chocolate Muffins are ready to be served.
