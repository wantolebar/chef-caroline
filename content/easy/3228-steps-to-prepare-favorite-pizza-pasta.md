##### The ingredients needed to make Pizza Pasta:

1. Get 1 large box pasta (you choose)
1. You need 2 large jars pizza sauce
1. You need 1 lb. hamburger
1. Provide 1 yellow onion, diced
1. Use 1 Can black olives, sliced
1. You need 1 Tablespoon onion powder
1. Use 1 Tablespoon garlic powder
1. Prepare to taste salt and black pepper,
1. Get 1 Pkg. sliced pepperoni, diced
1. Provide to taste grated parmesan / romano cheese


##### Steps to make Pizza Pasta:

1. In a large skillet add the hamburger, onion, pepperoni, onion and garlic powders, and Salt and pepper. Fry til Hamburger is no longer pink; drain grease and set meat mixture aside.
1. Bring a large pot half full of water to a boil. Add the pasta, stirring occasionally. Continue to boil til pasta is tender; drain. Add noodles and meat mixture to large pot along with the olives and sauces. Mix well. To Serve top with Parmesan and/or Romano Cheese and Enjoy ðŸ¥°
