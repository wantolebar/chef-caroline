##### The ingredients needed to prepare Pancakes with Chocolate Chips:

1. Use 256 grams (2 cups) flour
1. Use 2 tablespoons sugar
1. Provide 17 grams (4 teaspoons) baking powder
1. Take 1 pinch salt
1. Provide 2 eggs
1. Provide 300 ml (1 1/4 cups) milk
1. Take 2 tablespoons butter
1. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1. Get 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1. Take 1 handful chocolate chips


##### Instructions to make Pancakes with Chocolate Chips:

1. Mix everything in a bowl.
1. Lop mix scoop-by-scoop into buttered skillet on medium heat, turning when you see bubbles. Cook until light brown.
