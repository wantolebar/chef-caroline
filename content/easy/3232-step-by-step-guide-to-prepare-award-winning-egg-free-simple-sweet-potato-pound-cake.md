##### The ingredients needed to make Egg-free Simple Sweet Potato Pound Cake:

1. Provide 200 grams Sweet potato
1. Provide 100 grams Pancake mix
1. Get 50 grams Butter or margarine
1. Provide 100 grams Milk or soy milk
1. Get 1 Sesame seeds (optional)


##### Instructions to make Egg-free Simple Sweet Potato Pound Cake:

1. Microwave the unpeeled sweet potato until soft. (The peel acts as a substitute for plastic wrap.)
1. Peel the sweet potato and mash while still hot. (Start to preheat the oven to 180°C.)
1. Add the butter (margarine) to the sweet potato and mix.
1. Mix in the pancake mix and milk (or soy milk), then pour the batter into the tin. Bake in the oven for 20-30 minutes. Keep an eye on it while it is baking.
1. Poke a toothpick through the middle. It is done if the toothpick comes out clean It&#39;s tasty served with heavy whipped cream.
