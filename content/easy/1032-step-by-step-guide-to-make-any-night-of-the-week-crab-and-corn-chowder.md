##### The ingredients needed to cook Crab and corn chowder:

1. Use 1 small onion diced
1. Use 2 cloves garlic chopped
1. Use 1/2 jalapeno diced small
1. Provide 3 tbsp unsalted butter
1. Provide 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Get 1/4 cup white wine
1. Use 1 tbsp capers
1. Get 6 fresh basil leaves chopped small
1. Use 2 small red potatoes 1/2&#34; diced
1. Provide 1 can corn 15 oz
1. Provide 3/4 cup grape tomatoes sliced in half
1. Provide 1/2 cup crab claw meat
1. Take 1/2 cup shrimp cleaned and chopped
1. Use 3 tbsp flour
1. Get 1 cup heavy cream
1. Use 2 cups milk
1. Get to taste Salt and pepper


##### Instructions to make Crab and corn chowder:

1. Melt butter in pan add olive oil onions and garlic saute oh let&#39;s not forget the jalapeno
1. Make a roux with the flour. don&#39;t burn your roux. Now add wine
1. Add heavy cream and milk diced potatoes capers and crab and shrimp
1. Add tomatoes and basil corn salt and pepper to taste. A little zatarains seasoning always helps
1. Enjoy a cold beer with this
