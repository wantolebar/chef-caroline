##### The ingredients needed to prepare Eggroll wrapped pulled pork:

1. Use  pulled pork, see my recipe for Onion barbecue pork with homemade coleslaw and sweet potatoes
1. Get  large egg roll wraps
1. Get  oil to fry


##### Steps to make Eggroll wrapped pulled pork:

1. Make the pulled pork and set aside, to cool.
1. Heat oil
1. Turn the wrapper so the points are straight up and down. Add pulled pork to center fold the sides in, and roll the egg roll
1. Put the egg roll in hot oil with flap side down.
1. Fry till crispy and turn and crisp the other side.
1. Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive oils.
1. Serve I hope you enjoy!
