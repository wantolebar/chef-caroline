##### The ingredients needed to make Vanilla and Chocolate Muffins:

1. Prepare 4 cups flour
1. Use 4 teaspoons baking powder
1. Provide 1 Spoon salt
1. Prepare 4 cups sugar
1. Prepare 2 eggs
1. Provide 1/2 vegetable oil
1. Use 1/2 cocoa powder
1. You need 1 cup milk
1. You need 1 spoon vanilla essence


##### Instructions to make Vanilla and Chocolate Muffins:

1. Preheat oven to 180degrees then oil 12 cup muffin pan then in a large bowl mix oil,milk,eggs and beat lightly.
1. Add vanilla essence and stir - On another separate bowl mix flour,baking powder,sugar and butter and mix until the flour resembles bread crumbs then add into wet ingredients - Combine the dough well then divide the batter between two bowls - On one bowl add sifted cocoa powder and mix well - Then on the pan fill half of vanilla batter and add another half of chocolate batter then bake  - Allow them to cool before serving.
