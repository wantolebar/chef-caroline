##### The ingredients needed to prepare roasted red and sweet potatoes:

1. Use 1 large sweet potato
1. Get 4 each red potatoes
1. Provide 1/2 stick of butter
1. Provide 1 seasonings to taste


##### Instructions to make roasted red and sweet potatoes:

1. preheat oven to 375°
1. cube sweet and red potatoes and place on baking sheet. no need to peel potatoes!
1. drizzle 1/2 stick melted butter over potatoes and then toss to evenly coat.
1. sprinkle seasonings of your choice ( I used garlic salt, season salt and Montreal steak seasoning) over potatoes.
1. place in oven for 40 minutes turning once half way through
1. Enjoy!
