##### The ingredients needed to cook Easy Mini Muffin Meatloafs:

1. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
1. Take 1 egg
1. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
1. You need  ground black pepper
1. Provide  salt
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Mrs. Dash garlic &amp; herb salt-free seasoning
1. Take 1 packages crushed crackers
1. Provide 1/3 cup diced onions
1. Use 1/2 of a diced bellpepper
1. Provide 8 oz can tomato sauce


##### Instructions to make Easy Mini Muffin Meatloafs:

1. Preheat oven to 350°
1. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl and place into mini muffin pan.
1. Bake for about 35 minutes
1. Part way through baking I add a spiral of ketchup on top but this is optional.
1. Serve and enjoy!
