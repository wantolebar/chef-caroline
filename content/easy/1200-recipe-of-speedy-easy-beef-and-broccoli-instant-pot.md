##### The ingredients needed to cook Easy Beef and Broccoli instant Pot:

1. Get  round steak cut into strips
1. Get  large jars of Heinz Savory Beef Gravy
1. Get  frozen broccoli florets steamed
1. Get  olive oil
1. You need  beef broth


##### Steps to make Easy Beef and Broccoli instant Pot:

1. Sauté beef in olive oil in the instant pot until browned.
1. Add beef broth. Set instant pot to pressure cook for 12 minutes. Natural release for 8 minuets then quick release.
1. Drain liquid. Add beef gravy and broccoli. Mix and heat through.
1. Serve over rice
