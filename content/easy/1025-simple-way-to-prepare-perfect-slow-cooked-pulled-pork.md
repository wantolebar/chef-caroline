##### The ingredients needed to prepare Slow cooked pulled pork:

1. Prepare 1.4 kg Pork Shoulder
1. Take 3 onions
1. Take 2 tbsps Salt
1. Provide 2 tbsps Brown sugar
1. Provide 2 tbsps Paprika
1. Use 1 tbsps Black pepper


##### Steps to make Slow cooked pulled pork:

1. Preheat the oven to gas mark 5
1. Peel quarter the onions and place them in a preheated cast iron pot, after 10 minutes add water to deglaze the pot.
1. Mix the salt, pepper, paprika and sugar together in a bowl
1. Score the pork shoulder and rub the rub all over on every side, get into all the gaps!
1. Add the pork shoulder to the cast iron pot on top of the onions, put the lid on and place into the preheated oven for 4 hours
1. For the last hour, remove the lid to crisp up the skin
1. Once cooked, shred the meat with forks and serve however you like. (Between buns, on top of wedges or nachos)
