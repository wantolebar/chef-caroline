##### The ingredients needed to prepare chicken enchilada soup:

1. Use 1 lb skinless boneless chicken
1. Get 1 tbsp veg. oil
1. Prepare 1 clove garlic
1. Provide 1/2 cup diced onion
1. Prepare 1 cup maseca
1. Prepare 3 cup water
1. Get 1 cup red enchillada sauce
1. Get 2 cup shredded cheddar chese
1. Prepare 1 tsp salt
1. Use 1 tsp chili powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1. You need 1 quart chicken brooth


##### Steps to make chicken enchilada soup:

1. In large pot cook chicken in medium heat in oil till brown on all sides. remove set aside.
1. cook onion &amp; garlic in remaining oil till clear then add chicken broth
1. in a seperate bowl whisk maseca (1. cup) + 2 cups water till blended
1. pour measeca/water mix with 1 cup water+ enchilada sauce,  cheese, salt, chilli powder, cumin - bring to a boil.
1. shred cooked chicken and then add to pot.
1. reduce heat &amp; simmer 30-40 minutes
