##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet &amp; Smoky Chipotle Chicken:

1. You need 1 lb Bacon, diced
1. Provide 2 each Green peppers, diced
1. Use 1/2 Onion, diced
1. Prepare 2 can Stewed tomatoes
1. Prepare 7 oz Can of chipotle peppers in adobo
1. You need 2 tbsp Garlic powder
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Cumin
1. Provide 1 tbsp Chili powder
1. Prepare 1 cup Brown sugar
1. Use 2 lb Or more, boneless skinless chicken


##### Steps to make Sweet &amp; Smoky Chipotle Chicken:

1. Brown the bacon, onion, and peppers.
1. Add the tomatoes, chipotle peppers, spices &amp; brown sugar.
1. Break up the chipotle peppers &amp; let sauce cook at a low boil for 5 minutes.
1. Pour half the sauce into a large baking dish. Lay chicken pieces on top. Cover with remaining sauce.
1. Bake at 400°F , uncovered, for 45 minutes to 1 hour (longer if using thick chicken breasts).
1. Great served with mashed potatoes or rice.
