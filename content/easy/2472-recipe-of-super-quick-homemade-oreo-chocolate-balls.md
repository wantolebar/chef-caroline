##### The ingredients needed to prepare Oreo chocolate balls:

1. Get 120 gm oreo biscuit
1. Use 1/2 cup milk
1. Prepare 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
1. Get 100 gm dark chocolate for coating


##### Steps to make Oreo chocolate balls:

1. Separate the cream and cookies
1. Take grinder jar and make fine pwder
1. Add half cup milk and sugar syrup in cookies pwder and make a dough
1. Mash the cream generously
1. Fill the cream in between and make a ball shape
1. Dip the ball in melted chocolate
1. Garnish with some desecated coconut. Enjoyyyy
