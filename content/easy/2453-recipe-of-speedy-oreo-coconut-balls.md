##### The ingredients needed to cook Oreo Coconut Balls:

1. Provide 18 Oreo biscuits
1. Get 2-3 tbsp homemade condensed milk (for mixing)
1. You need 1 cup Grinded dry coconut
1. Take  as required homemade condensed milk(for coating)


##### Instructions to make Oreo Coconut Balls:

1. Grind Oreo biscuits in grinder &amp; make a fine powder paste.
1. Now add 2-3 tbsp condensed milk, mix it well &amp; make small balls of it with the help of hand.
1. Then take some as required condensed milk in a plate &amp; pour grinded coconut in a plate.
1. Now one by one start coating first coat each side of ball with condensed milk in plate just by rolling ball into plate &amp; then coat with grinded coconut nicely.
1. Put all balls into a separate plate &amp; enjoy delicious balls.
