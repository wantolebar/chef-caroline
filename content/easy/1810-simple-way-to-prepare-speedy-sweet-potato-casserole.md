##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Potato Casserole:

1. You need 1 1/2 cup Mashed sweet potatoes
1. You need 1/4 cup Sugar
1. Get 1 each Egg beaten
1. Get 1/4 cup Milk
1. Take 1/2 cup Coconut
1. Provide 1/4 stick Butter - melted
1. Get 1/2 tsp Vanilla
1. You need  topping
1. Use 1/2 cup Light brown sugar
1. Take 1/4 cup Flour
1. Take 1/2 stick Butter softened
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Pecans


##### Steps to make Sweet Potato Casserole:

1. Add mashed sweet potatoes, sugar, beaten egg, milk, coconut, melted butter and vanilla and place in 2 quart casserole.
1. Mix topping ingredients together and sprinkle on top of casserole.
1. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
