##### The ingredients needed to cook Ripe Banana Pakoda:

1. Get 6 Ripe Bananas
1. Prepare 250 gms Gram flour (Besan)
1. You need 1/2 cup water
1. Get 1/2 tsp Chilli powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp coriander-cumin powder (Dhanya jeera powder)
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp Asafoetida (Hing)
1. You need 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Provide As required Oil for frying


##### Instructions to make Ripe Banana Pakoda:

1. Take gram flour in a bowl. Mix turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander cumin powder, salt, asafoetida into it. Mix well till it becomes a smooth batter.
1. Cut ripe bananas into round pieces.
1. Dip the pieces of bananas into the batter properly so that whole piece of banana can be covered with batter.
1. Take oil in a pan and fry on medium flame till it becomes brown.
1. Serve with tomato ketchup and coriander chutney. Ready to serve.
