1. Use 1 tsp Ground spices powder
1. Provide as needed Oil (for greasing laddle)


##### Steps to make Mini egg rolls:

1. Take 2 eggs then beat it well.
1. Add pinch of salt and 1/4 tsp ground spices powder. Mix it well.
1. Take chopped onion, tomato and green chilli.
1. Take laddle grease with oil and put it on flame and rotate it all side in round shape.
