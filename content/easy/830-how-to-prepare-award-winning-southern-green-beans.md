##### The ingredients needed to prepare Southern green beans:

1. Use 1 lb Green beans
1. Take 6 pieces bacon
1. Take 1 small can mushrooms
1. Use 1/4 tsp Salt
1. Take to taste Fresh ground pepper
1. Prepare 2 tsp Garlic powder
1. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Cajun seasoning
1. Take 2 tsp Dehydrated onions


##### Instructions to make Southern green beans:

1. Fry bacon retain grease
1. Put all ingredients in pot with 2 cups water with bacon grease
1. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to med until 1/2 of water gone. (About 40 min)
