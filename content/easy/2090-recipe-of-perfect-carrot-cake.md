##### The ingredients needed to cook Carrot Cake:

1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup sifted flour
1. Use 1/2 cup sugar
1. Provide 1 tsp baking powder
1. Get 1 tsp baking soda
1. You need 1/2 tsp salt
1. Use 2/3 cup salad oil
1. You need 1 tsp vanilla
1. Provide 2 eggs
1. You need 1/2 cup crushed pineapple with juice
1. You need 1 1/2 cup shredded carrot
1. Take 1 tsp cinnamon
1. You need 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1. Get 1 packages Cream cheese frosting


##### Instructions to make Carrot Cake:

1. Preheat oven to 350
1. Add ingredients and mix on medium
1. Fold in walnuts
1. Spray two 9&#34; round cake pans with non stick spray
1. Divide mixture between both pans
1. Cook for 25-30 minutes, until toothpick inserted in center of cake comes out clean
1. Let cakes cool; frost the top of one round, then place the other round on top and frost the top and sides
1. Sprinkle walnuts over top for garnish (optional)
