##### The ingredients needed to prepare Spaghetti squash casserole:

1. Provide 2 spaghetti squash
1. Provide 4 tbsp sour cream
1. Provide 1/4 cup shredded velveeta cheese
1. Use 1 tsp onion powder
1. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
1. Provide 1/2 tsp creole seasoning
1. Prepare 1 tbsp minced dried onion
1. Get  Salt and pepper taste


##### Instructions to make Spaghetti squash casserole:

1. Cook spaghetti squash and shred and add to a bowl then add all the seasoning and sour cream. Spray an oven safe dish with cooking spray then add the spaghetti squash mixture to the dish and top with cheese. Bake on 350 till cheese melts.
