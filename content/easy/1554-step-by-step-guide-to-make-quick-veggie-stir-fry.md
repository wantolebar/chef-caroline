##### The ingredients needed to make Veggie stir-fry:

##### Steps to make Veggie stir-fry:

1. Chop the 3 egg plants how ever you want them
1. Chop the tomato how you want to
1. Mince the onion
1. Chop the green bell pepper however you want it
1. In a frying pan heat the oil. When the oil is hot put in the chopped tomato then the onion and green bell pepper. Then put in the chopped egg plants ( the purple skin will turn brown when done).
1. Serve over pasta or brown rice like I did.
