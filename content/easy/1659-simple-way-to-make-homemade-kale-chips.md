##### The ingredients needed to cook Kale chips:

1. You need  Kale (1 bundle)
1. Take  Olive oil (2 tbsp)
1. Use  Salt (1 pinch)


##### Instructions to make Kale chips:

1. Preheat an oven to 175 C. Line with baking sheet.
1. With a knife or kitchen shears carefully remove the leaves from the thick stems and tear into bite size pieces. Wash and thoroughly dry kale with a salad spinner. Drizzle kale with olive oil and sprinkle with seasoning salt.
1. Bake until the edges brown but are not burnt, 10 to 15 minutes.
