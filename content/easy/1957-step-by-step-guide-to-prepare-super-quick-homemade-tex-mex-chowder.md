##### The ingredients needed to make Tex-Mex Chowder:

1. Prepare 1 (10oz) container Philadelphia Cooking Creme - Santa Fe Blend
1. Use 1 (10 3/4oz) can Cream of Potato soup
1. You need 1 soup can of milk
1. Get 1 (15.25oz) can sweet corn, drained
1. Use 1 (15oz) can black beans, drained
1. Use 1 (10oz) can diced tomato with green chilies, drained
1. Take 1 (6oz) pkg. Tyson Southwestern style chicken breast strips, cut into 1in. pieces
1. You need 1 tsp chili powder
1. You need 1 tsp cumin
1. Take 1 dash of cayenne
1. Get 1 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder


##### Instructions to make Tex-Mex Chowder:

1. In large saucepan over Med. heat, mix together Cooking Creme &amp; soup.  Add can of milk; blend until smooth.  Heat just until bubbling.
1. Add vegetables and allow to heat through, about 5min.
1. Toss in diced chicken and stir; add spices.  Simmer another 5min. or until heated through.
1. Garnish with tortilla strips, crumbled corn chips, fresh cilantro, cheddar cheese, sour cream and/or chives
1. Serve with Pillsbury Cornbread twists.
