##### The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate mug cake:

1. Prepare 4 tbsp flour
1. Use 2 tbsp cocoa powder
1. Take 1/4 cup milk
1. Take 2 tbsp oil
1. Get 2 tbsp sugar
1. Take 1 tsp Vanilla essence
1. You need 1/4 tsp baking powder
1. Use Pinch salt


##### Steps to make Chocolate mug cake:

1. In a bowl, mix all the dry ingredients like flour, cocoa powder, sugar, salt, baking powder and coffee powder. Now add the wet ingredients oil, milk and essence. Mix well so that no lumps form.
1. If batter is thick, adjust consistency by mixing a tbsp milk. Pour batter to ceramic mugs till 1/2 or 3/4 level. Microwave on high for 1 minutes 30 seconds.
