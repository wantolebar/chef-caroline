##### The ingredients needed to make Mawa baby corn:

1. Prepare  Mawa/whole dried milk
1. Prepare  Sugar powder
1. Take  Milk powder
1. Provide  Grated coconut
1. Use  Cardamom powder
1. Take  Clarified butter
1. Prepare  Yellow food colour
1. Take  Green food colour


##### Steps to make Mawa baby corn:

1. Dry roast the mawa.
1. Take a large bowl add mawa,milk powder,sugar powder,grated coconut and cardamom powder and mix well.
1. Divide the dough into two parts.
1. Add yellow food colour in one parts and also add green food colour in other parts
1. Grease some clarified butter in hand and yellow colour dough divide into balls and make cylindrical shape
1. Make a leaf shape of green food colour dough and stick at the top of the cylindrical shape with yellow dough.
1. With the help of straw make round like baby corn
1. Keep in the fridge for two hours.
1. Enjoy with baby corn.
