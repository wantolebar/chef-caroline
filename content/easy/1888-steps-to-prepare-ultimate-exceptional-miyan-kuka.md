##### The ingredients needed to cook Exceptional Miyan kuka:

1. Take  Kuka
1. Provide  Fresh attarugu
1. Get  Fresh garlic and ginger
1. Get  Good daddawa
1. Get  Groundnut/palm oil
1. Provide  Maggi
1. Prepare  Salt
1. Provide  Beans
1. Take  Broth
1. Provide  Lamb fat (it’s the weapon here)
1. Prepare  Chicken or boned meat


##### Instructions to make Exceptional Miyan kuka:

1. Start by marinating the chicken/boned meat together with the lamb fat with ground daddawa grated ginger garlic scotch bonnet nutmeg salt maggi and oil for at least an hour
1. Add water and cook the chicken till fork apart
1. Mean while in a different pot hear your oil and fry the daddawa for few seconds
1. Add your scotchbonnet ginger mixture and continue frying
1. Add broth, beans and adjust seasonings. Cook till the water reduces and the beans is soft
1. Add the shredded chicken and whisk in your kuka
1. Serve with nan shanu and yajin daddawa✨
