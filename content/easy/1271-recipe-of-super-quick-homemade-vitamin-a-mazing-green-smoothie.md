##### The ingredients needed to cook Vitamin A-mazing Green Smoothie:

1. Take 1 generous handful of baby kale
1. Use 1/2 cup carrot juice(make sure its 100% juice and not a blend)
1. Prepare 1/2 cup skim milk
1. Get 1/2 cup water
1. Use 1 cup frozen mixed unsweetened berries
1. Get 1 small (preferably frozen) banana
1. You need 1/2 of a greek yogurt (5.3 oz) (I chose mixed berry by Dannon light n fit)
1. Prepare 1 tbsp ground flax seed
1. Prepare 5 or six ice cubes
1. Get 1 a stevia  or splenda packet or a bit of honey(optional but it sweetens up your smoothie, honey will add calories)


##### Steps to make Vitamin A-mazing Green Smoothie:

1. Add first four ingredients to a blender and blend for 30 seconds. Add all remaining ingredients and blend for 30-45 seconds more. Enjoy!!
