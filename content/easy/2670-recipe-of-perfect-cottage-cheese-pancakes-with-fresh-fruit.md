##### The ingredients needed to make Cottage Cheese Pancakes with Fresh Fruit:

1. Use 3 eggs
1. Provide 1 cup cottage cheese
1. Prepare 2 tbsp melted butter or coconut oil
1. Take 1/4 cup garbanzo flour
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
1. Provide 2 bananas


##### Steps to make Cottage Cheese Pancakes with Fresh Fruit:

1. Whirl eggs and cottage cheese in a blender
1. Add melted butter or coconut, flour,  and salt; whirl until smooth
1. Add one banana. Whirl.
1. Lightly grease a griddle or a large frying pan and preheat over low medium heat.
1. Pour 1/4 cup batter into pan for each pancake.
1. Cook until tops are bubbly,  turn,  and cook other sides until lightly browned
1. Fresh fruit: Add sliced banana or any berries as a topping and sprinkle lightly with cinnamon.
