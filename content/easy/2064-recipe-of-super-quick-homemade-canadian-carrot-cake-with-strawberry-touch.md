##### The ingredients needed to prepare Canadian Carrot Cake with Strawberry touch:

1. Provide 2 cup all-purpose flour
1. Provide 2 tsp baking powder
1. Take 2 tsp cinnamon
1. Get 1 tsp baking soda
1. Get 3/4 tsp salt
1. Provide 1 tsp nutmeg
1. Take 200 grams granulated sugar
1. Prepare 3 piece eggs
1. Prepare 180 ml vegetable oil
1. Provide 1 tsp vanilla extract
1. Prepare 2 cup grated carrots
1. Get 1/2 cup pekan nuts / walnuts
1. You need 1 cup canned strawberries
1. Prepare 250 grams Mascarpone / cream cheese
1. Provide 60 ml butter
1. Get 1/4 cup icing sugar
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Greek yoghurt


##### Instructions to make Canadian Carrot Cake with Strawberry touch:

1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease metal cake pan and set aside.
1. Mix together eggs,sugar,salt,nutmeg,vanilla, cinnamon.
1. Slowly add flour. If the mixture gets tough add 1/2 of vegetable oil and continue adding flour and whisking.
1. Add baking powder and baking soda.
1. Add canned strawberries and mix them into the dough. From this point you might need a spatula.
1. Slowly continue adding grated carrots and walnuts.
1. Bake at 180°C for 25-30 minutes.
1. For the cream cheese icing beat Mascarpone,butter,icing sugar and yoghurt. After cooling down the cake cover it with icing and put into a fridge for a night.
