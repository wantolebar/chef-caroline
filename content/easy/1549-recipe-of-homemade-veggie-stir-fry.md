##### The ingredients needed to make Veggie Stir Fry:

1. Prepare  Canola Oil
1. Get  Garlic (minced)
1. Use  Soysauce
1. Prepare  Ground Ginger
1. Prepare  Chicken Broth
1. Take  Cornstarch
1. Take  Black Pepper (season to taste)
1. Take  to 6 cups Cooked Rice
1. Provide  Stir Fry Vegetables


##### Instructions to make Veggie Stir Fry:

1. Heat canola oil in large frying pan or wok.
1. Add stir fry vegetables. Saute 4-5 minutes.
1. Add garlic, soy sauce and ginger. Saute for about 5 more minutes, depending how crunchy you want your veggies.
1. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Stir in cornstarch and simmer 1 minute.
1. Serve over rice.
