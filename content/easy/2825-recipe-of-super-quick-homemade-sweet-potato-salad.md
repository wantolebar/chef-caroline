##### The ingredients needed to prepare Sweet potato salad:

1. Use 2 medium size sweet potatoes
1. Get 1 finely chopped onion
1. Get 4 finely chopped spring onion
1. Use 1/2 finely chopped green Capsicum
1. Prepare 1/2 finely chopped red capsicum
1. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped coriander
1. Use 6 baby tomatoes thinly sliced
1. Prepare 1/2 cucumber chopped
1. You need  Few leaves of mint leaves
1. Take 1 teaspoon olive oil
1. You need 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
1. Take 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
1. Provide 1 red chillie
1. Provide to taste Salt
1. Get  Roasted cashew to garnish


##### Steps to make Sweet potato salad:

1. In a bowl mix all finely chopped vegetables
1. Microwave sweet potatoes 4 minutes at high power
1. Remove and let it cool. Remove skin and cut it cubes
1. In a pan heat olive oil and put the cumin seeds. Once the cumin seeds are brown in colour add the red chilli and sweet potatoes. Add salt to taste and let it cook until golden brown.
1. Let the sweet potatoes cool. Once done add it to salad and toss and sprinkle chilli flakes and roasted cashews.
1. Enjoy sweet potato salad. You can also add lemon juice if preferred
