##### The ingredients needed to make Five Spice Roast Duck:

1. You need 4 duck legs
1. Provide  Chinese five spice
1. Prepare  Salt
1. Take  cracked black pepper


##### Instructions to make Five Spice Roast Duck:

1. Place the duck legs in a bowl.
1. Rub the duck well with all the other ingredients. Cover with clingfilm or place in a sealed bag and place in fridge and let it marinade for atleast 4 hours before cooking or overnight. Marinating it over night will give it a lot more flavour.
1. When it&#39;s time to cook. Pre-heat oven to gas mark 4/350f/180c.
1. Place the duck, skin facing upwards, on a grill with a drip tray close below. You want the air to circulate and the fat to drain off the duck as it slowly cooks leaving a nice and crispy skin.
1. Transfer to the oven and cook for 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 hours. Baste the skin with the dripped fat every 20-30 minutes.
1. Once its ready let it rest for 10 minutes before serving with your choice of sides.
