##### The ingredients needed to cook Garlic Creem Cheese Dip:

1. Prepare  cream cheese
1. Prepare  mayonnaise
1. Provide  garlic cloves
1. Prepare  pasley (finely chopped)
1. Prepare  dried oregano
1. Use  salt and pepper


##### Steps to make Garlic Creem Cheese Dip:

1. Finely grate a clove of garlic.
1. Roughly mix garlic, cream cheese and mayonnaise together. Put in microwave oven(500W) for 30 sec
1. Stir the mixture until smooth, add chopped pasley, dried oregano and salt&amp;pepper.
1. Dip with chips, vegetable sticks or bread sticks. :)
