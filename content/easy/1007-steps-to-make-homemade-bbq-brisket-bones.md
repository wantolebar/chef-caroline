##### The ingredients needed to cook Bbq Brisket Bones:

1. Provide  Brisket Bones (at walmart)
1. Use  Chicago Steak House Seasoning
1. Provide  Brown Sugar
1. You need  Bud Light Lime
1. Prepare  Classic Memphis Sauce (recipe on profile)


##### Steps to make Bbq Brisket Bones:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
1. Mix together seasoning and brown sugar.
1. Add brisket bones to 9 x 13 pan.
1. Pour in beer until it reachs midway point on the meat.
1. Rub on seasoning mixture.
1. Cover with foil and bake for 2 hours.
1. After 2 hours uncover and add on sauce and cook for 15 minutes.
1. Remove add more sauce and cook for 15 minutes more.
1. Let sit 5 to 10 minutes. serve and enjoy.
