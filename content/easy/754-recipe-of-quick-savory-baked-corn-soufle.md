##### Steps to make Savory Baked Corn Soufle:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
1. Mix all ingredients together except thawed or fresh corn.
1. Now just incorporate the thawed corn or fresh corn into mixture.
1. Grease with softened butter a 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
1. Place in mixture and bake for 40- 50 minutes. Insert a toothpick in middle and see if it comes out clean.
1. *****optional serving suggestions.top with grated cheddar and a dollup of sour cream, or for a southwest flair top with a bit of salsa.
