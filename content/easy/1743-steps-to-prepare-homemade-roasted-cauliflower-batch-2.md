##### The ingredients needed to prepare Roasted Cauliflower Batch 2:

1. You need 1 head cauliflower
1. You need 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1. Use 1/2 stick butter
1. Take 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1. Provide 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1. Use 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder


##### Steps to make Roasted Cauliflower Batch 2:

1. Preheat oven 375° Fahrenheit. Look the cauliflower over remove the leaves. Slice 1 inch thick. Lay it out on a baking pan. Add the olive oil, Parmesan cheese, and salt.
1. Roast in the oven 25 minutes then turn the cauliflower over and add small pieces of butter chunks. Put back in the oven 15 minutes. Put into a serving bowl that holds heat well and pour the pan juices from the cauliflower over the top. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!
