##### The ingredients needed to make Beef Meatballs bathed with Veggies Sauce:

1. Take 1/2 stalk celery
1. Use 1/2 tsp pepper
1. You need 1 tsp dried onion
1. Provide 3 cloves garlic
1. Prepare  Chicken stock
1. Use 3 cloves roasted garlic
1. Take 4 ripe tomatos
1. You need 1 tbsp mediterranean oil (canola-olive-grape seeds)
1. You need 14 large beef meatballs
1. Use 16 oz diced tomatoes can
1. Get 1 pinch turmeric
1. Provide 1 pinch cumin
1. Take 1 tsp dried onion
1. Get 16 oz can of chick peas, drained
1. Use  chicken stock and water as needed
1. Provide 1 cup baby carrots
1. Use 6 potatoes, size of a baseball
1. Prepare 1 cup diced pumpkin


##### Instructions to make Beef Meatballs bathed with Veggies Sauce:

1. Blend the 6 first ingredients on the list plus the tomatoes. Covered with chicken stock.
1. In the pan that you will cook this recipe, pour the oil and  heat at medium setting. Add the blended seasoning, mix for one minute.
1. Add the meatballs, saute. I used the pre-cooked ones. It is much faster than making them from scratch and there are great ones in the market.
1. Add the carrots, chicken stock (I used home made). Water as required to obtain the desired consistency, potatoes, tomatoes, chickpeas.
1. Taste an add turmeric and cumin to taste. Lower heat setting and cook until vegetables are soft; when done, add a sprint of fresh oregano and turn off the heat. Wait 10 minutes before serving.
