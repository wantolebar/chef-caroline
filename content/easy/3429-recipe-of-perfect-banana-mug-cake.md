##### The ingredients needed to prepare Banana Mug Cake:

1. Use  I medium cup flour
1. Provide 1 egg
1. You need 1 ripe banana
1. Provide 1 tbls Brown Sugar
1. You need 1 tbls oil
1. Get 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
1. Use 1/4 tsp salt
1. Use 2 drops vanilla essence
1. Provide 1/2 tsp baking powder
1. You need 2 tsp melon seeds


##### Steps to make Banana Mug Cake:

1. Mash banana with the fork
1. In the same bowl add egg oil and brown sugar salt whisk well till smooth
1. In another bowl seive flour baking powder cinnamon powder add this to wet batter of banana mix bowl fold it
1. Pour this into the greased large mug up to 3/5 little higher to 1/2 mug sprinkle melon seeds and bake it in the microwave in normal setting for 1/2 minutes to 2 minutes
