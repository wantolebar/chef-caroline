##### The ingredients needed to cook Slow cooker pulled pork:

1. Provide 1 2-4 pound pork roast or beef roast
1. Provide 1 onion
1. Prepare 1 jar of mild/med/hot salsa (salsa verde works perfect also)
1. Prepare 1 Salt and pepper is optional. Be careful if u add salt as the salsa contains plenty already!
1. Get 1 Chopped bell peppers is also optional


##### Instructions to make Slow cooker pulled pork:

1. Chop onion and place in the slow cooker
1. Add meat
1. Pour salsa over the meat, cover with lid and cook on low for 8-10 hours.
1. Shred and enjoy on tacos, burritos, salads, burgers etc.
1. If u have leftovers, use in enchiladas!
