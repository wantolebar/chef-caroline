##### The ingredients needed to prepare Blueberry Crumble Pie:

1. Take  sheet Frozen pie crust
1. Prepare  Filling
1. Provide  Bluberries (frozen is okay)
1. Prepare  White sugar
1. You need  Corn starch
1. Get  Lemon juice
1. Use  Crumble Topping
1. Prepare  Cake flour
1. Prepare  White sugar
1. You need  Brown sugar
1. Take  Unsalted butter, chilled


##### Steps to make Blueberry Crumble Pie:

1. Preheat the oven to 180℃. Mix the filling ingredients in a bowl until the dry ingredients are well incorporated.
1. Prepare the crumble topping: Combine all of the ingredients except for the butter, and mix well. Finely cube the butter, add it to the mixture, and mix well.
1. Layer a pie pan with the frozen pie sheet, pierce with a fork, then pour in the filling. Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the top.
1. Bake for 1 hour in the 180℃ oven.
