##### The ingredients needed to prepare Ripe Banana Muffins:

1. Use  Ripe bananas
1. Use  Lemon juice
1. Use  Unsalted butter
1. Provide  Granulated sugar
1. Prepare  Eggs
1. Use  Cake flour
1. Get  Baking powder


##### Steps to make Ripe Banana Muffins:

1. Put the bananas and lemon juice in a bowl, and mash them with a masher. Sift powdered ingredients together in a separate bowl.
1. In another bowl, add the softened butter (leave at room temperature or microwave for 20~30 seconds) and sugar, and mix until it becomes pale in color.
1. Add the eggs in the bowl from step 2, and mix well. Add the bananas from step 1, and mix well.
1. Fold the flour mixture from step 1 in, and the batter is ready.
1. Fill the muffin tins 80% of the way up, and bake in a 170°C oven for 25 minutes.
1. I didn&#39;t have enough muffin tins, so I baked half of the batter with a square mold. It has chocolate chips in the picture.
