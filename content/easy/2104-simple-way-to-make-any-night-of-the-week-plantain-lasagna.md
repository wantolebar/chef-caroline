##### The ingredients needed to prepare Plantain lasagna:

1. Get 4 plantain medium finger
1. Use 5 bell pepper
1. Take 1 fresh tomatoes
1. Take 3 fresh pepper
1. Prepare 1 onion
1. Prepare 5 eggs
1. Use Dash salt
1. Get 1/2 cube knorr
1. Take  Vegetable oil for frying plantain
1. You need 8 pack mozarrela cheese


##### Instructions to make Plantain lasagna:

1. Preheat oven 150 degree ahead of your prepping
1. Mix the egg with the diced Bell pepper, onions, tomatoes and the red pepper. Add knorr cube and salt. Then pour in the plantain and mix well
1. Dice the plantain, Bell pepper, onions, tomatoes, red pepper. Fried the diced plantain. Break and mix the egg in a bowl and set aside
1. Pour into an oven dish, place the on it and put in the oven for 30mins
1. After 30minutes, remove from oven allow it cool off a bit then serve
