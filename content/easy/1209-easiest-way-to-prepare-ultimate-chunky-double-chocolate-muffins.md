##### The ingredients needed to make Chunky Double Chocolate Muffins:

1. Use 3 Eggs
1. You need 1 2/3 cups All Purpose Flour
1. Provide 2 tsp baking powder
1. Use 120 gm semi sweet or dark chocolate chopped
1. Get 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1. Get 200 ml Cream
1. You need 2/3 cup Sugar
1. Provide 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Use 1 tsp Vanilla extract


##### Instructions to make Chunky Double Chocolate Muffins:

1. Preheat oven to 180 and line muffin tin
1. Sieve flour, baking powder and salt. Keep it aside
1. Beat cream to form peaks, keep it aside. In another bowl, beats eggs with sugar until pale and frothy. Add vanilla extract and blend well. Now add cream and cocoa powder and blend
1. Slowly add dry ingredients and mix until well combined. Reserve 3 tbsp chopped chocolate and fold the remaining in batter
1. Fill muffin tin with batter to 2/3. Add the remaining chopped chocolate to top of batter
1. Bake for 18-25 minutes until done. Allow to cool for 15-20 minutes. Best served warm with cup of milk
