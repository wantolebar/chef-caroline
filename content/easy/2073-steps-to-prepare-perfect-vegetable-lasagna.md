##### Steps to make Vegetable Lasagna:

1. Heat up some oil in a pan &amp; add garlic, chilli flakes &amp; oregano to this. Saut√© for few minutes.
1. Add all other chopped vegetables &amp; add some salt to let them cook faster. Also add black pepper powder. Mix well &amp; saute for few minutes. Keep it aside.
1. Now preapare white sauce by adding plain flour, butter &amp; 1 cup milk to a pan &amp; heating it till the sauce becomes thick. You can add cheese into this sauce as well.
1. Preapare red sauce by taking half cup pizza sauce &amp; adding some water to it for a good consistency.
1. Now start assembling the lasagna in a baking dish.
1. First place a lasagna sheet. Spread white &amp; red sauce over it &amp; then make a layer of veggies. Top it with grated cheese.
1. Repeat this step twitor thrice to make layers of lasagna. Top it with some mozzarella cheese along with some processed cheese.
