##### The ingredients needed to prepare Cheese Chilli:

1. Get 3 tbsp corn starch
1. Get 200 gm cheese
1. You need to taste Salt
1. Take 1/2 tbsp black pepper
1. Provide  For vegetable
1. Get 1 capsicum
1. Provide 1 onion
1. Prepare 1 tbsp soya sauce
1. Use 2-3 tbsp ketchup
1. You need 1 green chilli
1. Get to taste Salt
1. Take 1 tbsp black pepper
1. Prepare 1/2 tbsp dalchini powder
1. Take to taste Kasuri methi
1. Take  Ginger garlic paste
1. Prepare  Butter/oil to fry


##### Steps to make Cheese Chilli:

1. Cut cheese in cubes and add corn flour and salt and black pepper into it and mix all them together and take a pan add butter and fry these cheese cubes in butter/oil.
1. Cheese should be little brown.Now keep aside the fried cheese..and chop onion and capsicum as shown
1. Now take a pan and add oil into it and then add green chilli and now add chopped onion and capsicum into it and saute for 3-4 minutes
1. Add salt to taste and black pepper and mix it well and saute for next 2 minutes
1. Now add soya sauce and ketchup into it and then add fried cheese into it and add dalchini powder and crushed kasuri methi and mix all the ingredients well and serve it
