##### The ingredients needed to cook Caramel Apple khandvi:

1. Provide  For khandvi
1. Provide 1 cup besan(gram flour)
1. Take 3 cups buttermilk
1. Prepare 1 tsp Turmeric
1. Provide 1 tsp Lemon juice mix all
1. Take  For caramelized Apple
1. Take 1/2 cup sugar
1. Get 1 cup Apples chopped
1. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
1. You need 1 tsp Sesame seeds
1. Prepare 1 Lemon juice
1. Get 1/2 tsp Cinnamon powder
1. Use 1 tbsp cream


##### Instructions to make Caramel Apple khandvi:

1. For khnandvi  -  - In a thick bottomed pan, cook this mixture by stirring continuously, till it becomes a smooth thick batter. It takes a few minutes to get this ready. Quickly spread portions of the mixture over the oiled inverted thali or marble table top as thinly as possible while the batter is still very hot.cut in circles.keep aside
1. Apple caramel -  - Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat; sprinkle sugar over. Stir until sugar begins to melt, about 1 minute. Add apples. Sauté until apples are brown and tender and juices form, about 10 minutes. Add cream and simmer until sauce thickens slightly, about 2 minutes. Let stand at room temperature.
1. Now stuff khandvi circles with caramelized apples &amp; garnish with sesame, mint, cashew
1. Top with butter scotch sauce
