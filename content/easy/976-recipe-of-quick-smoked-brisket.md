##### Instructions to make Smoked Brisket:

1. I have been forgetting the most critical step in doing awesome BBQ in my past recipes.  Crack a cold one!
1. Trim all but 1/4&#34; of the fat cap off of brisket and coat liberally with rub.
1. Smoke brisket @ 225° until internal temp is 165°-170°
1. Remove from smoker and double wrap in foil to keep juices from ruining out.

1. Remove from smoker and rest 45 min
