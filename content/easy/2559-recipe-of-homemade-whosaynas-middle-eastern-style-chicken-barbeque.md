##### The ingredients needed to cook Whosayna’s Middle Eastern Style Chicken Barbeque:

1. Get  Chicken (cut in quatre)
1. Provide  Salt
1. Provide  Lemon juice
1. Provide  Paprika
1. Use  Chilli powder
1. Provide  Cumin powder (freshly made)
1. Provide  Cinnamon powder
1. Provide  Garam Masala
1. Provide  Nutmeg powder
1. Prepare  Ginger paste
1. Provide  Garlic paste
1. You need  Oil
1. Provide  Yogurt
1. Take  Orange colour


##### Steps to make Whosayna’s Middle Eastern Style Chicken Barbeque:

1. Slit chicken pieces all over back and front
1. In a pan put oil and all spices with colour, ginger and garlic paste mix well, keep on flame
1. Let it boil once, remove from flame and add yogurt and lemon juice then beat well
1. Apply marinade on each piece very well.
1. Let it marinate for five hours.
1. Then barbeque it on medium flame, turning and basting it.
1. Leftover marinade mix with little oil and keep on basting as you grill it.
