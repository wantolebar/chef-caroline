##### The ingredients needed to prepare Katsudon:

1. Get 1/2 Onion
1. Use 1/3 leek
1. Provide 100 ml Dashi
1. Get 20 ml Soy sauce
1. Prepare 20 ml mirin
1. Use 2 teaspoons sugar
1. Get 3 eggs
1. Get  Katsu(deep fried pork)


##### Instructions to make Katsudon:

1. Slice onion and leek
1. Cook onions in dashi, soy sauce, mirin and sugar for 2 minutes.
1. Add Katsu and leek and beaten eggs on top
1. Cook until eggs are cooked to your preference. If you like it to be quite raw, use good eggs.
1. Pour it on top of rice
