##### The ingredients needed to prepare Easy Pudding cake:

1. Take  white bread
1. You need  eggs
1. Provide  milk
1. Get  granulated sugar
1. Prepare  raisins
1. Prepare  Some Butter for coating


##### Instructions to make Easy Pudding cake:

1. Preheat oven to 450F
1. Make the custard liquid. Mix the eggs and sugar well. And add the milk in a bowl.
1. Cut the bread into pieces(one slice into 9 pieces)
1. Coat the container with butter, pour half of the liquid.
1. Place the bread pieces and let them soak the custard.
1. Pour the rest of the liquid before sending it into the oven. Bake about 5 mins.
1. Pull out and add some raisins and bake for another 10 mins.
