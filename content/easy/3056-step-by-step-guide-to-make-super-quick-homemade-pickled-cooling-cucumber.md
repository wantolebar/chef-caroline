##### The ingredients needed to make Pickled Cooling Cucumber:

1. Prepare 1/2 cucumber
1. Take 1 pinch sea salt
1. Prepare 100 ml white wine vinegar
1. Prepare 1 small onion (optional)


##### Instructions to make Pickled Cooling Cucumber:

1. Wash and slice the cucumber as thinly as you can.
1. Place the cucumber in a bowl.
1. Optional: slice the onion and add to the cucumber.
1. Add the vinegar and salt and stir.
1. Leave it to rest for 15-20 minutes so that the cucumber absorbs the vinegar.
