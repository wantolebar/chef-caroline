##### The ingredients needed to prepare Rich moist chocolate cake:

1. Take 2 cups Flour
1. Prepare  Sugar 1 and 1/2 cups
1. Take  Cocoa powder 1/2 cup + 1/4 cup
1. You need 1/2 cup Butter
1. Get 1/4 cup Vegetables oil
1. Prepare 1 tsp Baking powder
1. Get 1/2 tbsp Baking soda
1. Take 1/4 tsp Salt
1. Provide 2 cups Hot water
1. Take  Eggs 3 large


##### Steps to make Rich moist chocolate cake:

1. Sift flour add cocoa powder,baking powder, baking soda, salt and sugar mix together.
1. Melt butter and vegetables oil in a bowl.
1. Add eggs to dry ingredients mix then add hot water and vegetable oil mix with hand whisk or electric mixer till well mixed.
1. Line a 9inch pan with parchment paper you could also butter the pan and dust with flour.
1. Pour in batter and bake for 45min or one hour. Insert a clean palette knife or toothpick when it come out clean then your done. Put in a wire rack and cool before serving.
1. Don&#39;t forget to preheat oven for 3minutes if your not using a timer oven then lower the heat to the Lowest when baking.
