##### The ingredients needed to cook Kale chips:

1. Take 1/2 bunch kale
1. Get 1 tbs olive oil
1. Get  Salt&amp;pepper


##### Steps to make Kale chips:

1. Preheat oven to 350° and line baking sheet with parchment paper.
1. Wash kale and remove leaves from stems and tear into small pieces. Toss with olive oil and any seasonings you want.
1. Line kale on baking sheet, do not overlap leaves. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top of kale. Bake 10-12 minutes.
