##### The ingredients needed to prepare Caprese Salad:

1. Get  Salad
1. Prepare 2 tomatoes
1. Prepare 20 pieces of basil
1. Take 11 oz fresh mozzarella
1. Provide  Topping
1. You need 1 salt and pepper
1. Prepare  balsamic vinaigrette reduction
1. Provide  pine nuts


##### Instructions to make Caprese Salad:

1. Cut tomato and mozzarella into about 20 slices
1. Lay down tomato, basil, and mozzarella on a plate/tray, and repeat in rows
1. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper on top
1. Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette reduction
1. Sprinkle some pine nuts over the salad
