##### The ingredients needed to make Carrot Cake Muffins:

1. You need 4 eggs
1. Prepare 1 3/4 cup veg. oil
1. Prepare 2 cup sugar
1. You need 2 cup all-purpose flour
1. You need 2 tsp vanilla extract
1. Use 2 tsp baking soda
1. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
1. Provide 1/2 tsp salt
1. You need 2 tsp ground cinnamon
1. You need 3 cup grated carrots


##### Instructions to make Carrot Cake Muffins:

1. Preheat oven to 350°; grease and floor pan.
1. In large bowl, beat together eggs, sugar, oil, and 2 tsp. vanilla extract.
1. Mix in flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
1. Stir in carrots.
1. Pour in half of mixture into the pan. Bake 18-22 min.
1. Cool muffins in pan for 10 min. Clean pan between uses.
