##### The ingredients needed to cook Southern smoky green beans:

1. Provide 1 (3 lb) can green beans, undrained
1. Provide 2 tablespoons bacon fat
1. Take 1 tablespoon minced onion, fresh or dry
1. Get to taste Onion and garlic powder
1. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
1. You need 1 teaspoon pepper
1. Get 1 tablespoon sugar


##### Steps to make Southern smoky green beans:

1. Empty can in a large pot. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to medium/low and cook with the lid offset and allow the liquid to reduce by half. Stir occasionally.
