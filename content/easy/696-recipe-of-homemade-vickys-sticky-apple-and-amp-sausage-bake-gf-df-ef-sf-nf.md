##### The ingredients needed to make Vickys Sticky Apple &amp; Sausage Bake, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Prepare  bacon
1. Provide  gluten-free pork sausages
1. Get  oil
1. Take  red apples, cut into wedges


##### Steps to make Vickys Sticky Apple &amp; Sausage Bake, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Preheat the oven to gas 7 / 220C / 450°F
1. Heat the oil in a frying pan
1. Wrap a slice of bacon around each sausage and brown off in the pan
1. Transfer the sausages to a roasting tin and put in the oven for 20 minutes, turning once halfway though. If your pan is ovenproof, just use that instead of dirtying a tin
1. Add the apple wedges to the tin and roast a further 10 minutes until the apples are caramelized and the sausages are cooked through
