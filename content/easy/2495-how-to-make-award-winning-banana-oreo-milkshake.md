##### The ingredients needed to prepare Banana oreo milkshake:

1. Take 2 pc overripe banana
1. Provide 6-7 Oreo biscuits
1. Use as needed colorful sugar balls to decorate
1. Provide 2 cup chilled milk


##### Steps to make Banana oreo milkshake:

1. Take 2 ripe bananas cut into pieces in a blender jar.
1. Add milk, 5 Oreo biscuits, and blend till smooth.
1. Take it out in a tall serving glass and garnish with some crushed Oreo biscuits and colorfull sugar balls.
1. It&#39;s ready to serve chilled.
