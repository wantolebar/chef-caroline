##### The ingredients needed to prepare Coated Prawns:

1. Use 5 large tiger prawns
1. Take  Cornstarch
1. Provide 1 egg
1. Take  White pepper
1. Get 1 little bit salt
1. Provide  Chilli powder
1. Take 1 little water
1. Use 1 little spring onion for garnishing


##### Instructions to make Coated Prawns:

1. Wash then remove shell and intestine of the prawns. Wash again and set aside
1. Mix all the ingredients above except the prawn. Make a creamy batter mixture. Then add prawns and coat it very well.
1. In a wok pour cooking oil make sure it&#39;s hot. Then deep fry prawn one by one. Make sure also that it is submerge in oil. To cook well inside.
1. Serve with mayonnaise or ketchup
