##### The ingredients needed to make Blueberry cream pie:

1. You need  Ready made Graham cracker pie crust
1. Take  softened cream cheese
1. Take  to 5 tablespoons of whip cream
1. Use  of 21 oz blueberry pie filling


##### Instructions to make Blueberry cream pie:

1. Turn your oven to 325°. Let it preheat. Once hot put your pie crust in oven for 5 min.
1. In a bowl add your cream cheese and whip cream mix it with a mixer till all blended and you have a nice and smooth cream.
1. Add your cream inside the pie crust spread your blueberry filling on top as much as you prefer.  Leave in fridge for 1 to 2 hours. And serve . Yum!
