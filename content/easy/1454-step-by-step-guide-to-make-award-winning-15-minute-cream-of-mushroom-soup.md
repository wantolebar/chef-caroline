##### The ingredients needed to make 15 Minute Cream of Mushroom Soup:

1. Use  Mushrooms
1. You need  Onion, finely chopped
1. Prepare  Garlic Cloves, finely chopped
1. You need  Butter
1. Use  Olive Oil
1. Use  Bay Leaves / Tej Patta
1. You need  Flour / Maida
1. You need  cold Milk
1. Get  or as required Water
1. Take  Salt
1. Use  Pepper Powder
1. Use  Fresh Cream


##### Steps to make 15 Minute Cream of Mushroom Soup:

1. Wash the mushrooms well, rubbing them well under running water.
1. Slice the mushrooms into quarters.
1. Heat the butter and the olive oil in a nonstick kadhai. Add the bay leaves and allow them to crackle and change colour, this should take 30-40 seconds.
1. Add the garlic &amp; the onions, saute until they turn light golden brown.
1. Add the sliced mushrooms, saute for 3-4 mins until they turn soft and shrink in size.
1. Add salt and pepper to taste. Mix.
1. After the mushrooms turn dry, add the flour, saute briskly for 1-2 mins until the flour coats the mushrooms.
1. Add the milk carefully, stir well on low heat.
1. Add the water on low heat, stirring all the while.
1. Bring the mixture to a boil, allow it to simmer on low heat until it turns thick.
1. When creamy, take the soup off heat.
1. Garnish with a little fresh cream, sprinkle a little more pepper powder if you wish. Serve hot.
