##### The ingredients needed to prepare Easiest Strawberry Cheesecake:

1. Take 250 grams Spreadable cheese portions
1. You need 100 ml Sweetened condensed milk ( u can adjust sweetness to test)
1. Take 1 dozen Butter cookies
1. You need 1 box Strawberry jello crystals
1. Take 4 Whole strawberries


##### Steps to make Easiest Strawberry Cheesecake:

1. Prepare jello mix as per packet instruction and layer at the bottom of small desert bowls or glass ( see picture) and keep in fridge to set.
1. Crush butter cookies in fine powder with roller pin. Layer over jello layer and press a little to make it a firm layer.
1. Mix cheese portions and condensed milk with a mixer at low speed, otherwise the mix will become too runny. Then pour on cookie layer and put in fridge to set.
1. Top with a strawberry and enjoy :-)
