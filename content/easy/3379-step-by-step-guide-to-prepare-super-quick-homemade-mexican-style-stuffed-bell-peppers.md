##### The ingredients needed to cook Mexican style stuffed bell peppers:

1. Provide 1 can mexican style corn
1. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
1. You need 8 oz tomato sauce
1. You need 1 cup minute rice
1. Prepare 6 bell peppers ( any color )
1. Use 1/4 cup sliced black olive
1. Take 2 tbsp each of cumin,  garlic powder,  chili powder and onion powder
1. Provide 2 tsp salt
1. Prepare 2 tsp black pepper
1. Prepare 1 cup each of white and yellow sharp cheddar cheese shredded ( divided )
1. Prepare 1 Half  cup of water


##### Steps to make Mexican style stuffed bell peppers:

1. Cut tops off of all bell peppers scoop out ribs and seeds.
1. Bring two quarts of water to a boil. Place bell peppers into boiling water for 3 to 5 minutes. Remove from water and let it drain until enough to handle
1. In the meantime put ground beef in a skillet with all of the dry ingredients and cook until no longer pink. Drain grease from ground beef. Add all remaining ingredients to ground beef mixture and bring to boil ( reserving one cup each of the white and yellow cheese )
1. Spoon meat mixture into each of the hollowed out bell peppers. Sprinkle mixture with reserved cheese. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.
