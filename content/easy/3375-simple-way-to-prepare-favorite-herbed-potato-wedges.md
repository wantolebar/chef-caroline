##### The ingredients needed to make Herbed Potato Wedges:

1. Prepare 3 tbsp parmesan cheese, grated
1. Prepare 1 tbsp basil, dried
1. Use 1/2 tbsp oregano, dried
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp garlic salt
1. Get 1/4 tsp pepper
1. Take 1 large Russet potato, cut into 12 wedges
1. Prepare 2 tsp vegetable oil


##### Instructions to make Herbed Potato Wedges:

1. Preheat oven to 400 F, spray cookie sheet with non-stick cooking spray
1. combine cheese, basil, oregano, garlic salt, and pepper in shallow bowl
1. brush potato wedges with oil
1. roll potatoes in cheese mixture, place on baking sheet
1. Bake, uncovered, for 20-25 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Serving size is 3 wedges.
