##### The ingredients needed to cook Tomato Ketchup Fried Pork Ribs:

1. Prepare 1 kg pork ribs
1. Provide 500 ml vegetable oil for deep frying
1. Take  Marinade sauce:
1. Take 5 cloves garlic, smoothly mashed using a mortar and pestle
1. Get 8 Tbsp tomato ketchup
1. Provide 3 Tbsp oyster sauce
1. Take 6 Tbsp tapioca flour
1. You need 1 tsp salt or as to taste
1. Get 1/2 tsp white pepper powder or to taste


##### Steps to make Tomato Ketchup Fried Pork Ribs:

1. In a big bowl, mix well pork ribs and the marinade sauce. Cover the bow with plastic wrap. Put it in the fridge for at least 1 hour.
1. Deep fry the pork ribs in the vegetable oil over low heat stove until cooked.
