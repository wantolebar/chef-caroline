##### The ingredients needed to prepare Chocolate pudding:

1. You need  Sugars, granulated
1. Use  Cocoa, dry powder, unsweetened
1. Prepare  Cornstarch
1. Provide  Salt, table
1. You need  Milk
1. Use  salted butter
1. Provide  Vanilla extract


##### Instructions to make Chocolate pudding:

1. In a saucepan, stir together sugar, cocoa, cornstarch and salt. Place over medium heat, and stir in milk.
1. Bring to a boil, and cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens enough to coat the back of a metal spoon.
1. Remove from heat, and stir in butter and vanilla.
1. Let cool briefly, and serve warm, or chill in refrigerator until serving.
