##### The ingredients needed to prepare Christmas Mice:

1. You need  almond bark
1. Provide  maraschino cherries
1. Take  chocolate kisses
1. You need  each coated candy pieces (I used m&amp;m&#39;s)


##### Steps to make Christmas Mice:

1. Drain cherries and set aside.
1. Unwrap the kisses and sort candy pieces by color if desired.
1. Melt almond bark in double boiler or on very low heat.
1. Coat cherry in almond bark and place on wax paper or foil. While bark is still soft, attach kiss to non-stem side of cherry. Before bark hardens completely, attach candy pieces to resemble ears.
1. After all pieces are assembled, paint eyes on kisses with white food coloring using a toothpick.
