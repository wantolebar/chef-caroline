##### Instructions to make Butter Cookies:

1. Sieve maida Bp and Bs. Keep aside.
1. Now in a bowl take butter and sugar.
1. Blend and beat it well till smooth and creamy texture.
1. Now add sieved flour, elaichi and vanilla essence.
1. Mix well till it get crumbled texture.
1. Now bind tight dough by adding milk.
1. Now make log and cut even pieces.
1. Give shape of cookies of ur choice.
1. Bake it for 15-20 mins at 180°C in preheated oven.
1. Once done let it remain in oven for 15-20 mins.
1. Delicious cookies are ready.
1. Enjoy with tea/coffee.
