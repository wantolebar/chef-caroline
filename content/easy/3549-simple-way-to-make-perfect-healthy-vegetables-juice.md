##### The ingredients needed to cook Healthy vegetables juice:

1. You need 2 medium carrot chopped
1. Use 2 tomato chopped
1. Get 2 inches Beetroot chopped
1. Take 1/4 Cucumber chopped
1. Use 10 leaves Mint
1. Provide 2-3 stems Curry leaves
1. Get 1/4 inch Ginger
1. You need 1 tsp Honey, optional
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp Lemon juice


##### Instructions to make Healthy vegetables juice:

1. Take a juicer jar add tomato, cucumber, blend it no need to add water.
1. Then add carrot, beetroot, mint, curry leaves, ginger, blend all those ingredients.
1. Strain and add lemon and honey mix it well.
1. Serve.
