##### The ingredients needed to prepare Cream of Mushroom Soup:

1. Use  Button mushrooms
1. Get 1 red onion
1. Get  Crushed garlic (dont be stingy with them)
1. Provide  Thyme
1. You need  White pepper
1. Prepare  Royco cubes/bullion
1. Prepare 6 tablespoons all purpose flour
1. Use to taste Salt
1. Provide  Butter
1. Take  Vegetable oil
1. You need Half a cup of milk or water


##### Instructions to make Cream of Mushroom Soup:

1. Chop the onions, slice the mushrooms and crush the garlics and keep aside
1. In a pan, pour some oil and add a few mushrooms. Let them cook for 6-8 mins until they are golden brown then keep aside. These are for garnishing.
1. In the same pan. Add a little oil then add butter. Once melted add the mushrooms and onions. Let them cook for 6 mins till they atart turning brown
1. Ad the garlic and stir for a few minutes
1. Add the half a teaspoon of white pepper and thyme and stir.
1. Add some water and rocyo cubes and stir. Wait till the water evaporates.
1. Add the flour and stir continuously to avoid forming lumps.then add milk or water. Keep stiring to to make a thick soup.
1. Add a table spoon of garlic and keep stirring.let it simmer for 5 mins as you stir
1. Switch of the fire and pour the thick soup in a blender and blend then return the thick soup to the same pot.
1. Cook for a min or 2 then serve the soup. Garnish with the mushrooms.
