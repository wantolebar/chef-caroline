##### The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Uthappam:

1. Prepare 2 Cup / Glass Idli Rice
1. Prepare 3/4 Cup Urad dal
1. Take 1 Cup Cooked rice or puffed rice soaked
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Methi
1. Prepare  Water as required
1. Take 1 Onion chopped
1. You need 1 Carrot Grated
1. Provide leaves Coriander
1. Get  Salt
1. Prepare  Coconut Chutney
1. Provide 1/2 Cup Grated Coconut
1. Use 1 small inch ginger
1. Take 1 small clove garlic
1. Get to taste Salt
1. Use 4/5 Green Chillies
1. Provide leaves coriander
1. Use 2/3 Tbsp Channa Dal
1. You need  Oil
1. You need leaves Curry
1. Provide 2 Dry Red Chilli
1. Prepare 1 tsp Mustard seed


##### Instructions to make Vegetable Uthappam:

1. Soak the rice and urad dal for 8 hours and Menthia.
1. Grind them into smooth batter along with cooked rice or puffed rice.Add water little
1. Transfer into a vessel and ferment over night. Next day add salt and make soft idli or dosa.
1. Coconut Chutney : In a mixer add coconut,ginger,garlic,green chilies and salt add little water and grind to smooth paste.Then add the channa dal and grind again.
1. For the tempering :Heat oil in a pan,once hot add mustard seed,dry red chilli and curry leaves and add it to chutney.
