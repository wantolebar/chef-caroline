##### The ingredients needed to cook Meatball subs:

1. Prepare 8 oz Frozen meatballs
1. Use 2 large Sub sandwich buns
1. Take 24 oz Canned spaghetti sauce
1. You need 1/3 cup Ricotta cheese
1. Prepare 1 cup Mozzarella cheese
1. You need 1 each provolone cheese


##### Steps to make Meatball subs:

1. Add spag sauce, meatballs &amp; ricotta &amp;mozz. Cheese together in slow cooker
1. Set slow cooker on high for 4- 6 hours
1. Hollow out sub buns
1. Spoon meatballs into bun, top with provolone cheese then top with sauce from slow cooker, put in broiler for 2 minutes until cheese is fully melted
