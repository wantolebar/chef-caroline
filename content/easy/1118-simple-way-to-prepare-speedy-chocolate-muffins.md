##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate muffins:

1. Prepare 1/2 cup flour
1. Get 1 1/4 cup cocoa
1. Take 3/4 tsp baking powder
1. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
1. You need 1/4 tsp baking soda
1. Take 1 1/4 cup butter
1. Take 1 1/2 cup sugar
1. Provide 1/4 tsp vannilla extract
1. You need 2 1/4 cup milk
1. Use 4 Eggs


##### Instructions to make Chocolate muffins:

1. preheat over at 200° for 30-40 mins.
1. seive the cocoa, flour, baking soda and baking powder together and keep aside.
1. In a bowl, whisk the butter and sugar till creamy.
1. Add the milk.
1. Add the seived ingredients.
1. Add the eggs. Whisk while you add all this stuff.
1. Place it in the baking tray. And cook for 15-20 mins.
1. Check if the knife comes clean, if no, cook for more time.
