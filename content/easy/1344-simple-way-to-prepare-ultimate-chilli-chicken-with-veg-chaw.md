##### Steps to make Chilli chicken with veg chaw:

1. In bowl mix 2tsp soya sauce,2 tsp red chilli sauce,egg,all flour,1tsp ginger garlic paste and salt properly add chicken pieces for marination.after marinating for 30 minutes fry in oil.
1. Put oil in another pan add capsicum,carrot and onion fry for awhile add masala.after cooking 2 minutes in high flame add some water and add chicken pieces.chilli chicken is ready
1. Now boil the chaw and drain the water.In a pan add tsp oil add veggies cook in high flame for 1 minutesa.add v1 tsp white vinigerand black pepper powder.And serve with chilli chicken
