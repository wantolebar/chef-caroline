##### The ingredients needed to cook strawberry cheesecake:

1. Prepare 3 box cream chese
1. You need 1 can condensed milk
1. Prepare 4 eggs
1. You need 60 grams flour
1. Get 1 lemon juice
1. You need 1 packages maria cookies
1. Get 120 grams softened butter
1. Prepare 1 zest of lemon
1. Prepare 1 strawberries
1. Use 200 grams strawberry jam


##### Instructions to make strawberry cheesecake:

1. Mix the grated cookies with the butter until it is all unified, like a paste
1. Make the base for the cheesecake: put the paste in a pie form
1. Put it in the oven to dry for 10 min (120 celsius). Then, let it rest out of the oven
1. Beat the cream cheese and the condensed milk
1. Add one egg at a time. Then, add the flour, lemon juice and its zest
1. Put it in the base that went to the oven and put it again in the oven for 50minutes
1. Let it cool for a while and spread strawberry jam
1. Decorate with strawberries
