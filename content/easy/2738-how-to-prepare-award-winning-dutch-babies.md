##### The ingredients needed to make Dutch Babies:

1. Use 3/4 cup AP flour
1. Take 3/4 cup milk
1. Prepare 3 eggs
1. You need 1 pinch salt
1. Prepare 2 tbsp butter


##### Steps to make Dutch Babies:

1. Preheat oven to 400F.
1. Whisk together all of the ingredients, except for the butter.
1. Melt the butter in a cast iron skillet on medium heat.
1. Swirl the butter to coat the skillet. Whisk the excess butter into the batter, then pour the batter into the greased skillet and slide the skillet into the oven.
