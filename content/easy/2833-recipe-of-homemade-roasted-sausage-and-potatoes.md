##### The ingredients needed to make Roasted Sausage and Potatoes:

1. Use  sweet potato

1. Use  medium yellow onion
1. Take  olive oil
1. Get  rosemary
1. Provide  salt
1. Take  pepper
1. Prepare  sauerkraut


##### Instructions to make Roasted Sausage and Potatoes:

1. Preheat oven to 350
1. Chop the sausage and vegetables and apple into 1 inch pieces
1. In a casserole dish put the potatoes on one half, and the sausage, onion and apple on the other half
1. Drizzle with olive oil
1. Add the rosemary to the potatoes, salt and pepper everything
1. Cover with foil. Bake 1 hour
1. Right before the dish is ready, heat sauerkraut in a skillet. Serve on the side of the Sausage and Potato mix.
1. BAM! You have zero mess. So simple. Cheers!
