##### The ingredients needed to prepare Chinese potato dumplings:

1. Use 3 big potatoes fully boiled
1. Provide 2 tb spoon soya sauce 1 for mashed potatoes 1 for filling
1. Get half t spoon black pepper
1. Get half t spoon salt
1. You need 2 tb spoon corn flour
1. Use  for filling
1. Get  quarter t spoon chicken mince
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
1. Provide 1/2 tsp black pepper
1. Take 1 onion finely chopped
1. Provide 1 tb spoon soya sauce
1. Get 1 b spoon oil


##### Steps to make Chinese potato dumplings:

1. First boil potatoes and mash well. Add,1/2 tsp salt, black pepper soya sauce and cornflour in it
1. Mix well then place a side
1. Now make filling by adding 1 tb spoon oil in a pan and sautee onion in it
1. Now add chicken mince and seasoning
1. Cook well until water gets dry.
1. Now take mashed potaoes put some filling
1. Make balls
1. Now put them in boiling water
1. Cover with lid and cook for 8 to 10 minutes
1. Ready. Serve it with desired sauce
