1. Get 1 1/2 tablespoon soya sauce

1. You need 2 tablespoon tomato chilli sauce
1. Prepare as per taste Salt
1. Prepare 1 tsp vinegar
1. Prepare 1 chopped green chilli
1. Provide 1 tbsp Cumin seeds
