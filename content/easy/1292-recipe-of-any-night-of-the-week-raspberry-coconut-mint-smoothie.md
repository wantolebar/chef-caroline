##### The ingredients needed to make Raspberry coconut mint smoothie:

1. Provide 1/2 cup coconut milk (make it 3/4th for a thinner smoothie)
1. You need 1 handful frozen or fresh raspberries (7-8nos)
1. Use 1/2 frozen banana
1. Prepare 2 sprigs mint leaves plus more for garnish
1. Use 1 tbsp flax seeds
1. You need 1/2 an apple for garnish
1. Prepare  Nuts/ dried coconut/seeds of your choice for garnish
1. Get 1-2 tbsp honey(optional)


##### Steps to make Raspberry coconut mint smoothie:

1. Blend the first 5 ingredients in the list until smooth. Add 1-2 tbsp honey if you feel the raspberries are too sour.Pour in a bowl and top up with apple, seeds, coconut and mint leaves!happy morning, happy tummy!!
1. If you want it to be a thinner smoothie, use 3/4th cup coconut milk and some ice cubes to dilute. Serve in a glass. Enjoy!
