##### The ingredients needed to cook Roasted Ranch Potatoes:

1. Get 6-8 red potatoes, chopped
1. Prepare 1 small onion, chopped
1. You need 1/2 lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled
1. Use 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1. Provide 3/4 cup Ranch dressing
1. You need  Seasonings as desired:
1. Prepare  black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, parsley


##### Instructions to make Roasted Ranch Potatoes:

1. In a large bowl combine potatoes, onion, ranch dressing and seasonings of choice; mix well.
1. Place onto a greased rimmed baking sheet. Cook for 30 minutes or until fork tender.
1. Remove from oven and give a quick stir. Top with bacon and cheese and return to oven for 5 more minutes or until hot and bubbly.
1. Enjoy!
