##### Instructions to make Masala corn cob:

1. First take corn.peel it and divide into the equal part.
1. In presser cooker.add 1tsp oil and 1tsp ghee.add ginger garlic paste,onion and saute it.
1. Add all the masala and curd.mix it properly.add water and corn.
1. And pressercooker for 3 whistles.
1. Release the air and cook till all water gets evaporated.
