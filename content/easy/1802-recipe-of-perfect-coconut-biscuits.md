##### The ingredients needed to prepare Coconut biscuits:

1. Provide 1 cup refined flour
1. Use 1/2 cup powdered sugar
1. Take 1/2 butter
1. Use 1/2 cup +3 tbsp desiccated coconut
1. Get 1/2 tsp Baking powder
1. Get 2 tsp milk


##### Steps to make Coconut biscuits:

1. In a mixing bowl take flour,sugar,butter,coconut, baking powder.mix all well. Add milk and make a soft dough.
1. Make small size balls from the dough and coated with coconut. Flat it with palm to make the shape of biscuit.prepared all the biscuits.
1. Place the biscuits on baking tray and bake in microwave on 180°for 12-15 minutes.
