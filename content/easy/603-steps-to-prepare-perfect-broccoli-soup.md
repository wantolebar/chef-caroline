##### The ingredients needed to prepare broccoli soup:

1. Provide 1 medium onion
1. Use 2 cup vegetable stock
1. Get 1/2 kg broccoli
1. You need 1 tsp salt and pepper
1. Prepare 1/4 cup milk
1. Get 1 1/4 tbsp flour
1. You need 1 tbsp veg oil


##### Steps to make broccoli soup:

1. in a pot, fry onion with oil. When it starts to turn brown,add flour and 2 min later add the milk, mix together until smooth.
1. add the vegetable stock , salt and pepper and when it boils (5 to 10 min) add the broccoli.
1. blend half of the soup, and when it&#39;s.well blended add it to the other half
1. heat it up again.and serve hot.
