##### Steps to make Pork and Pablano Stew:

1. char pablanos and set aside covered for 10 minutes. peel and cut into 1 inch squares.set aside.
1. cut pork into 1-2 inch squares.  add cumin salt and pepper. brown well.
1. add chopped onion and diced garlic. cook 5 minutes then add pablanos and water to cover.
1. bring to simmer. add cilantro after 80 minutes.
1. add cubed potatoes and corn,  cover with water.  bring to simmer for 1 hour or till pork is tender.
