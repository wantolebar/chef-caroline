##### The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate Banana Smoothie:

1. Provide 2 bananas, frozen, sliced
1. You need 2 cup skim milk with a squirt of chocolate syrup
1. Use 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1. Take 2 tbsp cocoa powder (sweetened or unsweetened depending on your sweetness preference)


##### Instructions to make Chocolate Banana Smoothie:

1. Combine all ingredients into blender.
1. Blend on high until smooth.
1. Serve and enjoy!
