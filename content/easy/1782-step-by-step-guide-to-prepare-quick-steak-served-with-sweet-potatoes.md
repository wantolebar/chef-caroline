##### The ingredients needed to cook Steak served with sweet potatoes:

1. You need 120 gm Roast Beef filé (1.5 inch thickness)
1. Provide 50 gm salted butter
1. Provide 1 pinch thyme
1. Take 1 pinch salt
1. You need 1 pinch black pepper
1. Use  Sweet potatoes mashed
1. You need 250 gm sweet potatoes (slice into chunks)
1. Take 0.5 dl cream
1. Get 10 gm salted butter
1. Prepare 1 pinch salt
1. Prepare 2 pinch black pepper


##### Instructions to make Steak served with sweet potatoes:

1. Heat the frying pan, add in the roast beef filé in the same time add in the rest of the ingredients. Fry each side for 3.5min.
1. Sweet potatoes mashed. Boiled a pot of hot water, pour in the sweet potatoes chunks, cook for 20min. Take it out and use the potatoes mashed tool to compress the sweet potatoes until smooth texture then add in the rest of the ingredients and mixed well.
