##### Steps to make Drop scones, juice, boiled eggs, tea &amp;salad #Eldy bfast contest:

1. To make drop scones, put flour in a bowl add sugar, stir add the two beaten eggs add melted butter n little milk and stir till its fairly smooth for pancakes
1. Heat a large based frying pan on moderate heat, grease with 1tsp of oil
1. Using a dinner soon pour the mixture from the pointed end to make round drop scones space them up, a pan should carry 4-6 depending on the size
1. Cook until golden Brown use a spatula to overturn them to cook both sides... Keep them in a bowl to remain warm
1. To make tea, boil a cup of milk, add half cup of water then add the right amount of tea leaves depending on the number of people....
1. U can add other spices, like tea masala, ginger or cinnamon to taste as prefered
1. To make the juice.. Blend pineapples oranges and beetroot all together and then sieve to make a fine juice solution and serve
1. Chop ovacados, onions and tomatoes to make the salad
1. Serve both the drop scones, eggs, salad, juice and tea... Enjoy
