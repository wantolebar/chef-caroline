##### The ingredients needed to cook pulled pork:

1. You need  crock pot
1. You need  pork shoulder/ butt roast
1. Take  onion soup
1. Prepare  rootbeer
1. Get  BBQ sauce


##### Instructions to make pulled pork:

1. rub onion mix on pork
1. sprinkle salt and pepper
1. place in crook pot
1. mix rootbeer and 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
1. pour BBQ rootbeer on pork
1. cook on low for  11 hours on low
1. after 11 hours, shred pork
1. mix 3/4 cup of the juice and 1 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
1. mix shredded pork with sauce and slow cook on high for 20 min.
