##### The ingredients needed to make Polish Pierogi:

1. Get  dough
1. Provide 350 grams flour
1. Use 1 egg
1. You need 125 ml hot water
1. Provide  filling
1. Take 100 grams cottage cheese/quark
1. Use 200 grams pre-boiled potatoes
1. Prepare 50 grams onion


##### Steps to make Polish Pierogi:

1. Sift flour,add 1 egg, hot water and mix to a dough
1. Mix all filling ingredients
1. Using rolling pin,roll the dough,using round cutter,cut circles
1. Fill the dough circles with the cheese filling,and close half creating little crescents
1. Cook in salted water for 3 min
1. Serve with fried pancetta and onion or melted butter and enjoy!
