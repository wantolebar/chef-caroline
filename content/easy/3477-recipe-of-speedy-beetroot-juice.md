##### The ingredients needed to cook Beetroot juice:

1. You need 2 medium beets - scrubbed clean and tops trimmed
1. Get 1 medium - cucumber chopped
1. You need 1 inch - ginger
1. Take 1 medium lemon


##### Instructions to make Beetroot juice:

1. Chop the beets, cucumber and ginger into thin pieces small enough to easily go through the juicer.
1. Grind it and make a smooth pulp.
1. Strain it properly. Pour into a clean glass. Add lemon juice. Drink it taste is yummy
