##### The ingredients needed to make Air Fried Pepper Poppers:

1. Use 8 oz. Cream cheese (softened)
1. Take 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1. Provide 3/4 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1. You need 6 bacon strips, cooked &amp; crumbled
1. Provide 1/4 tsp salt
1. Get 1/4 tsp garlic powder
1. Use 1/4 tsp paprika
1. Use 1/4 tsp chilli powder
1. Take 9-10 jalapenos halved lengthwise &amp; seeded
1. You need 1/2 bread crumbs


##### Instructions to make Air Fried Pepper Poppers:

1. Preheat the air fryer to 325 degrees.
1. In a large bowl combine the 1st 8 ingredients. Mix well.
1. Spoon 1 1/2-2 tablespoons into the pepper. Then roll in the breadcrumbs.
1. Spritz the basket with cooking spray. Then place the pooper in a single layer in the basket.
1. Cook at 325 degrees for 15 minutes for spicy flavor, or as long as 25 minutes for a more mild flavor. Be careful not to over cook.
