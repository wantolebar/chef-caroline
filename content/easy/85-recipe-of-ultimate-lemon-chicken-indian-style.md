##### Instructions to make Lemon Chicken Indian Style:

1. In a bowl mix boneless chicken, lemon juice, lemon zest, black pepper powder and salt
1. Marinate for 1 hour in refrigerator In a pan heat oil. Add cumin seeds, minced ginger, minced garlic, turmeric powder and coriander powder once oil is hot. Mix it.
1. Add 1 tbsp water and mix again.
1. Add marinated chicken and keep stirring for Approximate 5 minutes.
1. Add more 1 tbsp water and stir once
1. Let it cook until chicken is tender. [Approximate 5 min]
