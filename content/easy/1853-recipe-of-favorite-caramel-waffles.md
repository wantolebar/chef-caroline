##### The ingredients needed to prepare Caramel waffles:

1. Take 90 GMs Maida,oil, milk, buttermilk each
1. Take 1 spoon sugar
1. Take 2 drops Vanilla essence
1. Get Half teaspoon of baking powder and cooking soda
1. Take Pinch salt
1. Get  For sandwich filling
1. Use 2 teaspoons sugar butter
1. Use 2 drops vanilla essence


##### Instructions to make Caramel waffles:

1. Mix maida baking powder baking soda salt oil milk buttermilk and vanilla essence and mix well.and rest for 10 mins.
1. Spray oil on waffle maker and spread the batter. Now you have two options bake in waffle iron or if you have a silicon mould you can bake for 180 degrees for 10 mins in an oven.
1. For filling.. take a microwave safe bowl add butter sugar and vanilla essence and keep it on high for 30 seconds. Repeat 3 times do not keep it at a stretch for more than 30 seconds.
1. Now take one waffle spread the Carmel sauce and layer it another one. Serve to kids to enjoy their expression.
