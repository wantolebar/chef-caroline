##### The ingredients needed to cook Bacon Cinnamon Rolls:

1. Use  Pillsbury Cinnamon Rolls:Grand
1. Take  Applewood Smoked Bacon
1. Get  Confectioner&#39;s Sugar: Sifted
1. You need  Maple Syrup
1. Use  Unsalted butter


##### Steps to make Bacon Cinnamon Rolls:

1. Open can of Pillsbury Cinnamon Rolls, and separate each roll carefully.
1. Unroll them and place 2 slices of bacon inside each roll.  Pre-cook bacon by cooking it for 2minutes for extra crispness
1. Roll up the cinnamon rolls from the inside out. Place rolls in an 8in baking dish.
1. Bake for 25-30 minutes at 350°F. Look for a nice golden brown.
1. To make maple syrup frosting, combine confectioner&#39;s sugar, maple syrup, and butter in a bowl.
1. Whisk together and pour over cinnamon rolls.
