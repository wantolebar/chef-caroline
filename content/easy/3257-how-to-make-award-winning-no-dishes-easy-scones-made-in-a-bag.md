##### The ingredients needed to prepare No Dishes! Easy Scones Made in a Bag:

1. Get 200 grams Pancake mix
1. You need 40 grams Margarine (or butter)
1. Use 50 ml Milk
1. Prepare 1 Chocolate bar
1. Get 1 Freezer bag


##### Steps to make No Dishes! Easy Scones Made in a Bag:

1. Put the freezer bag on a kitchen scale, then add the margarine and pancake mix while measuring. Rub through the bag.
1. Add half of the milk and rub again. Add the remaining milk a little at a time while keeping an eye on the consistency. When the dough comes together together, it&#39;s ready.
1. Add the broken up chocolate and fold it into the dough. (I add milk chocolate at this point )
1. Shape the dough into a long piece, then remove from the bag and cut into 3-4 pieces. Then, cut each piece diagonally into triangles. (This will make them look like the scones from Starbucks.)
1. Bake in an oven at 170℃ for 25 minutes to finish.
