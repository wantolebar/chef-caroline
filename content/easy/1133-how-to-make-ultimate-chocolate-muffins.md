##### The ingredients needed to cook Chocolate muffins:

1. Get 1 cup oats
1. Use 1 cup whole wheat flour
1. You need 1 cup milk
1. Provide 3 tbsp yoghurt
1. You need 1/2 tsp coffee powder
1. Use 1 tbsp cocoa
1. Get As per taste honey
1. Provide 1/2 tbsp vanilla essence
1. You need As required choco chips
1. Use 1/4 tbsp baking soda


##### Steps to make Chocolate muffins:

1. Take oats, wheat flour, cocoa, coffee powder in a jar and grind.
1. Dry ingredients are ready.
1. Take milk, curd, vanilla essence, honey and blend.
1. Wet ingredients are ready.
1. Add wet ingrdeients into dry ingredients and mix. Add choco chips and mix.
1. In the end add 1/4 tbsp of baking soda and mix.
1. Preheat the oven, and pour the batter into silicon molds, or greased cup.
1. Top with chocochips and bake for 10 minutes on 185 degree celsius. Serve hot.
