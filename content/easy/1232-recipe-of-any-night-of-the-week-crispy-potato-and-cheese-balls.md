##### Steps to make Crispy potato and cheese balls:

1. Take a pan add 1tsp oil then add mashed boiled potatoes then add red chilli flakes black and white pepper salt and chicken powder mix it well cook for 5 min then put aside
1. Then put it in refregerator for 15 mints then stuff cheese cube if the middle of batter and make small balls
1. Now for coating add egg in a bowl and add cornflour in it beat it well then dip the ball in it first then coat with bread crumbs put them in refregerator for 10 mints then add oil in a wok and deep fry and serve..
