##### The ingredients needed to make Beetroot juice:

1. Prepare 1 large beetroot
1. Use 3 fresh ginger (small)
1. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
1. Get  Ice
1. Get  Lemon
1. Use 1 cup water


##### Instructions to make Beetroot juice:

1. Wash beetroot and peel
1. Cut into cubes and put in a blender
1. Wash ginger and add to blender
1. Squeeze in lemon and add sugar
1. Add water and blend
1. Sieve and serve with ice
