##### The ingredients needed to prepare Apple tart:

1. Provide  flour
1. Take  sugar
1. Take  salt
1. Get  or 85 grams unsalted butter,
1. Prepare  chilled water
1. Get  apples peeled, cored and sliced
1. Use  unsalted butter, melted
1. Use  sugar


##### Instructions to make Apple tart:

1. Preheat oven to 200°C
1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl; add 2 tbsp of the butter. combine until dough look rough. Add remaining butter; mix until its mixed together
1. Add in little water, combine then add in more water, until the dough holds together; form dough into a ball and knead for about 5 minutes. Flatten into a 4-inch-thick disk; refrigerate. After at least 30 minutes, remove;
1. Flour the surface and place dough and knead for another 5 minutes, then roll into circle.
1. Heat up a small pot; add apple, butter and sugar stir to caramelized for about 3minutes and remove from heat then set aside to cool off
1. Use a cookie cutter to cut your desired shape and place a spoon of your caramelized apple and cover with another dough
1. Once done, use a pastry brush and brush either butter or whisk egg on it and place in a baking tray and bake for about 18 - 20 minutes
1. Remove from oven once its done and serve
