##### The ingredients needed to make Beet root juice:

1. You need 3 beet root
1. Use 6 tbsp sugar
1. Get  Mint
1. You need  Lemon
1. Get 1 tbsp ginger
1. Use  Cold water


##### Instructions to make Beet root juice:

1. Peel the beet root and cut it into small
1. Add all ingredients in a blender and blend
1. Sieve the beet root pour it in a glass cup ganished with mint and lemon
1. Enjoy!
