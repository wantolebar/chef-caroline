##### The ingredients needed to prepare Stuff Tomatoes:

1. Take 4 tomatoes
1. You need 1 cup poha
1. Use 1 tbsp oil
1. Use 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp chilli powder
1. Use 1/4 tsp garam masala
1. Take 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
1. You need 1 tbsp sugar powder
1. Provide  Salt as per taste
1. Take 1/2 tbsp chopped chilli
1. Prepare 1/2 tbsp peanut powder
1. Take 1/2 tbsp coconut Dessicated


##### Steps to make Stuff Tomatoes:

1. Take poha in seive and wash it properly in running water. Then keep for 10 min to drain excess water.
1. Take tomatoes cut the eyes of it. Now put half cross cut on the base of tomatoes.
1. For tempering. Heat the oil and add turmeric, chilli powder, salt, lemon juice, peanut powder, garam masala, sugar and mix it well. Switch off the gas and add coconut.
1. Now fill the tomatoes wth poha stuffing. Garnish wth coriander leaves and sev.
