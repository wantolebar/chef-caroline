##### Instructions to make Chicken potato curry:

1. Prepare and mix the spice mix and set aside.
1. Heat butter or oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat.
1. Soften onion and garlic for a few minutes, stirring often.
1. Add the spice mix and cook for a few minutes, stirring often.
1. Add chicken and cook until completely white, about ten minutes, stirring often.
1. Add the yogurt, tomato paste, potato and water. Ingredients should be covered by liquid, so add more water if not covered.
1. Bring to boil, cover, reduce the heat and simmer for about an hour, until sauce has reduced and thickened to the consistency you like.
1. Serve with rice, with a side of flat bread, additional greek yogurt and mango chutney.
