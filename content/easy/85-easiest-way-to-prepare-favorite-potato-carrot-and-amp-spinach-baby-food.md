##### The ingredients needed to cook Potato, carrot &amp; spinach baby food:

1. Prepare  new potatoes
1. Use  a carrot
1. Get  spinach
1. Prepare  breast or formula milk


##### Steps to make Potato, carrot &amp; spinach baby food:

1. Prep ingredients, wash potato and carrot and chop for cooking. Boil potato and carrots
1. When carrots and potato are soft take off the boil and let them cool
1. When cool blend with spinach and milk. Add more milk for a thinner consistency. Younger babies may want it to be more liquid.
1. Note on storage. For baby food mixed with breast milk this can be kept in the fridge for 3 days from when the milk was pumped and 6 months in the freezer. For formula follow your providers instructions.
