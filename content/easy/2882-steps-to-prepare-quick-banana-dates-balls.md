##### The ingredients needed to make Banana Dates Balls:

1. Prepare  ripe Bananas
1. You need  dates
1. Provide  vanilla essence
1. Get  choco chips


##### Steps to make Banana Dates Balls:

1. Mash all bananas in a bowl.
1. Have a medium blend of dates in a blender.
1. Take it out and add this into mashed banana bowl and give a good mix.
1. Then add vanila essence to it and mix it.
1. Stir until everything is combined.
1. The mixture should be slightly sticky and clump together nicely.
1. Take about a tablespoon of the mixture and roll into balls.
1. Stick chocochips on the balls.
1. Continue until all the mixture is used up and store them in the fridge for 15 to 20mins.
1. Take out and rest it for few mins.
1. Enjoy this yummy recipe for kids.
