##### The ingredients needed to cook Delicious Chewy Chocolate Chip and M&amp;M Cookies:

1. Use 1 3/4 cup flour
1. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1. Get 1 teaspoon salt
1. You need 12 tablespoons melted butter
1. Get 3/4 cup brown sugar packed
1. Provide 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
1. You need 2 teaspoons vanilla
1. Take 2 cups chocolate chips
1. You need 1 cup M&amp;M


##### Instructions to make Delicious Chewy Chocolate Chip and M&amp;M Cookies:

1. In a large bowl mix flour, baking soda, and salt
1. In a separate bowl mix butter, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla together
1. Then slowly add dry ingredients and mix
1. Then add chocolate chips and M&amp;Ms
1. Spray baking sheets with cooking spray and scoop cookies onto sheet
1. Bake for 10 minutes at 350°F
