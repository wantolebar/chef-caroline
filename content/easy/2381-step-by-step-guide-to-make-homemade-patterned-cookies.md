##### The ingredients needed to make Patterned cookies:

1. You need  Flour300g
1. You need 100 g Sugar
1. Provide 80 g Icing sugar
1. Prepare 200 g Margarine
1. You need 50 g Cornflour
1. Take 1/4 tsp Butterscotch flavour


##### Steps to make Patterned cookies:

1. Sieve flour,set aside.Cream margarine,add icing sugar,sugar,fold indry ingredients gradually,add flavour last,mix well
1. Grease baking tray with flour,put little flour on palm,place dough little by little on palm,flatten,put on floured working table,cut to desired shape and put on tray,bake
