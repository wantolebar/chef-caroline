##### The ingredients needed to prepare Quick Mexican Shrimp Cocktail:

1. Take 10 large /12 medium/16 small cooked shrimp
1. Provide 1 ripe but firm diced avocado
1. Prepare 1 jalapeno seeded and finely chopped
1. Take 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1. Take  Juice of 1 fresh lime
1. Take 2 scallions chopped (white and green part)


##### Steps to make Quick Mexican Shrimp Cocktail:
