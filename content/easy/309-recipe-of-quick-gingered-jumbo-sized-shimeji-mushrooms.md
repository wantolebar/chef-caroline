##### The ingredients needed to cook Gingered Jumbo-Sized Shimeji Mushrooms:

1. Use  Jumbo-sized shimeji mushrooms
1. Prepare  Grated ginger
1. Get  Soy sauce
1. You need  Mirin
1. Use  Sake
1. Use  Sesame oil


##### Instructions to make Gingered Jumbo-Sized Shimeji Mushrooms:

1. Chop off the stem end of the jumbo shimeji mushrooms and shred them into single stems.
1. Pour sesame oil into a frying pan and sauté the jumbo shimeji.
1. Combine the grated ginger, soy sauce, mirin, and sake, and pour into the frying pan. Briskly sauté to coat the mushrooms, then serve.
