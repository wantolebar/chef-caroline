##### The ingredients needed to prepare Farali sweet potato:

1. You need 2 big sized sweet potatoes
1. Provide 2 green chillies
1. Provide 2 tbsp roasted peanut coarse powder
1. You need 1 lemon juice
1. Take 1/2 tsp each black salt
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Provide 2 spoons ghee
1. You need 1 tbspn jeera
1. Prepare 2 teaspoons coriander leaves finely chopped
1. Get 1 Teaspoon fresh grated coconut


##### Instructions to make Farali sweet potato:

1. Wash and boil sweet potato till soft with enough water.
1. Then chop sweet potato in medium size pieces.
1. Heat ghee in a pan add jeera, chillies finely chopped and saute for a while then add sweet potato and mix it well.
1. Add black salt and salt,peanuts,lemon juice top with coriander leaves, coconut.
1. Serve in plate and it&#39;s ready to serve.
