##### The ingredients needed to prepare Vickys Berry &amp; Rice Muesli, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Prepare 125 grams cooked brown rice - see instruction below
1. Provide 125 grams dried cranberries or strawberries
1. Take 50 grams millet flakes
1. You need 50 grams mixed raisins &amp; sultanas
1. You need 25 grams shelled sunflower seeds
1. You need 25 grams dessicated/shredded coconut


##### Steps to make Vickys Berry &amp; Rice Muesli, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. For 125g cooked rice you need 50g dry grains. Simmer in twice as much water until cooked then put into a sieve and rinse with cold water. Spread out evenly on a baking sheet and sit it in the oven until it&#39;s dried out. I use gas 4 / 180C / 350°F and every now &amp; again fork it through so it dries evenly
1. Combine all ingredients in an airtight container and keep in the fridge for up to 3 days
1. Serve with some chilled milk of choice - hemp, rice, almond or coconut milk are some dairy-free types
1. You can sub out some of the dried fruit for others such as papaya, pineapple, banana chips etc to your own taste. Any dried fruits &amp; berries work well
