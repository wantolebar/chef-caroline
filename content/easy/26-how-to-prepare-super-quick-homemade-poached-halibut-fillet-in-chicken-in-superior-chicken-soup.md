##### The ingredients needed to make Poached Halibut Fillet In Chicken In Superior Chicken Soup:

1. Use  EASY SUPERIOR SOUP
1. Use  skinless chicken thigh
1. Provide  Chinese red dates
1. Take  GOJI berries / wolf berry
1. Get  bottle of essence of chicken (70gram)
1. Take  water
1. Take  lightly crush ginger
1. Take  salt
1. You need  GARNISH
1. Use  fresh coriander leaf and spring onion
1. You need  HALIBUT FILLET
1. Use  HALIBUT fillet or any fish


##### Instructions to make Poached Halibut Fillet In Chicken In Superior Chicken Soup:

1. Put all soup ingredients into pressure cooker on high for 20 minute
1. Season with salt then simmer for 3 minute set aside
1. SERVE OPTION SUPERIOR CHICKEN SOUP AS IT IS
1. POACHED HALLIBUT
1. On a pan bring 2 Cup of superior soup to a boil then simmer down add hallibut fillet and poached for 4 minute or longer depending on the thickness of the fish
1. Serve immediately
