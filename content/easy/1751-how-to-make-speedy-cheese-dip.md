##### The ingredients needed to cook Cheese dip:

1. Use  cheese slices, torn into pieces
1. Prepare  milk
1. Prepare  dried mixed herbs
1. Prepare  dry red chilli flakes (paprika)


##### Steps to make Cheese dip:

1. Combine the cheese slices, milk, mixed herbs and chilli flakes in a broad non-stick pan, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 2 to 3 minutes, while stirring continuously.
1. Allow the cheese dip to cool completely.
1. Serve as you like.I serve with chips and rusk too.
