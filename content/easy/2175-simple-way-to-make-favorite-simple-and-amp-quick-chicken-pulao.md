##### The ingredients needed to prepare Simple &amp; Quick Chicken Pulao:

1. Provide 300-400 gm chicken with bones
1. Take 2 bay leaves
1. Take 2 star anise
1. Use 2-3 green cardamoms
1. Prepare 5-6 cloves
1. Provide 2 marathi moggu
1. Prepare 1 mace
1. You need 2 onions, sliced
1. Provide 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1. Provide 1 tsp tomato paste
1. Take 1 tsp lime juice
1. You need 1 tsp red chilli powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1. Prepare 1 tbsp coriander-cumin powder
1. Use 1 tsp garam masala powder
1. Get to taste salt
1. You need 1-2 potatoes, cut into half
1. Take 2 green chilies
1. Use 1 cup basmati rice, soaked in water for an hour
1. You need 1 tbsp ghee
1. Take 1 tbsp oil
1. You need As needed mint leaf to garnish
1. Provide As needed raita &amp; salad to serve


##### Instructions to make Simple &amp; Quick Chicken Pulao:

1. Marinate the chicken with ginger-garlic paste, tomato paste, lime juice and all the dry powdered spices for an hour.
1. Heat the ghee and oil. Temper with bay leaves and all the whole spices. Saute for a few seconds.
1. Add the onion and fry till light brown.
1. Add the marinated chicken and saute on a medium flame for 2-3 minutes.
1. Add the drained rice and continue to saute for 2 minutes.
1. Now add 1 &amp; 1/2 cups water, green chilies and the potatoes. Pressure cook for 1-2 whistles.
1. When done, fluff it with a fork. Garnish with mint and serve with raita and salad for a wholesome one-pot meal.
