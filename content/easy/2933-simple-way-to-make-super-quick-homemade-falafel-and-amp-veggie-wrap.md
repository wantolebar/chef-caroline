##### The ingredients needed to prepare Falafel &amp; Veggie wrap:

1. Prepare 1 Wrap or Tortilla Wrap
1. Take  Main ingredients
1. Use 2 tbsp Hummus
1. Get 1 tbsp of Mango Chutney OR 3 Sundried Tomatos. Not both.
1. Get 4 Falafels
1. You need  Veggie blend
1. Get 200 grams Spinach (cooked &amp; shredded)
1. Get 200 grams Carrots (grated)
1. Use 1 bunch Coriander to taste (optional)
1. You need  Optional Cheese
1. Use 100 grams of soft cheese like Feta or Goats&#39; cheese is best for this recipe. Cheddar and similar cheeses also work well.


##### Steps to make Falafel &amp; Veggie wrap:

1. Spread Hummus and, if using, Mango Chutney.
1. Add veggie blend and Sundried Tomatos, again if using.
1. You can warm the Falafels according to packet instuctions, but I prefer not to. Otherwise, wrap and heat for 30 odd seconds in a microwave. Enjoy!
