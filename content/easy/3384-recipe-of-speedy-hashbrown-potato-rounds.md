##### The ingredients needed to prepare Hashbrown Potato Rounds:

1. Provide 5 lb bag idaho potatoes
1. Prepare 5 tbsp bacon grease
1. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste


##### Steps to make Hashbrown Potato Rounds:

1. peel then rinse potatoes.
1. slice potatoes thin and blot with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
1. heat bacon grease in the bottom of a large skillet with a lid.
1. place sliced potatoes on top.
1. sprinkle with salt and pepper.
1. place lid on potatoes and cook on medium/high heat for ten minutes.
1. scrape up and flip potatoes. replace lid. and cook for 15 min. flipping every 5 minutes. ( when removing lid let the water/condensation drip into the skillet.)
1. remove lid and cook another 10 min.. still flipping every 5 min.
1. remove from heat and serve.
1. enjoy!
1. u can also add nearly any type of meat to this as well. or serve alongside your meal.
1. serves 4-5 people.
