##### The ingredients needed to prepare Banana &amp; Hazelnut Chocolate Muffins:

1. Prepare 210 g plain flour
1. Take 150 g caster sugar
1. You need 1 pinch salt
1. Use 2 teaspoons baking powder
1. Take 75 ml vegetable oil
1. Provide 75 ml full fat milk
1. Prepare 1 medium sized egg
1. Use 3 medium sized ripe bananas, mashed
1. Take 12 heaped teaspoons hazelnut chocolate spread


##### Instructions to make Banana &amp; Hazelnut Chocolate Muffins:

1. Pre-heat your oven to 200 C / Gas 6. Place 12 muffin cases in a 12 hole muffin tin.
1. Add the flour, sugar, salt and baking powder to a large bowl and mix well. Measure the oil and milk into a jug together and stir into the flour mixture. Stir to just combine, no need to overdo it. Add the egg and, again, just stir it in to combine. Finally stir in the mashed up banana.
1. Add 2 teaspoons of mixture into each muffin case and then one heaped teaspoon of hazelnut chocolate in the centre but onto the top of each. Add another teaspoon of mixture over the top of the chocolate to cover it. Finally drop a tiny ‘blob’ of hazelnut chocolate on the top and create a swirl effect by dragging a sewer through it over the top.
1. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove from the oven and transfer from the muffin tin to a wire rack to cool immediately.
