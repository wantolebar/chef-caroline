##### The ingredients needed to prepare Seafood Supper:

1. Get 1 bag mixed frozen seafood, defrosted
1. You need 1 onion, diced
1. Provide 1 clove garlic, crushed
1. You need 1 tin plum tomatoes
1. Get Splash Fish sauce
1. You need Pinch dried chilli
1. Get 1 tbsp tomato paste
1. Prepare  Rapeseed oil for sautéing
1. Prepare 1 lemon


##### Steps to make Seafood Supper:

1. Sauté diced onion until soft. Add crushed garlic and dried chilli and cook for a minute over a low heat.
1. Tip in the tinned tomatoes and break up. Loosen with approx 50ml hot water.
1. Add a splash of fish sauce to season.
1. Cool, covered for 10 minutes.
1. Check seasoning of the tomatoes. Add a grind of black pepper. Don’t add salt at this stage as the seafood is salty.
1. Add seafood to the hot tomato mix, give a thorough stir and heat through for 5 minutes or until piping hot.
1. Add a squeeze of lemon juice check seasoning and adjust if needed.
1. Serve with your favourite vegetables and perhaps some rice or crusty bread if you’re especially hungry.
