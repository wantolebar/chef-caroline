##### The ingredients needed to prepare Broccoli and chicken casserole:

1. Provide  onion, chopped
1. Provide  granulated garlic powder
1. Provide  salt
1. Take  eggs
1. Get  broccoli
1. Take  chicken breasts cooked and shredded
1. Take  chicken broth


##### Steps to make Broccoli and chicken casserole:

1. Preheat oven 400° Fahrenheit
1. Microwave the broccoli for 9 minutes on high. Drain any liquids off.
1. Add to an oven safe dish onion, chicken, and broccoli.
1. In a mixing bowl mix the salt, garlic, broth, milk, and eggs.
1. Pour over the chicken, broccoli,  and onions. Incorporate well. Bake in oven uncovered for 40 minutes or until done. Especially in the middle.
1. Let sit 10 minutes.  Serve I hope you enjoy!
