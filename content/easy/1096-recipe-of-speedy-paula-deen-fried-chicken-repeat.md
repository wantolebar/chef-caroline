##### The ingredients needed to prepare Paula Deen Fried Chicken Repeat:

1. Use 1 packages Leg quarter
1. Take 1 cup Hot sauce tx Pete
1. Get 1 tbsp Garlic pepper and Cajun seasoning

1. Take 1 Canola oil
1. You need 3 large Eggs
1. Prepare 1/3 cup Water


##### Instructions to make Paula Deen Fried Chicken Repeat:

1. Wash and clean chicken,cut up,season with garlic pepper/Cajun seasoning let rest.put your oil on to heat,beat eggs,hot sauce,water to well mix,dip the chicken in egg wash then season flour to hot oil and repeat.
