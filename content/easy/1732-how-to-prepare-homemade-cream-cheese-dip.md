##### The ingredients needed to cook Cream Cheese Dip:

1. Prepare  milk
1. Prepare  grated cheese
1. Prepare  finely chopped red capsicum
1. Prepare  finely chopped yellow capsicum
1. Get  finely chopped green capsicum
1. You need  cornflour
1. You need  chilli flakes
1. Take  oregano
1. Take  freshlyground black pepper powder
1. Provide  Salt


##### Instructions to make Cream Cheese Dip:

1. Mix milk and corn flour in a microwave safe bowl. Add grated cheese and capsicum to it.
1. Mix well. Cook for 2 minutes.  - Again mix and add salt, oregano,black pepper, chilli flakes.  - Be careful while adding salt.
1. Pour Cream Cheese sauce in a serving bowl and serve with nachos.
