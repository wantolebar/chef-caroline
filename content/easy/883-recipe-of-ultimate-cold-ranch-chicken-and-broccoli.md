##### The ingredients needed to make Cold ranch chicken and broccoli:

1. Prepare  marinated cooked chicken breasts, cut in pieces
1. Use  shredded carrots
1. Prepare  raw broccoli florets
1. Provide  shredded cheddar cheese
1. Take  give or take of Ranch dressing
1. Provide  bacon bits
1. You need  Pepper
1. Prepare  Pepper


##### Steps to make Cold ranch chicken and broccoli:

1. Chop any long stems of the broccoli florets, and cut the florets into bite sized or smaller pieces
1. Add the cut up chicken, broccoli and shredded carrots into a large mixing bowl
1. Stir in the ranch slowly so you can determine if you need all of it or more of it. I used a flavored Garlic ranch which just adds a bit more flavor.
1. Stir in cheese and bacon bits and season with pepper
1. Chill for 30 min to an hour
