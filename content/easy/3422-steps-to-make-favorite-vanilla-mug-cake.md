##### The ingredients needed to cook Vanilla mug cake:

1. Use 4 tsp all-purpose flour
1. Use 5 tsp condensed milk
1. Prepare  skim milk
1. Take 3 tsp oil
1. Provide 1/3 tsp vanilla extract
1. Provide 1/4 tsp baking powder and baking soda
1. You need 1 pinch of salt


##### Steps to make Vanilla mug cake:

1. Mix the flour , baking powder and soda, salt together well in a big mug
1. Add the condensed milk , oil , vanilla extract together and mix the batter
1. Keep adding milk slowly on and on , till u acheive ribbon like flowy batter .
1. Bake at 600 watts fr 2 min or 900 watts for 90 seconds .
1. Add your favourite topping or enjoy plain !
