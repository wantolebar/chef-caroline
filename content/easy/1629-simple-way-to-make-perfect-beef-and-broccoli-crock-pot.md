##### The ingredients needed to make Beef and broccoli crock pot:

1. Provide  boneless beef chuck roast sliced into strips
1. You need  beef broth
1. Use  soy sauce
1. You need  brown sugar
1. Provide  sesame oil
1. You need  minced garlic
1. Use  cornstarch
1. Get  sauce from crock pot after being cooked
1. Provide  Fresh broccoli
1. Take  Cooked rice


##### Instructions to make Beef and broccoli crock pot:

1. Place beef in crock pot. In a small bowl combine broth, soy sauce, sugar, oil and garlic. Pour over the beef. Cook on low 6-8 hours.
1. In a cup combine cornstarch and cooked sauce and stir until smooth.  Add to crock pot. Stir well to combine. Add broccoli to crock pot and stir to combine.
1. Cover and cook 30 mins more on high. The sauce will boil and thicken. Serve over rice.
