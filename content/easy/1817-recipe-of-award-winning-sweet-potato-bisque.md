##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Potato Bisque:

1. Prepare 2 tbsp Butter
1. Get 3 garlic cloves
1. Prepare 1 onion (sweet)
1. Use 1 dash ground ginger
1. Use  salt and pepper
1. Provide 1/8 tsp ground chipotle pepper
1. Provide 4 cup chicken broth
1. Get 1 1/4 cup apple cider
1. Prepare 4 cup cooked &amp; mashed sweet potatoes
1. Take 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
1. Prepare 1/3 cup white sugar
1. Prepare 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
1. Provide 2 tbsp molasses
1. Provide 2 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon


##### Steps to make Sweet Potato Bisque:

1. Sauté garlic, onions, and ginger. 3-4 mins.
1. Add chipotle pepper, sauté 1 min.
1. Add all other ingredients. Bring to simmer. Simmer until warm throughout and serve.
