##### The ingredients needed to prepare Inari Sushi (Sushi Rice in Deep Fried Tofu, Aburaage):

1. You need 1 tablespoon sugar
1. Provide 2 tablespoon water
1. Use 1 bowl cooked rice
1. Provide  sesame
1. Prepare 1 teaspoon sweet vinegar
1. Get  carrot
1. Use  Green vegetable


##### Instructions to make Inari Sushi (Sushi Rice in Deep Fried Tofu, Aburaage):

1. Cut Aburaage and open it like pocket.
1. Put soy sauce, sugar and water.
1. Cook Aburaage till soy sugar sauce is thickened.
1. Put sesame and sweet vinegar in cooked rice. And make 4 rice balls.
